## Not yet harvested

- **Radio Uswal Nasa Yuwe** (Internet Archive) — episode 10 only (other episodes weren't found via search). Direct download URL is in `_meta.json`. ~60 MB of conversational/educational audio in Nasa Yuwe; no transcript published.
- **ELAR `dk0381`** (Díaz-Montenegro deposit) — 30 hours of audio with 6 hours transcribed and Spanish-translated. **Highest-value oral resource that exists.** Requires ELAR/SOAS account.
- **Faith Comes By Hearing audio NT** (https://4.dbt.io) — full Nasa Yuwe NT with timestamp-aligned audio per verse. Useful for ASR or for sentence-level audio-text training if combined with the eBible NT in `02_bible_raw/`.
- **AILLA Tulio Rojas collection** — narrative recordings; needs AILLA account.
- **Global Recordings Network** Pitayo evangelistic audio (https://globalrecordings.net/en/language/15395) — short Christian audio messages.
- **Scripture Earth read-aloud audio** — available as `.mp3` per chapter at https://www.scriptureearth.org/data/pbb/MP3-Audio/ (not yet probed; should be directly downloadable).
