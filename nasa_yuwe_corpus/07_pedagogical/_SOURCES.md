# 07_pedagogical — Teaching materials and vocabulary cards

Materials designed for teaching Nasa Yuwe (mostly as L2 / heritage language). Almost entirely thematic vocabulary with parallel Spanish — good source of single-word and short-phrase pairs but heavily redundant with the dictionary corpora.

## What's in the catalog

`CRIC_UAIIN_PEBI_Catalogo_2024.pdf` lists every PEBI-CRIC educational publication available from the Centro de Documentación Indígena "José María Ulcué" in Popayán. Titles to ask for include:

- **Sa't Luuçx 1, 2** — bilingual literacy primers (Grades 1–2)
- **Lecto-Escritura Nasa Yuwe. Quichaya** — monolingual literacy material (48 pp)
- **La Comunicación Comunitaria 1 & 2** — ethnodidactics for grades 1–9
- **Tata Wala We'sxa' Na'ji'tx** — bilingual myth collection
- **NASAWE'SX JII** — bilingual Nasa worldview reader
- **Cartilla matemáticas Nasa** — bilingual math cartilla
- ... many more

Pricing: 17,000–37,000 COP per title (~$4–9 USD). Direct outreach to PEBI-CRIC via their Facebook page is the recommended path; they may share PDFs for research.

## Parallel-data value

The Kwesx_Yuwe thematic PDFs contain a few hundred word-image-translation triples each (e.g., "el sol" / "ahsx" / sun image). After OCR, they yield ~500–1,000 word-level pairs — but these almost entirely duplicate Living Dictionaries / Swarthmore. **Not a priority unless you want explicit thematic semantic-domain tags.**

The PEBI catalog itself contains zero parallel text — it's an index.
