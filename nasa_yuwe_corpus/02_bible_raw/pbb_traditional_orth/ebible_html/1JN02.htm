2
Dxus tasxte kwe'sx ju'gu we'we pu'çxsa' Kristo'
1 Adx luuçxwe'sx, pkalte fxi'zekahmenthu pta'sxna fxi'ja'. Txã'wẽtewa maaíi yu' pkalte wetewá'kwe nawa Dxus tasxte kwe'sx ju'gu we'we pu'çxsa ji'ptha'w, txã' Jesukristo' isasa yu's jxuka kxtey yuusáa. 2 Jesukristok kwe'sx pkal pa'gate vxite' naa kiwete nasa jxukaysa pkal pa'gate yã'jçxa ya'dukhçxa uu.
3 Dxus jxkaahnitxi's kxtey yuuna fxi'zeçxa yuuçxáa Dxusa's jiiyuhth sũhwa'ja's ya'ji'phuna. 4 Nawa maaíi yu' Dxusa's jiith jĩwáana nawa txajx jxkaahnitx kxtey nwẽese'jsame' txã' ĩsxiisa yuuna. 5 Maawa Dxus jxkaahnitx kxtey nwẽese'jena txã' Dxus peeygãani's ũuste jxuka ji'phsaçxa txã'wẽ yuuna. Txã'wẽ yuuçxáa Dxus yakh fxi'zena ũsne'tka'w sũhwa'ja's ya'ji'phuna. 6 Dxus yakh fxi'zena ũsthu jĩsa', Jesukristo na'wẽy kĩh pkal yuhwa ji'phmée fxi'zena.
Dxus jxkaahni u'sesa
7 Adx peeygãanisawe'sx, i'kwe'sxtxi' Dxus jxkaahni u'sesa pta'sxna fxi'jaçmeeth, Dxus jxkaahni nyafxíi pta'sxte iiwẽse'jyã'ji'kwe txãaíiçxá'. 8 Txã'wẽçxawa na'wẽ nyafxíi jxkaahni's kaayaki'jna fxi'jaçthu txã' u'sesa na'wẽ yuh kaseje'k. Txajíiçxáa i'kwe'sxa' Kristo's ũusuh jxuka peeygãhçxa', i'kwe'sx pwe'sxwa txã'wẽy yũuna fxi'zeçi'kwe. Txãa pa'ga ãçxha' skhẽuuça' jxpubana çxhi'dxte fxi'zenisa', aça' jxpubamée fxi'zeni eena'sa vxyaaça'.
9 Maaí' jxpubamée eena'te ũsthu jĩçxawa txajx yakhthẽ'ja's açena, txã' jxpubana çxhi'dxte fxi'zesaçxa txã'wẽ yuuna. 10 Jxpubamée eena'te fxi'zesa' txajx yakhthẽ'ja's ahçemée peeygãhna fxi'zena, kĩh yuhwa pkalte kweete'jwa'j yu'sa' vxitmée. 11 Nawa txajx yakhthẽ'ja's ahçena fxi'zesa' çxhi'dxte uyya' ãjamée mtee pa'jwa'ja's yuuwa jiimée jxpubana fxi'zesaçxa txã'wẽ yuuna.
12 Adx luuçxwe'sx, i'kwe'sx pkala' peltunãynitx yuu Jesukristo uute', txãa pa'gath fxi'ja'. 13 Txã'wẽy thẽ'jsawe'sx, i'kwe'sxa' na'wẽ nyafxíi entey Jesukristo' ũsyã'kh yu' txã'sna jiyupa'gath fxi'ja'. Sa' piçtakxwe'sx, eçxthẽ'ja's knayi'kwe txãa pa'gath fxi'ja'.
Adx luuçxwe'sx, Dxusa's jiyui'kwe txãa pa'gath fxi'ja'. 14 Thẽ'jsawe'sx, na'wẽ nyafxíi entey Jesukristo' ũsyã'kh yu' txã'sna jiyupa'gath fxi'ja'. Sa' piçtakxwe'sx, Dxus pta'sxni's ũuste yu'akhçxa eçxthẽ'ja's ũukhmée knayi'kwe txãa pa'gath fxi'ja'.
15 Ãçxha' kxazsay mpa'yajkxwe, naa kiwetewe'sxa's yajkxya'mée. Maawa naa kiwetewe'sxa's yaakxsa' Dxusa's yaakxsameeçxa txã'wẽ yuuna. 16 Txãa pa'gatey naa kiwete' na'wẽ yaakxnisaçxáa ũsta': naa kakwe' kazx yũuna fxi'zeni, yafxju uyçxa kazx ũus yaakxni, kĩhwa ji'phçxa' iiwejçx iiwejçx yajkxna fxi'zenisaçxáa ũsta'. Txã'wẽ ewmeesa yuuçxá'sku naa kiwe' ũsu', aça' txã'wẽsa' Dxus jxthãasnisame'. 17 Naa kiwete txã'wẽ yũuwa'jçxáana yajkxna fxi'zesa' jxuka pçuwa'jsata' petx naa kiwe yã'jçxa. Nawa Dxus jxthãasniçxá's kxtey yuusa', bagaçxte yuhwa pçumée ĩtxi fxi'zewa'ja's ji'phutxna.
Dxus Espiritu' kaajiyu'ju'k maa ĩsxiinisatewa, txã'wẽy maa isasatewa
18 Adx luuçxwe'sx, ãçxha' nmeh en ãjk. I'kwe'sxa' iiwẽse'jne'kwe Kristo's açesa pa'jana jĩni's. Txãasa ãçxha' wala kuh ũsta' Kristo's açesa. Txãa pa'gatha'w jiyu' nmeh en yuuçtewa. 19 Txãawe'sxa' kwe'sx yakh ũswa'jsamée ne'ta yu', txãasatx kwe'sx ksxavxyuh kaseh. Kwe'sx yakh ũswa'jsawaça', txuteewa'jmée ne'ta yu'. Jxukay yu' kwe'sx yakh fxi'zewa'jsameetxna, txãasatx txã'wẽ txutee.
20 Nawa i'kwe'sxa' Dxus Espiritu's ũuste ji'phi'kwe, txãasa maa isasatewa txã'sa' jxpubawa'jsamée i'kwe. 21 I'kwe'sxa' isasa's jxpubawa'jsamée i'kwe naa pa'gaíiçxáath fxi'ja', peekx jii i'kwe ĩsxiinisa' bagaçxte yuhwa isa kaseje'çme'.
22 ¿Kim txã'wẽ ĩsxiisa yuukx? Jesusa' Dxus kaahnisame' jĩna we'wesa txã'wẽ ĩsxiisa yuuna. Txã'wẽ we'wesa' Dxus Nçxi'knawa jxpa'gamée yã'jçxa', Neynawa jxpa'gasameeçxa txã'wẽ yuuna. 23 Maawa Dxus Nçxi'ka's açesa', Neynawa açesa yuuna. Nawa Dxus Nçxi'ka's jxpa'gasa', Neynawa jxpa'gasa yuuna.
24 Txajíi nyafxíi pta'sxni Dxus yuwe isasa's i'kwe'sx ũuste ew mjxaawwe. Txã'wẽ yũuçxa' Dxus Nçxi'k yakh txutemée fxi'zene'kwe, sa' Ney yakhwa txã'wẽy txutesamée yuune'kwe. 25 Aça' txã'wẽ yũusatxi' Jesukristo' peesya' neeyũuk bagaçxte yuhwa pçumée ĩtxi fxi'zewa'ja's.
26 I'kwe'sxtxi maawa kaypuba'jwe'sxteth pta'sxi' jxpa'yakxkahn. 27 Nawa i'kwe'sxa' Dxus Espiritu's ũuste ji'phi'kwe na' maa ĩsxiinisana aça' vxite' maa isasatewa thẽymée jiyune'kwe, txãa pa'gatey Dxus Espiritu' kĩhtewa jxukak kaajiyu'ju', sa' txajx kaapiya'jni' ĩsxiinisameeta'. Txã'wẽ jiyuçxa', Kristo yakh txutemée fxi'zene'kwe.
28 Adx luuçxwe'sx, ãçxha' Kristo yakh txutemée mfxi'zewe. Txã'wẽ fxi'zeçxa', thaamemée weçxana jxpa'ga'nja'w Kristo' naa kiwete kĩhçxa vxyaatewa. 29 I'kwe'sxa' jiiyã'ji'kwe Jesukristo' kĩhtewa jxuka ew yũusaatewa, aça' maawa txãa na'wẽy kĩhtewa jxuka ew yũuna fxi'zeçxa', Dxus luuçxçxa txã'wẽ yuuna.
