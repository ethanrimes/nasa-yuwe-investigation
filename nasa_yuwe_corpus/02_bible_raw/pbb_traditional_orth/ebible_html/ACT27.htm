27
Pablo's Roma çxhabna kaahni
1 Italia kiwen kwe'sxtxi kaajxa' puutx we'weyã'jçxa', Pablo vxite preesu yã'jçxa dukhtxi Batalxón Augusto yaasesate kpiitan Julio kusete. 2 Txajũ' Adramitio çxhabte barko Asia kiwen u'jsate khẽetha'w, aça' Aristarkowa yuwe'k, Macedonia kiwete Tesalónika çxhabtewe'sx. 3 Kuskuskhẽ' Sidón çxhabte pa'tha'w. Aça' kpiitan Julio' Pablo's namiku yat-suk pa'jya' kaah txãawe'sx sã'jĩkahn. Txãa kpiitana' Pablo yakh wala ewsa yu'k, txãasak txã'wẽ yũu. 4 Sidón çxhabuh kasehçxa', Çxipre kiwe's jebu ju'gutha'w nvxihtna khẽw, wejxa yuppa'ga. 5 Txajũ' Cilicia Panfxilia kiwesu utxa skhẽwna u'jçxa, Lisia kiwete Mira çxhabtetha'w pa'j.
6 Kxtea' soldau kpiitana' teeçx barko Alejadriajuwe'sx Italia kiwen u'jsa'sku uy. Sa' kwe'sxtxi kxtek kaateka'j txajũ ki' u'jya'. 7 Nawa txã'wẽ wejxa yupna ũste', wala kuh en tuudkuẽçxah u'jçxa, wala thẽy pa'tha'w Gnido çxhab dxi'pte. Txajũ u'jçxa', Salmon kiwee dxi'psutha'w skhẽw, Kreta kiwesu ta'guna. 8 Sa' txãa kiwe pukasu wala thẽy u'jçxa, teeçx ahte pa'tha'w Bwenos Pwertos yaasesate. Txã' Lasea çxhab utxatek neeyũ'.
9 Txã'wẽ thẽy u'jçxa wala en ivxiitpa'ga, nus pa'jwa'j en utxaak ĩkhte u'jwa'j seena' ũukhũukhsa. Txãa pa'gak Pablo' yu'kxpeh 10 na'jĩna:
—Jxukaysa mwẽese'jwe, kwe'sx u'jwa'ja' wala ũukhũukha'. Barko petx karga vxituna, aça' kwe'sxwa uuwa'ja's ji'phuwá'nja'w.
11 Txã'jĩtewa, soldau kpiitana' Pablo's nwẽese'jmée barko namu vxite' barko thegsawe'sx we'weniçxá'sku nwẽese'j. 12 Kxte çxhaba' nus ente ũswa'j ewmeek yu'. Txãa pa'gatx jxukaysa u'jya' yajkx Kreta kiwete Fxenice çxhabte pa'jçxa nus ena's kxte yu'skhẽwya' sũhçxa, Fxenice çxhabte' nus ente ũswa'j ũukhũukh meek yu' naa pa'ga.
Mar pxãhte wejxaa wala kiiteni
13 Bwenos Pwertos yaasesajũ kasejeçte yu', wejkuẽçxáak wejxana ũsu', aça' Fxenice çxhabte pa'jya' ãjawá'nja'w sũhçxa u'jtx Kreta kiwe pukasu tuudkuẽçxah. 14 Nawa wejkuẽ u'jweçpkaçxha', wejxaa wala kwe'sx u'jwa'j dxi'puh sẽhçxa barko's yupku. 15 Sa' txajũyã'ja' ma'wẽwa jxũna u'kh. Aça' barko's kaajxa' ãjameeyã'jçxa', kuçx yuumeetha'w wejxa ma'wẽwa jxũna u'khan. 16 Ĩkh pxãhte kiwe le'çxkuẽsa Klauda yaasesa e'ssutha'w skhẽw, kxsũa' wejxa txakwe çxhãçxhameek ũs yu'. Aça' barko le'çxkuẽ jxũçxa u'jnisa's wala thẽyçxáawa kaateka'jya' ãjãtha'w barko walate. 17 Txajũ' barko wala's lasu walaju pxãjũjx tudtha'w suweekahmen. Sa' txitx wala Sirte yaasesate wáa waçuya' u'jwena sũhna ũukhçxa, ẽete tela wala's spajkxtxi wejxa ma'wẽwa barko's jxũna u'khan. 18 Txã'wẽ wejxa seena' çxhãçxha ũste', kuskuskhẽ' karga's ĩkhkhẽ pũsxya' takhtxi. 19 Aça' tekh en yuuçte', kwe'sxíiwa ĩkhkhẽ pũsxya' pu'çxtha'w barko ẽseekahn. 20 Txã'wẽ wejxa seena' yuuna ũste', sek, estrelxa, kĩh yuhwa vxyaameeta' maazkuẽe enwa. Aça' kwe'sx yuuwa ĩtxĩ yuume'nja'w sũhwa'ja'sçxáatha'w ji'phu'.
21 Txã'wẽ maazkuẽe en kĩh yuhwa ũ'mée yuhna ũssatx Pablo' na'wẽk we'we:
—I'kwe'sxa' adx we'weni's nwẽese'jçxa Kreta kiwejũ kasehmeewaça', na'wẽ nxus knayna ivxiituwa'jmeene'tka'w yu'. 22 Nawa ãçxha' ũukhnuwe. Ayte u'jsa kim yuhwa uumeetxna, makwe barko yu' suweeçxa vxitutewa. 23 Adxa' Dxusna selpisaath, aça' ãçxh kus Dxus angel vxyaaçxa 24 na'wẽk we'we: Pablo, ũukhnu. Idxa' Romate jxkaahsa tasxte utxaawa'j ji'phgu, aça' txãa pa'ga Dxusa' nwe'weya'wa' ay barkote u'jsa jxukaysatx jĩk. 25 Txãa pa'ga i'kwe'sxa' ũukhmée çxhãçxha miiyajkxwe. Adxa' Dxusçxáanath yaaki', angel we'weni' kxtey yuuna sũhna. 26 Sa' ĩkh pxãhte kiwe le'çxkuẽsate nvxiitni yu'nja'w jĩk Pablo'.
27 Adria ĩkhte wejxa jxũna u'jni e'z ki'su ãjaçte', txãa kuspxãh yuuçpkaçxha', barkote thegsawe'sxa' kiwe utxasu u'jweççxatx jiyu. 28 Sa' makhwe ĩkh dxiitewa jiyuya' isatx, aça' treinta y seis metroçxáak dxii yu'. Txajũ' yaç yaçte u'jçxa ki' isatx, aça' kxtea' weintisiete metroçxáak dxii yu'. 29 Txajũ' penxate wáa iika'nja'w sũhna ũukhçxa, barko's neewewa'jsa pahz çam walatx ĩkhkhẽ akh barko e'skhẽ, wejxa jxũna u'khamen. Sa' wala ũukhna ũythastxi khijkxpkaçx. 30 Naapkaçxha' barkote thegsawe'sxa' barko yaçkhẽ vxite çam walatx ĩkhkhẽ akheçtha'w jĩnaçxáa barko le'çxkuẽ's ĩkhkhẽ spaakiçtxi yu', txãate khẽeçxa kasehna u'j wẽeçxa. 31 Nawa Pablo' pta'sxku soldau kpiitan vxite' soldauwe'sxtxi:
—Naawe'sxa' barkojũ kasehna u'the', i'kwe'sxa' ĩtxi yuumeene'kwe jĩte', 32 soldauwe'sxa' barko le'çxkuẽ tudni lasutx spẽ'thçxa ĩkhkhẽ wãatahtx barko le'çxkuẽ's.
33 Txajũ' Pablo' kusíitey neewe'wek jxukaysatx kĩhçxáawa ũ'kahn:
—I'kwe'sx dehmée txã'wẽy ũ'wamée ũsni e'z ki'su yã'j yuuça'. 34 Aça' i'kwe'sxtxi neewe'we'th kĩhçxáawa ũ'kahn. Txã'wẽme', kasejxa' ya'çxhãçxhameena. Ãçxha' ũukhnuwe. I'kwe'sx maa yuhwa vxitumeene'kwe.
35 Txã'jĩna'wa, Pablo' jxukaysa dxi'pte Dxusa's weçxaçxa pana's ũ'k. 36 Txajũ' jxukaysa wejx ũukhmée txãawe'sxwa ũ'tx. 37 Barkote u'jsa jxukaçxah yu' doscientos setenta y seis nasatha'w yu'. 38 Kĩh ũ' wẽeçxawa txã'sna ũ'na jxã'jçxa', skuutxha's ĩkhkhẽ pũsxtha'w barko jweeíiçxáa ẽseekahn.
Barko ĩkh dxiikhẽ khẽeni
39 Khijkxna jxã'jnite', barkote thegsawe'sxa' jiyumeeta' maa kiwete pa'jçxawa. Nawa ĩkh pukasu kiwe ukwesa u'pte uyçxa', kxte nuypa'jya' ãjãwá'nja'w sũhçxa kxtetx u'jya' yajkx. 40 Sa' çam wala ĩkh dxiikhẽ akhnisa lasutx spẽ'thna'wa, tela's ẽete ki' kiisçxa, barko's tuudkuẽçxah jxũna u'jtx ukwete pa'jya'. 41 Nawa teeçx ahte ĩkh jwee yu' dxiimeete yu' seena' çxhãçxha ukapajkxna ũsu'k kxtek kũjxa' u'j barko'. Aça' barko ĩçha' kiwete waçuna kũhte', ma'wẽ yuhwa ẽseme', naapkaçxha' barko e'su yu'tũçxhasáa ujkana kũhçxa jxuka suwuuçku yu'. 42 Txã'wẽ yuuçte', soldauwe'sxa' preesutx jxuka ikhya'watx yu', pehdna kasehçxa iyuwe'sxte. 43 Nawa soldau kpiitana' Pablo's nwe'weya' yajkxku, sa' kiikhe'jmeeçxa na'wẽk jxkaah:
—Maawa pehdxa' jiisa' i'kwe'sx khuẽ'y yaçte me'jwe, 44 naapkaçxha' vxite' taplasu mkhẽewe, meeçxawa barko pe'lasu txã'wẽ yũuçxa mkaasehwe jĩte', txã'wẽ yũuçxa jxukay kiwete pa'tha'w uumée.
