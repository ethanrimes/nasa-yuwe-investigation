15
Vxitesa yakh ewçxáa yũuna fxi'zewa'ja'
1 Dxus yakh çxhãçxhayã'j fxi'zesa' peekxçxáa wẽt fxi'zewa'ja's pakwemée, pu'çxwa'j ji'ptha'w Dxus yakh fxi'zewa'ja's açxamée ew jiisatx. 2 Kwe'sxa' teeçxsa na'wẽçxah vxite nasatx pu'çxna wẽt kaafxi'ze'jwa'j ji'ptha'w ewçxáa yũuna, txãawe'sxa' Dxus yakh jweeíiçxáa çxhãçxha fxi'zeya' ãjakahn. 3 Txãa pa'gatey Kristo txakwe jxtuhthesa yuhçxawa, peekxçxáa wẽt fxi'zewa'j yu'sa' pakwemée, wejxwa vxitetx wẽesxuni's txãak jxpa'ga. Txã'sna we'weçxak Dxus librute na'jĩ': Idxna wẽesxuni's adxthu jxpa'ga jĩ'k. 4 Dxus librute fxi'jni ũsta' txã' kwe'sxtxi kçxhãaçxha'jwa'jsata', nxuswa'j en ma'wẽsa pa'thewa nxusmée çxhãçxha yajkxna fxi'zeya' piyakahn. 5 Nxuswa'j en pa'thewa çxhãçxha fxi'zewa'ja's Dxusku ũsu' na' txãaíi pu'çxhina puutx ewçxáa yũuna Kristo Jesus na'wẽy fxi'zekahn, 6 kxah kxteeçxah jxukaysa kwe'sxtxi jxpe'jsa Jesukristo Tata yuuçxá's tuuthe'jene'kwe.
Judiu ji'jmée vxite nasatx Dxus yuwe's pta'sxni
7 Kristowa ma'wẽga i'kwe'sxtxi jxpa'ga txã'wẽy i'kwe'sxwa wa'lmée mpa'gawe maa yakhthẽ'jtxwa, Dxus weçxakahn. 8 Miiyajkxwe: Dxusa' kwe'sx yaçgawe'sx yakh puutx we'wek Kristo kĩiwa'ja's, aça' txãa puutx we'weni kxtey yuukahnku Kristo' kĩh judiuwe'sxtxi selpiiya'. 9 Sa' judiu ji'jmée vxite nasawa Dxusa's jxpa'gaçxa weçxana tuuthe'khanku Kristo' kĩh. Txã'sna we'weçxak Dxus librute na'jĩ':
Txã'wẽ ew yũupa'ga txãawe'sx ksxavxyte idxa's weçxana memna tuuthe'je'nja jĩ'k.
10 Sa' vxite ajkhẽwa na'wẽk we'we':
Judiu ji'jmée vxite nasaçxawa judiuwe'sx yakh ja'daçxah wẽtwẽt miiyajkxwe jĩ'k.
11 Sa' vxite ajkhẽwa pta'sxi' íiçxáak:
Judiu ji'jmée vxite nasa jxukaysawe'sx Dxusa's weçxana mtuuthe'jwe,
Dxusa' seena' yuh walasa' jĩna nasa jxukaytxi pta'sxna.
12 Txajíiçxáak Isaiaswa na'jĩna fxi'j:
Jesé ji'j teeçx kasejena, judiu ji'jmée vxite nasatx jxuka yamuya'.
Aça' txãawe'sxa' nwe'wenisa yuutxna jĩ'k.
13 Kwe'sxtxi nwe'wesa Dxusa' i'kwe'sx ũuste wẽtwẽt weçxana fxi'zewa'ja's ũsuna txã'sna jxpa'gasatxi's, kxah Dxus Espiritu pu'çxte', Dxus nwe'wenisatha'w yuu sũhna weçxana fxi'zene'kwe.
14 Adx yakhthẽ'jwe'sx, adxa' ew jiith i'kwe'sxa' ew yũuwa'j yuuçxá's ne'kwe ũuste ji'ph fxi'ze', ma'wẽ yũuwa'ja'swa sxiigmée. Txãasa ne'kwe teeçxsa na'wẽçxah puutx ew ya'yu'kxpehna fxi'ze' sũju'th. 15 I'kwe'sxa' txã'wẽyã'j sxiigmée fxi'zesa ne'kwe, nawa adxwa fxii yu'kxpehna fxi'jaçthu Dxus yakh wejx ew fxi'zewa'ja's kaajiyu'jna. 16 Txã'wẽ yũuwa'ja's Dxusku adxa's neewe'weçxa nvxiht, judiu ji'jmée vxite nasatx Jesukristo kaapiya'jni's pta'sxkahn. Txãa Jesukristo kaapiya'jnisaíi Dxus yuwe ewte nvxiitwa'jsa' txã'snath adxa' pta'sxi', judiu ji'jmée vxite nasaçxawa Dxus Espiritu pu'çxte', Dxusa's jiyuçxa ewte neeyũukahn.
17 Txãasath adx ũuste wẽtwẽt yaaki', Jesukristo kaapiya'jni's pta'sxna Dxusa's selpi'th sũhna. 18 Nawa txã'wẽ yũuwa'ja's Kristok adxa's pu'çx judiu ji'jmée vxite nasaçxawa Dxusa's nwẽese'khan, txãawe'sx ew jiyukahn yuwejuwa pta'sxna, kim yuhwa ãjanimeesatx ãatewa kaavxya'ja'jnik yuu Dxus Espiritu pu'çxte. 19 Txã'wẽ yũunath Kristo yuwe ewte nvxiitwa'jsa's mtee mteewa pta'sxna jxã'j, Jerusalente takhçxa Ilíriko kiwete pa'phkaçx. 20 Txã'wẽ vxite pta'sxnisu yu' pekũjxa'meeth yajkx, wejxwa bagaçxteçxáa yuhwa Kristo yase's kxsusnimeesuth txajx yuwe ewsa's pta'sxya' yajkx, Dxus librute na'jĩ'k txãa kxtey yuukahn:
21 Txãawe'sxa' bagaçxteçxáa yuhwa Dxus yuwe's pta'sxni meesaçxawa, wẽse'jetxna,
Sa' jiyutxna, bagaçxteçxáa yuhwa jiimeesaçxawa jĩ'k.
Pablo Roma çxhabna u'jya' yaakxni
22 Txã'wẽ Dxus yuwe's kaapiya'jna u'juçxath i'kwe'sx tasxna yujxa' yu' tudu'çmée, maz uswa yujxa' yajkxçxawa. 23-24 Nawa ãçxha' naa ja'dasu pekũhna jxã'th na' Espanxa kiwen u'jweççxa', i'kwe'sx tasxsuwa iipa'j uykhẽu'nja txã'wẽ yũuwa'ja's nyafxiith yaakxni ji'phu'. Aça' i'kwe'sx yakh pkhaakhena ũsçxa Espanxa kiwen u'jweçte', pu'çxhine'kwe sũju'th. 25 Nawa ãçxhíi yu' Jerusalenna u'jweçthu kxtee yakhthẽ'jwe'sxtxi kĩhçxáawa pu'çxwa'j jxũna. 26 Jerusalente yakhthẽ'jwe'sx kĩhwa ji'phmeesa ũsta' txãawe'sxtxi pu'çxya' jĩçxa vxyuu pkhakhtxi Macedonia vxite' Akaya kiwesuwe'sx txã'sna jxũna u'jweçthu. 27 Txã'wẽ pu'çxte ewna sũhwa'ja's ji'phutx, txãa pa'gatey isa ew yajkxte yu' Macedoniasuwe'sxa' yuultxi yu', txã'wẽ judiu ji'jwe'sxa' Dxus yuwe ewsa's jxtxuhdetx judiu ji'jmée vxite nasatxwa. Txãa pa'gatx Dxus yuwe ewsa's kwe'sxtxi pta'sxpa'ga ka'tuwa kaasxwedu'jwa'j ji'ptha'w jĩçxa txãawe'sx ji'phunisa yakh pu'çxya' yajkx. 28 Aça' txã'wẽ Jerusalente pa'jçxa' pkhakhni vxyu's txãawe'sxtxi dukhçxa', Espanxa kiwen u'jya'wath, txãasa i'kwe'sx tasxsuwa dudte ne'ka'nja. 29 Adx i'kwe'sx tasxte pa'the', i'kwe'sxa' jweeíiçxáa Kristo yakh çxhãçxha fxi'zene'kwe sũju'th.
30 Yakhthẽ'jwe'sx, i'kwe'sxtxith neewe'we': peeygãawa'ja's Dxus Espiritu ũste ji'phi'kwe, txãasa adxa's peeygãhçxa Jesukristo's pi'kxçxa adx pa'gate Dxus yakh puutx we'wekahn. 31 Judea kiwete açesa ũsta' txãawe'sx adxna kĩhwa ewmée yũukahmén Dxusa's mpi'kxwe, sa' Jerusalente yakhthẽ'jwe'sxtxi pu'çxwa'j jxũna u'jweçthu txã'snawa weçxana jxpa'gakahn, 32 kxah Dxus pu'çxte', ewçxáa ki' sxawedçxa i'kwe'sx yakh weçxana ũswa'ja's ji'phu'nja. 33 Dxusa' i'kwe'sx jxukaysa yakh pi'kxna nes yuuna, ewçxáa kaafxi'ze'jna.
