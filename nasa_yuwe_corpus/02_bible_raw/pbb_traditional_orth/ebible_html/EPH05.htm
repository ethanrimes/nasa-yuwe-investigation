5
Dxus luuçxyã'ja' txãa na'wẽy fxi'zeni
1 I'kwe'sxa' Dxus luuçx yã'ji'kwe na' txãa na'wẽy fxi'zeya' mpa'pçxuwe. 2 Puutx ya'peeygãhna mfxi'zewe, txã'wẽ yũukahníiçxáak Kristowa kwe'sxtxi peeygãhçxa Dxus dxi'pte peekíi ya'dukhçxa piisxakuẽ na'wẽ ikhni yuu.
3 Txãa pa'ga i'kwe'sxa' Dxus jĩiyã'jçxa', ptamsa ma'tx yuu meeçxa' ptamsamée ma'tx yuu nawa kazx yuuna fxi'zeni's jxuka nvxihtçxa ũusteçxáa yuhwa yaakxwa'j ji'phmeei'kwe. Sa' Dxus yu's yajkxmée ji'phu wẽeniçxá's yaakxwa'j ji'phmeei'kwe. Kĩhwa kazxsa yu'sa' kxsuswa'jçxáa yuhwa ji'phmeei'kwe. 4 Thaampa' yũuni kĩh yuhwa seelpimeesa yu'sa' txã'wẽ we'wena fxi'zewa'jme', wejxwa Dxusa'sçxáa weçxana fxi'zewa'ja'. 5 I'kwe'sxa' jii yã'ji'kwe: Dxus yu'sa' yajkxmée ji'phu wẽeniçxá's yajkxna fxi'zesa (txã'wẽ ji'phu wẽeniçxá's yajkxte', txã'sçxáanak dxus na'wẽ yaakxwa'j kaseje'), kazx pkal yũuna fxi'zesa, kĩhwa kazxsaçxá's kxtey yuuna fxi'zesa, txã'wẽ yũusa' Dxus Kristo yakh jxkaahna ũsnite u'kaya' ãjameetxna. 6 Txãa pa'ga txã'wẽ pkal kazxsate fxi'zewa'ja's kaapiya'jni's mpa'yajkxwe. Txã'wẽme' txãa pa'gaíiçxáa Dxus kastigo seena'sa yuja' Dxus jxkaahni's nwẽese'jmée fxi'zesawe'sx jĩi. 7 Txãawe'sx yakh ja'da ewmée yũuna fxi'zenuwe.
8 Nyafxiia' i'kwe'sxa' jxpubana ũsçxa', çxhi'dxte na'wẽi'kwe fxi'zesa yu'. Nawa ãçxha' Kristo's ũuste jxpa'ganijũ' eena'te ũsi'kwe. Txãa pa'ga peena yu' jxpubamée eena'teçxáa mfxi'zewe Kristo nwe'wenijũyã'ja'. 9 Eena'te fxi'zeççxa', puutx peeygãhna isasaçxá's we'wena fxi'zewa'ja', Dxus dxi'pte jxpubamée. 10 Naasáa Kristo jxthãasniçxá's kxtey yuuya' mpa'pçxuwe. 11 Jxpubana çxhi'dxte ũssa yakh ja'da ewmée yũuna fxi'zenuwe, wejx yuuwa txã'wẽ yũusatxi' mkaapiya'jwe ewmée yũukahmen. 12 Txã'wẽme', paaçte txãawe'sx ewmée yũuna fxi'zeni's ãate we'wewẽete thaame yuja'. 13 Nawa txãawe'sx ewmée kazx yũuna fxi'zeni's thaamemée ãate we'wewa'ja', kxah kĩh yuhwa paaçtemée jxuka ãateçxaçxáa kaavxya'ja'jni yuuna. 14 Txã'sna we'weçxak na'jĩ':
Jxpa'yakxmeeg fxi'ze', miiyajkx.
Sa' pkalte uuníi na'wẽ fxi'zenijũ mkaaseh,
Aça' Kristo' eena'te kaafxi'ze'jena jĩ'k.
15 Txãa pa'ga wala jxpa'yajkx fxi'zewa'jçxá's dud mpa'pçxuwe, jiimeesa na'wẽ jxpa'yajkxmée yu' fxi'zenuwe. 16 Txã'wẽme' naa ensu' nxuswa'j ençxáa kasejeça', naa pa'ga en ewsatxi' ivxiitunuwe. 17 I'kwe'sxa' jxpa'yajkxmée yu' fxi'zemée, ewsa's jiyuwa'ja's mpa'pçxuwe, Kristo jxthãasniçxá's ew jiyuya'. 18 Txãasa tũuna fxi'zenuwe. Txã'wẽme', tũunisa' vxituwa'the pe'jna u'jsa'. Aça' txã'wẽ yũumée wejxwa Dxus Espirituçxá's ũuste jxuka jxpa'gaçxa, 19 i'kwe'sx pwe'sx jxukaysa Dxusa'sçxáa ũusuh jxuka yajkxna weçxana memna meswe. 20 Makwe wala thẽysa vxyaatewa, txãatxi's nxusmée bagaçxtewa Dxusçxáana weçxana meswe, Jesukristo's pi'kxçxa.
Jxukaysa puutx ya'nwẽese'jna fxi'zeni
21 Ãçxha' i'kwe'sx pwe'sx jxukaysa puutx ya'nwẽese'jna mfxi'zewe, Dxusa's yajkxna. 22 Nyuua' nmi'a's nwẽese'jwa'j ji'pha', Kristo' txã'wẽk jxkaaja' naa pa'ga. 23 Txãa pa'gatey nmi' txã'wẽ nyuua's thegna jxpe'jwa'jsa', Kristowa ma'wẽga txajx nwe'wenisatxi's thegna jxpe'je' txã'wẽy. Aça' Kristo' ĩ'neeçxáa thegna jxpe'jmée, vxitukahmen nwe'wesa'. 24 Aça' Kristo nwe'wenisawa ma'wẽta Kristo's nwẽese'je', txã'wẽy nyuuwe'sxwa nwẽese'jwa'j ji'phta' txãawe'sx nmi'tx kĩhtewa jxuka.
25 Ãçxha' nmi'a' nyuua's peeygãawa'j ji'pha', Kristowa ma'wẽga txajx nwe'wenisatx peeygãhçxa txãawe'sx pa'gate kluuste uu txã'wẽy. 26 Kristo' txã'wẽk yuu txajx nwe'wenisa nasatx kĩh pkal yuhwa ji'phmée jxuka yaatena pa'jçxa, 27 txajx tasxte iiméh zhiçxkwe pheu'jni txãa yuuçxá's selpiwa'jsa vxit-ya'k txã'wẽ yũu. 28 Nmi'a' txã'wẽ peekx nyuua's peeygãawa'j ji'pha' peekx kakwe'swa ma'wẽga ya'peeygãja' txã'wẽy, txãa pa'gatey nyuua' peekx kakweíiçxá' naa pa'ga. 29 Kim yuhwa peekx kakwe's açe'çme', wejxwa wala ew jxpa'yaki'k. Aça' Kristowa txã'wẽyku txajx nasatxi' wala ew jxpa'yakxna jxpe'je'. 30 Txãa pa'gatey txãa jĩi yã'tha'w, txajx tasxte txuteeya'mée utxasáa. 31 Txã'wẽyku ya'yu', ptamusawa peekx neywe'sxtxi nvxihtçxa nyuu yuuwa'jsa yakh ya'utxa'k txuteeya'mée, aça' e'zçxawa teeçxçxáak yuuna pa'ja'. 32 Ptamuuçxa fxi'zenisa' Kristo txajx nwe'wenisa kxteeçxah utxaaçxa teeçxçxáa yuuna pa'jwa'ja's kaajiyu'jnisa', aça' txã'wẽ teeçxçxáa yuuna pa'jwa'ja's kim yuhwa jiimeetx yu' txã'snath pta'sxi'. 33 Aça' ptamsayã'ja', teeçxsa na'wẽçxah peekx nyuutx peeygãhna fxi'zewa'ja', i'kwe'sx kakwetx ma'wẽkwe peeygãja' txã'wẽy. Aça' nyuua' peekx nmi'a's nwẽese'jna fxi'zewa'ja'.
