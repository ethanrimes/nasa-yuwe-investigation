16
Yakhthẽ'jwe'sxtxi pu'çxwa'j vxyu pkhakhni
1 Nyafxíi Galaciate ũsçxa', Dxus luuçxtxi kaapiya'th Dxus yakh fxi'zesatx pu'çxwa'j ma'wẽ vxyu's pkhakhwa'ja'swa aça' txã'sa' kxtey myũuwe. 2 Ki'sen iisa teeçxsa na'wẽçxah i'kwe'sx ji'phuni's makwe ũswa'ja'swa txuhdeçxa jxaawna me'jwe, adx pa'the pkhakhna ũsya'mée. 3 Kxte adx pa'jçxa' txãa pkhakhni vxyu'sa' Jerusalenna kaja'nja, aça' u'jwa'jsa pakweene'kwe txãawe'sxtxi karta ũsu'nja, Jerusalente yakhthẽ'jwe'sxtxi dukhkahn petx txãa vxyu yã'jçxa. 4 Meeçxa' adx yuu wáa u'jwa'j kasejena aça' u'jwe'nja, nawa txãawe'sxwa adx yakh pi'kxna yuweetxna.
Pablo u'jya' yaakxni
5 Adxa' yuhçxa', Macedonia kiwey uykhẽwna yuwe'nja i'kwe'sx tasxte pa'jya'. 6 Kxte pa'jçxa' maz en jxthẽeyuuwá'nja meeçxa' nus en skhẽwpkaçx. Aça' i'kwe'sxa' pu'çxhine'kwe txãajũ adx ki' u'jwa'ja's. 7 Dudteçxáa i'kwe'sx yakh puutx uyya' yuu wẽje'çmeeth. Kxte txaçxthu ũs wẽje' i'kwe'sx yakh, Dxus txã'wẽ wedxwaça'. 8 Nawa ayte Efxesote nee ũsya'wath, Pentekostes fxiesta skhẽwpkaçx. 9 Txã'wẽ açesa ũstewa, Dxus jĩi mjĩiwa'j wala ew yuuça' naa pa'ga txã'sa' ivxiitume'nja sũju'th.
10 Timoteo pa'jawáana aça' mpu'çxwe, kĩhtewa nxusmée wẽt i'kwe'sx yakh ũskahn. Txã'wẽme', txãawa adx na'wẽyçxáa Jesukristo's selpisá'. 11 Txãa pa'ga kimwa açenuwe, wejxwa mpu'çxwe dxi'the peejxmée yuuwa'ja's, kxah adx tasxte ki' sxawedna pa'jya' ãjana. Txã'wẽme', adxa' Timoteo's ũythasna ũsthu ayte yakhthẽ'jwe'sx yakh.
12 Apolosa's wala pxthaa we'weth yakhthẽ'jwe'sx yakh i'kwe'sxtxi puutx uyya' u'khan. Nawa ãçxhíi yu' duduuçme', sa' maa ente u'jwa'j kasehteçxáawa yuweena.
Yuwe weçxaa kaahnitx pta'sxna fxi'jni
13 Jxpa'yakx çxhãçxha mfxi'zewe, Dxus yuuçxá's ũuste peçxkanamée yajkxna. 14 Kĩh yũuççxawa peeygãawa'ja's yuuçxáa yajkxna txã'wẽ myũuwe.
15 Yakhthẽ'jwe'sx, jiine'kwe Akaya kiwete nyafxçxah Kristo yuwe ewsa's jxpa'gasa' Estefxanaswe'sxta', sa' txãawe'sxa' wala selpiitx Dxus tasxte utxasaawe'sxtxi pu'çxna. 16 Aça' i'kwe'sxa' peeygãawa'j ji'phi'kwe txãawe'sxtxi's meeçxa' maawa txãawe'sx na'wẽy pu'çxna selpisaatx. 17 Adxa' wala weçxa'th, Estéfxanas, Fxortunato, Akáiko txãawe'sx ayte pa'pha'ga. I'kwe'sxa' ayte adx yakh ũsmeeçxa', adxa's pu'çxya' ãja'çmeei'kwe. 18 Nawa txãawe'sxa' adxa's kçxhãaçxha'jtx Dxus yakh weçxana fxi'zewa'ja's, sa' i'kwe'sxtxiwa txã'wẽy ne'ta yuu. Aça' nasa txãawe'sx na'wẽysatxi' peeygãawa'ja'.
19 Dxus yakh fxi'zesawe'sxa' Asia kiwete yuwe weçxaa kaja'tx. Txã'wẽy Akila ptamwa yuwe weçxaa kaja'tx, kxtee pkhaakhesawe'sx yã'jçxa. 20 Yakhthẽ'jwe'sx jxukaysa yuwe weçxaa kaahni's mpa'gawe, sa' teeçxsa na'wẽçxah puutx mweeçxawe.
21 Pablo yaasesath adx kuseju fxi'ja' i'kwe'sxtxi weçxana. 22 Maaíi yu' Jesukristo's açesa ũswáatxna, aça' txãawe'sxtxi' Dxus kastigote ivxiituwa'j yuutxna. Kwe'sxtxi jxpe'jsa Jesukristo dud kĩhwaça' sũju'th.
23 Jesukristo' i'kwe'sxtxi bedekĩina. 24 I'kwe'sx jxukaysatxthu adx peeygãani's kaja', Jesukristo's neewe'wena.
