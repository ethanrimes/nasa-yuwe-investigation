Selpiiya' ãjawa'ja's Dxus ma'wẽ ũste' kxtey yuuwa'ja'
1-2 Kristowa ma'wẽga txajx kakwete akasa's jxpa'gana pxthaa yuu, txã'wẽy i'kwe'sxwa pxthaa yuuwa'jsatha'w sũhna meswe, txãa pa'gatey peekx kakwete ewmée yũuya' yaakxni pkala's nvxihtçxa, Dxus jxthãasniçxá's uu entepkaçx kxtey yuuya' yaakxpa'gak pxthaa yuuwa'j en pa'ja'. 3 Pkala's nwẽese'jmée nvxiht-ya'íi nyafxíi entey wala yã'jne'kwe nwẽese'j Dxusa's jiimeesawe'sx yakh ja'daçxah. Txãa ensu' i'kwe'sxa' ewmée i'kwe yũuna fxi'ze peekx kakweju ewmée yũuya' yajkxna, tũuna, ku'jna, pkhaakheçxa tudxna, Dxus ĩtximeesatx neewe'wena. 4 Txã'wẽ yũusai'kwe yu'. Nawa ãçxha' i'kwe'sxa' peena yu' txãawe'sx yakh txã'wẽ iiméh seena' ewmée yũuna fxi'ze'çmeene'kwe, txãa pa'ga ne'ta i'kwe'sxtxi' ewmée nuywe'wena açe'. 5 Nawa txãawe'sxa' Dxusna paswa'j ji'phutxna, ĩtxisa ma'tx yuu meeçxa uuniisa ma'tx yuu nawa jxukaysatx paapẽywa'j ente'. 6 Uusaawe'sx ma'tx yuu nawa paapẽy ente' kakwe ji'phsa na'wẽyçxáa paapẽyni yuutxna. Aça' uute' ũus yu' Dxus na'wẽyçxáa ĩtxi ũskahnku txã'wẽ pta'sxni yuu. Txã'wẽ yuuwa'jsate íiçxáak uusaawe'sxtxiwa Dxus yuwe's pta'sxni yuu txãawe'sx uumeyna.
7 Naa kiwe pçuwa'j ena' utxaaça'. Txãa pa'ga i'kwe'sxa' wala jxpa'yakx meswe, naasáa Dxus yakh yuuçxáa puutx we'wena mfxi'zewe. 8 Nawa i'kwe'sx pwe'sx puutx peeygãawa'ja's wejx jxthaakwe mpa'pçxuwe, txãa pa'gatey peeygãanisa' pkal makwesanawa jxuka kaapeltunaĩ'kh jĩ'k. 9 Yakhthẽ'jwe'sxtxi yat mpeeki'jwe wa'lmée. 10 Teeçxsa na'wẽçxah puutx ya'mpu'çxwe, i'kwe'sx selpiiya' ãjawa'ja's Dxus ma'wẽne' ũs txã'sa' kxtey yuuna. Dxusa's selpiwa'ja's nakwe wala kuh jwed ũsni yuuk txã'sa' jxpa'gaçxa', ew kaaselpi'jsa myuuwe. 11 Maaíia' pta'sxwa'j ji'phçxa', peekx ũuste yaakxni's we'wemée Dxus we'weniçxá's pta'sxwa'ja'. Maawa vxitetx pu'çxya' yaakiççxa', Dxus makwe kçxhãaçxha'jana txã's yu' jxuka kxtey yuuwa'ja'. Kĩh yũuççxawa Dxusa's weçxaawa'ja'sçxáa yajkxna txã'wẽ yũuwa'ja' Jesukristo's pi'kxçxa, txãa pa'gatey kĩjkĩhwa jxuka Dxus jĩiçxaçxáata' bagaçxtewa nes yuuwa'jsa.
Nxuswa'j en pa'the weçxana fxi'zewa'ja'
12 Yakhthẽ'jwe'sx, Kristo's nwẽese'pha'ga nxuswa'j en pa'the', fxiy yuh yu'k sũhna kuh yaakxnuwe, 13 wejxwa Kristo na'wẽy nxuswa'ja's ji'phuçthu sũhna wẽt miiyajkxwe. Txã'wẽ yũuçxa', txajx kĩi ente' weçxaawa'ja's wala uyuune'kwe. 14 Kristo yakh fxi'zepa'ga ahçena wẽesxute', i'kwe'sxa' nxusmée mweeçxawe, txãa pa'gatey Dxus Espiritu' i'kwe'sx ũuste ũsa' nxusmée weçxweçxa kaayaki'jwa'jsa. Kristo' txãawe'sx yaaçenik fxi'ze', nawa i'kwe'sx yu' txã'sa' weçxana i'kwe fxi'ze'. 15 I'kwe'sxa' maa yuhwa nxus knaywa'j ji'phuwa'j ji'phmée i'kwe, na'wẽ yũuçxa: nasa ikhna, pesweena, ewmée yũuna, ĩsxiina yuwe ksuusu'jna fxi'zena. Txã'wẽ yũuçxa nxus knaywa'j yu'sa' ji'phunuwe, wala thaampa'sa'. 16 Nawa Kristo's selpiipa'ga nxuswa'j en ji'phuçxa', thaamewa'jme', wejxwa Dxusa's weçxana tuuthe'jwa'ja'. 17 Txã'wẽme', makwe Dxus luuçxte yuhwa nxuswa'j ena' txãawe'sx yakh khuẽ'yku takh na' ãçxh nmeh ente' ¿ma'wẽ seena' thaakwe nxus yujkx Dxus yuwe's nwẽese'jsamée yakha'? 18 Dxus luuçx yuhwa wala nxus knayçxa nwe'weni yuute', pkalsa ewmeesa yu' ¿mtee yuuya' u'jwetxki? 19 Txã'wẽ Dxus jxthãasnite nxuswa'j en ji'phuçxa', nxusmée fxi'zewa'ja' peekx ũustxi Dxus kusete neewe'wena. Txãak kwe'sxtxi taki'j na' txãaíiçxáa nwe'wena.
