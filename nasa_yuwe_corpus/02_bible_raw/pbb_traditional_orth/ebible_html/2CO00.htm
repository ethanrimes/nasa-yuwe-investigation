2 KORINTIOS
Dxus yakh fxi'zesatx Pablo fxi'jni
Pablo pxthaa nxuste fxi'zeni
Pablo' kĩh yuuçxaga Korinton u'jmée
Kazx pkal yũusa's peltunãyçxa kvxiisxa'khan yu'kxpehni
Troaste Tito's uymeeçxa, Pablo wala nxus yaakxni
Kristo yakha' kĩhtewa jxukak ewçxáa ya'fxi'ze'
Nwe'wewa'ja's u'se pheu'jçxa Dxus nvxiitnisa
Dxusa' pu'çxhina sũhnaçxáa fxi'zeni
Dxus yakh yuuwemée neeyũuni's pta'sxni
Dxus ĩtxisa ũswa'jçxáatha'w
Korintotewe'sx ewmée yũunijũ sxawedni
Ji'phuni's wa'lmée weçxana peesni
Tito yakh vxite kaapi'ki'jçxa kaahni
Yakhthẽ'jwe'sxtxi pu'çxwa'j vxyu pkhakhni
Pablo juuna' jxkaahwa'ja's Kristo ũsni
Kristo kaahnisatha'w jĩna ĩsxiina u'jusa
Kristo kaahnisa Pablo pxthaa yuuna fxi'zeni
Kristo kaavxya'ja'jni's uyni
Dxus yakh fxi'zesa Korintotewe'sx ewmeewáa fxi'zena ũstxna sũhçxa Pablo kuh yaakxni
Yu'kxpehna yuwe weçxaa kaahni
