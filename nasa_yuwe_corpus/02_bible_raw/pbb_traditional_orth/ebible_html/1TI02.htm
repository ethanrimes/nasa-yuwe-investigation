2
Dxus yakh ma'wẽ puutx we'wewa'ja'swa kaapiya'jni
1 Nyafxte' Dxus yakh puutx we'wewa'ja's khuẽ'ythu neewe'we'. I'kwe'sxa' nasa jxukaysa pa'gate Dxusa's weçxana mneewe'wewe txãa yakh puutx we'wena, sa' kĩh pẽjxçxawa pẽywa'ja'. 2 Txã'wẽy naa kiwete jxkaahna ũssawe'sx jxukaysa pa'gatewa Dxus yakh puutx we'wewa'ja', kxah Dxus pu'çxte' txãawe'sx ew jxkaathe', wẽt fxi'zeya' ãja'nja'w kĩhwa kuçx yuunimée Dxus yakh yuuçxáa. 3 Kwe'sxtxi nwe'wesa Dxusa' txã'wẽ yũuni'sku jxthãasu'. 4 Txãa pa'gatey Dxusa' kim yuhtxwa vxitu jxthãasu'çme', jxukaysa ewsa's jiyuçxa ewte neeyũuni'sku jxthãasu'. 5 Txã'wẽme', ewte nvxiitwa'jsa' teeçx Dxus yuuçxáa ũsa', aça' txã'wẽy Dxus dxi'pte nasaa ju'gu yuuçxa we'we pu'çxsawa teeçx íiçxáa ũsa', txã' Jesukristo' kwe'sx kakwe's íiçxáa ji'phsa. 6 Txãa Jesukristok jxukaysa ewte neeyũuwa'ja's vxitna pkalsa pa'gate uu txã'wẽ yũuwa'j en ãhte'. 7 Aça' txã'wẽ yũuni's pta'sxwa'jsa txhitxçxa nvxiitnisath, Jesukristo jxkaahnisa. Txãasa adxa' ĩsxiinimeesa isasa's kaapiya'jwa'jsath, judiu ji'jmée vxite nasawa Dxus yakh jxpubamée fxi'zekahn. Txã'wẽ kaahnisath jĩçxa' ĩsxiija'çmeeth txã'sa' Kristo' jiia'.
8 Txãasath neewe'we': Nasa msuuwe'sxwa Dxus yakh puutx we'wewa'j ji'phta' puiina fxi'zemée Dxus yuuçxáana ũusuh jxuka yajkxna. 9 Txã'wẽy u'ywe'sxwa ropa iijxũni's jxpa'yakxwa'ja' nasa we'wekahmen. Sa' dxkhasa's kiduççxa' txakwe wala vxyuu beh ki'pna ew pheu'jmeetewa ewna. 10 Athnisawa jwee pa'gasa iijxũmeeçxawa, Dxus yakh ew fxi'zeniçxáa wejx ewsa', txãa yakhçxáa fxi'zesayã'ja'. 11 U'ywe'sxa' wala jxpa'yakx wẽse'jna u'pwa'jsata' kaapiya'jni's. 12 Nasaa ksxavxyte u'y we'wewa'ja's ũsme'nja, sa' piçthẽ'ja's jxkaahwa'ja'swa ũsme'nja, wejxwa sxuuna'çxáa u'pwa'jsa'. 13 Txã'wẽ piçthẽ'ja's jxkaakhameníi, Dxusa' Adana's khuẽ'yku pheu'j, e'suk Ewa's pheu'j. 14 Aça' Adan ya'ĩsxiini yuume', u'yku ya'ĩsxii. Txã'wẽ ya'ĩsxiiçxak pkalte wete. 15 Nawa u'ya' peekx luuçxtxi ew jxpe'jna Dxus yakhwa jxpubamée ew fxi'zeçxa', ewte neeyũuna.
