10
Dxus ĩtximeesatx neewe'wekahmen yu'kxpehni
1 Yakhthẽ'jwe'sx miiyajkxwe: nyafxiitey kwe'sx yaçgawe'sxa' jxukay Dxus nwe'wena jxpe'jni tãaph aphnitx fxi'zena ũsu', sa' jxukay ĩkhwala bejuywa ew uykhẽwtxi. 2 Jxukay Moises yakh txutemée fxi'zeçxa yuuçxáatx jxukay tãaph dxiite Dxus nwe'weni yuu, sa' txã'wẽy ĩkhuywa uykhẽwtxi, 3 sa' jxukaysatx Dxus sã'jĩni fxi'zena ũsu'. 4 Txã'wẽytxi jxukaysa Dxus yusni yu'a'swa tudxna fxi'zena ũsu', sa' Dxusa' txãawe'sx yakh ih dxi'the pi'kxna u'jweççxa', penxate yu' kutxi'jçxa yusku. Aça' txãa penxa yu' Kristok yu'. 5 Txã'wẽ Dxusa' ew yũutewa, nasa wejx kuhsa yu' Dxus dxi'pte ew fxi'zemeeta', txãa pa'ga Dxus kastigãyte uutx nasa u'pnimée kiwesu.
6 Txã'wẽ kastigãyni uutx nawa txãa yu' kwe'sx jxpa'yajkxçxa txãawe'sx na'wẽy ewmée yũukahmençxáak txã'wẽ yũu. 7 Txãa pa'ga i'kwe'sxa' dxus ĩtximeesatxi' neewe'wenuwe, txãawe'sx na'wẽ yu'. Txãawe'sx dxus ĩtximeesatx neewe'weni's na'wẽk pta'sxi' Dxus libru': Nasa' weçxana fxiesta'jya' takhtxi ũ'na tudxna jĩ'k. 8 Txãawe'sx na'wẽ ptamsamée kazx pkal yuuwa'j ji'phmeetha'w. Txã'wẽ yũusa' uutx teeçx enteçxáa weintitres mil nasa. 9 Kxtee ũssa maaíi yu' Dxusa's juuna' ũusçxawe'wetx, aça' txã'wẽ we'wepa'ga ul weesweesa wa'kxte uutx. Nawa kwe'sxa' juuna' we'wewa'j ji'phmeetha'w Dxusa's. 10 Maaíi yu' Dxusa's açewe'wetx ewmeek yũu jĩna. Aça' txã'wẽ we'wepa'ga, Dxus kaahni angel txãawe'sxtxi' ivxiituk. Nawa kwe'sxa' txã'wẽ açewe'wewa'j ji'phmeetha'w.
11 Txãniitey kwe'sx yaçgawe'sxtxi txã'wẽk yũu, aça' txã'sa' Dxus librute fxi'jnik neeyũu kwe'sxwa jxpa'yakx fxi'zekahn, nmeh ensa' utxaana u'jweça' txãa pa'ga. 12 Txã'wẽ Dxus yakh ew ũsthu sũhna yaakxsa' jxpa'yakxwa'ja' pkalte weteya'mée. 13 I'kwe'sxtxi pkalte pẽtxwa'jsa pa'ja'ne' txã' u'sesa thaakwe seena' thẽysameeta', jxukaysa txã'wẽsaçxaçxáatx ji'phusatha'w. Nawa Dxusa's pi'kine'kwe aça' txã' i'kwe'sx wantãyya' çxhãçxhawa'jmeesa yu'sa' kaavxya'ja'jmeena, wejxwa pkalte pẽtxwa'jsa pa'jaçpkaçxha', ma'wẽ txãa pkaluh iyuwa'ja'swa ih pu'çxhina Dxusa'.
14 Txãa pa'ga yakhthẽ'jwe'sx, dxus ĩtximeesatx neewe'wenijũ kasejkahn yu'kxpehni's mwẽese'jwe: 15 I'kwe'sxa' peekx jii i'kwe adxa' kĩh yuupa'ga ne'tka txã'wẽ jxkaaja' nawa adx we'weni's mpa'yajkxwe. 16 Ũus miiyajkxwe: Kristo' uuççxa' txajx eea's pkawku aça' txãa uuni's kaayaki'jsa wasotewe'sxa's jxpa'gaçxa oraĩ'tha'w. Txã'wẽ yũunisa' Kristo yakh utxaaçxa txãa yakh txutemeetha'w fxi'ze' jĩnisa'. Txã'wẽy Kristo' uuya' txajx kakwe's dukhku aça' txãa uuni's kaayaki'jsa pana's pe'ltedeçxa jxtxude'tha'w. Txã'wẽ yũunisa' Kristo yakh utxaaçxa txãa yakh txutemée ihtha'w fxi'ze' jĩnisa'. 17 Makwe wala kuhçxawa teeçx pana'sçxáa jxukaysatha'w ũ'we', txãasatha'w teeçxçxáa na'wẽsa yuuna pa'ja'.
18 Miiyajkxwe Israelwe'sxtxi. Txãawe'sxa' Dxusna weçxaya' tajkx ikhni çxiçxa's ũ'sa' Dxus yakh txutemée fxi'zesaçxatx txã'wẽ yũ'. 19 Txã'jĩte', txã'wẽ yũuya' tajkx ikhnisa çxiçxte kĩh pẽethejene' sũjkahn txã'jĩçmeeth, txãa pa'gatey dxus ĩtximeesa' kĩhwa meesata'. 20 Txã'wẽtewa, dxus ĩtximeesatx weçxaya' tajkx ikhe'tx Dxus cielutewe'sxa's jiimeesa', aça' txã'wẽ yũuni' dxus ĩtximeesaçxáatx weçxanisame', eçxthẽ'ja's weçxanisa'. Aça' i'kwe'sxa' txã'wẽ eçxthẽ'j yakh utxaaçxa fxi'zenuwe jĩçxath txã'wẽ we'we'. 21 Txã'wẽ eçxthẽ'j yakh utxaaçxa weçxana ũsçxa', Kristo puuçni mesatewe'sxa's yu' jxpa'gaya' ãjameene'kwe. 22 Kwe'sxtxi jxpe'jsa Jesukristo's kaaũusçxa'jwa'jsameetha'w kwe'sxa', txãa pa'gatey txãate jxthaakwe çxhãçxhasameetha'w naa pa'ga.
Kĩhnawa ũ'ya' ãjawa'jsatha'w, nawa jxpa'yakxwa'ja' vxitetx peeygãhçxa
23 Isa kwe'sxa' kĩhnawa ũ'ya' ãjawa'jsa yuhtha'w, nawa jxpa'yakxwa'j ji'ptha'w. Makwe kĩh ũ'nawa ũ'ya' ãja'nja'w nawa txã' Dxus yakh çxhãçxha fxi'zewa'j yu'sa' pu'çxhi'çmeeta'. 24 Peekx fxi'zewa'jçxá's yajkxmée, vxitetxwa peeygãawa'ja' ew kfxi'ze'jya'.
25 Merkaute çxiçx ma'wẽsanawa weyçxa maa sũhmée ũ'wa'ja', 26 txãa pa'gatey Dxus jĩiçxaçxáata' naa kiwe, txã'wẽy naa kiwete ũssa kĩjkĩhwa jxuka.
27 Meeçxa' Dxusa's jiimeesa pi'kxte', i'kwe'sxa' u'jçxa kĩh puuçtewa txã'sa' ũ'weçxáane'kwe maa sũhmée. 28 Nawa Dxusa's açxamée ew jiisa' na'jĩna we'wena: Naa çxiçxa' dxus ĩtximeesatx weçxanisa' na' ewmeena jĩte', idxa' txã'sa' ũ'meene'ga, txã'wẽ pta'sxsa's kaypuba'jya'mée. 29 Vxitesa ũus yaakxni's jxpa'yakxkahnthu we'we', iidx ũus yaakxni's we'weçmeeth.
Nawa Dxusa's sxiigmée ew jiisa' na'wẽ pẽyina: Adx kĩhnawa ũ'ya' ãjani's ¿kĩh yũuçxa vxitesa neeweya' ãjakx? 30 Adxa' kĩh ũ'weççxawa Dxusa's weçxana orãyçxath ũ'we', aça' adx ũ'ni's ¿kĩh yũupa'ga ewmée nuywe'wetxki? jĩtxna. 31 Ew yuja', nawa i'kwe'sx ũ'nisa ma'k yuu kĩh yũuççxawa Dxus dxi'pte selpiiwa'jsaçxá's thegçxa kxtey myũuwe. 32 Judiuwe'sx vxite' judiumeesa, meeçxa' Dxus luuçx maatxwa kaypuba'jwe'sxte' mpa'yajkxwe. 33 Adxa' na'wẽçxáath pa'pçxu': peekx wẽt fxi'zewa'j yu's yajkxmée, vxitesatx pu'çxwa'ja'sçxáath pakwena fxi'ze', txãawe'sx ũusa' ewte neeyũukahn.
