16
Pablowe'sx yakh Timoteo u'jni
1 Pablo Silas yakh Derbe Listra çxhabsu pa'jçxa kxte puutx uytxi Jesukristo's jxpa'gasa Timoteo yaasesa yakh. Txajx njĩ'ja' Jesukristo's jxpa'gasa judiu ji'thewa, Timoteo ney yu' judiu ji'jmée griegok yu'. 2 Listra Ikonio çxhabsu u'psa yakhthẽ'jwe'sx maawa jxukatx txãa Timoteo's wala tuuthe'je'. 3 Txajũ' Pablo' txãa Timoteo's pe'jçxa u'jya'k yajkx, sa' kaacirkuncidaĩ'kh txã'wẽ griego nçxi'ka' jĩna kxsũ judiuwe'sx açewe'wekahmén. 4 Sa' Jerusalente Jesus jxkaahnisa vxite' ancianowe'sx fxi'jni karta's jxukatx pta'sxna u'jwe' maa çxhabsuwa yakhthẽ'jwe'sxtxi. 5 Txã'wẽ jiyuçxa', kxsũ pkhaakhesawe'sxa' jweeíiçxáatx çxhãçxha Dxus yakh fxi'ze', aça' nasa yu' een isatx pehnana u'jwe' Jesus kaapiya'jni's nwẽese'jna.
Pablo ksxa'wte teeçx piçthẽ'j Macedoniajũwe'sx pi'kxni
6 Txajũ' Pablowe'sxa' Fxrigia Galaciasutx ktejkana u'j, Dxus Espiritu' Asia kiwesu jwee yu' Dxus yuwe's kaapta'sxi'jme' naa pa'ga. 7 Sa' Misia kiwete utxa kũhçxa', Bitinia kiwen u'jwe'nja'w sũhtewa, Espiritu' u'khaahme'. 8 Txã'wẽ yũuyã'the', Misia kiwen teehwed u'jçxa, Troas çxhabtetx pa'j ĩkh wala pukate. 9 Kxte ũsçxak Pablo' teeçx kuste ksxa'w teeçx piçthẽ'j Macedoniatewe'sx na'wẽ pxthaa we'wena ũste: Macedoniate ktejkaçxa, kwe'sxtxi myuh pu'çxya' jĩna. 10 Pablo ksxa'wte txã'wẽ pa'ya yã'the', Macedonia kiwen u'jya' pheu'tha'w, kwe'sxtxi' Dxus txã'wẽ kajaçna Macedoniatewe'sxtxi txajx yuwe ewte nvxiitwa'jsa's pta'sxya' sũhçxa.
Pablo Silas yakh Fxilipos çxhabte kxuuphni
11 Troas çxhabuh barkote khẽeçxa u'tha'w ĩkh pxãhte Samotracia kiwen. Sa' vxite kuskuskhẽ' Neapolis çxhabte pa'tha'w. 12 Txajũ ki' u'jçxa', Fxilipos çxhabte pa'jçxa maz en ũsu'tha'w. Txãa çxhaba' romanowe'sx iiyamunisa wala jxtuhthesa yu'k Macedonia kiwete. 13 Teeçx jxkaase ente' çxhab eka kasehtha'w yu'wala pukate Dxus yakh puutx we'weya' pkhaakhenite, sa' Dxus yuwe's pta'sxtha'w kxte u'y pkhaakhesatx. 14 Aça' kxte pkhaakhesa teeçxsa' Lidiak yaase yu', Tiatira çxhabuwe'sx, tela zhiçxkwesatx txweysáa. Txãa u'ya' Dxusna nwẽese'jsak txã'wẽ Pablo pta'sxni's wẽse'jna u'pu'. Aça' Dxusa' txajx ũuste kaajiyu'kh Pablo pta'sxni's. 15 Txajũ' txajx yattewe'sx yã'jçxa jxuka yu'te ya'bautisãyçxa', na'jĩnak kwe'sxtxi pxthaa we'wena pi'kx:
—Ũ'kwe' Dxus yakh fxi'zewa'jsate i'kwe'sxa' ew jiiçxa', ũ'kwe yatte myuhwe dejxa' jĩçxa ãadxijimeeçxa pe'jna u'kh.
16 Teeçx ente nyafxíi ũsnitey u'jweççxa', puutx uytha'w teeçx kna'sa's eçxthẽ'j txajx ũuste kaajiyu'the pta'sxna u'jusa yakh. Txãa kna'sa' patron jxpe'jnisa ku'ga yu', sa' patronwe'sxtxi wala vxyu kaaknayu'ju'ku'ga txã'wẽ kĩhnawa ũuste jiyuçxa nasatx pta'sxna. 17 Txãa kna'sa' kwe'sx e'stey yuwe'k Pablo's na'wẽ sus we'wena:
—Naa piçthẽ'jwe'sxa' cielute Dxusna selpisaata', txãasatx i'kwe'sxtxi' pta'sxi' ewte u'jwa'j dxi'ja's jĩna.
18 Maz enwa txã'wẽ yũute', nmehte' Pablo' kuçxçxa na'wẽk we'we txãa kna'sa ũuste u'psa eçxthẽ'ja's:
—Jesukristo jxkaahni's nwẽese'jçxa, txãa kna'sa ũusuh mkaaseh jĩçpkaçxha', eçxthẽ'ja' kasejk.
19 Nawa patronwe'sxa' txãa kna'sa pa'gaçxáa vxyu knayna fxi'zeni's ivxiituyã'jçxa', Pablowe'sxtxi uweçxa çxhab pxãhte jxũna u'jçxa 20 kxte khuẽswe'sxtxi na'jĩnatx pãatxhĩ'kh:
—Naa piçthẽ'j judiuwe'sxa' wala kuçx yuuna ũsta' kwe'sx çxhabte. 21 Kĩhwa kwe'sxtxi selpiwa'jmeesaçxáanatx kaapiya'ja', aça' txã'sa' kxtey yuuya' ãjãme'nja'w, kwe'sxa' romano nasatha'w naa pa'ga jĩte', 22 nasa' wala ũusaçxana ahçetx. Aça' khuẽswe'sxa' Pablowe'sxtxi ksũupi'jçxa peçxuukwe kaahtx. 23 Txã'wẽ wala pkxuuçxa', karcelte ktxaaja'jçxa kxte thegna ũssa's wala jxpa'yakx theg kaathe', 24 kxte thegsa' karcel dxiite jwee dxiiju pe'jna u'jçxa telte çxidatx se'wçxak ki'p.
25 Nawa Pablowe'sx kuspxãh Dxus yakh puutx we'wena memna u'pte', vxite preesu' wẽse'jna u'ppkaçxha', 26 ehd seena' çxhãçxha u'the', karcel vxitxa' jxuka phaatetx, aça' preesu tudni kadenawa çxuktetetx. 27 Txajũ' karcelte thegsa yajkxçxa', karcel vxitx jxuka phaateçxa ũste uyçxa', kçxilxa's kutxi'jçxa peekíiçxáa ya'ikhya'wak yu', preesu' jxuka kasehçxa u'jne'ta sũhna ũukhçxa. 28 Aça' Pablo' na'wẽk we'we:
—Txã'wẽ yũunu. Jxuka ũstha'w ayte jĩte', 29 karcelte thegsa' bela pki'taçxa ũukhna wuwuuçxáa kũhçxa, Pablowe'sx tasxte peejxũkwek. 30 Txajũ' Pablowe'sxtxi kutxi'jçxa na'wẽk pẽjx:
—I'kwe'sxa' mpeeta'sxwe, ¿ma'wẽ yũuçxa Dxusa's uyuuki'th? jĩte', 31 Pablowe'sxa' na'wẽtx pta'sx:
—Kwe'sxtxi jxpe'jsa Jesukristo's jxpa'gaçxa yuuçxáa Dxus yakh fxi'zewa'ja's uyuune'ga petx iidx yattewe'sx yã'jçxa jxuka.
32 Txajũ' Dxus yuwe's pta'sxtxi txajx yattewe'sx jxukaysatx. 33 Sa' karcelte thegsa' txãa kustey Pablowe'sx kpã'tx pkhi'kxna'wa txajx yattewe'sx yã'jçxa jxukaysa ya'bautisãytxi. 34 Txajũ' txajx yatna pe'jna u'jçxa puuçku, sa' wala weçxana ũsu'k Dxus yakh fxi'zewa'ja's ji'phutha'w jxukaysa jĩna.
35 Txajũ' khuẽswe'sxa' kusíi policia kaahtx karcelte thegsa tasxna Pablowe'sxtxi' txhweetekahn pta'sxya'. 36 Aça' karcelte thegsa' pta'sxku Pablo's:
—Khuẽswe'sxa' txhweetekaahtx i'kwe'sxtxi'. Aça' kasehçxa ũukhmée me'jwe jĩte', 37 Pablo' na'wẽk we'we policiawe'sxtxi:
—Kwe'sxa' romano nasatewa ahçeçxa nasa jxuka uyna ũste pkxuuçxa karcelte kxuuphtxi, kĩh yũupa'ga yuuwetewa txã'sa' jiyumey. Sa' ãçxha' paaçte kim yuhwa jiyunimeete txhweete'nja'w ¿sũjuwá'nta? Txã'wẽ yũuya' yu' ãjameetxna. Kwe'sxtxi kutxi'jya' txãawe'sx yuu yuwetxna jĩte', 38 policiawe'sxa' u'jçxa khuẽswe'sxtxi txã'wẽy pta'sxte', txãawe'sxa' wala ũukhtxi, Pablowe'sxa' romano nasatewa jiyuçxa'. 39 Txajũ' khuẽswe'sxa' Pablowe'sx tasxna u'jçxa jxpubane'tka'w jĩna pxthaa we'weçxa karceluh kutxi'jçxa çxhabuh kaseh kaahtx. 40 Aça' Pablowe'sxa' karceluh kasehçxa', kxte çxhabte Lidia yatnatx u'j. Sa' ũukhmée meswe jĩna yakhthẽ'jwe'sxtxi kvxiisxa'jçxatx txajũ kaseh.
