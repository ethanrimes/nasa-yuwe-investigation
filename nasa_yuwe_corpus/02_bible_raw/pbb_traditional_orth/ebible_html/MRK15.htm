15
Jesusa's Pilato kusete dukhni
(Mt 27.1-2, 11-14; Lk 23.1-5; Jn 18.28-38)
1 Txajũ' khikhyna sẽjẽçte', sacerdotewe'sx judiu nthẽ'jsawe'sx, Dxus leya's kaapiya'jsawe'sx yã'jçxa walasa pwe'sx pkhaakheçxa Jesusa's tudçxa jxũna u'jtx Pilato tasxte kuutxa'jya'. 2 Aça' Pilato' Jesusa's na'jĩnak pẽjx:
—¿Idx wáa judiuwe'sxtxi jxkaahsane'ga? jĩte', Jesusa' pasku:
—Txã'wẽ íith. Isa yuh we'weg jĩk.
3 Txajũ' sacerdotewe'sxa' Jesusa' kĩhtewa jxuka ewmeesa' jĩnatx yuwe ũsu'. 4 Aça' Pilato' ki' we'wek Jesusa's:
—Mwẽesẽ'j, idxa's na'wẽtx kĩhtewa jxuka ewmeesa' jĩna yuwe ũsu', aça' ¿ma'wẽçxáawa pasya'wameegá'? jĩte', 5 Jesusa' teeçx yuweçxáa yuhwa we'weme', aça' Pilato' kujk yajkxna neeyũu.
Jesusa's ikhwa'j vxitni
(Mt 27.15-31; Lk 23.13-25; Jn 18.38–19.16)
6 Pilato' Paskua fxiesta iisa teeçx preesu txhweete'k nasaa pẽyni's. 7 Txãa ensu' kxte preesu ũsu'k Barabás yaasesa. Txãa piçthẽ'ja' vxite nasa yakh pkhaakheçxa gobierno yakh puíi kiisçxa nasatx wala kiikhe'jsa npiiçthẽ'kh yu'. Txãa pa'gak preesu ũsu'. 8 Txajũ' kxtee nasa pa'jsa', Pilato's pẽjxtxi fxiesta iisa ma'wẽne' yũ' txã'sa' ãçxwa txã'wẽy yũukahn. 9 Aça' Pilato' pẽjxku:
—I'kwe'sxa' ¿judiuwe'sxtxi jxkaahsa'skwe txhweete jxthãasu'á'? jĩk. 10 Nawa Pilato yu' sacerdote ne'jwe'sxa' Jesusa's ahçeçxa dukhtewa jiiçxak txã'wẽ we'we. 11 Nawa sacerdotewe'sxa' nasa jxukaysatx yu'kxwe'wetx, wejxwa Barabasna mitxhwete jĩkahn. 12 Aça' Pilato' pẽjxku:
—I'kwe'sx we'weçxa', Judiuwe'sxtxi Jxkaahsa jĩ'i'kwe ¿txã'sa' kĩjkwe yũu jxthãasu'? jĩte', 13 txãawe'sx sus we'wena pasçxa':
—Txã'sa' kluuskhẽ mkaafxuçu'j jĩtx.
14 Txajũ' Pilato' pẽjxku:
—Txã'wẽ yũukahna' naa piçthẽ'ja' ¿kĩhga ewmée yũu? jĩtewa, txãawe'sx yu':
—Kluuskhẽ mkaafxuçu'j jĩnaçxáatx sus we'we'.
15 Aça' Pilato' nasa yakh ew neeyũu wẽeçxa, Barabasa's txhweetekaakh, sa' Jesus yu'sa' kaapeçxukwe'jçxa kluuskhẽ fxuçkahn dukhku.
16 Txajũ' soldauwe'sxa' Jesusa's jxũna u'jtx txãawe'sx ũsni patxute. Sa' soldauwe'sx jxukaysatx kxteeçxaçxáatx pa'ya. 17 Khĩçxatx Jesusa's jxkaahsa ropa na'wẽsa kaapã'çxi'jçxa, dxikthete yu' çhũçh korona vxitçxa kaafxĩkhe'j. 18 Txajũ' na'wẽ sus weyna:
—Judiuwe'sxtxi jxkaahsath jĩ'ne'ga jĩnatx weeçx we'we.
19 Sa' dxikthega fxtũu zuzsaju pekwe'kwena, fxne'sx avxythethnatx txajx tasxte peejxũkwe'. 20 Txã'wẽ yũuna jxã'jçxa', jxkaahsa ropa na'wẽsa's ksũupi'jçxa, txajx ropa's ki' kaathe'jtx. Txã'wẽ yũuçxatx kluuskhẽ fxuçya' jxũna u'j.
Jesusa's kluuste fxuçni
(Mt 27.32-44; Lk 23.26-43; Jn 19.17-27)
21 Aça' çxhabu kasehna u'jweçte', teeçx piçthẽ'j Simon yaasesa's puutx uyçxa soldauwe'sxa' txã'snatx Jesus yakhni kluusa's kxaakhe'jçxa pe'jna u'j. Txãa Simon yu' Alejandro Rufxowe'sx neyku yu', Cirene kiwetewe'sx.
22 Txajũ' Jesusa's teeçx ahte Golgota yaasesatetx pe'jna u'j. Txãa Golgota yu' Uusáa Dxikthé Dxi'thçxáasa jĩni'. 23 Kxtee pa'jçxa', vxinote yaasa ka'daçxatx yus wẽe, nawa Jesusa' tudxme'.
24 Txajũ' kluuskhẽ fxuççxa', soldauwe'sxa' Jesus ropatxi's txudeedeçxa swerte kaahtx txajx kaaçni atxha's maa soldau knaytewa jiyuya'.
25 Kusíi las nwewetetx Jesusa's kluuskhẽ fxuç. 26 Sa' kĩh yũusate txã'wẽ kluuskhẽ fxuççxawa pta'sxna fxi'jçxa ki'ptxi kluus vxiçte: Judiuwe'sxtxi Jxkaahsa' jĩna. 27 E'z nasa seena' yuuwesa yu'tx txãawe'sx yakhtxi Jesusa's pdxi'p kluuskhẽ fxuç. 28 Aça' Dxus librute fxi'jni' kxtey yuuk: Nasa ewmeesathas yuuwesa yakhku ki'pni yuu jĩna fxi'jni'.
29 Txajũ' kluus tasxuy uykhẽwsa' dxikthetx kaawũhnatx weeçx we'we':
—Dxuus yata's idxa' pẽtxçxa tekh ente' ki' kiisu'nja ¿jĩ'çmeegá'? 30 Txã'wẽ ãjãwa'jsaçxa' ¿mjĩitega kluusuh peekíi iyuçxa sa'ja'çmée? jĩ'tx. 31 Aça' Dxus leya's kaapiya'jsawe'sx, vxite' sacerdotewe'sxwa na'jĩnatx weeçx we'we':
—Vxite yuutxi' nwe'weçxawa, peekíi yu' ya'nwe'weya' ãjã'çme'. 32 Israelwe'sxtxi jxkaahsa Kristoth jĩ'k na' txã'wẽ dxihçxa', ãçxhíi kwe'sx uyna ũste kluusuh sa'jana aç yuuçxáa txã'wẽ dxih yu'ga sũjũ'nja'w jĩ'tx. Txã'wẽy txãa yakh kluuskhẽ fxuçni u'psawe'sxwa Jesusa's wẽesxu'tx.
Jesus uunii
(Mt 27.45-56; Lk 23.44-49; Jn 19.28-30)
33 Txajũ' ẽepxãh wala ãanite', naa kiwete jxuka çxhi'dxku kusuçte las trestepkaçx. 34 Txã'wẽ çxhi'dxni las tres ãjãçpkaçxha', Jesusa' na'jĩnak sus we'we:
—Eloi, Eloi ¿lama sabaktani? jĩk. Txã': Adx Dxusgu sa' ¿mjĩi pa'gaga adxçxáana nvxiht? jĩni'.
35 Aça' nasa kxtee wẽsẽ'jna ũssa' Eliasa's pa'yaça' jĩtx. 36 Naapkaçxha' teeçxsa' wuwuuna u'jçxa, vxino pussate esponja's akhçxa fxtũute açhçxa Jesus yuwete utxak çxazxakahn. Sa' na'jĩk:
—Sxuuna' thegna meswe, aan Eliasa' kluusuh spajkxya' pa'jana jĩk.
37 Txajũ' Jesusa' ki'kin sus we'wenitee uuna kãjk. 38 Naapkaçxha' dxuus yatte txudenisa liensu pesath a'kxnisa' peekxçxáa sutek puupxãjkhẽ kute ẽeju kiwete kĩh. 39 Aça' kluus dxi'pte ũssa soldau kpiitana' Jesus txã'wẽ sus we'weçxa uuçte uyçxa', na'wẽk we'we:
—Naa piçthẽ'ja' isa Dxus Nçxi'k yuh yu'ga jĩk.
40 Txã'wẽy u'ywe'sxwa jxu'ju thegna ũsu'tx. Aça' txãawe'sx ksxavxyte', Salomé, vxite' Maria Magdalena vxite' Maria Jakobo Josewe'sx njĩ'j txãawe'sxtxi yu'. (Txãa Jakobo's nuuçxsa' jĩ'tx). 41 Naa tekh u'ysa' Jesusa's selpiina u'jusatx yu' Galilea kiwesu. Nawa txãawe'sxçxáamée, vxite u'ywe'sxwa Jesus yakh ih Jerusalente pa'jsatx ũsu'.
Jesusa's pedani
(Mt 27.57-61; Lk 23.50-56; Jn 19.38-42)
42 Jxkaasewa'j en ãjãçte', kĩhnawa jxuka pheu'jçxatx nvxiitu'. 43 Txãa pa'gak kusuçte' José Arimateajũwe'sxa' Pilato tasxte ũukhmée utxáa Jesus kakwe's pẽjxya'. Txãa José yu' judiu ne'jwe'sxsa íiçxáak yu', sa' Dxus jxkaahwa'j ena' dud ãhwaça' sũhna yaakxsak yu'. 44 Txã'wẽ Jesusa' uuyã'kh jĩni's jiyuçxa', Pilato' wala kuh yajkxçxa soldau kpiitana's pa'yak pẽjxya' txã'wẽ Jesus uudxihtewa jiyuya'. 45 Txajũ' kpiitana': Jesusa' uuyã'kh jĩna pta'sxte', Pilato' Jesus kakwe's jxũna u'khaakh. 46 Aça' Josea' Jesus kakwe's spajkxçxa, liensu fxinusa weyçxa kxteek yap. Sa' jxũna u'jçxa kwet kafx u'se phadenisa ji'phuk txkaa khikhçxa, u'kani kafxte yu' kwet walasajuk aph. 47 Txã'wẽ yũuçte', Maria Magdalena vxite Maria José njĩ'j txãawe'sxa' Jesus kakwe's mkaa khikheçtewa uytxi.
