13
E'z eçx seena' ũukhũukhsa
1 Txãa e'su' mar pukateth yujuçxa ũsu', sa' uythu eçx seena' kazx ũukhũukhsa mar pxãjũ kasejeçte, sa' siete dxikthé ji'phçxa diez kaaçxuk ji'phu', kaaçxu iisa teeçx koronak ji'phu'. Sa' txãa dxikthesu yu' Kristo's seena' kazx açewe'wenak fxi'jni yu'. 2 Txãa eçx seena' kazx ũukhũukhsa' txiklxi na'wẽk jxkajkwe yu', çxida yu' oso na'wẽtx yu', sa' yuwe yu' león na'wẽk yu'. Aça' eçxthẽ'ja' txãa eçx seena' kazx ũukhũukhsa's kĩh yũuya'wa ãjãwa'ja's ũsçxa txajx jxkaahna ũsni'swa dukhku, sa' kĩhtewa jxuka pu'çxya'k neeyũu. 3 Txãa eçx seena' kazx ũukhũukhsa dxikthé teeçxsa yu' uupçxãjk kpã'yuuni yu', nawa txãa kpã' yu' wẽetniyã'kh yu', aça' naa kiwete nasa jxukaysa txãa eçx seena' kazx ũukhũukhsaçxá'stxi weçxana utxaaçxa neewe'we. 4 Sa' eçxthẽ'ja'swa weçxana neewe'wetx, txã'wẽ eçx seena' kazx ũukhũukhsa's jxkaahwa'jsa vxitçxa ki'ppa'ga. Sa' eçx seena' kazx ũukhũukhsa yu'sa' na'jĩnatx neewe'we':
—¿Kim naa na'wẽy seena'sa ũski, sa' kim txãa yakh puiiya' çxhãçxhakx? jĩ'tx.
5-6 Sa' eçx seena' kazx ũukhũukhsa's kwe'we'jnik yuu, Dxusa's wẽesxuuna açewe'wekahn. Aça' kxtey yuuçxa, Dxusa's wẽesxuuçxa açewe'wena Dxus ũsni'swa açewe'wek, cielute ũssawe'sxtxiwa txã'wẽyku açewe'we. Sa' txãak jxkaahwa'jsa yuu kuarenta y dos a'te ãhpkaçx. 7 Sa' Dxus luuçxtxi ahçeçxa gweçxa'swa vxit kaahnik yuu knaypkaçx. Txãasak naa kiwete jxukaysawe'sxtxi jxkaahwa'jsa yuu, nasa ma'wẽsawa maa çxhabuwe'sxwa fxiyfxiy we'wesawa jxukaysatx. 8 Aça' na'wẽ Piisxakuẽ jĩnisa ikhni yuuk txã' libru ji'pha' bagaçxte yuhwa uuwa'jsamée yasetx fxi'jni, aça' naa kiwetewe'sx jxukaysa txãawe'sx yase's naa kiwe's vxitiynaçxáa yuhwa txãa librute fxi'jnimeesa' jxukaysa txãa eçx kazx ũukhũukhsa's neewe'wetx.
9 Txãa ente' na'wẽ seena' yuutxna txã'sa' mwẽese'jwe: 10 Nasatx preesu'jsa' txãawe'sxwa preesu'jni yuutxna. Aça' nasatx kçxilxuh ikhsa' txãawe'sxwa kçxilxuh ikhni yuutxna. Txãa pa'ga Dxus luuçxa' Dxus yakh yuuçxáa çxhãçxha fxi'zewa'ja's ji'phuwa'j peejxta'.
11 Txãa e'su' vxite eçx seena' kazxsa kiwe dxiijũ kãjaçte uythu. Txã' piisxáa kaaçxu na'wẽsa e'zku ji'phu', nawa txãa eçx seena' kazx ũukhũukhsa na'wẽyku Kristo's açewe'we'. 12 Sa' eçx kazxsa nyafxtewe'sx na'wẽy jxkaajxa' ãjãwa'ja'sku ji'phu', txãasak naa kiwete nasa jxukaysatx jxkaaja', eçx na'wẽ dxikthete kpã' seena'sa ji'phuçxawa wẽetku txã'sna neewe'wekahn. 13 Sa' kĩjkĩhwa seena'sa'swa kaavxya'ja'jya' ãjã'k, cielujũ ipx wala seena'sa kaakĩiji'jna naa kiwete nasa uyna ũste. 14 Eçx kazxsa nyafxtewe'sx tasxte seena'sa's kaavxya'ja'jya' ãjãçxa, naa kiwete nasa jxukaysatx kaypuba'kh Dxus yuwe pta'sxsa na'wẽ kĩhwa kaavxya'ja'jçxa. Sa' seena' dxikthete kpã' ji'phçxawa uumée çxhãçxhak txãa na'wẽysa piisakaakh ĩtxĩmeesa. 15 Sa' eçx kazxsa kiwe dxiijũ kãasa' txãa piisanisa ĩtxĩmeesa's ĩtxĩ vxit-ya' ãjãwa'ja's ji'phu'k. Aça' txãa ĩtxĩ vxitnisa's neewe'wesamée yuutxi'sa' kiwe dxiijũ kãasa eçxa' kiikhe'jwa'jsak yu'. 16 Txajũ' eçx kiwe dxiijũ kãasa' na'wẽk yũu jxukaysatx: le'çxkwesa, walasa, ji'phsa, ji'phmeesa, patron ji'phsa, patron ji'phmeesa, teeçx yuhwa nvxihtmée jxukaysatx iisawa'khaakh paçu kusete meeçxawa dxi'pte. 17 Sa' txãa eçx kiwe dxiijũ kãasa yaseju iisawa'jni's meeçxawa txãa yase numero's ji'phmeesa yu', kĩh yuhwa weyya' ãjãmeetxna, sa' txweyya'wa ãjãmeetxna maa yuhwa. 18 Sa' txãa eçx kazxsa numero' kwe'sx jiyunisa'. Aça' txã'sa' jiyuwa'jsa ũsçxa', ũus miiyajkxwe: txãa numero' seiscientos sesenta y seisa'.
