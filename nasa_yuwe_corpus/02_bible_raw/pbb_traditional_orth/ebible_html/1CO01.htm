SAN PABLO KARTA NYAFXTE FXI'JNI KORINTO ÇXHABTEWE'SX JĨI
1
Korintotewe'sx Dxus yakh fxi'zesawe'sxtxi Pablo fxi'jni
1 Adxa' Pablo yaaseth, Dxus peeygãhçxa pa'yate Jesukristo jxkaahnisa. Txã'wẽ yũuwa'ja's Dxusku txhitxhçxa nvxiht. 2 Korinto çxhabte yakhthẽ'jwe'sxtxi weçxana fxi'jaçthu, txã'wẽy yakhthẽ'j Sosteneswa yuwe weçxaakaja'k. I'kwe'sxa' Kristo Jesus yakh txutemée fxi'zeya' utxaapa'ga Dxus luuçxi'kwe. Majũwe'sxwa kwe'sxtxi jxpe'jsa Jesukristo yasena kxsussa ũstxna txãawe'sx yã'jçxa ja'daçxah txajx tasxte yuuçxáa fxi'zekahnku Dxusa' pa'ya. Txãa Jesukristo' kwe'sx jxukaysatx jxpe'jsa'. 3 I'kwe'sxtxi' Dxusa' pu'çxhina vxite' kwe'sxtxi jxpe'jsa Jesukristowa. Txãa pa'ga Dxus yakhçxáa mfxi'zewe.
Jesukristo pu'çxpa'ga Dxusa' kĩh yuhwa peejxmée jxukak pees
4 Dxusa's weçxana nesthu yu' i'kwe'sx pa'gate, txã'wẽ Dxusa' i'kwe'sxtxi peeygãhçxa kĩh yuhwa peejxmée jxukak pees Jesukristo pu'çxpa'ga. 5 Txã'wẽ Kristo yakh fxi'zeya' utxaapa'ga, Dxusa' txajx dxi'pte kĩhtewa jxuka i'kwe'sx sxiigmée fxi'zewa'ja's jxukak ũs, Dxus yuwe's pta'sxwa'j ma'k yuu, meeçxa' maa isa pta'sxsatewa jiyuwa'j ma'k yuu nawa jxuka. 6 Txãa pa'ga Kristo kaapiya'jni's i'kwe'sx ũuste kaavxya'ja'i'kwe. 7 Txã'wẽ Dxus pu'çxte', i'kwe'sxa' kĩhte yuhwa sxiigmée i'kwe txajx dxi'pte fxi'ze' Jesukristo kĩiwa'j ena's ũythasna. 8 Dxusa' txã'wẽ ewçxáa jxpe'jna nes yuuna nmeh entepkaçx, kxah Jesukristo kĩi ente' i'kwe'sxa' kĩhte yuhwa yuuwemeesa yuune'kwe. 9 Dxusa' na'wẽ ewçxáa jxpe'jya' neeyũuk na' kxtey yuuna. Txã'wẽ ewçxáa jxpe'jya' íiçxáak txajx Nçxi'k Jesukristo yakh fxi'zeya' pa'ya i'kwe'sxtxi'.
Dxus yakh fxi'zesawe'sx puutx puiina txuteni
10 Kwe'sxtxi jxpe'jsa Jesukristo dxi'pte yakhthẽ'jwe'sxtxi yu'kxpejeçthu, puutx ewteçxah puutx we'weçxa txutemée kxazíi ja'daçxah teeçxsa na'wẽçxaçxáa ew yajkxna fxi'zekahn. 11 Kloé yattewe'sxa' adxa's pta'sxtxi txã'wẽ i'kwe'sxa' puiina fxi'zena ũsta' jĩna, txãa pa'gath adx yakhthẽ'jwe'sxtxi txã'wẽ we'we'. 12 I'kwe'sxa' teeçxsa na'wẽçxah fxiyfxiy we'wena ũsi'kwe: Maaíi yu', adxa' Pablo yakh u'jsath, naapkaçxha' vxite' Apolos yakh u'jsath. Vxite' Pedro yakh u'jsath, aça' vxiteíi yu' Kristo yakh u'jsath jĩna i'kwe we'we'. 13 Nawa Kristo' ¿teeçx ahtewe'sx yakhçxáa fxi'zewa'jsaga yuuá'? ¿I'kwe'sx pa'gate Pabloga kluuste uuá'? Meeçxawa ¿i'kwe'sxa' Pablo yase's kxsusçxakwe yu'te bautisãyni yuuá'? 14 Ewçxáa ne'tka i'kwe'sxtxi maa yuhtxwa yu'te bautisãymée, naasáa Krispo vxite' Gayo txãawe'sxçxáatxthu bautisãy. 15 Txãa pa'ga kim yuhwa txã'wẽ Pablo yase's kxsusçxa bautisãynith jĩya' ãjameetxna. 16 Vxite' Estefxanaswe'sxçxáatxthu bautisãy, meeçxa' vxitetx bautisãy wá'ntka nawa txãa yuutxi' yaaki'çmeeth. 17 Kristo' adxa's bautisãyya' kaahme', naasáa kwe'sx ewte neeyũuwa'ja's pta'sxya'çxáak kaah. Nawa naa kiwetewe'sxa's jiyunisa yakhméek pta'sxkaah. Txã'wẽme', Kristo kluuste uuni's pta'sxnisa' e'skhẽ neeyũuwe'sxte.
Nwe'weya' ãjawa'ja's Dxus pheu'jçxa nvxiitnisa
18 Kristo' kwe'sx pa'gate kluuste uuk jĩna pta'sxni' kĩhwa seelpimeesa na'wẽ ne' pthũuse' vxituçxa nxus kiwete u'jwa'jsatxi'. Nawa Dxus yakh ũsya' u'jwa'jsa kwe'sx yuutxi' txã'wẽ pta'sxni' seena' wala seelpisa', kwe'sxtxi ewte nvxiitwa'jsa' naa pa'ga. 19 Dxus librute we'weçxa':
Ivxiitu'nja naa kiwetewe'sxçxá's jiyuni's,
Sa' naa kiwetewe'sxçxá's jiyuni's kĩhte yuhwa selpiiwa'jmée nvxiitu'nja jĩ'k.
20 Txã'wẽ' aça' naa kiwetewe'sxçxá's jiyuni' ¿kĩhte selpiikx? Kĩhtewa jxuka jiisa kaapiya'jsatha'w sũhsa' ¿mtee yuutxki? Naa kiwete kĩh ũsna txã'sna jxuka jiisathẽ'j ¿kĩh yuukx? Ãçxha' txã' ¿kĩhteçxá' selpiima'k? Naa kiwetewe'sxçxá's jiyunisa's kĩhtewa seelpimeek vxit Dxusa'. 21 Dxusa' jiyuk naa kiwetewe'sxçxá's jxuka jiyusa' Dxusa's jiyuya' ãjawa'jmeesatewa. Txãasak kĩhtewa jiimeeçxawa txã'sna ũusuh jxuka yajkxçxa txajx pta'sxni's wa'lmée jxpa'gateçxáa nwe'wewa'ja's vxit, makwe txajx pta'sxni' vxite yakh yu' seelpimée na'wẽtewa.
22 Judiuwe'sxa' cielujũ seena'sa kaavxya'ja'jni'stxi uy wẽje', naapkaçxha' griegowe'sxa' naa kiwete jiyunisa'stxi pakwé'. 23 Nawa kwe'sxa' Kristo's kluuskhẽ ki'pçxa ikhniçxá'stha'w pta'sxi'. Judiuwe'sxa' txã'wẽ pta'sxni'stxi jxpuba', naapkaçxha' griegowe'sx yuutxi'sa' kĩhtewa seelpimée luuku we'weni na'wẽ ne' pthũuse'. 24 Nawa Dxus txhitxhnisa judiu vxite' griego txãawe'sx yakh yu', Kristo yuuçxáa nwe'wewa'jsa', nwe'wewa'ja's Dxus pheu'jçxa nvxiitnisa. 25 Naa kiwete nasa jiisa ũstxna txãawe'sx yakh yu', Dxus jiimeesa na'wẽsaçxawa, txãawe'sxte jxthaakwe jiisa' Dxusa'. Aça' Dxus çxhãçxhameeçxa ãjasamée na'wẽsaçxawa, nasa naa kiwetewe'sxte jxthaakwe çxhãçxhasa'. 26 Yakhthẽ'jwe'sx, i'kwe'sxtxi Dxus txhitxhni's mjiyuwe. I'kwe'sxa' naa kiwetewe'sxa's yu' walamée jii i'kwe sa' çxhãçxhasawa kuhmée i'kwe, sa' jxtuhthesawa kuhmée i'kwe. 27 Txã'wẽçxawa i'kwe'sxa' Dxus txhitxhnisa i'kwe, i'kwe'sxtxi jiimeesata' jĩna naa kiwetewe'sx nasa we'we'tx txãawe'sxtxi kaythame'jwa'jsa. Sa' txã'wẽy naa kiwete nasa we'weçxa': Çxhãçxhamée ãhmeesata' jĩ'tx txãawe'sxtxiwa kaythame'jwa'jsa. 28 Naa kiwete nasa we'weçxa': Kĩhtewa seelpimeesata' jĩnatx açewe'we' txãa açewe'wesatx seelpimeekhẽ nvxiitwa'jsa i'kwe. 29 Txã'wẽ i'kwe'sxa' ãhmeekwesatx txhitxhnisa i'kwe na' kim yuhwa Dxus dxi'pte iiwejçx yajkxna fxi'zeya' ãjameene'kwe. 30 Kristo Jesus yakh fxi'zewa'ja's Dxusku vxit. Aça' Kristo's jiyuwa'ja'swa Dxus íiçxáak vxit. Sa' txãa Kristopa'ga yuuçxáa kwe'sxa' Dxus dxi'pte ewtetha'w neeyũu, txajx tasxu txutemée fxi'zeya'. 31 Txãa pa'ga Dxus yuuçxá's weçxana tuuthe'jwa'ja's Dxus librute na'wẽ fxi'jni ũsa': Maawa jiith sũhna jxtuhthe yaakxwẽje'ççxa', Dxus yuuçxá's weçxana tuuthe'jwa'ja' jĩ'k.
