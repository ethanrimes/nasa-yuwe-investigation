1 JUAN
Ĩtxi kaafxi'ze'jwa'jsa vxyaani
Dxusa' eena'sa'
Dxus tasxte kwe'sx ju'gu we'we pu'çxsa' Kristo'
Dxus jxkaahni u'sesa
Dxus Espiritu' kaajiyu'ju'k maa ĩsxiinisatewa, txã'wẽy maa isasatewa
Kwe'sxa' Dxus luuçx yã'tha'w
Teeçxsa na'wẽçxah puutx ya'peeygãhna fxi'zewa'ja'
Kwe'sx ũuste ewçxáa ũusutheçxa', Dxus dxi'pte jxpubamée ũste txã'wẽ yuuna
Dxus Espiritu's ji'phsa vxite' Kristo's açewa'j espiritu's ji'phsa
Peeygãanisa' Dxus tasxuh yuja'
Dxus luuçxa' ewmeesa's nwẽese'jmée jxuka knayu'tx
Jesukristo' Dxus nçxi'ktewa kaajiyu'jsa' naata'
Pkal perdon ji'phmeesawa ũsta'
