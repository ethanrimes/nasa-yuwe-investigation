2
Ewte neeyũuwa'ja's jxpa'yajkxmée yu' yuuwa'jme'
1 Txãa pa'ga wala jxpa'yakxwa'j ji'ptha'w, na'wẽ Dxus Nçxi'k yuu kwe'sxtxi we'wete yuwe ewsa's wẽse'tha'w txãajũ txuteeya'mée. 2 Dxus yuwe's angeleswe'sxçxáa pta'sxni yuhwa ĩsxiini yuumée kxteyku yuu. Txãa pa'gaíiçxáatx nwẽese'jmée pkalte wetesawe'sxa' kastigãyni yuu txãawe'sx ãhmée yũupa'ga. 3 Txãaçxáa yuhwa kxteyku yuu, aça' kwe'sx ewte neeyũuwa'j txakwe walasa's Dxus Nçxi'k Kristo txajx yuweju yuuk pta'sx txã'sa' jxpa'yakxmeeçxa', ¿ma'wẽ Dxus kastigo seena'sajũ iyukxtha'w? Aça' Kristo pta'sxni's nyafxte wẽse'jsawe'sxa' kwe'sxtxitx pta'sx, isasa yuja' jĩna. 4 Txãawe'sxçxáa isasa yuja' jĩmeeta', Dxus yuuk isasa yuhna sũhna jiyukahn kim yuhwa ãjanimeesa's kaavxya'ja'j. Sa' Dxus Espiritu pu'çxte kĩhte yuhwa sxiigmée selpiina fxi'zewa'jtx ũsku teeçxsa na'wẽçxah, txajx ũs wẽeni yu's jxuka.
Jesukristo' kwe'sx yakh pxakhsa na'wẽk yuu
5 Na'wẽ Kristoçxaçxá's nwẽese'jwa'j en pa'jana jĩ'tha'w txãa ente jxkaahwa'ja's angeleswe'sxtxi nvxihtme' Dxusa'. 6 Txã'wẽ meek yu' txajíiçxáak na'jĩna teeçx ajkhẽ pta'sxi' Dxus librute:
Nasa' ¿ma'wẽsana açga txã'sa' peeygãja'?
Nasa pçuwa'jsaçxá's ¿kĩjxa'ga txakwe wala pa'pçxuna yaaki'?
7 Nasa's maz en yu' angeleswe'sxte ãhmeekwesa na'wẽg nvxiht,
Nawa jxthaakwe walasa yuuwa'j vxitçxag nvxiht,
Txã'wẽ yũuçxa iidx vxitnitxi' txãaçxaçxáa jxkaajxa' ãjawa'ja's ũsgu.
8 Kĩhwa jxuka txãaçxaçxá's nwẽese'jwa'ja's vxitgu jĩ'k.
Dxusa' txã'wẽ jxuka txãaçxaçxá's nwẽese'jwa'ja's vxit yã'jçxa', kĩh yuhwa nwẽese'jwa'jsamée yu' nvxihtme'. Txã'wẽtewa ãçxpkaçx txãaçxaçxá's kĩhwa jxuka nwẽese'jni yu's iiuymeetha'w. 9 Na'wẽ maz en yu' angeleswe'sxte ãhmeekwesa vxitku txã' Jesusa' txã'snak Dxusa' kwe'sx jxukaysa pa'gate uuwa'jsa vxit. Nawa ãçxha' Dxus pukate u'pa', txãaçxaçxáa jxkaahwa'jsa yuuçxa txã'wẽ pxthaa yuuçxa uupa'ga.
10 Kĩjkĩhnawa jxuka vxit-sa Dxusku txajx luuçx yuuwa'jsa yuutxi' jxuka nwe'weçxa txajx tasxte nuypa'jya' Jesukristo's pxthaa yuuçxa uuwa'j vxit, kĩhte yuhwa sxiigmée nwe'wesa yuukahn. 11 Txã'wẽ pkaltxi peltunãynisa vxite' pkaltxi peltunãysa txãa yakh e'zsa' teeçx Neytewe'sxçxaçxáata' txãa Neya' Dxusa'. Txã'wẽ teeçx Neytewe'sxçxáa ne'tka'w, txãa pa'gak Dxus Nçxi'ka' kwe'sxtxi's thaamemée Adx nyakh jĩna we'we'. 12 Txã'snak Dxus librute na'jĩ':
Idx ma'wẽsatewa adx nyakhwe'sxtxi pta'sxi'nja,
Sa' adx nyakhwe'sx ksxavxyte idxa's weçxana tuuthe'je'nja jĩ'k.
13 Sa' vxite ajkhẽwa na'wẽk we'we':
Adxa' Dxusçxáana yajkxna fxi'ze'nja.
Sa' ki'kin we'weçxa':
Dxus ũsni luuçx ayte ũsta' txãa yakh ayte ũsthu jĩ'k.
14 Nasawa pxakhsa jĩnisa' maazkuẽeçxawa kxazíi teeçx ney kakwe petx ee jxpa'gaçxa yuuçxáatx pxakhsa yu', txãa pa'gak Jesuswa pxakhsa yuuya' nasaa kakwe's jxpa'ga uuya' ãjaya'. Txã'wẽ yũuçxak kluuste uuçxa uuwa'j vxit-sa's knay. Txã'wẽ nasatx uuwa'j vxit-sa' eçxthẽ'kh yu'. 15 Txã'wẽ yũuçxak Jesusa' jxukaysatx nwe'we bagaçxtewa uuwa'ja's ũukhna yuuçxáa fxi'zenijũ. 16 Txã' angeleswe'sxtxi pu'çxya' kĩhme', wejxwa nasa Abrahan ji'jtxku pu'çxya' kĩh. 17 Txã'wẽ nasatx pu'çxya' ãjaya'k kwe'sx na'wẽsa yuu kĩhtewa jxuka. Txãasak nyakh na'wẽ yuuna pa'jçxa kwe'sxtxi peeygãawa'ja's ji'phuçxa pkalsawe'sx pa'gate Dxus tasxte we'wewa'jsa yuu. Sa' nasaa pkaltxi jxuka peltunãyya'k pxthaa yuuçxa uuwa'j ji'phuna ũsu'. 18 Txã'wẽ nasaa kakwe's jxpa'gaçxak pkalte kwe'sx çxhãçxhamée fxi'zeni's jxuka jiyu. Txãasa ãçxha' pkalte çxhãçxhamée fxi'zesatxi's kĩhte yuhwa sxiigmée nwe'weya' ãjawa'jsa'.
