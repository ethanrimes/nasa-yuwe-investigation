3
Nwe'wewa'ja's u'se pheu'jçxa Dxus nvxiitnisa
1 Txã'wẽ we'wena fxi'jçxa', peekíiçxáa ewsath jĩnak we'we' ¿sũjuwá'kwe? Meeçxa' vxitewa na'wẽtx neewe'wenisa karta jxũçxa pa'ja' aça' txãawa txã'wẽy jxũçxa pa'jwa'j ji'pha' ¿sũjuwá'kwe? Meeçxawa i'kwe'sx neewe'wenisa karta kutxi'jwa'j ji'pha' ¿sũjuwá'kwe? 2 Adxa' txã'wẽ vxitesa neewe'wenisa karta jxũçxa pa'jwa'j ji'phmeeth, txãa pa'gatey adxna neewe'wewa'jsa' i'kwe'sxíiçxáa i'kwe. Txãasa adx mjĩini's maatxwa kaajiyu'jwa'jsa' i'kwe'sxi'kwe, karta na'wẽsa. 3 Kwe'sxtxi neewe'wesa' Kristo', txãasak txajx kuseju yuu fxi'jçxa karta ũs, aça' txãa karta' i'kwe'sxi'kwe. Nawa txãa karta' tintaju fxi'jni yuume'. Dxus Espiritujuk fxi'jni yuu, sa' kwet taplatewa fxi'jni yuumée, nasaa ũustek fxi'jni neeyũu.
4 Kristo pu'çxpa'ga, Dxusa's selpiiya' ãjãth, txãasath ĩsxiimée isa we'we'. 5 Dxusa's selpiiya' ãjãnisa' peekxçxáa txã'wẽ ya'ãjã'çme'. Txã'sa' Dxus pu'çxtek txã'wẽ ya'ãjã'. 6 Dxusa' naa ensu nasatx nwe'weya' ãjãwa'ja's pheu'jçxa nvxihtku txã' leya's nwẽese'jwa'j yu'çme', naasáa Dxus Espiritu's yuuçxáa ũuste jxpa'gawa'kh yu', aça' txã'sna pta'sxya' ãjãwa'ja'sku Dxusa' adxa's pu'çx. Ley fxi'jçxa nvxiitnisa' uuwa'theçxáak vxit, naapkaçxha' Dxus Espiritu' Dxus yakh fxi'zewa'ja'sku vxit.
7-8 Moises ley kwet taplate fxi'jnisa's jxpa'ga enteçxáa yuhwa seena' yuh zhiçxkwe eena' kweethku. Txãa pa'ga Moises dxi'pwa txã'wẽyku eena' kweethna neeyũu. Aça' Israelwe'sxa' Moises dxi'pte thegya' ãjãmeeta', Moises dxi'pa' seena' eena' kweethpa'ga. Nawa tuhdtuhdçxah ũçxhana u'kh. Txã'wẽ uuwa'theçxáa vxit-sa leya's jxpa'ga enteçxáa yuhwa txã'wẽ seena' zhiçxkwe eena' kweethçxa', Dxus Espiritu kaapiya'jni en yu' wejx thaakwe zhiçxkwesa'. 9 Txã'wẽ yuuwesa i'kwe jĩna pta'sxsa leya's jxpa'ga enteçxáa yuhwa seena' zhiçxkwe yuuk aça' kwe'sxtxi yuuwemée ewte nvxiitwa'jsa's pta'sxni en yu' wejxku thaakwe zhiçxkwesa yu'. 10 Ley jxpa'ga ente' txã'wẽ yuh seena' zhiçxkwesa yuuk. Nawa ãçxh naa ensu thaakwe zhiçxkwesa vxyaak txãa yakh kaaja'da'the yu', nyafxiiwe'sxa' seelpimeesak yuu. 11 Txaçxmeeçxáa selpiwa'jsa pa'j enteçxáa yuhwa zhiçxkwe yuuçxa', bagaçxte yuhwa pçumée selpiwa'j en yu' wejx thaakwe zhiçxkwesa'.
12 Txã'wẽ bagaçxte yuhwa pçumée selpiwa'ja's jiiçxath ãate pta'sxna we'we'. 13 Adxa' Moises na'wẽ yũuçmeeth. Moisesa' txaçxmeeçxáa eena' kweethnisa ũçxhaçte Israelwe'sx uywe'sxte dxi'pa's yaaphku. 14 Sa' Moisesa' kĩh yuukahn txã'wẽ yaaphtewa Israelwe'sxa' jiyumeeta'. Sa' ãçxh pa'jwa aphni ũsna jiyukahmen, txãa pa'gatx txãa leya's lẽeyçxawa jiyu'çmée. Aça' txãa aphnisa's Kristo's pi'kxçxa yuuçxáa phaadeya' ya'ãjana. 15 Ãçxpkaçx aphni ũsna, Moises fxi'jnitx lẽeyçxawa jiyukahmen. 16 Aça' maawa Jesukristo tasxte utxaate yuuçxáak txãa aphnisa' phaadeni yu' jiyukahn. 17 Kwe'sxtxi jxpe'jsa' Dxus Espiritu', aça' txãa yakh ũste yu', jiyuwa'ja's weh yuhwa ya'jypubameena. 18 Txãa pa'gatey kwe'sxa' dxi'pa's yaaphni ji'phmeetha'w, txãa pa'gak Dxus kweethni' kwe'sx dxi'pte speeju na'wẽ eena' kweethya' ãjã'. Aça' Dxus Espiritu pu'çxte', jxukaysa jwee íiçxáatha'w Kristo ma'wẽsate', txãa na'wẽy fxi'zena u'jya' ãja', kwe'sxtxi jxpe'jsa Dxus Espiritu' txã'wẽ yũuwa'ja'sku pu'çxhi' naa pa'ga.
