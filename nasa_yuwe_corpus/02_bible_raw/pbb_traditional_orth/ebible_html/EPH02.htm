Dxus peeygãapa'ga yuuçxáa nwe'wenisa
1 Dxusa' Kristo'swa ma'wẽga ĩtxi vxit, txã'wẽyku i'kwe'sxtxiwa ĩtxi vxit. Txã'wẽme', nyafxiia' i'kwe'sxa' pkalpa'ga Dxus dxi'pte uuníi na'wẽ yã'ji'kwe ũs yu'. 2 Txãasai'kwe txagaçxtey yu' eçxthẽ'j naa kiwete jxkaahni yuuçxá's nwẽese'jna ũsu'. Dxusa's nwẽese'jya' wa'lsa yuutxi's eçxthẽ'ja' jweeíiçxáak kwa'lu'ju', aça' txã'snai'kwe nwẽese'jsa yu'. 3 I'kwe'sxçxáamée, kwe'sxwa nyafxiia' txã'wẽçxaçxáatha'w fxi'zesa yu' jxukaysa, ewmée yaakxniçxá's jxuka kxtey yuuna. Txãa pa'ga Dxus kastigo seena'sa's jxpa'gawa'jsatha'w yu' maawa jxukaysa.
4 Nawa txã'wẽ pkalpa'ga Dxus dxi'pte uuníi na'wẽ yã'j ũstewa, Dxusa' kwe'sxtxi iiméh wala ew yũuk peeygãhçxa. 5 Txãasak Kristo'swa ma'wẽga uuniijũwa ĩtxi vxit, txã'wẽy kwe'sxtxiwa ĩtxi vxit. Kĩhte yuhwa seelpimeesaçxawa, Dxus peeygãapa'ga yuuçxáai'kwe nwe'wenisa yuu. 6 Dxusa' Kristo'swa ma'wẽga uuniijũwa ĩtxi vxit, txã'wẽy kwe'sxtxiwa ĩtxi vxitçxak cielute Kristo yakh kaykaçxi'j. 7 Nawa kwe'sxtxi txajx peeygãani txakwe seena' walasa's naa e'su' ãate kaavxya'ja'jya'k txã'wẽ yũu kwe'sx txakwe wala ãhmeekwetewa, naasáa Kristo pa'ga yuuçxáa. 8 Txãa pa'ga i'kwe'sxa' kĩhte yuhwa seelpimeesaçxawa, naasáa Dxus peeygãhçxa nwe'wenisaçxáa i'kwe, txã'wẽ Kristo's ũusuh jxuka yaakxpa'ga. Dxus nwe'weni's i'kwe'sx kakweju ew yũuna fxi'zeçxa uymeei'kwe, txã'sa' Dxusku pees peeygãhçxa. 9 Dxus nwe'weni's kakweju ew yũuna fxi'zeçxa ya'jypa'ga'çme' naa pa'ga maa yuhwa iiwejçx yaakxwa'j ji'phmeeta'. 10 Txãa pa'gatey kwe'sx u'se yu'pthehwa'ja's Dxus pheu'jçxa nvxiitnisatha'w, ewçxáa yũuna Kristo Jesus jxpe'jni fxi'zewa'jsa. Txã'wẽ yũuna fxi'zewa'ja's Dxusa' nyafxiiteyku pheu'jçxa nvxiht, txã'wẽ yuuçxáa yũuna fxi'zekahn.
Dxus tasxte pa'jwa'ja's ji'phuni
11 I'kwe'sxa' nyafxiia' ma'wẽsakwe yu' txã'sna miiyajkxwe: i'kwe'sxa' judiu ji'jmeei'kwe yu', aça' judiuwe'sxa' i'kwe'sxtxi's Dxus txhitxnimeesata' jĩnatx we'we'. 12 Sa' txãa ensu' judiuwe'sx yakhmée fxi'zesa i'kwe yu', Kristo's ũuste ji'phmeesa. Sa' i'kwe'sxa' Dxus pu'çxwa'ja'swa ji'phmeesa i'kwe yu'. Txãa pa'ga i'kwe bagaçxte yuhwa Dxus tasxte pa'jwa'j yu'sa' ji'phmeesa yu'. 13 Makwe nyafxíi yu' Dxus tasxte pa'jwa'ja's ji'phmeesaçxawa, ãçxha' Kristo Jesusa's nwẽese'jya' utxaapa'ga, Dxus tasxte pa'jwa'ja's uyi'kwe, Kristo' i'kwe'sxtxi nwe'weya' kluuste uupa'ga. 14 Kristo' txã'wẽ yũuçxak judiuwe'sx judiu ji'jmeesatx ahçena fxi'zeni's ivxiituçxa e'zsatx teeçx jwedçxáa vxitna pa'j. 15 Sa' Moises leya'swa kaapçu'ju'k, Kristo tasxteçxaçxáa utxaaçxa e'zsa puutx yahçemée kxteeçxah ih fxi'zekahn. 16 Kristo' kluuste uuçxak e'zsatx kxteeçxah ih fxi'zewa'ja's vxitçxa, Dxus yakh yuuwemeesa vxit.
17 Kristo' kĩhçxa' e'zíi Dxus yakh yuuwemée neeyũuwa'ja's pta'sxku, i'kwe'sx Dxus tasxte pa'jwa'ja's ji'phmeesatxwa pta'sxku, sa' judiuwe'sx Dxus tasxte fxi'zesatxwa pta'sxku. 18 Txãa pa'ga ãçxha' judiumtha'w yuu meeçxa' judiumeemtha'w yuu nawa Dxus Espiritu's jxpa'gaçxa e'zíi Dxus tasxte pa'jwa'ja's ji'ptha'w. 19 Ãçxha' i'kwe'sxa' nasa ekajuwe'sx meei'kwe, Dxus luuçx yakh kxteeçxah utxaaçxa teeçx yattewe'sxçxáa i'kwe yuuna pa'j jxukaysa txãa na'wẽsaçxaçxáa. 20 Txã'wẽ kxteeçxah utxaapa'ga ew jiyute yu' i'kwe'sxa' yat na'wẽsa i'kwe: yata's nyafxte' kwet yakhku ya'kaaçxida'ja', aça' txãa kaaçxida'jnisa yu' Jesukristo'. Txã'wẽ yuuna mjĩiya' takhsa' Dxusna selpisaawe'sx vxite' Jesus jxkaahnisawe'sxtxi yu'. 21 Aça' i'kwe'sxa' txãawe'sx kaaçxida'jna takhni kajkwete yata's ew pheu'jna umna u'jnisai'kwe, teeçx yatçxáa yuuna kãhçxa Dxus yuuçxá's selpiwa'jsa. 22 Txãa pa'ga i'kwe'sxa' Jesukristo yakh utxaaçxa', Dxus txhitxhnisa maa yakhwa kxteeçxah utxasáa i'kwe yuu, teeçx yatçxáa yuuna, txãa yatte Dxus ũswa'j yuuya'.
