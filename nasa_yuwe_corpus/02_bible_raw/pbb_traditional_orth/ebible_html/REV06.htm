6
Siete selxotx sudeni
1 Txajũ' uythu Piisxakuẽ jĩnisa' siete selxo nyafxtewe'sxa's sudeçte, sa' wẽse'th Dxus vxitni ĩtxĩsa teeçxsa' seena' sus na'jĩna:
—Yuhçxa mtheg jĩni's. 2 Aça' txã'wẽ wẽse'jçxa', thegçxa uythu teeçx jiba çxihmesa's, kxtee a'jsa yu' kusete ũpanisa arko yaasesa'sku ji'phu'. Txajũ' txã'sa' teeçx koronak ũsni yuu, aça' txãa yu' kĩhtewa jxuka puiiya' jiisak kaseh, mteewa puiiçxa knaywa'jsa.
3 Txajũ' Piisxakuẽ jĩnisa' vxite selxo's sudek e'stewe'sxna, aça' wẽse'th Dxus vxitni ĩtxĩsa e'zsatewe'sx na'jĩna:
—Mtheg jĩni's. 4 Sa' uythu vxite jiba behsa kasejeçte, aça' kxtee a'jsa yu's jxkaahnik yuu, naa kiwete wẽt fxi'zeni's ivxiitute nasaa pwe'sx puutx ikhna u'khan. Sa' teeçx espada walasak ũsni yuu.
5 Txajũ' Piisxakuẽ jĩnisa' vxite selxo's sudek tekhtewe'sxna, aça' wẽse'th Dxus vxitni ĩtxĩsa tekhsatewe'sx na'jĩna:
—Mtheg jĩni's. Aça' uythu vxite jiba khũçxsa's aça' kxtee a'jsa yu' kusete isanisa balansa yaasesa'sku ji'phu'. 6 Sa' wẽse'th Dxus vxitni ĩtxĩsa ksxavxyu teeçx na'jĩna we'weni's:
—E'z libra skuutxhçxáana teen mjĩinite ãhpa'ga txweywa'j yuuna, sa' seis libra cebada'swa txakwey pa'gate txweywa'j yuuna. Nawa aceite vxino txã'wẽsa yuutxi'sa' ivxiitupu'n jĩ'k.
7 Txajũ' Piisxakuẽ jĩnisa' vxite selxo's sudek pahztewe'sxna, aça' wẽse'th Dxus vxitni ĩtxĩsa pahzsatewe'sx na'jĩna:
—Mtheg jĩni's. 8 Aça' txã'wẽ wẽse'jçxa' thegçxa uythu teeçx jiba seena' ũukhũukhsa's, aça' kxtee a'jsa yu' Ikhsa jĩna kxsusni yasek ji'phu'. Naapkaçxha' txãa e'ste' uusaawe'sx yujuni jĩnisak vxyaa. Txãa jibate a'jsa yu'sa' jxkaahnik yuu naa kiwete nasatx puupxãhn ãatuw ikhkahn gweçxa vxitçxa wẽjẽ kaapa'ja'jçxa weewa txã'wẽy kaapa'ja'jçxa yu'kh eçx juuna'sawa kaapena'jçxa.
9 Txajũ' Piisxakuẽ jĩnisa' vxite selxo's sudek tahçtewe'sxna. Aça' Kristo's selpiipa'ga ikhni yuutx txãawe'sxçxaçxáa altal tasxte ũsteth uy. 10 Sa' txãawe'sxa' na'jĩnatx sus we'we':
—Kwe'sxtxi jxpe'jsa atesa idxa' kĩhtewa kxtey yuusáa ¿makwe txaçx jxthẽeyuuya'waga, kwe'sxtxi ahçeçxa ikhtxi txãawe'sxtxi kastigãyya'? jĩte', 11 pasçxa':
—Ãçxhíi yu' ũuçxkwe uythãsna jxkaasena meswe, Kristo's selpiipa'ga i'kwe'sx na'wẽy ikhni yuuwa'j peejxta' txãawe'sxtxi kxtey yuuna jxã'phkaçx jĩçxa', ropa çxihmesatx kaathe'jni yuu.
12 Txajũ' Piisxakuẽ jĩnisa' vxite selxo's sudek seistewe'sxna. Aça' uythu seena' ehd u'jweçte seka' jxuka khũçx kãjk kweethmeepkaçx, a'tewa jxuka ee na'wẽk beh yuu. 13 Naapkaçxha' cielute estrelxa' ũsxitx naa kiwete, fxtũ yũnwa ma'wẽta wejxa çxhãçxha kẽese'the jxuka ũsxi' txã'wẽtx yuu. 14 Aça' cielu' eç na'wẽ jxuka pelni yuuçxa vxyaame'. Naapkaçxha' thã'j vxiç vxite' ĩkh pxãhsu kiwe u'psa jxuka upenitx yuu ẽse'sete. 15 Aça' naa kiwete jxkaahsawe'sxa' wala ũukhçxa kiwe kafxsu vxite' kwet kafxsu jxuka jxsũuthtxi, walasawe'sx, ji'phsawe'sx, npiiçthẽ'jwe'sx, jxtuhthesawe'sx, vxite' patron kusete selpiina fxi'zesawe'sx vxite' patron ji'phmée wẽt fxi'zesawe'sx jxukatx txã'wẽ yũu. 16 Sa' txã'wẽ yũuçxa thã'j vxiç vxite' kwet penxatx na'jĩnatx we'we':
—Kwe'sxtxi jxuka akaaçxçxa kaavxya'ja'jnu na'wẽ jxkaahna u'pa' txajx dxi'pte, vxite' Piisxakuẽ jĩnisa seena' yuh kastigãyya' ũsa' txã'wẽsa dxi'pte. 17 Txã'wẽme', txã'wẽ seena' kastigãywa'j en ãjk, aça' ¿kim kxte' wantãyya' ãjãkx? jĩ'tx.
