7
Ptamuní ley yakh kaaja'da'jni
1 Yakhthẽ'jwe'sx maawa leya's jiisa' peekx jii i'kwe leya' naa kiwete nasa nee ĩtxiyteçxáa jxkaajxa' ãjawa'jsa'. 2 Ptamuuçxa fxi'zeni leya's miiyajkxwe wakx: U'y iimi'sa' txajx nmi' uu ja'damée yu' vxite yakh iimi'ya' ãjãwa'jsame'. Txã'wẽ txajx nmi' uumey vxite piçthẽ'j yakh utxaaçxa fxi'zeçxa', pkal yuusak yu' jĩ'k. 3 Nawa nmi' uuniijũyã'ja', peena yu' txã'wẽ jxkaahna nes yuuya' ãja'çme', aça' txajx nmi' uuniijũ' vxite piçthẽ'j yakh iimi'ya' ãjawa'ja'sku jxkaaja'.
4 I'kwe'sxwa txã'wẽy Kristo tasxte utxaasá' uusáa na'wẽsai'kwe, aça' leya' uusa's jxkaajxa' ãjawa'jsame'. Aça' ãçxha' i'kwe'sxa' Kristo uuçxa ki' ĩtxi yuuk txajx nasai'kwe, Dxus yuuçxáana selpiina fxi'zewa'jsa. 5 Txã'wẽ Kristo tasxte utxamey, kwe'sx ewmée yũuya' yaakxnitx ley pta'sxçxa': Ewme' jĩ'k txãatxiçxáak wejx thaakwe yaakx wẽe yuu. Aça' txã'wẽ yaakxpa'ga Dxus kastigote uuwa'jçxáak yuu. 6 Nawa ãçxha' leya' kwe'sxtxi thegwa'j ji'phme'. Txãa pa'ga ãçxha' Dxus Espiritu jxkaahniçxá's nwẽese'jwa'j ji'ptha'w, leya's yajkxmée.
Adx ũuste pkal ũssa
7 Txã'wẽte', leya' ewmeesana ¿sũju'kwe'? Weh yuhwa txã'wẽme'. Txãa leya' naasáa kĩh txã'wẽ pkaltewa pta'sxsaçxá'. Ley pa'gameewaça', adxa' jiyuwa'jmée ne'tka yu' kĩh txã'wẽ pkaltewa. Ley pta'sxçxa': Vxite jĩitx kusa'j wẽena ewmée yũuwa'ja's yajkxna fxi'zewa'jme' jĩmeewaça', adxa' jiyuwa'jmée ne'tka yu' txã'wẽ yaakxnisa' pkaltewa. 8 Ley pta'sxçxa': Txã'wẽ yaakxni' ewme' jĩnijũk adx ũuste jweeíiçxáa ewmée yũu wẽenisaçxáa ũusuthe. Ley txã'jĩmeewaça', pkalna sũhwa'j kasehwa'jmeene' yu'. 9 Adxa' nyafxiia' ley pta'sxni's jiyumey yu', yuuwemeesath sũju'çxáath. Nawa ley pta'sxni's jiyunijũk jweeíiçxáa ewmée yũu wẽe yuu. Txãa pa'gath isa yuuwesa ne'tka sũhwa'ja's ji'phu. 10 Txajuth isa ew jiyu, leya' nwe'wepkaçxte, ewmée yũuwa'ja's kçxhãaçxha'jsaçxáak yuu adx yakha'. 11 Txãa pa'gatey pkala' leypa'gaíiçxáak jweeíiçxáa çxhãçxhaçxa adxa's Dxus kastigote uuwa'j vxit.
12 Txã'wẽçxawa, leya' ewmeesame', txãa pa'gatey leya' kĩhtewa jxuka ew yũuna fxi'zewa'ja'sçxáak jxkaaja'. 13 Txã'wẽ leya' ewsaçxa', ¿ma'wẽki'k adxa's Dxus kastigote uuwa'j vxit? sũju'th. Nawa ley txã'wẽ uuwa'j vxitme'. Ewmée yũu wẽenisak Dxus kastigote uuwa'j vxit. Isa ew jiyute yu', ley jxkaahni ewsa's nwẽese'jmée ewmée yũu wẽenisaçxá's nwẽese'pha'gak txã'wẽ pkal yu'. Aça' txãa pa'gak nwẽese'jnimeesa' pkalna sũhna jiyunijũ' yuuwesa uuwa'j ya'yu'.
14 Jiitha'w leya' Dxus yakh fxi'zewa'ja's yuh pta'sxi'k. Nawa Dxus yakh fxi'zemée ewmée yũuwa'ja'sçxáath yajkxna fxi'ze', naa pa'ga yuuwesa' adxthu. 15 Adxa' jiyu'çmeeth ma'wẽçxa ne'tka adxna selpiwa'jsa yu'sa' kxtey yuumeeçxa', adxna ewmée vxitwa'jsa yu'sa' kxtey yu'. 16 Ley pta'sxçxa' ewme' jĩ'k aça' ewmeesatewa jiiçxath txãa ewmeesa's íiçxáa kxtey yu', txãa pa'gath leya' yuuwemeesana sũju'. 17 Peekxçxáa txã'wẽ ya'yu'çmeene'. Adx ũuste ewmeesa ũsna txãane' txã'wẽ ewmée yũuwa'ja'sçxáa kaaũusuthe'je'. 18 Txãa pa'gath adx ũuste' kĩh yuhwa ewsa yu' meena sũju'. Txajíiçxáa ne'tka ewsa's nwẽese'j wẽeçxawa, kxtey yuuya' ãja'çmée. 19 Naasáa selpiwa'jmeesaçxá's kxtey yuuçxa', selpiwa'jsa yu'sa' kxtey yu'çmeeth. 20 Na'jĩ'th na': Adxna ewmée vxitwa'jsa yu'sa' kxtey yuuçxa', peekxçxáa txã'wẽ ya'yu'çmeene', adx ũuste ewmée kaaũusuthe'jsa ũsna txãane' ewmeesaçxá's kxtey yuuwa'ja's kaaũusuthe'je' açne' txã'wẽ ya'yu'.
21 Txajíiçxáak ewsaçxá's kxtey yu'nja sũhtewa, ewmée yũuwa'jçxáa kaseje'. 22 Adx yu', Dxus leya's kxtey yu'nja sũhtewa, 23 adx ũuste ewmeesa ũsna txãak ew yũuwa'ja's kaaũusuthe'je'çmée. Txãasak adxa's ewmeete yuuçxáa jxpe'je'.
24 Adxa' seena' yuh ãhmeesath. Aça' adxa's ¿kim nwe'wekx naa kakwe na'wẽ uuwa'the yuuçxáa pe'jna u'jweça' txãajũ? 25 Txã'wẽtewa Jesukristo' pejka nwe'wek txãajũ, txãa pa'gath Dxusa's wala weçxá'. Txã'wẽme', nyafxíi yu' Dxusa's selpi'nja sũhçxawa, adx ũuste ewmée yũuya' yaakxniçxá'sthu selpi'.
