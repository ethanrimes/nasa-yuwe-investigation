21
Vxiuda ofxreda akhni
(Mr 12.41-44)
1 Wala ji'phsawe'sx dxuus yatte ofxreda wala ãsxiçtek uy Jesusa'. 2 Txã'wẽy teeçx vxiuda puuplesawa kũhçxa', e'z vxyuu musxkuẽ akheçte uyçxa' 3 na'jĩk:
—Isath we'we', naa vxiuda puuple' maatewa jxthaakwek wala akh. 4 Vxite yu' akhçxawa, neeyũusáa peejxmeesaçxáan yã'jtx akhe'. Nawa naa u'ya' kĩh yuhwa ji'phmeesaçxawa txajx ũ' weywa'ja's jxukak akh jĩk.
Dxuus yat pçuwa'ja's Jesus pta'sxni
(Mt 24.1-2; Mr 13.1-2)
5 Txajũ' kxtee ũssa maaíi yu' dxuus yata's thegçxatx we'wena ũsu': Ofxreda peesni yakh wala yuh zhiçxkwe pheu'jna txaahni' jĩte', Jesusa' na'jĩk:
6 —Ãçxhíi yu' txã'wẽ yuh zhiçxkwe thegna ũsa', nawa kĩh yuhwa nvxihtmée jxuka suwní yuuwa'j en pa'jana. Aça' teeçx kwetçxáa yuhwa kẽesẽ'jnimée yu' neeyũumeetxna naa yattewe'sx jĩk.
Naa kiwe pçuwa'j en ãjaçte seena'sa vxyaawa'j
(Mt 24.3-28; Mr 13.3-23)
7 Txã'jĩte':
—¿Bagaçx txã'wẽ yuukx? meeçxawa txã'wẽ yũuwa'j en ãjãçte' ¿kĩhçxa yuukx? jĩna pẽjxte', 8 na'wẽk pta'sx:
—I'kwe'sxtxi kaypuba'jwe'sxte mpa'yajkxwe. Txã'wẽme' wala kasejetxna Dxus kaahnisatha'w jĩna na'jĩna: Adxa' Kristoth, aça' ãçxha' nmeh en ãjk jĩtxna. Nawa txãawe'sxtxi' nwẽesẽ'jnuwe. 9 I'kwe'sxa' wẽsẽ'jene'kwe gweçxa seena'sa yuja' jĩni, nawa txã' nyafxtewe'sxçxáata' aça' ũukhmeene'kwe. Nmeh en yu' ãhméy txã'wẽ yuuna.
10 Txajũ' ma'wẽ yũuwa'ja'swa jxuka pta'sxku na'jĩna:
—Txãn en ãjãçte', gobierno' vxite kiwete gobierno yakh puiitxna. Naapkaçxha' puutx jxu'j kiweju pwe'sxwa puiitxna. 11 Sa' msuu msuuwa seena' ehd u'jwena. Wẽjẽwa txã'wẽ wala pa'jana. Naapkaçxha' kakwete wee seena'sawa pa'jana. Sa' cieluju kĩjkĩhwa seena'saçxáa vxyaatxna. 12 Nawa txã'wẽ seena' yũuwa'j en pa'jmeyna, i'kwe'sxtxi ahçeçxa preesu'jya' pakweetxna. Sa' judiuwe'sx pkhaakhenisuwa jxũna u'jwetxna: Nasa ewmeesa' jĩna. Txã'wẽ yũuna karceltewa txajatxna, i'kwe'sxa' adxna nwẽesẽ'pha'gaçxáa jxkaahsawe'sx tasxsu vxite' ne'jwe'sx tasxsuwa kuutxa'jni yuune'kwe. 13 Txã'wẽ yũute', Dxus yuwe's txãawe'sxtxiwa pta'sxwa'j en ji'phune'kwe. 14 I'kwe'sxa' txãawe'sxtxi' ¿ma'wẽ pasukxtha'w? sũhna kuh yajkxmeene'kwe, 15 txãa pa'gatey adxa' i'kwe'sxtxi açesawe'sxtxi ma'wẽ paswa'ja'swa kaaũusuthe'je'nja. Aça' txãawe'sxa': Ĩsxĩija'g jĩya' ãjãmeetxna maa yuhwa. 16 Sa' i'kwe'sx nwe'sxíi yuhwa açeni yuune'kwe, tatawe'sx, yakhthẽ'jwe'sx, namikuwe'sx preesu'jutxna. Aça' maaíi yu' ikhni yuune'kwe. 17 I'kwe'sxa' adxna nwẽesẽ'pha'ga naa kiwete jxukaysa açeni yuune'kwe. 18 Nawa i'kwe'sx dxkhasçxáa yuhwa vxitumeetxna teeçxçxáa yuhwa. 19 I'kwe'sxa' uu entepkaçx Dxus yakh txutemée fxi'zeçxa', Dxus tasxte ĩtxĩ fxi'zewa'ja's ji'phune'kwe.
20 Jerusalen çxhaba's soldauçxaçxáa jxuka ktadxiiçxa ũste uyçxa', ãçxha' naa çxhaba' jxuka pçuwa'jsana sũhna jii ũsune'kwe. 21 Naapkaçxha' Judeasuwe'sxa' ũphna u'jwetxna yu'khsu, aça' Jerusalen çxhabtewe'sxwa txãa çxhabu ũphna kasejetxna, vxite' çxhab ekajuyã'j ũssa' çxhabte u'kameetxna. 22 Txã'wẽme' txãn ensu' kastigo seena'sa yuuna Dxus librute ma'wẽga pta'sxi' txã' jxuka kxtey yuuna. 23 Aça' txãn ensu' nasa ji'phsa meeçxawa luuçx ãpãkuẽ ji'phsa' wala yuh pxthaa yuutxna, txã'wẽ txãa nasatx kastigo seena'sa pa'the', naa kiwete wala yuh nxuswa'j yuuna. 24 Maaíitxi's gweçxate ikhetxna, sa' vxite yuutxi's preesu'jçxa jxu'j kiwen jxũna u'jwetxna. Naapkaçxha' Jerusalen çxhaba's vxite nasa u'kaçxa jxuka suwçxa kxtee makwe txaçxwa ũsutxna.
Dxus Nçxi'k Nasa Yuusáa seena' zhiçxkwe vxyaana
(Mt 24.29-35, 42-44; Mr 13.24-37)
25 Txã'wẽ yũunijũ' sek a'te estrelxa txãasu seena'çxáa vxyaatxna. Aça' naa kiwete nasa mtee mteewe'sxwa jxuka jxpubana iiméh wala ũukhutxna txã'wẽ ĩkh wala seena' yuuna susni's wẽsẽ'jçxa. 26 Txã'wẽ seena' yuuçte', naa kiwete nasa' ũukhçxa ũus yu'swa ivxiitutxna, naapkaçxha' ẽe walate kĩjkĩh ũssawa jxuka seena' kẽesẽ'jni yuutxna. 27 Txajũ' Dxus Nçxi'k Nasa Yuusa's uyuutxna, tãaph pxãhte sek na'wẽ seena' zhiçxkwe khikhyna kĩjaçte. 28 Txã'wẽ seena' yuuya' takheçte', i'kwe'sxa' ũukhmée weçxana ũythasna meswe, jwee yu' jxthẽeyuumée nwe'weni yuune'kwe jĩk.
29 Txãa e'su' vxite ejemplo pta'sxku:
—Mthegwe higwera fxtũu tasx meeçxawa vxite fxtũu tasxtxi. 30 Sa' txãa fxtũu tasx yeeçuçte uyçxa', yũnya'watewa jiyu'i'kwe. 31 Txã'wẽy adx pta'sxni kxtey yuuçte uyçxa', Dxus naa kiwete kĩiwa'j en utxaaçna sũhna jii meswe.
32 Isath we'we': naa ensu nasa uumeyna, txã'wẽ kxtey yuuna. 33 Cielu naa kiwe jxuka skhẽuutxna, nawa adx we'weni' jxuka kxtey yuumey yu', skhẽwmeetxna.
34 Wala jxpa'yakx meswe, ewmée yũuna tũuna kĩjkĩhwa naa kiwetewe'sxçxá's yajkxna fxi'zenuwe. Txã'wẽme' Dxus yu's yajkxmée ũsiyna, Dxus kĩiwa'j en ãhçxáa yuuna. 35 Niimalwa jiimeeçxa' ma'wẽga yajkxte khẽje', txã'wẽyçxáa Dxus kĩiwa'j ena' kpaakina naa kiwete nasa mtee mteewe'sxtxiwa jxukaysatx. 36 I'kwe'sxwa wala jxpa'yakx meswe, na'wẽ seena' yuuya'wa' txajũ iyuya'. Dxus yakh puutx we'wena nes myuuwe bagaçxtewa, sa' txã'wẽ yũuçxa' Dxus Nçxi'k Nasa Yuusáa tasxte utxaaya' ãjãne'kwe jĩk.
37 Jesusa' en yu' dxuus yattek kaapiya'ja', nawa kus yu' Oliwos vxiçnak u'jwe'. 38 Aça' nasa' een isa kuusíitx dxuus yatte pa'ja' Jesus kaapiya'jni's wẽsẽ'jya '.
