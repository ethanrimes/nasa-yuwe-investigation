24
Pablo ma'wẽsaçxawa Fxelix tasxte we'weni
1 Tahç ente' sacerdote npiiçthẽ'j Ananiasa' pa'kh Cesarea çxhabte judiu nthẽ'jsawe'sx yã'jçxa, sa' teeçx yuwee pu'çxsa Tértulo yaasesa'sku pe'jçxa pa'j. Txãawe'sxa' utxaatx ne'jwe'sx tasxte Pablo' ewmeesa' jĩna pãatxhĩ'khya'. 2 Txajũ' Pablo's nuykũhte', Tértulo' Fxelixa's pta'sxku:
—Weçx u'pu'ga, ne'jwe'sx. Iidx jxkaahnite, mteewa wẽt fxi'zena ũstha'w. 3 Mteewa txã'wẽçxaçxáa wẽt fxi'zena ũstha'w, Fxelix idx txã'wẽ ew yũupa'ga. Txãasatha'w wala weçxá'. 4 Nawa idxa's jwee yu' en kaavxitu'jya'wameeth, dudte meen mwẽese'j kwe'sx we'weni's. 5 Naa piçthẽ'ja' iiméh seena' ewmeesatewa kwe'sxa' kpajkxtha'w. Maa kiwesuwa jxuka judiuwe'sxtxi txuhdena puii kiisna ũsa'. Sa' txãaíi Nasarethuwe'sxa's nwẽese'jsa npiiçthẽ'ja'. 6 Dxuus yatte yuhwa nasa pkalsatx ka'ga'jya'wak yu' kazx vxitna. Txãa pa'ga kwe'sxa' tudtha'w, kwe'sx ley ma'wẽ jxkaathe' kxtey yuuya'. 7 Nawa komadante Lisias kwe'sx yakh ja'da wala juuna' kũhçxa kusa'kh, 8 sa' açesawe'sxa' ne'jwe'sx tasxte pãatxhĩ'khya' u'jwetxna jĩnak jxkaah. Kwe'sx we'weni' ĩsxiinimée jxuka isatewa jiyuya' idx yuu mpẽjx naa piçthẽ'ja's jĩnak we'we.
9 Txajũ' judiuwe'sx kxte ih ũssawa txã' jxuka isasa yuja' jĩna we'wete', 10 ne'jwe'sxa' kuseju pta'sxku Pablo's we'wekahn. Aça' Pablo' na'wẽk we'we:
—Adxa' jiith idxa' wala kuh anxu yã'j kwe'sx kiwete jxkaahna ũsgu. Txãa pa'ga adxa' iidx dxi'pte ũukhmée pasya'wath adx ma'wẽsaçxawa. 11 Idx yuu mpaapẽjx txãawe'sx we'weni' isa dxihtewa jiyuya'. Dxusa's Jerusalente neewe'weya' adx pa'jni doce ençxáa yuuça'. 12 Adxa' txãa doce ensu' dxuus yatte nasatx kaapui'jna judiuwe'sx pkhaakheni yat-su çxhabte msuu yuhwa txã'wẽ yũuna ũsu'çmeeth. 13 Aça' ewmée yũunisa' naata' jĩna ãate kaavxya'ja'jya' yu' kim yuhwa ãjameetxna naa nasa'. 14 Nawa ãateth pta'sxi' Dxus tasxte pa'jwa'j dxi'ja's ãçxh u'se kaajiyu'jnisa' ĩsxiinisa' jĩ'tx txã'snath nwẽese'je' kwe'sx yaçgawe'sxtxi jxpe'jsa Dxusa's selpiina. Sa' txã'wẽ Dxus jxkaahni leyte fxi'jni vxite' Dxus yuwe pta'sxsawe'sx fxi'jnitx jxukath kxtey nwẽese'je'. 15 Txãawe'sxwa ma'wẽne'ta Dxusa's nwẽese'jna yaaki' txã'wẽyçxáath adxwa nwẽese'jna yaaki'. Uusaawe'sxa' ki' ĩtxi yuutxna ewsa ma'tx yuu meeçxa' ewmeesa ma'tx yuu nawa ja'daçxah sũhnath yaaki'. 16 Txãasath Dxus dxi'pte vxite' piçthẽ'jwe'sx dxi'ptewa yuuwemée fxi'zewa'ja's pa'pçxu'.
17 Adxa' vxite kiwesu maz anxu pekũhna u'juçxa', adx kiwen u'th judiuwe'sxtxi vxyu peesya' kaahnitx dukhna Dxus ofxreda'swa ũsya'. 18 Adxa' txã'wẽ yũuna ley jxkaahni'swa ki'sna jxã'jçxa dxuus yatte ũspkaçxha', judiuwe'sx Asia kiwejuwe'sx pa'jçxa dxuus yatte uytxi. Nawa nasatx kĩh yuhwa kaapui'jna ũsu'çmeeth. 19 Txã'wẽ uysaawe'sx ayte pãatxhĩ'khya' yuuwa'j ji'phta', adx ewmée yũuna ũstewa uyçxa'. 20 Meeçxa' komadante pkhakhte judiu nthẽ'jsawe'sx vxite' sacerdote npiiçthẽ'jwe'sx kxte ũssa i'kwe'sxçxáawa adx ewmée yũuni's uywá'kwe na' mpeeta'sxwe. 21 Adxa' kxte ũsçxa', Uusaawe'sxa' ki' ĩtxi yuutxna jĩnaçxáath we'we txã'sna wá'kwe ewméek yuu sũju'kx jĩk.
22 Fxelixa's ew yã'kh pta'sxni yu', txãasak txã'wẽçxáa wẽse'jçxaçxáa:
—Komadante Lisias pa'the, txãa yuwe's pheu'ju'nja jĩçxa nvxiht.
23 Txajũ' Fxelixa' kpiitana's jxkaakh:
—Pablo's txhweetenu. Nawa jwee jxthaakwe juuna' yũumée miipe'j, sa' txajx namikuwe'sx yakh puutx we'wewa'ja'swa mes jĩk.
24 Maz en skhẽwniite', Fxelixa' txajx nyu Drusila yakh ki' kũhçxa Pablo's pa'ya kaakh, Jesukristo pta'sxni's we'wekahn. Txãa u'ya' judiuk yu'. 25 Aça' Pablo' pta'sxku Dxus dxi'pte ew fxi'zewa'ja', sa' peekx kakwe's ewmée yũuna jxpe'jwa'jme'. Txã'wẽme', nmeh ente' Dxusa' kastigaĩna jĩna we'wete', Fxelixa' wala ũukhçxa na'wẽk we'we:
—Ãçxhíi yu' txã'wẽçxáa mpeeta'sx. Vxite ente adx en ji'phuçxa ki' pa'ya'nja jĩk.
26 Pablo' vxyu dukhe'nja txhwetekahn jĩwáana sũhçxak txã'wẽ maz uswa pa'ya Fxelixa' txãa yakh puutx we'weya'. 27 Txã'wẽ e'z anxupkaçx Pablo's karcelte jxpe'jna ũsçxaçxáa Fxelixa' ne'jwe'sx kasejk, aça' vxite ne'jwe'sx Porcio Fxestok u'ka. Nawa Fxelixa' judiuwe'sx yaaçeni fxi'ze wẽemeeçxa, Pablo's karcelte nvxihtku.
