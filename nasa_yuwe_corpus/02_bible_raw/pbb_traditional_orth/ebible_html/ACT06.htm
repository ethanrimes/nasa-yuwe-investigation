6
Dxuus yatte thegwa'jsa siete nasa txhitxhni
1 Txãa ensu' Kristo's jxpa'gasawe'sx wala pehnate', kxtee griego yuweju we'wesawe'sxa' judiu yuweju we'wesawe'sxtxi ũusçxa we'wewa'j uytxi kwe'sx ji'j vxiuda yuutxi' ew sã'jĩmée ũsta' een isa peesni ũ'a's jĩte', 2 Jesus jxkaahnisawe'sx docesa jiyuçxa', Kristo's jxpa'gasawe'sxtxi jxuka pkhakhçxa na'jĩtx:
—Dxus yuwe's pta'sxmée, sã'jĩwa'jçxá's jxthaakwe pa'pçxuya' yu' ãjãme'nja'w kwe'sxa'. 3 Txã'wẽ yũuya'mée i'kwe'sx yakh ũssa yakhthẽ'jwe'sx siete piçthẽ'j mtxhiitxhwe, Dxus dxi'pte ew fxi'zesa, Dxus Espiritu's ũuste jxpa'gasa, ma'wẽ yũuwa'ja'swa ew jiisatx. Txã'wẽ yuuçxa txãawe'sxtxi dukhe'nja'w sã'jĩna ũswa'j mjĩia's. 4 Aça' kwe'sxa' Dxus yuwe's kaapiya'jwa'jçxá's thegu'nja'w, Dxus yakh pu'vxitumée puutx we'wena jĩte', jxukaysa txã'wẽ yũuwa'ja's yajkxtxi. 5 Sa' Dxus yakh jxpubamée fxi'zesa Estebana'stxi txhitxh Dxus Espiritu's ũuste ji'phsa. Vxite' Fxelipe, Prókoro, Nikanor, Timón, Parmenas, Nikolas Antiokía çxhabuwe'sx txãawe'sxtxi txhitxhnisa neeyũu. Txãa Nikolasa' judiumeeçxawa judiuwe'sx na'wẽy yuuna fxi'zeya' utxasaak yu'. 6 Txãa piçthẽ'jtx txhitxhçxa', pe'jna u'jtx Jesus jxkaahnisawe'sx tasxte, aça' txãawe'sxa' dxikthesu peekizeçxa Dxus yakh puutx we'wetx txãawe'sx pa'gate.
7 Txajũ' Dxus yuwe' walaana u'kh, aça' Kristo's jxpa'gasawa Jerusalente seena' wala pehnak, txãa pa'ga kxtee judiuwe'sx sacerdote yuhwa Jesus kaapiya'jni's jxpa'gatx wala kuhsa.
Estebana's preesu'jni
8 Aça' Estebana' Dxus pu'çxte', kim yuhwa ãjanimeesa'swa kaavxya'ja'jnak fxi'ze' txãa çxhabte. 9 Txã'wẽ yũute', judiu vxite kiwejuwe'sx Cirene, Alejadría, Cilicia, Asia txãa kiwesuwe'sxa' Esteban yakh ũusçxa we'wena kasehtx. Txãawe'sxa' karceluh txhweetenisa txãawe'sx pwe'sxçxáa fxiitx pkhaakheni yat ji'phu'. 10 Nawa txãawe'sxa' pasya' ãja'çmeeta' Estebana' Dxus Espiritu kaaũusuthe'jni's we'wete'. 11 Txajũ' vxitetx vxyu dukhtxi txã'wẽ Moisesa's açewe'wena Dxusnawa açe'k jĩna ĩsxiina pãatxhĩ'khkahn. 12 Txã'wẽ yũuçxatx kaaũusçxa'j nasa ekajuwe'sx vxite' judiu nthẽ'jsawe'sx vxite' Dxus leya's kaapiya'jsawe'sxtxi. Sa' Estebana's tudçxa jxũna u'jtx judiu nthẽ'jsawe'sx tasxte. 13 Sa' pakwetx na'wẽ ĩsxiina pta'sxwa'jsa: Naa piçthẽ'ja' een isa dxuus yata's açewe'wena txã'wẽy Dxus leya'swa açewe'wena nesku yu'. 14 Jesus Nasarethũwe'sxa' naa dxuus yata's ivxiituçxa, Moises kaapiya'jni'swa ivxiituwa'jsa' jĩna kaapiya'jni's kwe'sxa' wẽse'tha'w.
15 Txã'jĩna ĩsxiite', judiu nthẽ'jsawe'sx vxite' kxtee pkhaakheçxa ũssa' Esteban dxi'pte thegçxa' Dxus angel na'wẽtetx uy.
