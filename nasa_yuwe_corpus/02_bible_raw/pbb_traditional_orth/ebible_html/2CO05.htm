5
1 Txajíiçxáa kwe'sxa' ew jiitha'w naa kiwete ĩtxĩ fxi'zenisa kakwe pçuutewa, vxite kakwe bagaçxte yuhwa pçuwa'jsamée thaakwe ewsa kwe'sx jĩi pheu'jni ji'pha' Dxusa' cielute. Txã' pçuwa'jsa naa kiwetewe'sx na'wẽsame'. 2 Txahtha'w naa kakwete nxusna nee ĩtxĩ fxi'zeççxa', dud cielute ũswa'j kakwe's jxpa'gawaça' sũhna yaaki'. 3 Txãjũ yuuçxa', kakwe ji'phmée yu' ũsmée, kakwe ji'ph neeyũ'nja'w. 4 Naa kakwete nee ĩtxĩ fxi'zeççxa', nxus yajkxnatha'w fxi'ze'. Nawa naa kakwe ĩisxsa's ahçeçxa dud uuwaça' sũhçxa txã'wẽ nxus yaaki'çmeetha'w, naasáa naa kakwe's dud phu'se'jni yuute', kwe'sx kakwe pçuwa'jsaçxawa kxtee u'se yuuna pa'jana sũhçxatha'w txã'wẽ yũuwa'ja's dud jxthãasu'. 5 Txã'wẽ yũuwa'ja's Dxusku pheu'j, txãasak txã'wẽ yũuwa'ja's jxpa'gawa'jsa yuji'kwe jĩçxa nyafxte txajx Espiritu's khuẽ'y ũs.
6 Pejka txã'wẽ yũuwa'jsa yuhtha'w, nawa naa kakwe yakh nee ĩtxĩ ũsíi yu', cielute kwe'sxtxi jxpe'jsa Jesukristo ũsna kxtee ãçxhíi yu' ũsya' ãjã'çmeetha'w. 7 Nawa pejka kxtee pa'jwa'jsa yuhtha'w sũhnaçxáatha'w fxi'ze'. 8 Txã'wẽ kwe'sxtxi jxpe'jsa tasxte pa'jwa'ja's jiitha'w, txãasatha'w naa kakwete ĩtxĩ fxi'zeni's nvxihtçxa txãa yakh ũsya' u'j wẽje'. 9 Txãasa naa kakwe yakh nee ũsíi mtha'w yuu, khĩmeeçxa' naa kakwe's ji'phmée yã'jmtha'w yuu, nawa Kristo dxi'pte ew fxi'zewa'ja's yuuçxáatha'w pa'pçxu'. 10 Txã'wẽ jxpa'yakxmeete', Kristo tasxte jxukaysa kuutxa'jnisa yu'nja'w. Naa kiwete ew mtha'w selpiisáa yuu, meeçxa' ewmée mtha'w selpiisáa yuu nawa teeçxsa na'wẽçxah peekx selpiini's jxpa'gawa'jsatha'w.
Dxus yakh yuuwemée neeyũuni's pta'sxni
11 Txã'wẽ Kristo tasxte utxaaçxa ma'wẽ selpiisaçxawa txã'sa' paswa'j ji'ptha'w, txãasath Dxusna yuuçxáa selpi' i'kwe'sx dxi'pte. Adx ma'wẽ selpini'swa Dxusa' jiiyã'ja', aça' i'kwe'sxwa adx kĩh yũusatewa jiyuwaça' ewna sũju'th. 12 Adxa' thaakwe ew selpisaath jĩna iiwejçx we'wena ũsmeeth, naasáa adx ew selpini's i'kwe'sx yaakxkahnçxáath txã'jĩ'. Txã'wẽ adx ew selpini's i'kwe'sxa' jii ũsçxa íiçxáa, adxna ewmée nuywe'wesawe'sxtxi' pasya' ãjãne'kwe. 13 Adxa' luuku na'wẽ wá'ntka we'we', nawa txã' Dxus jĩ'. Aça' luuku na'wẽmée we'weçxa yu', i'kwe'sxtxi ne'tka we'we' jiyukahn. 14 Kristo' jxukaysa pa'gate peeygãhçxa uuk, aça' txãa peeygãani's yajkxte', txã'sna selpiwa'j yuuçxáak kaseje', na'wẽ yajkxna: 15 Kristo' jxukaysa pa'gate uupa'ga, maawa txãa yakh ũssa' jxukaysa uusáa na'wẽsatha'w yuu sũhna. Aça' txãa yakh fxi'zesawe'sxa' ma'wẽwa fxi'zemée, wejxwa na'wẽ txãawe'sx pa'gate uuçxa ki' ĩtxĩ yuuk txã'sçxáana selpiina fxi'zewa'jsata'.
16 Txãa pa'ga ãçxhũyã'ja' vxite ma'wẽsatewa txãatx thegna ũusteçxáa yuhwa yaakxwa'j ji'phmeetha'w. Adxa' nyafxíi yu' wala ne'tka jxpuba, txãasaíith Kristo's naa kiwete kwe'sx na'wẽysaíiçxá' sũhna yaakxni ji'phu'. Nawa ãçxha' txã'wẽ yaakxni's yu'pthehwa'ja', 17 txãa pa'gatey maawa Kristo tasxte utxasá' jxuka yu'pthehnik yu'. Nyafxíi yajkxna fxi'zeni's nvxihtçxa, u'sesaçxaçxáak ya'jypa'ga'.
18 Txã'wẽ u'se yuuwa'ja's Dxusçxaçxáak vxitu', txãasak txãa yakh ew fxi'zewa'ja'swa vxitu', kwe'sx pa'gate Kristo's uuwa'j vxitçxa. Dxusa' txã'wẽ yũuçxak adxa's neewe'weçxa nvxiht, txãa yakh ew fxi'zewa'ja's jxukaysatx pta'sxkahn. 19 Ew jiyute yu', Dxusa' naa kiwete nasaa pkaltxi jxuka peltunãyçxa txãa yakh ew fxi'zewa'ja's pheu'jya'k txã'wẽ Kristo's uuwa'j vxitna ũsu'. Dxus txã'wẽ yũuni's pta'sxkahnku adxa's neewe'weçxa nvxiht. 20 Txãa pa'ga adxa' Kristo nwe'weni's pta'sxya' Dxus kaahni yuhth. Txãasath Dxus yuu we'we'ç na'wẽ adxa' i'kwe'sxtxi jxkaaja' na'jĩna: Dxus tasxte mutxaawe, txãa yakh fxi'zeya'. 21 Kristo' kĩh pkal yuhwa ji'phu'çme', nawa kwe'sx ewmeesapa'gaçxáa txãak Dxus kastigo's jxpa'ga. Aça' kwe'sxa' Kristo's jxpa'gaçxa', ewmeesaçxawa Dxus dxi'pte yuuwemeesa yuukahnku txã'wẽ yũu.
