12
Fxariseowe'sx yakh jxpa'yakx fxi'ze kaahni
1 Txajũ' nasa iiméh seena' wala pkhaakheçxa jxuka utate', Jesusa' txãa yakh u'jusawe'sx khuẽ'ytxi na'wẽk we'we:
—Fxariseowe'sx ewmée yũuna fxi'zeni's mpa'yajkxwe. Txã'wẽme' txãawe'sxa' ãajuçxáa ew we'weçxa', ũus dxiite yu', ewmeeçxáatx yaaki'. 2 Nawa makwe paaçte sũthnisaçxawa kĩh yuhwa ãate vxyaamée yu' neeyũumeena, sa' makwe ũusteçxáa yaakxnisawa kĩh yuhwa jiyunimée yu' neeyũumeena. 3 Txãa pa'ga i'kwe'sxa' kim yuhwa wẽsẽ'jnimeete wá'kwe we'we' nawa jxuka pthũusena. Meeçxa' i'kwe'sx ũusteçxáa wá'kwe ewmée yajkx, nawa maazkuẽe yat-suwa jxuka kaajiyu'jni yuuna.
Kimna wejx ũukhwa'jkx
(Mt 10.28-31)
4 Ãçxha', adx namiku i'kwe'sxtxi na'jĩ'th: Kakweçxá's ikhsatxi' ũukhnuwe. Txãawe'sxa' kakweçxá's ikhçxa', jwee yu' kĩhwa yũuya' ãjãmeetxna. 5 Nawa pta'sxiçthu kimna ũukhwa'thewa: Naa kiwete ĩtxĩ u'juni's kusa'jçxa', ũusa's infxiernote kaahwa'jsaçxá's ũukhna mfxi'zewe. Txã'sçxáa ũukhwa'ja'.
6 Tahç vxiçxakuẽtx e'z centawoteçxáak ya'txweyú'. Nawa makwe pa'gameesatewa Dxusa' teeçxçxáa yuhnawa peçxkanaméek thegna fxi'ze'. 7 I'kwe'sx jxkwette dxkhas yuhwa jxuka isani ũsta'. Aça' i'kwe'sx yu' maazkuẽe vxiçxakuẽtewa jxthaakwe pa'gasai'kwe. Txãasa ũukhmée mfxi'zewe.
Jesus kimtewa thaamemée pta'sxni
(Mt 10.32-33; 12.32; 10.19-20)
8 I'kwe'sxtxi na'jĩçthu: maawa naa kiwete nasaa dxi'pte thaamemée Kristo jĩith jĩna ãate pta'sxsatxi', adxwa Dxus Nçxi'k Nasa Yuusa' txã'wẽy Dxus angeleswe'sx dxi'pte thaamemée: Na' adx nasata' jĩna pta'sxi'nja. 9 Nawa maawa naa kiwete nasaa dxi'pte thaameçxa Kristo's jiimeeth jĩna ĩsxĩijana aça' adxwa Dxus angeleswe'sx dxi'pte txã'sa' jiimeeth jĩ'nja.
10 Maawa Dxus Nçxi'k Nasa Yuusa's makwe açewe'wetewa, ya'peltunaĩna. Nawa Dxus Espiritu's açewe'wete', bagaçxte yuhwa peltunãynimée yuuna.
11 Na'jĩ'th na': i'kwe'sxtxi judiuwe'sx pkhaakhenisu meeçxa' khuẽswe'sx tasxte kuutxa'thewa, ũukhnuwe kuh yajkxna kĩh we'weki'th sũhna. 12 Txãa pa'gatey Dxus Espiritu' ma'wẽ we'wewa'ja'swa kaaũusuthe'jena txãa dudte jĩk.
Teeçx ji'phsa ũ'a's jxaawya' yaakxni
13 Txajũ' teeçx piçthẽ'j nasaa ksxavxyte ũssa' Jesusa's na'jĩk:
—Adx yakhthẽ'ja's meen we'we, tatawe'sx ji'phuni's pxãhn adx jĩi txudeekahn jĩte', 14 Jesus pasçxa':
—¿Kimga adxa'sa' txudeedewa'jsa meeçxawa khuẽs na'wẽsa ki'p? jĩçxa', 15 na'wẽk we'we:
—Mpa'yajkxwe, naa kiwete ji'phuniçxá's yaakxnisa's. Txã'wẽme' naa kiwete kĩjkĩhwa ji'phuçxaçxáa txã'wẽ ĩtxĩ ya'fxi'ze'çme'.
16 Txajũ' Jesusa' na'wẽk ejemplo pta'sx:
—Teeçx piçthẽ'j wala ji'phsa u'pu'k. Txajx kiwete uuthasx wala yũnku. 17 Aça' txãa ji'phsa' yajkxya' kaçxku: ¿Ma'wẽ yũuki'th? Mtee yuhwa adx ũ'a's jxuka jxaawya' ãjãnimeeth sũjũ'k. 18 Sa' yajkxku: Jiiyã'th ma'wẽ yũuwa'ja'swa. Ũ' jxaaw yattxi pẽtxya'wath, vxite wejx walasa txajxa'. Sa' kĩjkĩh ũ'wa thẽ'the' vxite' kĩh ji'phunitxwa kxtee jxaawu'nja. 19 Txajũ' adx ũuste na'jĩ'nja: Wala kĩhwa jxaawni ji'phgu, wala kuh anxu selpiwa'j. Ãçxha' mjĩimée kaasena kĩhwa ũ'na weçxweçxa miyajkx sũjũ'k. 20 Nawa Dxusa' na'jĩk: Ãhmeeg yaaki'. Naa kuste uuya'wag. Aça' iidx ji'phuni jxaawni' ¿kiim jĩi yuuwa'jtxna? jĩk. 21 Jesus we'weçxa': Txã'wẽk ya'yu' naa kiwete ji'phuniçxá's nuywalaana ũsçxa', Dxus dxi'pte yu' puuplek neeyũ' jĩk.
Kwe'sx peejini's Dxusa' jxuka pu'çxhina
(Mt 6.25-34)
22 Jesusa' txãa yakh u'jusawe'sxtxi' na'wẽk yu'kxpeh:
—I'kwe'sxa' ¿kĩhte ũ'wa'j uyuukxtha'w? Sa' ropawa ¿kĩhte uyuukxtha'w? sũhna pa'pçxunuwe. 23 Txã'wẽ yuukahmeníi ĩtxĩ fxi'zenisa' ũ'nite jxthaakwe ewsa', aça' txã'wẽy kakwewa ropate jxthaakwe ewsa'. 24 Vxiçxakuẽtx mthegwe wakx. Txãawe'sxa' uja'çwameeta', sa' ũ' jxaaw yatwa txahçxa ũ' pkhakhe'çwameeta'. Nawa Dxusa' txãawe'sxtxi' sã'jĩ'k na' i'kwe'sx yuutxi's wejx txã'wẽ sã'jĩna vxiçxakuẽte jxthaakwesai'kwe naa pa'ga. 25 I'kwe'sxa' ¿maaçxá' iiméh wala pa'pçxuçxa peena medio metro thaakwe walaaya' ãjama'kwe? 26 Txã'wẽ ãjameeçxa', ¿kĩjxa'çxáakwe naa kiwetewe'sxçxáatx txakwe wala pa'pçxu'?
27 Kitetx mthegwe, ropa uyya' mjĩimeeçxawa ma'wẽta zhiçxkwe walá'. Isath we'we': Jxkaahsa Salomon txakwe ji'phsa yuhçxawa txãa kite na'wẽ yu' zhiçxkwesa ropa athme'. 28 Kite' teeçx en fxi'zena', kuskuuskhẽ' ipxte kabwa'jçxáa yã'thewa txã'wẽk zhiçxkwe pheu'j. Dxusa' txã'wẽy i'kwe'sx yuutxi's wejx ropa ewsa ũsna nes yuuna na's i'kwe Dxusa' txã'wẽ yuuna sũjũ'çmée. 29 Txã'wẽ yã'the', ũ'wa'j ¿kĩhte uyuukxtha'w? sũhna pa'pçxuna fxi'zenuwe. 30 Naa kiwete nasaçxáatx kakwete peejiniçxáana yajkxna fxi'ze'. Nawa i'kwe'sxa' cielute Tata ji'phi'kwe txã' jxuka jiiyã'ja' i'kwe'sx kĩh peejini'swa. 31 Txãa pa'ga wejxwa Dxus jxkaahnitewe'sxa's khuẽ'yna wejx thaakwe mpa'pçxuwe. Naa kiwetewe'sxtxi' wejx pa'pçxumeetewa, pejka yuh ũsni yuuna.
Cielute ji'phuwa'jçxá's mpa'pçxuwe
(Mt 6.19-21)
32 I'kwe'sxa' kuhmeekwe i'kwe nawa ũukhnuwe, txãa pa'gatey i'kwe'sx Tata cielutewe'sxa' i'kwe'sxtxi peeygãhçxa txajx jxkaahna ũsni's i'kwe'sxtxi ũsya'wa'. 33 Ji'phunisa's txweyçxa jxpeejxsatx mpeeswe. Vxyu jxaawni suwewa'jmeesa mvxitwe. I'kwe'sx ji'phuni cielutewe'sxa' pçumeena, kxteea' kĩh peswée yuhwa u'kameena, sa' çxikaçxáa yuhwa suwmeena. 34 Mtee jxaawni ji'phçxa', kxte yuuçxáak wejx jxthaakwe yajkxna ya'fxi'ze'.
Jxpa'yakx ũythasna ũsni
35 Jxuka pheu'jçxa lamparatx pki'taçxa ũythasna meswe. 36 Patrona' boda yatu sxawedna pa'jçxa', vxitxte tu'kakaçpkaçxha', selpisaawe'sxa' dudtey vxitxa's phaadeya' jxpa'yakx ũythasna ũssa na'wẽ myuuwe. 37 Jxpa'yakx ũste patron pa'the', selpisaawe'sx yakha' wala yuh weçxa yuhna. Isath we'we': Txã'wẽ jxpa'yakx ũssatxi' patrona' pheu'jçxa mesate kaaçxi'jina puuçya'. 38 Kuspxãh ma'k yuu meeçxa' khikhiiçte ma'k yuu nawa jxpa'yakx ũste pa'the', selpisaawe'sx yakha' wala yuh weçxa yuhna. 39 Ũus miyajkxwe wakx: Maa yat namuwa peswée kĩh orate txajx yatte pa'jwa'ja's jiyuwaça', dehmée jxpa'yakx ũsçxa' ka'ga'jmeena txajx yatte pesweewe'sxte. 40 I'kwe'sxwa txã'wẽy pheu'jçxa jxpa'yakx meswe. Txã'wẽme', yajkxmée ũsiyna, Dxus Nçxi'k Nasa Yuusá' kĩhçxáa yuuna jĩk.
Selpisáa ewsa vxite' ewmeesa
(Mt 24.45-51)
41 Txajũ' Pedro' na'wẽk pẽjx:
—Naa ejemplo' ¿kwe'sx jĩiçxáa wáana meeçxa' jxukaysatx wá'ga pta'sxi'? jĩte', 42 Jesusa' na'wẽk pta'sx:
—Maa patronwa yultumu nwẽesẽ'jsa ewsa's neewe'weçxa nvxiituna, txajx yatte mjĩisatx sã'jĩna nes yuukahn. 43 Aça' patron jxkaahni's kxtey yuuna ũste patron pa'the', txãa selpisáa yakha' wala yuh weçxa yuhna. 44 Isath we'we': Txã'wẽ yũusa's patrona' txajx ji'phuni's kĩjkĩhnawa jxuka txãa yultumuçxaçxá's neewe'weçxa thegkahn nvxiituna. 45 Nawa txãa yultumu': Adx patron dud pa'jmeena sũhçxa kxtee mjĩisatx pkxuuya' kiiteçxa, txãa yu' tudxna tũuya' kiitena. 46 Naapkaçxha' txajx patrona' sxaweduuna txã'wẽ yultumu' patron pa'jwa'ja's jiimeeçxa' ũythasmée ũsiyna. Sa' wala seena' kastigaĩna, nwẽesẽ'jsameewe'sxtxi ma'wẽ kastigaĩkx txã'wẽy.
47 Maa selpisaawa txajx patron jxthãasni's jiiçxawa kxtey nwẽesẽ'jsame', wala kujus kastigãyni yuuna. 48 Nawa maa selpisaawa patron jxkaahni's jiimeeçxa' ãhmée yũuna txã'sa' kujusmée ya'kastigaĩna. Maanawa wala ũsçxa', wala ya'pẽyina. Aça' maanawa jxthaakwe kĩhnawa neewe'weçxa', jxthaakwe ew jxpe'khaníi yuuna.
Jesusa' nasatx kaapui'jsa na'wẽ yuuni
(Mt 10.34-36)
49 Naa kiwete ipx pki'taya' kĩisa na'wẽth kĩh, behnayã'j ũswaç ma'wẽ wala weçxaaki'th. 50 Adxa' uuwa'jsath txãasa wala nxusna u'juth uuwa'j en ãhpkaçx. 51 Naa kiwete puiimée wẽt kaafxi'ze'jya'ne' kĩh ¿sũjũ'wá'kwe adxa'sa'? Nawa txã'wẽ yũuya' kĩhmeeth. Adx pa'gateçxáa maa nasaíi yu' puutx yahçena puiina fxi'zetxna. 52 Ãçxhũyã'ja' teeçx yattewe'sx tahç nasaçxawa tekhsa' e'zsatx ahçena puutx txã'wẽçxaçxáa puiina fxi'zetxna. 53 Neya' nçxi'ka's açeyajkxna fxi'zena. Aça' nçxi'ka' neya's txã'wẽy yũuna fxi'zena. Njĩ'ja' niisa's ahçena fxi'zena, aça' niisa' njĩ'ja's txã'wẽy yũuna fxi'zena. Swegra' nkuẽmyu's açena, naapkaçxha' nkuẽmyu', swegra's açena.
Naa ensu kĩh yũuya'watewa txãa yu'sa' jiimée fxi'zeni
(Mt 16.1-14; Mr 8.11-13)
54 Txajũ' Jesusa' na'wẽk we'we:
—I'kwe'sxa' sek khẽenijũ tãaph kiitena yuhte', nus pa'jya'wa' jĩ'i'kwe, aça' kxtey yu'k. 55 Sa' wejxaaçte', sek ya'wa' jĩ'i'kwe, aça' kxtey yu'k. 56 ¿Ma'wẽ yuuçxa nus pa'jna ki' sek ya'watewa jiisatha'w jĩna ũuste yu' iiwejçx i'kwe yajkxna fxi'ze'? Sa' jiisaçxa' ¿ma'wẽtekwe naa ensu' kĩh yũuçtewa txã's yu' jiyu'çmée?
Açesa yakh yuwe's puutx we'weni
(Mt 5.25-26)
57 I'kwe'sxa' ¿mjĩitekwe ewteçxah puutx we'wena yuwe's pheu'ju'çmée? 58 Maawa idxa's pãatxhĩ'khte', txãa yakh khuẽs tasxte utxaaya' u'jweççxa', dxi'the ewteçxah puutx we'wena yuwe's mpheu'jwe, kxah khuẽs tasxte kuutxa'jmeena. Txã'wẽme', khuẽsa' idxa's policia's dukhena, aça' policia yu', karcelte kxuuphana. 59 Na'jĩ'th na': Txajũyã'ja' vxyu nmeh ji'phuni's dukhmeeçxa yu', karceluh kasehmeene'ga jĩk.
