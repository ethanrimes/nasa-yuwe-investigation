5
Yuuwemeesa yuupa'ga Dxus yakh fxi'zewa'ja's uyni
1 Txã'wẽ Dxusa's ũusuh yuuçxáa jxuka yaakxpa'gaçxáa Dxusa' kwe'sxtxi's yuuwemeesak vxit aça' ãçxha' Dxus yakh wẽt fxi'zewa'ja's ji'ptha'w, kwe'sxtxi jxpe'jsa Jesukristo nwe'wepa'ga. 2 Txã'wẽ Kristo kwe'sxtxi nwe'wepa'ga yuuçxáatha'w Dxus yakh wẽt fxi'zewa'the pa'j, Jesukristo's ũusuh jxuka yaakxpa'ga. Txãasa ãçxha' Dxus tasxte pa'jwa'ja's ji'ptha'w, txãasatha'w wala weçxá'. 3 Sa' Dxus tasxte pa'jwa'ja'sçxáa yajkxçxa weçxamée, naa kiwete nxuswa'ja'swa weçxa'tha'w, txãa pa'gatey nxuswa'j en pa'the', jweeíiçxáak Dxusa's çxhãçxha yaakxwa'j kaseje'. 4 Aça' Dxusa's çxhãçxha yaakxwa'j kasehte, Dxus yakh txuteméek ya'fxi'ze'. Aça' Dxus yakh txutemée fxi'zeçxa', Dxus luuçxtha'w sũhwa'ja's ya'ji'phu'k. 5 Aça' Dxus luuçxyã'ja', weh yuhwa ya'thame'çme', txãa pa'gatey kwe'sx ũuste Dxus Espirituçxáa jxuka khẽeçxa ũsna Dxus peeygãaniçxá's kaajiyu'jna.
6 Kwe'sxçxáa yu' ya'nwe'wewa'ja's ãjameeyã'the', Kristo naa kiwete kĩiwa'j en ãhte' pa'jçxa kwe'sx ewmeesa pa'gate uuk nwe'weya'. 7 Kim yuhwa nasa ewmeesatx peeygãhçxa nwe'weya' uumeena. Meeçxa' maaíi yu' txã'wẽ yuuwáatxna, nawa nasa ewsateçxáa txã'wẽ yuutxna. 8 Kwe'sx yakh yu' txã'wẽ ewmeesa pa'gate uuwa'ja' wala thẽya', nawa Dxusa' kwe'sx ewmeesatewa peeygãhçxa txajx Nçxi'ka's naa kiwete kaakh, ewmeesa kwe'sx pa'gate uukahn. 9 Txã'wẽ Kristo kwe'sx pa'gate uupa'ga nwe'wenisatha'w yuu, sa' nmeh ente kastigo iiméh seena'sate yu' wejx nwe'wenisa yu'nja'w. 10 Txãa pa'gatey kwe'sxa' Dxusa's açesateyuhwa, txãa yakh fxi'zewa'ja's vxitku Kristo's uuwa'j vxitçxa, na' ãçxh yu' Kristo uupa'ga yuuwemeesatha'w na' wejx nwe'wenisa yu'nja'w. 11 Txã'wẽ yũupa'gatha'w Dxusa's wala weçxá', kwe'sxtxi nwe'wesa Jesukristo' Dxus yakh fxi'zewa'ja's vxitku naa pa'ga.
Adan yakh Kristo yakha' ja'dameeta'
12 Pkala' naa kiwete teeçx nasate yuuçxáa u'kaçxak uuwa'ja's vxit, txãa pa'gatx jxukaysa naa kakwe uuwa'ja's ji'phu, pkalte wetepa'ga. 13 Ley kasehmey, pkala' naa kiwete ũsyã'kh yu'. Nawa ley pta'sxçxa: Naa ewmeesa' jĩna pta'sx ja'damée yu', ewmeesana sũhwa'ja's ya'ji'phu'çmeene'. 14 Ley yu' meetewa, pkal ũsyã'the íitx Moises meetey Adanwe'sx jxukaysa peekx kakwe uuwa'j ena's ji'phu, Adan na'wẽy yu' mée yũuçxa pkalte wetetewa, uu ena' ja'daçxaçxáak pa'j. Ew yajkxte yu', Adana' naa kiwete kĩiwa'jsa ũsu'k txãa na'wẽsak yu'.
15 Nawa Adan pkal yakh vxite' kwe'sx ewte neeyũunisa yakha' weh yuhwa ja'dameeta'. Teeçx piçthẽ'j pkalte wetepa'ga yuuçxáa jxukaysatx uuwa'ja's ji'phu, naapkaçxha' teeçx piçthẽ'j Jesukristo pa'ga yuuçxáa jxukaysatx txãa pkaluh ewte neeyũuwa'ja's ji'phu. Txã' jxukaysatxku nwe'we naa pa'ga jxthaakwe wala ewsak yu'. 16 Teeçx piçthẽ'jsa pkal yuuk txãa pkal yakh Dxus ũsku ewte neeyũuwa'ja's txãa yakha' weh yuhwa ja'dameeta'. Txãa pa'gatey teeçxsa teedxi'j pkal yũunisaçxáa maazkuẽe us pkalsatewa ãhsak yu', naapkaçxha' Dxus ũsku ewte neeyũuwa'ja's txã' maazkuẽe pkal ji'phsatewa txãaju yuuwemeesak vxitna pa'j. 17 Teeçxsa yuuwe yuupa'gaçxáa, uuwa'ja' nesku yuuna pa'j. Aça' bagaçxte yuhwa pçumée ĩtxi fxi'zewa'ja' wejx txã'wẽ bagaçxtewa nes yuuna Jesukristopa'ga.
18 Adan ewmée yũunisa' jxukaysatxku ewmeekhẽ vxitna pa'j, naapkaçxha' Jesukristo ew yũunisa' nasa jxukaysatxku yuuwemée ewsa vxit. 19 Txã'wẽ teeçx piçthẽ'j Dxusa's nwẽese'pha'gameeçxáa jxukaysatx pkalsa yuu, naapkaçxha' teeçx piçthẽ'j Dxusa's nwẽese'pha'gaçxáa jxukaysa yuuwemeesatx yuuna pa'j.
20 Kĩhçxah yũunisa txã'wẽ pkaltewa jxuka ãate jiyukahn íiçxáak leya' kaseh. Aça' pkala's txã'wẽ ew jiyuçpkaçxha', Dxus peeygãanisa yu' jwee íiçxáak thaakwe wala vxyaa. 21 Pkala' uuwa'ja'sku vxit, naapkaçxha' Dxus peeygãani' pçumée ĩtxi fxi'zewa'ja'sku vxit Jesukristo nwe'wepa'ga.
