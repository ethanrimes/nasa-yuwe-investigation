24
Dxuus yat pçuwa'ja's Jesus pta'sxni
(Mr 13.1-2; Lk 21.5-6)
1 Dxuus yatu kasehçxa Jesusa' u'jweçpkaçxha', txãa yakh u'jusawe'sxa' utxaaçxa we'wetx dxuus yata's pta'sxna. 2 Aça' Jesusa' na'wẽk pas:
—Txã'wẽ yuh seena' zhiçxkwe wala'. Nawa na'jĩ'th na': jxuka pçuuna, aça' txãa yattewe'sx teeçx kwetçxáa yuhwa kẽese'jnimeesa yu' neeyũumeena jĩk.
Naa kiwe pçuwa'j en ãjaçte seena'sa vxyaawa'j
(Mr 13.3-23; Lk 21.7-24; 17.22-24)
3 Txajũ' Oliwos vxiçnatx u'j. Kxtee pa'jçxa Jesus kaçxçxa u'pte', txãa yakh u'jusawe'sx maaíi yu' na'jĩnatx we'we paaçte:
—Meen peeta'sx, ¿bagaçx txã'wẽ jxuka pçuukx? Sa' iidx kĩiwa'j en vxite' naa kiwe pçuwa'j en ãjaçte', ¿kĩh seena'sa vxyaakx? jĩte', 4 Jesusa' pta'sxku:
—Mpa'yajkxwe, i'kwe'sxtxi kaypuba'jwe'sxte. 5 Txã'wẽme', wala pa'jatxna adx yase's kxsusna na'jĩna: Dxus kaahnisa Kristoth jĩtxna, sa' txã'wẽ yũuçxa wala kaypuba'jatxna ĩsxiina. 6 I'kwe'sxa' wẽse'jene'kwe gweçxa msuu msuuwa u'pa' jĩni. Txã'wẽ yũutewa i'kwe'sxa' ũukhnuwe, txã' nmeh en yu' me'. 7 Gobierno' vxite kiwete gobierno yakh puiitxna, naapkaçxha' puutx jxu'j kiwejũ pwe'sxwa puiitxna. Txã'wẽ mtee mteewa jxuka gweçxa kiiteena. Sa' wẽje enwa pa'jana, wee seena'sawa pa'jana, ehdwa seena'sa u'jwetxna mtee mteewa. 8 Txã'wẽ seena'sa pa'jçxawa txãa yu' nyafxte nxusya' takhnisaçxáa yuuna.
9 Txajũ' i'kwe'sxtxi uweçxa dukhetxna pãpana ikhkahn, sa' naa kiwete mteewe'sxwa jxukaysa açetxna adx jxkaahni's nwẽese'pha'gaçxáa. 10 Txã'wẽ yũute', maazkuẽesawa Dxus tasxu txuteetxna, sa' puutx yahçena vxitesa uweçxa ikhwa'ja's vxitutxna. 11 Wala ĩsxiisa vxyaatxna, adxa' Dxus kaahni yuhth jĩna nasa maazkuẽesatxwa ĩsxiijatxna. 12 Sa' ewmée yũuna fxi'zewa'jçxáa txakwe wala pa'the', puutx ya'peeygãhna fxi'zewa'j yu'sa' ivxiitutxna maazkuẽesawa. 13 Nawa uu entepkaçx Dxus yakh txutemée fxi'zesa' ewte neeyũutxna. 14 Naapkaçxha' Dxus jxkaahwa'ja's pta'sxnisa' maa kiwetewa jxuka pta'sxni yuuna mteewe'sxwa jxuka jiyukahn. Txã'wẽ yũunijũ nmeh ensa pa'jana.
15 Na'wẽ yũuwa'ja's jxpa'yakx lẽeyçxa jii mfxi'zewe Dxus yuwe pta'sxsa Daniela' pta'sxna fxi'kh dxuus yatte kazx ewmée yũuna pa'jwa'ja's. Aça' txã'wẽ seena' kazxsa pa'jçxa dxuus yatte u'kaçte uyçxa', 16 Judea kiwesuwe'sxwa jxuka ũphna u'jwene'kwe vxiç walasu ũsya'. 17 Sa' ẽe yat-su ũssawa sa'jçxa kĩhçxáanawa kutxi'jwa'j en ji'phumeetxna. 18 Txã'wẽy mjĩi ehsu u'jusawa txajx atxha's nikxya' yatna u'jwa'j ençxáa yuhwa ji'phumeetxna. 19 Txãa ente' u'ywe'sx nasa ji'phsa meeçxawa luuçx ãpãkuẽ ji'phsawe'sxa' wala yuh pxthaa yuutxna. 20 I'kwe'sxa' Dxusa's mpẽjxwe txã'wẽ nus ente meeçxawa jxkaase ente ũphna u'jwa'j yuukahmén. 21 Txã'wẽ txãa ente' seena' yuh wala nxuswa'j yuuna. Naa kiwe's pheu'jiyna bagaçxte yuhwa txã'wẽsa yu' iiu'pume', sa' txã'wẽsa skhẽwniijũyã'ja' peena yu' u'pumeena txã'wẽysa yu'. 22 Dxusa' en txã'wẽ seena'sa's txaçxmée vxitmeete yu', kim yuhwa ĩtxi neeyũumeetxna. Nawa txajx nasa txhitxhnisatx peeygãhçxa, en txã'wẽ seena'sa's txaçxmeeçxáa vxituna.
23 Maawa i'kwe'sxtxi we'weçxa', mthegwe Kristo ayte ũsa' jĩtxna, meeçxawa kxtee ũsa' jĩtxna, nawa txã'jĩni's nwẽese'jnuwe. 24 Txãa pa'gatey wala ĩsxiisa pa'jatxna, adxa' Dxus yuwe pta'sxsath sa' Dxus kaahnisa Kristoth jĩtxna, txã'wẽ kim yuhwa ãjanimeesa's kaavxya'ja'jatxna kaypuba'j wẽeçxa. Txã'wẽ yũuçxa maaíitxi' kaypuba'jatxna Dxus txhitxhnisa yuhtxwa. 25 Txã'wẽ yũuwa'ja's pta'sxyã'th i'kwe'sxtxi dxiikhẽy. 26 Txãa pa'ga nwẽese'jmeene'kwe makwe txã'wẽ kxtee ũsa' nasa u'pnimeete jĩtewa meeçxa' ay dxiite ũsa' jĩtxna. 27 Txãa pa'gatey Dxus Nçxi'k Nasa Yuusáa kĩi ente', kpi'sxwa ma'wẽga sek kãanijũ kwehneçxa' sek khẽenijũwa vxya' txã'wẽyçxáa yuuna naa dudte jxukaysa dxi'pte. 28 Kakwe uuniisa mkaa ũstewa kxtee pkhaakhetxna mẽewẽjxa' ũ'ya'.
Dxus Nçxi'k Nasa Yuusáa seena' zhiçxkwe vxyaana
(Mr 13.24-37; Lk 21.25-33; 17.26-30, 34-36)
29 Txã'wẽ seena' nxuswa'j en skhẽuuçpkaçxha', seka' çxhi'dxina, aça' a'te' kweethmeena. Naapkaçxha' estrelxa ũsxitxna, ẽe walasu kĩh seena'sawa jxuka kẽese'jni yuutxna. 30 Txã'wẽ yũunijũ', Dxus Nçxi'k Nasa Yuusáa kĩiwa'ja's kaajiyu'jsa vxyaana cieluju, aça' naa kiwete nasa maazkuẽesawa ũ'netxna Dxus Nçxi'k Nasa Yuusáa txã'wẽ cieluju tãaph pxãhte seena' zhiçxkwe vxyaana yuhte uyçxa. 31 Sa' txajx angeleswe'sxtxi jxkaajana seena' sus tujkaçxa txajx txhitxhnisatxi' pkhakhkahn naa kiwetewe'sx mtee mteewe'sxwa jxukaysatx.
32 Naa ejemplo's peçxkananuwe: higwera fxtũu tasxa' eç ubuçxa ki' u'se yeeçuçte uyçxa', yũnya'watewa jiyu'i'kwe. 33 Txã'wẽy na'wẽ seena'sa vxyaaçte uyçxa', jii mfxi'zewe, pçuwa'j en utxaaçte txã'wẽ yuuna. 34 Isath we'we', naa ensu nasa' uumey txã'wẽ seena'satx uyuune'kwe. 35 Cielu naa kiwe jxuka skhẽuutxna, nawa adx we'weni yu' kxtey yuumey yu' skhẽwmeetxna.
36 Nawa kĩh ente kĩh orate txã'wẽ yũuwa'j yu'sa' kim yuhwa jiimeeta', cielute angeleswe'sx Dxus Nçxi'k txãawe'sx yuhwa, naasáa Dxus yuuçxáa jiia'.
37 Noe entewa ma'wẽga yuu txã'wẽyçxáa yuuna, Dxus Nçxi'k Nasa Yuusáa kĩi entewa. 38 Txãa ensu nasa' ĩkh utameyna yu' wala weçxana ũsu'tx ũ'na tudxna kabana Noe arkate u'kapkaçx. 39 Sa' Dxus kastigo's jiimée ũsíi, ĩkh utate jxuka pçuuçyã'jçxaçxáatx jiyu, txã'wẽyçxáa jiyumeetxna Dxus Nçxi'k Nasa Yuusáa kĩi entewa. 40 Txãa ente' e'z piçthẽ'j mjĩina ih ũsutxna, aça' teeçxsa's jxũna u'jni yuuna, nawa vxite's nvxiitni yuuna. 41 Txã'wẽy e'z u'y ih ũ'khna ũsutxna, aça' teeçxsa's jxũna u'jni yuuna, nawa vxite's nvxiitni yuuna.
42 I'kwe'sxa' jxpa'yakx meswe. Txã'wẽme', kĩh ente kĩh orate Dxus Nçxi'k kĩiwa'jsatewa jiimeei'kwe. 43 Nawa jxpa'yakx meswe. Yat namuwa ma'wẽ peswée kĩh orate yuhtewa jiyuçxa' dehmée jxpa'yakx ũythasna u'pukx yatte pesweekahmén, txã'wẽy myũuwe. 44 Txãa pa'ga i'kwe'sxwa jxuka pheu'jçxa meswe. Txã'wẽme', yajkxmée ũsiyna Dxus Nçxi'k Nasa Yuusa' kĩhçxáa yuuna.
Selpisáa ewsa vxite' ewmeesa
(Lk 12.41-48)
45 Selpisáa ¿maa ewki patron neewe'weçxa nvxihtte kxte yattewe'sxtxi kwẽeje'jmée sã'jĩwa'jsa? 46 Patron neewe'weçxa nvxiitni's jxuka kxtey yuuna ũste patrona' thegna pa'the', txãa selpisáa yakha' wala yuh weçxa yuhna. 47 Isath we'we': txã'wẽ yũusa's patrona' txajx ji'phuni's jxuka neewe'weçxa nvxiituna. 48 Nawa selpisáa ewmeesa' patron pa'jya' jxthẽeyuuna sũhçxa 49 kxte vxite selpisaatx pkxuuna tũusaawe'sx yakh tudxna ũ'na yũuna ũste', 50 yajkxmée ũsiyna patrona' kĩh ente kĩh oratewa pa'jçxáa yuuna. 51 Sa' seena' wala nxus nxus kastigãina txã'wẽ yuwejuçxáa ew we'weçxa' ũuste yu' ewmeeçxáa yaakxsawe'sxtxi na'wẽy. Txajũyã'ja' txãa selpisáa ewmeesa' seena' wala pebeena ki'thtxi ksuusu'jna.
