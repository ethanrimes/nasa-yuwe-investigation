3
Jesus, Nikodemo yakh puutx we'weni
1 Teeçx fxariseo Nikodemo yaasesa u'pu'k. Txã' judiu npiiçthẽ'jsatewe'sxku yu'. 2 Txãa piçthẽ'ja' Jesus tasxte kus pa'jçxa', na'jĩk: Jiitha'w idxa's Dxusne' txã'wẽ kaah, kwe'sxtxi kaapiya'khan. Dxus pu'çxmeewaça', kim yuhwa ãjawa'jsame's kaavxya'ja'jmeena, idx na'wẽg yu' txã's jĩte', 3 Jesusa' na'wẽk pas:
—Isath we'we', maawa ki' upxmeeçxa yu', Dxus jxkaahna ũsnite u'kaya' ãjameena.
4 Txã'jĩte', Nikodemo' na'wẽk pẽjx:
—Nasa ĩisxyã'jsa' ma'wẽ ki' upxya' ãjakx? Njĩ'j tutxhte ki' u'kaçxa upxya' ãjama'k? jĩte', 5 Jesusa' pta'sxku:
—Adxa' naa kakwe upxwa'ja's we'we'çmeeth. Maawa Dxus jxkaanite u'ka wẽeçxa', yu'te vxite' Dxus Espiritute upxwa'ja'sthu txã'jĩ'. 6 Nasaa kakwe' nasaa kakweteyçxáak upxhi', naapkaçxha' Dxus luuçxa' Dxus Espiritutek upxhi'. 7 Maawa ki' upxwa'ja' jĩ'th aça' fxiy yuh txã'wẽ we'we'k sũhna yaakxnu. 8 Wejxa'swa ma'wẽga susni's wẽse'jçxawa, majũ yuhte ki' maa dxi'j u'jweçtewa ya'jiyu'çmée, txã'wẽyku Dxus Espiritute upxni's ya'jiyu'çmée jĩte', 9 Nikodemo' jiyuya' ãjameeçxa' ki' pẽjxku:
—¿Ma'wẽ txã'wẽ yuja' ki' ya'upxhikx? jĩte', 10 Jesus pasçxa':
—Idxa' Israelwe'sxtxi kaapiya'jsaçxawa, na'wẽsa yuutxi' ¿jiimeewá'ga? 11 Isath we'we': Dxus Espiritute upxnisa' wejxa na'wẽsaçxá' txã' ma'wẽsatewa iiuythu sa' jiiçxath txã'jĩ'. Nawa i'kwe'sxa' jiyu'çmeei'kwe. 12 Naa kiwetewe'sxtxi we'wena pta'sxte jiyumeeçxa', ¿ma'wẽ jiyuki'kwe cielutewe'sxna pta'sxte'?
13 Maa yuhwa cielute u'kasa yu' meeta', naasáa cielujũ kĩisa yuuçxáa cielute u'kasa', Dxus Nçxi'k Nasa Yuusáa txã' cielutewe'sxa' naa pa'ga. 14 Dxus Nçxi'k Nasa Yuusa's ẽekhẽ a'kxçxa ki'pni yuuna, Moiseswa ma'wẽga nasa u'pnimée kiwete ũsçxa' ul weeswe's fxtũukhẽ a'kxçxa ki'p txã'wẽy, 15 kxah maawa jxukay txã'sna uyçxa ũuste jxpa'gasa', bagaçxte yuhwa pçuwa'jmée ĩtxi fxi'zewa'ja's ji'phutxna vxitumée.
Dxusa' nasatx iiméh wala peeygãani
16 Dxusa' naa kiwete nasatx iiméh wala peeygãhçxa yuuçxáak txajx Nçxi'k teeçxçxáasanawa naa kiwete kaah, kxah maawa txã'sna ũuste jxpa'gaçxa' bagaçxte yuhwa pçumée ĩtxi fxi'zewa'ja's ji'phukahn vxitumée. 17 Dxusa' naa kiwete txajx Nçxi'ka's kaahçxawa, nasatx ewmeete nvxihtxa' kaahme', wejxwa nwe'weya'k kaah. 18 Maawa Dxus Nçxi'ka's ũuste jxpa'gasa', ewteyã'j neeyũutxna. Nawa txã'wẽ Dxus Nçxi'k teeçxçxáasana ũuste jxpa'gasame', ewmeeteyã'j neeyũuwa'jsata'. 19 Maawa ũuste jxpa'gasame', ewmeeteyã'jtx nvxiitni yu', txã'wẽ eena'sa naa kiwete kĩhtewa, ewmeeçxáa yũuna ũsçxa', çxhi'dxteçxáa wejx fxi'ze wẽepa'ga. 20 Sa' maawa jxukay ewmeeçxáa yũuna ũssa', eena'sa's ahçeçxa utxa'çmeeta' eena'te, txãawe'sx ewmée yũuni ãate vxyaakahmen. 21 Nawa ew yũuna fxi'zesa yu' eena'sa's utxa'tx, txãawe'sx ew yũuni' Dxus jxkaahnisatewa ãate vxyaakahn.
Jesusa's tuuthe'jni's wẽse'jçxa' Juan weçxaníi
22 Txãa e'su' Jesusa' Judea kiwen u'kh txãa yakh u'jusawe'sx yã'jçxa. Sa' kxte' maz en ũsu'k nasatx yu'te bautisãyna. 23 Juanwa bautisãynak ũsu' teeçx ahte Enon yaasesate Salim utxate, txãa ja'dasu yu' ũsu'k naa pa'ga. Aça' Juana' nasa wala kuh yu'te bautisãyku. 24 Karcelte kxupmeynaçxáak txã'wẽ bautisãy.
25 Txajũ' judiuwe'sxa' Juan yakh u'jusawe'sxtxi paapẽjxtxi txã'wẽ bautisãyni's. 26 Aça' txãawe'sxa' Juana's pta'sxtxi na'jĩna:
—Kaapiya'jsa, mwẽese'j. Sek kãani ju'gu Jordan yu'wala kutete ũste pta'sxgu txãa piçthẽ'jwa ãçxha' bautisãyna ũsa', aça' nasa jxukay txãa yakhçxaçxáa u'jweçta' jĩte', 27 Juana' na'wẽk pas:
—Kim yuhwa txã'wẽ tuuthe'jni yuumeena, Dxus jxkaahnimeesa yu'. 28 I'kwe'sxa' yaçtey adx pta'sxni's jiiyã'ji'kwe: Adxa' Kristomeeth, naasáa txajx yaçte kaahnisaçxáath jĩni's. 29 Naa ejemplo's mjiyuwe: Ptamu ente' novxio' novxia yakhku u'pu'. Aça' novxio namiku' pukate u'pçxa' wala weçxweçxa yaaki'k novxio we'weni's wẽse'jçxa. Adxwa txã'wẽythu wala weçxweçxa yaaki', Jesusa's tuuthe'jni's wẽse'jçxa. 30 Txã' jweeíiçxáa tuhthesa yuuna u'jwena, naapkaçxha' adxa' jweeíiçxáa ãhmeekwesa yuuwa'th.
Jesusa' cielujũ kĩisa'
31 Cielujũ kĩisa' maatewa jxthaakwe tuhthesa'. Kiwete ũssa' naa kiwetewe'sxta', sa' naa kiwetewe'sxçxá'stxi pta'sxi'. Nawa cielujũ kĩisa' maatewa jxthaakwe tuhthesa'. 32 Txã'wẽçxaíik txajx wẽse'jçxa uyni's pta'sxi', nawa kim yuhwa nwẽese'je'çmeeta' txajx we'weni's. 33 Nawa txajx pta'sxni's maaçxáawa nwẽese'jwaça', Dxusa' isak pta'sxi' sũhna jiyuna pa'jatxna. 34 Txã'wẽ Dxus jxkaahnisa' Dxus kwe'we'jni'sku we'we', txã'sa' Dxusa' txajx Espiritu's makwewa ũsu'k txãa pa'ga. 35 Neya' Nçxi'ka's wedxçxa kĩhnawa jxuka txajx kusete nvxihtku. 36 Aça' maawa Dxus Nçxi'ka's ũuste jxpa'gasa' pçumée ĩtxi fxi'zewa'ja's ji'phutxna. Nawa Dxus Nçxi'ka's nwẽese'jsame' pçumée ĩtxi fxi'zewa'ja's ji'phumeetxna, naasáa Dxus kastigo seena'saçxá's jxpa'gawa'jsata'.
