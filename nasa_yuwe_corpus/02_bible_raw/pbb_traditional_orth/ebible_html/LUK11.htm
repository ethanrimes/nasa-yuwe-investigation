11
Dxus yakh na'wẽ puutx we'wewa'ja'
(Mt 6.9-15; 7.7-11)
1 Teeçx ente' Jesusa' Dxusna orãynak u'pu' teeçx ahte, sa' orãyna jxã'the', txãa yakh u'jusa teeçxsa' na'jĩk:
—Kwe'sxtxi mkaapiya'j, Dxus yakh puutx we'wena orãyya', Juanwa ma'wẽga txãa yakh u'jusawe'sxtxi' kaapiya'ja' txã'wẽy jĩte', 2 Jesus pasçxa':
—Dxus yakh puutx we'wena oraĩççxa', na'jĩne'kwe:
Kwe'sx Tata cielute ũssa,
Idxa' bagaçxtewa wala jxtuhthesag.
Iidx jxkaahwa'j ena'sçxáatha'w ũythasu'.
Naa kiwete idx ma'wẽne'ga jxthãasu' txãa yu' jxuka kxtey tee yuu cielute na'wẽy.
3 Ãçxh kwe'sx ũ'wa'ja's meen pees.
4 Kwe'sx ewmée yũuni's meen peltunaĩ, kwe'sxwa peltunãytha'w kwe'sxtxi ewmée yũusatx txãa pa'ga.
Ewmeete wetekahmén meen nwe'we jĩna oraĩne'kwe.
5 Txajũ' na'jĩnak we'we:
—I'kwe'sxa' namiku yatte kuspxãh u'jçxa': Namiku, tekh pan mpeeki'j. 6 Dxi'j u'jsa adx namikuk yatte pa'j, aça' kĩh yuhwa ji'phmeeth puuçwa'j, ¿jĩmeema'kwe kĩhwa ji'phmeeçxa'? 7 Aça' dxiiju passa' na'jĩna: Adx luuçx deeni yã'tha', aça' kuçx susnu. Naapkaçxha' vxitxwa aphni yã'j ũsa', aça' kĩh yuhwa ũsya' ewume'nja jĩçxawa, 8 jwee kuçx susna nes yuukahmén kiiteçxa kĩh pẽjxtewa jxuka ũsuna. Na'jĩ'th na': Namiku pa'gaçxáa kuspxãh kiiteçxa ew yũumée naasáa kuçx suskahménçxáa txã'wẽ yũuna. 9 Txãa pa'gath adxa' i'kwe'sxtxi Dxusna pẽykaja' aça' txã' ũsuna. Kĩh peejini'swa pakweçxa' uyuune'kwe. Vxitxte pa'yate' phaadena. 10 Na'jĩ'th na': maawa Dxusna pẽysa', jxpa'gana. Aça' pakwesa', uyuuna. Aça' vxitxte tu'kakate', phaadena.
11 Ũus miiyajkxwe: i'kwe'sx luuçx pan pẽjxte', ¿kweta's ũsma'kwe? Meeçxa' wedx pẽjxte', ¿ula's ũsma'kwe? 12 Meeçxawa ziç pẽjxte', ¿usmitxa's ũsma'kwe? 13 I'kwe'sxa' Dxus dxi'pte ewmeesaçxawa, i'kwe'sx luuçxtxi' ewsaçxáa i'kwe peesu'. Aça' i'kwe'sx Tata cielutewe'sxa' wejx jxthaakwe ewsa Dxus Espiritu's peesuna pẽysatxi's jĩk.
Dxus Espiritu's açewe'weni
(Mt 12.22-30; Mr 3.19-27)
14 Jesusa' eçx luuku vxit-sa kwe'we'jsame's piçthẽ'j ũusuh kutxi'the', txãa eçxa' ũusuh kasejk, aça' txãa piçthẽ'ja' we'weya' ãjak. Txã'wẽ yũute', nasa' wala kuh yajkxna neeyũutx. 15 Nawa vxite yu', na'jĩnatx we'we':
—Eçxthẽ'j pu'çxteçxáak naa piçthẽ'ja' eçx luuku vxit-satxwa nasaa ũusuh kutxi'ji' jĩ'tx.
16 Naapkaçxha' vxite yu' Jesus kĩh yũutewa jiyuya' sũhçxa cielujũ seena'sa mkaavxya'ja'j jĩte', 17 Jesusa' txãawe'sx ewmée ũus yaakxni's jiyuçxa', kxtey yuumée na'wẽçxáak we'we:
—Maa gobiernowa peekx nasa yakhíiçxáa puiiçxa', txãawe'sx pwe'sxíiçxáa jxuka pçuutxna. Maa yattewe'sxwa txãawe'sx pwe'sxíiçxáa puutx puiiçxa', txãawe'sx pwe'sxíiçxáa jxuka pçuutxna. 18 Txã'wẽy eçxthẽ'jwa txãawe'sx pwe'sxíiçxáa puutx puiiçxa', puutx pçuutxna. Adxa'sa' eçxthẽ'j pu'çxteçxáak eçx luuku vxit-satxwa nasa ũusu kutxi'jya' ãjã' jĩ'i'kwe txãa pa'gath txã'jĩ'. 19 Txã'wẽ dxihte', i'kwe'sx yakh u'jusatxwa eçxthẽ'jíiçxáane' pu'çxhi' eçx luuku vxit-satx kutxi'jya'. Ew yajkxwaç yu', i'kwe'sx yakh u'jusaíiçxáa i'kwe'sx jxpubani's khiyu'jwa'jsata'. 20 Adxa's eçxthẽ'j pu'çxmée, Dxus pu'çxteth eçx luuku vxit-satx nasaa ũusuh kutxi'ji'. Isa ew jiyuwaç yu', i'kwe'sxtxi Dxus jxkaahwa'j en ãjk txãa pa'ga txã'wẽ yũuçthu.
21 Teeçx piçthẽ'j çxhãçxhasa' kĩjkĩhwa txajx ji'phuni's ivxiituya'mée sũhçxa puiiwa'j tũ'sçxa yatte yupu'k ka'ga'jya'mée. 22 Nawa vxite wejx jxthaakwe çxhãçxhasa pa'jçxa', txãa yupsa's knayçxa puiiwa'ja'swa kusa'jna yuuçxa ji'phunitxi's txãak iiyamu.
23 Adx ju'gumée ũssa', adxa's açesata'. Aça' adx yakh pkhakh pu'çxsame' neeusaçxa txã'wẽ yuuna.
Eçxa' nasa ũuste ki' sxawedni
(Mt 12.43-45)
24 Eçx luuku vxit-sa' nasa ũusu kasehçxa', nasa u'pnimeesuk jxkaasewa'j pakwena u'ju'. Nawa uymeeçxa' naa sũju'k: Adx kasehni yatnay sxawedna u'jwe'nja sũhçxa 25 ki' sxawedte' txãa nasa ũus yu' yata's ew padçxa txaahni na'wẽ ũstek uyna pa'ja'. 26 Sa' u'jçxa vxite siete eçx txãate jxthaakwe ewmeesatx nuypa'jçxa, txãa nasa ũuste pejka u'katx. Aça' txãa nasa' jxthaakwek ewmeesa yuuna pa'ja'.
Kim wejx thaakwe weçxawa'jsatxki
27 Jesusa' txã'wẽ pta'sxiçpkaçxha', teeçx u'y nasaa ksxavxyte na'jĩnak sus we'we:
—Idxna kçxu'çxusa mama yakha' seena' yuh wala weçxa yuhna jĩte', 28 Jesus pasçxa':
—Txã'wẽme', wejxwa Dxus we'weni's wẽsẽ'jçxa', txajx jxkaahnitxi's kxtey yuusáa yakhçxáa seena' wala weçxa yuja' jĩk.
Nasa ewmeesa' cieluju kĩhçxáawa kaavxya'ja'khaahni
(Mt 12.38-42; Mr 8.12)
29 Txajũ' nasa' Jesus we'weni's wẽsẽ'jya' txajx tasxte pkhaakhesatxi' na'jĩnak we'we:
—Nasa ewmeesa naa ensuwe'sxa' kĩhçxáawa seena'sa kaavxya'ja'khaja'tx. Nawa kĩh yuhwa jwee yu' kaavxya'ja'jmée, naasáa Dxus yuwe pta'sxsa Jonas ma'wẽsaga yu' txã'sçxáana khiyu'the ewna. 30 Jonaswa ma'wẽga Níniwe çxhabte nasa ewmeesatx khiyu'j, txã'wẽy Dxus Nçxi'k Nasa Yuusaawa naa ensu nasa ewmeesatx khiyu'jsa'. 31 Naa ensu nasatx Dxus kastigãywa'j en ãhte', txãniitey jxu'j kiwete jxkaahsa u'ythẽ'ja' ãçxga nasatxi' ewmeete nvxiituna. Txãa u'ya' txakwe jxu'juwe'sx yuhçxawa, wa'lméek Salomon pta'sxniçxá'swa wẽsẽ'jya' pa'j. Naapkaçxha' ãçxh yu' Salomonte jxthaakwesa ayte ũsa' nawa wa'lu'tx naa pa'ga txãa u'ya' ewmeete nvxiituna. 32 Txã'wẽy Níniwe çxhabtewe'sxwa Dxus kastigãywa'j en ãhte', naa ensu nasatx ewmeete nvxiitutxna. Txãawe'sxa' Jonas pta'sxniçxá'swa wẽsẽ'jçxa', txãawe'sx pkaltxi nvxihtçxa Dxus tasxte sxawedtxi. Naapkaçxha' ãçxh yu' Jonaste jxthaakwesa ayte ũsa' nawa wa'lu'tx naa pa'ga Níniwe çxhabtewe'sxa' ewmeete nvxiitutxna.
Yafxa' kakwe's ewte kaafxi'ze'jsata'
(Mt 5.15; 6.22-23)
33 Lampara's atũu dxiite aphçxa txajxa' txã'wẽ ya'pki'taçme', wejxwa ẽetek ya'txaja' eena' kweethkahn, kxah maawa u'kasa' eena'te u'kakahn. 34 Peekx kakwete yafxa' lampara na'wẽsata'. Aça' iidx yafxa' suwenimée ewçxa' iidx kakwete jxuka eena' kweethna nes yuuna. Nawa iidx yafx ewmée suweniite', iidx kakwe' jxuka çxhi'dxteçxáa neeyũuna. 35 Iidx kakwete kĩhte yuhwa çxhi'dxsamée jxuka eena'saçxáa ũste', jxuka ew uyuune'ga, lamparawa ma'wẽga ew eena' kweethe' txã'wẽ. 36 Nawa mpa'yajkx. Txã'wẽme', eena' khikhysa ji'phgu txãa kweethmeete', çxhi'dxteçxáa neeyũune'ga.
Fxariseo vxite' ley kaapiya'jsawe'sxtxi yuuwesa Jesus nvxiitni
(Mt 23.1-36; Mr 12.38-40; Lk 20.45-47)
37 Txajũ' Jesusa' txã'wẽ we'wena jxã'the', teeçx fxariseo txajx yatte sã'jĩya' pi'kxku. Aça' Jesusa' u'jçxa ũ'ya' kaçxku. 38 Txajũ' txãa fxariseo' naasũjk: Naa piçthẽ'ja' wala yuh fxiy yu'k kusekhẽ yu' kaahmée ũ'ya' kaçxku sũjũ'k. 39 Aça' Jesusa' na'jĩk:
—Fxariseowe'sx i'kwe'sxa' plaatu waso ma'k yuu nawa txãa yuutxi' ekakhẽ yu' pkhi'khi'i'kwe. Nawa i'kwe'sx ũus dxiite yu', pesweena kĩjkĩhwa ewmeeçxáa yũunisaçxáa jxuka uta ũsa'. 40 I'kwe'sxa' jiimeewá'kwe: Dxusa' kakwekhẽ ãakhẽwe'sxçxá's uyçxa', ũustewe'sx yu's uyu'çmeene' ¿sũjũ'wá'kwe? 41 Txã'wẽ kĩhwa ji'phmeesatx ũusuh jxuka peeygãhna lmusxnu peeswaça', ãakhẽçxáa yaatesa yuumeene'kwe.
42 Fxariseowe'sx, i'kwe'sx yakha' seena' yuh ũukhũukha', i'kwe'sxa' putatxhã', me'sukwe, kĩjkĩhwa uuthasxte ũsna txãa yu'sa' jxuka diesmo dukhçxawa, Dxus yu'sa' ũusu jxuka wedxna nwẽese'jna fxi'ze'çmeei'kwe naa pa'ga seena' yuh ũukhũukha'. Diesmo's dukhwa'j yuja', nawa Dxusna nwẽese'jni wejx jxthaakwe ewa'.
43 Fxariseowe'sx, i'kwe'sx yakh seena' yuh ũukhũukha', i'kwe'sxa' judiuwe'sx pkhaakhenisu kaçxni wejx ewsaçxáana i'kwe pakwé', sa' çxhabsu msuuwa ewkuẽçxáa weçxa jxthãasna i'kwe pekũju' naa pa'ga seena' yuh ũukhũukha'.
44 Ley kaapiya'jsawe'sx vxite' fxariseowe'sx, i'kwe'sx yakha' seena' yuh ũukhũukha', i'kwe'sxa' ãajuçxáa ew we'weçxa', ũus dxiiju yu' ewmeeçxáa yajkxna fxi'zepa'ga. I'kwe'sxa' uusáa pedani kafxa's aphnisa na'wẽi'kwe. Txãa kafxa's aphte', nasa' ma'wẽ dxiiga kĩh ũstewa jiimée kajkwesu skhẽuukx txã'wẽçxáa i'kwe jiyunimeete fxi'ze' jĩte', 45 ley kaapiya'jsa pasçxa':
—Kwe'sxtxig açewe'we' jĩtewa, 46 Jesusa' ki' we'weçxa':
—Ley kaapiya'jsawe'sx, i'kwe'sx yakha' seena' yuh ũukhũukha', i'kwe'sxwa vxiteçxáatx txã'wẽ kim yuhwa ãjãwa'jmée thẽysa's ũsçxa', i'kwe'sx yu', ma'wẽkuẽçxáa yuhwa pu'çxhiçmeei'kwe naa pa'ga.
47 Txã'wẽy i'kwe'sx ji'j yaçgawe'sx txã'wẽ Dxus yuwe pta'sxsatx ikhçxa pedaate', i'kwe'sx yu' txãa pedani's zhiçxkwe vxitnai'kwe pheu'ju'. 48 Sa' txã'wẽ yũuçxa', kwe'sx ji'j yaçgawe'sx ew yuh yuutx jĩçxane'kwe txãawe'sx ikhçxa pedani's zhiçxkwe vxitna pheu'ju' naapa'ga i'kwe'sx yakha' seena' yuh ũukhũukha'.
49 Kxajíi Dxusa' txã'wẽ yũuni's we'weçxak na'jĩ': Adx yuwee pta'sxsa txãniitey ensuwa kaja'nja, sa' naa ensuwa kaja'nja. Nawa maaíitxi' ikhetxna, sa' vxite yuutxi' wala ahçena pkxuutxna jĩ'k. 50 Naa ensu nasa' jxukaysa kastigo's knayuutxna, 51 txã'wẽ Abela's ikhnijũjx Dxus yuwe pta'sxsatx ikhçxa Zakariasa's ikhni entepkaçx ee pkawpa'ga. Txãa Zakarias yu'sa' dxuus yat patxute altal utxatetx ikh. Txãa pa'ga naa ensu nasatx Dxusa' kastigaĩna, txã'wẽ Dxus kaahnisatx ikhpa'ga.
52 Ley kaapiya'jsawe'sx, i'kwe'sx yakha' seena' yuh ũukhũukha', i'kwe'sxa' txakwe wala leya's jiisatha'w jĩçxawa, vxite yuutxi's kaapiya'ja'çmeei'kwe. Txãasa vxite jiyu wẽesatx i'kwe'sxa' yupnaçxáa ũsi'kwe jĩte', 53 ley kaapiya'jsawe'sx vxite' fxariseowe'sxa' wala ũusaçxaçxa kĩhçxáawa wala paapẽjxtxi, 54 kĩhteçxáawa ewmée paste' pãatxhĩ'khya' sũhçxa.
