14
Ciento kuarenta y kuatro mil nasa memniisa
1 Txãa e'su' uythu Piisxakuẽ jĩnisa Sion thã'j vxiçte yujuçxa ũste. Txãa yakh vxite ũsu'tx, ciento kuarenta y kuatro mil nasa, txãawe'sx dxikthesu yu' Piisxakuẽ jĩnisa yase vxite' txajx Tata yase's ji'phsa. 2 Txajũ' cielujũ susni pthũusek yu'wala kuhsa susni na'wẽ vxite' kpi'sx çxhãçxha we'weni na'wẽ susni, iiméh wala kuhsa txãawe'sx arpa tukani na'wẽk pthũuse'. 3 Txãa nasa' mem u'sesatx memu', Dxus jxkaahna ũsni vxite' Dxus vxitni ĩtxĩsa pahzsawe'sx vxite' Dxus luuçxtxi jxkaahsawe'sx txãawe'sx tasxte. Txã'sa' kim yuhwa piyaya' ãja'çmeeta', naasáa naa kiwetewe'sx ksxavxyu ewte neeyũusáa ciento kuarenta y kuatro mil txãawe'sx yuuçxáatx ãjã'. 4 Txãawe'sxa' Dxus yuuçxáana selpiina fxi'zesatx yu', sa' txãawe'sxa' nasaa ksxavxyu nwe'wenitx yuu Dxus jĩi vxite' Piisxakuẽ jĩnisa txãa jĩi yuuwa'jsa, nyafxte nwe'wenisa. Txãasatx Piisxakuẽ jĩnisa ikhni yuuk txãa mtee u'thewa txãa yakh ja'da u'jwe'. 5 Sa' kĩhçxáa yuhwa ĩsxĩisa yu'çmeeta', sa' Dxus jxkaahna ũsnite kĩh pkal yuhwa ji'phmée ũsta'.
Dxus angeleswe'sx tekhsa pta'sxya' kasehni
6 Txajũ' Dxus angel fxuhna u'jweçteth uy ẽe walasu. Sa' ewte neeyũunisa bagaçxte yuhwa pçuwa'jsame's pta'sxwa'ja'sku ji'phu' naa kiwetewe'sx mtee mteewe'sxtxiwa, nasa ma'wẽsa, fxiyfxiyçxáa we'wesa, maa çxhabsuwe'sxtxiwa jxuka. 7 Sa' na'jĩnak sus we'we':
—Dxusa's mtuuthe'jwe ũukhna. Txã'wẽme' jxukaysatx kastigãywa'j ena' ãjxã'kh. Mneewe'wewe cielu, kiwe, mar, yu', kĩjkĩhnawa jxuka vxit-sa's jĩ'k.
8 Txajũ' Dxus angel vxite na'jĩnak sus we'we:
—Babilonia çxhab txakwe çxhãçxhasaçxawa, Dxus kastigo's jxpa'gawa'the yã'j ũsa'. Txã'wẽ yuukahníi naa kiwete nasa mteewe'sxtxiwa jxukaysa pkal kazxte fxi'zewa'ja'sku kaapiya'j naa pa'ga jĩ'k.
9 Txajũ' Dxus angel tekhtewe'sxku na'jĩna sus we'we:
—Maawa eçx seena'sa vxite' txãa na'wẽy piisani yuuk txãatx neewe'weçxa, txajx iisawa'jni's kaaki'pu'juna dxi'pte meeçxawa kusete na', 10 txã'wẽ yũusa' Dxus kastigo seena' nxussa's jxpa'gatxna txã'wẽ yũupa'ga. Sa' seena' yuh kastigãyni yuuna asufxre ipx walate, Dxus angeleswe'sx vxite' Piisxakuẽ jĩnisa ikhni yuuk txãawe'sx dxi'pte. 11 Kastigãyni ipx wala aja' bagaçxte yuhwa pçumée sxi'tana nes yuuna. Aça' kxteea' en ma'k yuu kus ma'k yuu nawa bagaçxte yuhwa jxkaasewa'ja's ji'phumeetxna, txã'wẽ eçx seena'sa vxite' txãa na'wẽy piisani yuuk txãatx neewe'weçxa txajx yaseju iisawa'jni's kaaki'pu'pha'ga. 12 Txãa pa'ga Dxus luuçxa' txajx jxkaahni's kxtey nwẽese'jna Jesus yakh yuuçxáa çxhãçxha fxi'zewa'ja's ji'phuwa'j peejxta' jĩ'k.
13 Txajũ' ki' wẽse'th we'weni cielujũwe'sx, txã' adxa's na'jĩ'k:
—Naa skhẽwniijũ Kristo's jxpa'gaçxa uusaawe'sxa' wala yuh wẽtwẽt fxi'zesa yuutxna jĩni's mfxi'j jĩte', Dxus Espiritu pasçxa': Txã'wẽ yuja', txãawe'sxa' jxkaaseetxna Dxusna selpiina pxthaa yuuna fxi'zenijũ, sa' cielute uyuutxna txãawe'sx mjĩini's jĩ'k.
Skuutxh uwatx pkhakhni
14 Txajũ' thegçxa uythu tãaph çxihme kajkwete nasa yuusáa na'wẽsak teeçx kaçxçxa u'pu'. Dxikthete vxyuu bee koronak ji'phu', sa' kusete yu' skuutxh wakani zeksa'sku ji'phu'. 15 Cielute Dxus ũsnijũ vxite angel sus we'wena kasejk tãaphte kaçxçxa u'psa's na'jĩna:
—Skuutxha's wakaçxa mepkhakh. Txã'wẽme' jxuka thẽ'kh aça' pkhakhwa'j en ãjk jĩte', 16 tãaphte kaçxçxa u'psa' naa kiwete skuutxha's wakaçxa jxuka pkhakhku.
17 Txajũ' cielute Dxus ũsnijũ vxite angelwa kasejk uwa udeni zeksa's atçxa. 18 Naapkaçxha' Dxus altaluh vxite angel kasejk txã' ipxa's kaahwa'jsak yu'. Sa' uwa udenisa's ji'phsa's na'jĩnak sus we'we:
—Uwa's uhdeçxa mepkhakh. Txã'wẽme' jxuka thẽ'jni yã'j ũsta' naa kiwete jĩte', 19 Dxus angela' naa kiwete uwa's jxuka uhdeçxa pkhakhku, sa' teeçx ahteçxaçxáak jxuka ãsx kxtee ku'sya'. Txãa yu' Dxus kastigo' na'wẽ seena' yuuwa'jsa' jĩnisak yu'. 20 Txãa uwatxi's çxhab ekajk ku'sni yuu, aça' eeçxaçxáak uwu jiba fxrenusu kãhpkaçx, tres cientos kilómetro peeku'jsu pkaçxku txã'wẽ uta.
