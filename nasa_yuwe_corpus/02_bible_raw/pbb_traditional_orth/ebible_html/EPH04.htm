4
Dxus Espiritu' ma'wẽsatxwa kxteeçxah yuutxani
1 Kristo's selpiipa'ga adxa' preesu ũsthu, nawa i'kwe'sxtxi neewe'we'th: ma'wẽ fxi'zekahn ne' Dxusa' i'kwe'sxtxi txhitxh txã'sa' kxtey yuuna meswe. 2 Dxus yakh fxi'zesayã'ja' maa yuhwa iiwejçx yajkxmée wejxwa puutx ya'peeygãhna ewçxáa yũuna mfxi'zewe. 3 I'kwe'sxa' puutx ũusçxa yajkxna fxi'zemée, wejxwa kxteeçxah txutemée fxi'zewa'jçxá's mpa'pçxuwe, Dxus Espiritu' kxteeçxah yuutxa yã'kh naa pa'ga. 4 Txãasa Kristo nwe'wenisa yã'j yu', ma'wẽsaçxawa kxteeçxah teeçxçxáak yuuna pa'ja', sa' kxazíi Dxus Espiritu teeçxsaçxá'stxi jxukaysa jxpa'ga', sa' kxazíi teeçx ahte Dxus tasxteçxaçxáa pa'jwa'ja'sku ya'ũythasu', 5 txãa pa'gatey jxukaysatx nwe'wesa' teeçxçxá', aça' txã'sçxáanak jxukaysa ya'nwẽese'je'. Sa' jxukaysa txã'sçxaçxáanak yajkxna bautismo's ya'jypa'ga'. 6 Sa' teeçx Dxusçxáa ũsa' jxukaysate Tata txã' maatewa puutejka walasa' jxukaysatx jxkaahsa, sa' jxukaysa yakh ũsa'.
7 Sa' teeçxçxáa vxitna pa'jçxawa, Dxusna selpiiya' ãjawa'j yu'sa' fxiyfxiyçxajk Kristo' ũs teeçxsa na'wẽçxah. 8 Txã'wẽ yũuwa'ja's we'weçxak Dxus librute na'jĩna fxi'jni yuu:
Ewmeesa kusejũ nwe'weçxa cielun pe'jna u'kh,
Sa' naa kiwete nasatxi's ãjawa'ja's ũsku jĩ'k.
9 ¿Ma'wẽçxaga txã'wẽ cielute tejkak jĩna we'wé'? Kristo' nyafxte' uusaawe'sx ũsnite khuẽ'y khẽeçxa txãajũk ki' cielute tejka jĩçxak txã'jĩ'. 10 Txã'wẽ uusaawe'sx ũsnite khuẽ'y khẽesa íiçxáak cielute iiméh ẽe walate kãhpkaçx tejka, mteewa txãaçxaçxáa jxuka thegna ũsya' ãjaya'. 11 Txã'wẽ yũuçxak Dxusna selpiiya' fxiyfxiyçxah ãjawa'ja's ũs jxukaysatx teeçxsa na'wẽçxah: maaíitxi's txajx yuwe's nyafxte pta'sxwa'jsak nvxiht; vxitetxi's Dxus ma'wẽ yũuya'watewa dxiikhẽy pta'sxwa'jsak nvxiht; vxitetxi's Dxus yuwe pta'sxwa'jsak nvxiht; vxitetxi's Dxus yuwe's kaapiya'jna jxpe'jwa'jsak nvxiht. 12 Txajx nasatxi' txã'wẽk mjĩiwa'jsa pheu'jçxa nvxiht, txã'wẽ Kristo nwe'wenisa yuutxi' jweeíiçxáa kxteeçxah çxhãçxha fxi'zewa'ja's kaapiya'khanku txã'wẽ yũu. 13 Kxah jxukaysa Dxus Nçxi'ka'sçxaçxáa nwẽese'jçxa txã'sa' ew jiyuna jxã'jçxa kĩhte yuhwa jxpubamée ew fxi'zekahnku txã'wẽ kaapiya'jçxa nvxiht. 14 Txajũ yũuçxa' luuçx na'wẽ yuume'nja'w. Txã'wẽme' nasa ewmée yajkxçxa ĩsxiina pta'sxnite thẽyméek wetena pa'jçxa kxte jxpubana ya'fxi'ze'. 15 Txã'wẽ jxpubamée, wejxwa isasaçxá's we'wena peeygãawa'j yuuçxá's yajkxçxa Kristo yakh jweeíiçxáa çxhãçxha fxi'zena u'jwa'j ji'ptha'w. 16 Aça' maawa Kristo tasxte utxaaçxa jxukaysa kxteeçxah pkhaakheçxa puutx ya'kaapiya'jna ew yũuna fxi'zete', thẽyméek peeygãhna fxi'zewa'ja' walaana u'jwe'.
Ewmée yaakxni's jxuka yu'pthehni
17 Txã'wẽ' aça' kxahth i'kwe'sxtxi neewe'we', vxite nasa Dxusna jiimeesa na'wẽ fxi'zewe'sxte. Txãawe'sxa' peekx ũus yaakxni seelpiwa'jmeesaçxá'stxi kxtey yuuna fxi'ze'. 18 Txã'wẽ Dxusa's jxpa'gaya' wa'lpa'ga Dxus yakhmée ũsçxa ew eena' yaakxwa'j yu'sa' ji'phmeeta'. 19 Txãasatx txãawe'sxa' thaame ũus yu'sa' ji'phmée, pkal seena' kazxsateçxáa fxi'ze'. 20 Nawa i'kwe'sxtxi' Kristo' txã'wẽ fxi'ze kaahme'. 21 Txã'sa' i'kwe'sxa' peekx jiine'kwe Jesus kaapiya'jni isasa's wẽse'jsa dxihçxa', 22 nyafxíi ũus yaakxni ewmeeteçxáa kaafxi'ze'jsa's nwẽese'jmée jxuka nvxihtçxa, 23 ew ũus yaakxwa'ja's kaapiya'jni yakh mphu'se'jwe. 24 Kxah i'kwe'sx ũuste ewsa yuuçxáa yu'pthehni yuute', Dxus ma'wẽne' jxthãasu' txãa yuuçxáana ãate kaavxya'ja'jna fxi'zene'kwe, Dxus dxi'pte kĩh pkal yuhwa ji'phmée.
25 Txãasa ãçxha' peena yu' ĩsxiina fxi'zemée, wejxwa teeçxsa na'wẽçxah isasaçxá's mwe'wewe maa yakhwa, txãa pa'gatey jxukaysa kxteeçxah teeçx jwedçxáa yuuna pa'jsatha'w txãa ji'jçxaçxáatewe'sx.
26 Mpa'yajkxwe, ũusçxani yakhçxáa yu' fxi'zewa'jme', ewmeete weteya'mée. Txãa entey ya'mpeltunaĩwe. 27 Eçxthẽ'ja's weh yuhwa ka'ga'jnuwe.
28 Pesweesawa Dxusna jiyunijũyã'ja', pesweewa'jme', wejxwa pesweemée kuseju mjĩiçxa vxite peejiina fxi'zesatxwa pu'çxna fxi'zewa'ja'.
29 Sa' kazx we'wenisa yu' teeçx yuweçxáa yuhwa i'kwe'sx yuweju kasehwa'jsame', wejxwa Dxus yakh fxi'zewa'j selpiwa'jsaçxá's we'wewa'ja', kxah vxitesawa Dxus yakh fxi'zewa'jçxá's piyaatxna. 30 Sa' Dxus Espiritu'swa kxũusu'jnuwe, txãa pa'gatey Dxus Espiritu íiçxáak i'kwe'sxtxi's Dxus jĩi vxitna pa'jçxa nvxiht, Dxus tasxna u'jwa'j en ãhtewe'sx.
31 Sa' ewmée yajkxna, ahçena, pxũuskwe yuute juuna' we'wena, ũusaçxana ma'wẽwa wẽesxuna fxi'zenijũ jxuka mtxuteewe. 32 Wejxwa puutx ewçxáa yũuna, teeçxsa na'wẽçxah puutx peeygãhna, puutx ya'peltunãyna mfxi'zewe, Kristo kwe'sxtxi nwe'wete Dxuswa ma'wẽga kwe'sxtxi peltunãy txã'wẽy.
