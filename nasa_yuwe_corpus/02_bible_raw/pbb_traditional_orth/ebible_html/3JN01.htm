SAN JUAN KARTA NMEHTEWE'SX
1
Gayo ew yũuna fxi'zeni's we'weni
1 Thẽ'j jĩna kxsusu'tx txãath fxi'ja' naa karta's adx peeygãanisa Gayo's weçxana.
2 Yakhthẽ'j peeygãanisa, adxa' iidx pa'gate Dxus yakh puutx we'wena ũsthu kĩhtewa ewçxáa ãça'mée fxi'zekahn, ma'wẽga Dxus yakh çxhãçxha fxi'ze' txã'wẽy. 3 Idxa' Dxus we'weni isasa's jxpubamée Dxus yakh ew fxi'zeni's yakhthẽ'jwe'sx pa'jçxa pta'sxte', wala weçxath. 4 Adxa' kĩh yuhnawa txã'wẽ yu' weçxa'çmeeth, naasáa adx luuçxwe'sx txã'wẽ Dxus we'weni isasa's jxpubamée ũsta' jĩna pta'sxni yuuçxáak jxthaakwe ya'weçxa'.
5 Yakhthẽ'j peeygãanisa idxa' bagaçxtewa pu'vxitumée selpiina ũsgu vxite kiweju yakhthẽ'jwe'sx kxtee pa'jsatx pu'çxna. 6 Txãawe'sxa' ayte yakhthẽ'jwe'sx pkhaakhenite pta'sxtxi iidx peeygãhna pu'çxni's. Txãasa bagaçxtewa meen pu'çx txãawe'sx dxi'the u'jwa'j peejini's Dxus weçxakahn. 7 Txãawe'sxa' kasehtx Jesukristo kaapiya'jni's pta'sxna u'juya', nawa dxi'the u'jwa'j peejini's pẽyi'çmeeta' Dxusa's jiimeesa yuutxi's. 8 Txãa pa'ga kwe'sxa' kĩh peejiiçtewa pu'çxhi ji'ptha'w Dxus we'weni isasa's pta'sxsatxi'. Txã'wẽ yũunisa Dxus yuwe's jxukaysa ja'daçxah mjĩini yaase'.
Diótrefxes ewmée yũuna fxi'zeni
9 Adxa' teeçx karta fxi'th kxtee pkhaakhesawe'sxtxi, nawa Diótrefxes yu' nwẽese'jme' kwe'sx we'weni's txãaçxáa jxkaahna ũs wẽeçxa. 10 Txãa pa'ga adxa' pa'jçxa txã'sa' pa'yaçxa we'we'nja, txã'wẽ yuwe susna kwe'sxtxi nuywe'wena u'jupa'ga. Txã'wẽçxáa yũumée, vxite ekaju yakhthẽ'jwe'sx pa'jsatx jxpa'ga'çme', sa' vxite jxpa'ga wẽesa yuutxi's ahçeçxa txãa pkhaakhenijũ kutxi'ji'k.
11 Yakhthẽ'j peeygãanisa, ewmeesa na'wẽy yu' fxi'zewa'jme', naasáa ewsa ma'wẽ fxi'zeçte', txã'wẽ yuuçxáa fxi'zewa'ja'. Ew yũuna fxi'zesa' Dxus yakh fxi'zesa'. Nawa ewmée yũusa', Dxus yakhmée fxi'zesaçxa txã'wẽ yuuna.
Demetrio ew fxi'zeni
12 Demetrio' wala ewsa' jĩnatx we'we' jxukaysa. Txã'wẽ ew fxi'zete', txajx ew fxi'zeni íiçxáa nwe'we'k. Aça' kwe'sxwa jiitha'w txã'wẽ ew fxi'zeni's. I'kwe'sxa' peekx jii i'kwe kwe'sx isa we'weni's.
Nmehte we'wena fxi'jni
13 Adxa' wala puutx we'wewa'j ji'pthu, nawa fxi'jçxa kajaçmeeth, 14 dud puutx uyçxa puutx we'we'nja sũhçxa.
15 Ayjxuwe'sx namiku idxa's wala yuwe weçxaa kaja'tx. Kxsũ namikuwe'sxtxiwa teeçxsa na'wẽçxah yuwe meen weçxa. Dxus yakh mfxi'ze.
