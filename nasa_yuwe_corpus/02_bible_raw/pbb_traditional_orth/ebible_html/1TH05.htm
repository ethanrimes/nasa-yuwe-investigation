5
1 Nawa bagaçx kĩi ente kĩiwa'thewa txãa yu'sa' pta'sxna fxi'jwa'j peejxme' yakhthẽ'jwe'sxtxi. 2 I'kwe'sxa' ew jii i'kwe Kristo' kĩh entewa kĩhçxáa yũuna yajkxmée ũsiyna, pesweewa ma'wẽga kus pxãh yaakxnimeete pa'ja' txã'wẽy. 3 Nasa we'weçxa': Kĩhwa yuumeena, jxuka ewçxáa ũstha'w jĩna ũsiyna, txãa dudte Dxus kastigãywa'j ena' pa'jana, u'y nasate ãça'açte ma'wẽ akasa pa'jakx txã'wẽy, aça' íyuya' ãjameetxna. 4 Nawa Kristo kĩiwa'ja's jiimée çxhi'dxte yu' ũsmeei'kwe, txãa pa'ga i'kwe'sx jxpa'yakxmée ũsiyna Kristo' kpajkxmeena peswée na'wẽ. 5 Txãa pa'gatey i'kwe'sx jxukaysa jiisa eena'tewe'sxi'kwe, kwe'sxa' jiimeesa çxhi'dxte fxi'zesa na'wẽsameetha'w. 6 Txãa pa'ga vxite nasa deesa na'wẽ fxi'zewa'jsameetha'w, wejxwa ma'wẽ fxi'zewa'ja'swa jxpa'yakx ũsuga. 7 Kusçxáa deenisa' aça' tũusa' kustewe'sxçxak tũ'. 8 Nawa kwe'sxa' eena'tewe'sxtha'w na' jxpa'yakx fxi'zega, txãa pa'gatey Dxusa's ũuste peçxkanamée yaakxwa'ja's jxpa'gaçxa peeygãawa'ja'swa ji'ptha'w, sa' ewte neeyũuwa'ja's ũuste ew jiitha'w naa pa'ga. 9 Dxusa' kwe'sxtxi' kastigãyya' sũhçxa nvxihtme', wejxwa Jesukristo nwe'wete ewte neeyũuwa'ja's ji'phukahnku txã'wẽ nvxiht. 10 Txãa pa'gak Jesukristo' kwe'sx pa'gate paa ya'dukhçxa uu, aça' ãçxha' ĩtxi mtha'w yuu meeçxa' uuníi mtha'w yuu nawa bagaçxte yuhwa txãa yakh txuteme'nja'w. 11 Txãa pa'ga nxusmée mfxi'zewe. Puutx teeçxsa na'wẽçxah kçxhãaçxha'jna na'wẽ yũuna ũsi'kwe txã'wẽçxáa meswe.
Dxus yakh fxi'zesatx Pablo yu'kxpehni
12 Yakhthẽ'jwe'sxtxi neewe'weçtha'w i'kwe'sxtxi yu'kxpehna: Dxus yakh fxi'zewa'ja's pu'çxsatxi' ew miipe'jwe. 13 Txã'wẽ pu'çxna ũspa'ga txãawe'sxtxi' wala weçxana mpeeygãhwe. Txã'wẽy vxitesa yakhwa puutx ewçxáa mfxi'zewe.
14 Na'wẽ yũuwa'ja'swa neewe'weçtha'w yakhthẽ'jwe'sxtxi: Mjĩiya' wa'lsatx myu'kxpehwe. Dxusna açxamée çxhãçxha yaakxsatxi' mekçxhãaçxha'jwe. Dxusna ew jiimeesatxwa mpu'çxwe. Jxukaysatx peeygãawa'ja's mji'phuwe.
15 Mpa'yajkxwe puutx ewmeeçxáa yũuna fxi'zemée, wejxwa i'kwe'sxa' puutx teeçxsa na'wẽçxah ew myũuwe naa kiwete jxukaysa yakh.
16 Bagaçxtewa weçxana wẽtçxáa mfxi'zewe. 17 Een isa Dxus yakh puutx we'wena mfxi'zewe. 18 Kĩh nxus en pa'thewa, Dxusa'sçxáa weçxana meswe, txãa pa'gatey Dxusa' txã'wẽk fxi'ze jxthãasu' i'kwe'sx Kristo Jesusa's jxpa'gasatx.
19 Dxus Espiritu's ivxiitunuwe. 20 Dxus kaaũusuthe'jni's pta'sxni's weeçxnuwe. 21 Wala jxpa'yakx pakwena atxahçxa ewsaçxá's mpa'gawe. 22 Kĩhwa ewmeesajũ yu' jxuka mtxuteewe.
23 Dxus ewte kaafxi'ze'jsa kĩh pkal yuhwa ji'phmée vxitna pa'jana i'kwe'sx kakwe ma'k yuu, i'kwe'sx ũus ma'k yuu, i'kwe'sx yaakxni ma'k yuu nawa jxuka, kxah Jesukristo kĩi ente yuuwemeesa yuune'kwe. 24 I'kwe'sxtxi pa'yasa' ĩsxiiwa'jsame' na' txã' kxtey yuuna.
Pablo yuwe weçxaa kaahni
25 I'kwe'sxa' Dxus yakh meen puutx we'wewe kwe'sx pa'gate.
26 Yakhthẽ'jwe'sx jxukaysatx meen weçxawe Dxusçxáana yajkxna.
27 Jesukristo jxkaathe', txã'wẽ i'kwe'sxtxi neewe'weçthu naa karta's lẽeykahn jxukaysa yakhthẽ'jwe'sx ksxavxyte.
28 Kwe'sxtxi jxpe'jsa Jesukristo' i'kwe'sxtxi pu'çxhina.
