13
Yu'kxpehna yuwe weçxaa kaahni
1 Naa yakha' tekh us i'kwe'sx tasxna pa'jya' yuuwa'ja's yaakiçthu. Kxtee pa'jçxa', kĩh yuwe's pheu'ju'ççxawa, jiisa e'z meeçxawa tekh txahçxa yuuwesa's kastigãywa'j yuuna. 2 I'kwe'sx yakh kxtee nyafxíi ũsçxa', jxpa'yakx fxi'zekahn dxiikhẽy we'weth pkalte fxi'zesatx maatxwa na'jĩna: I'kwe'sx tasxte ki'kin pa'jçxa', peltunaĩme'nja jĩni's ãçxha' ki' kaayaki'jiçthu nee jxu'the ũsíi. 3 I'kwe'sxa' adxa's jiyu wẽena ũsi'kwe Kristo kaahnisa dxihtewa, txã'sa' adx pa'the jiyune'kwe. I'kwe'sx ãhmée yũusatxi's Kristo' juuna'meekuẽ jxkaja'çme', txãasa txajx juuna' jxkaahni's i'kwe'sx ksxavxyte kaavxya'ja'jya' ũsa'. 4 Txã' naa kiwete çxhãçxhameekuẽ fxi'zesa na'wẽ yuh kluuste fxuçni uuk, nawa ĩtxĩ yuuk seena' çxhãçxha yuuwa'ja's Dxus ũste. Adxwa txãa na'wẽy naa kiwete çxhãçxhameekuẽ fxi'zeni's ji'phuçxawa, txãa yakh fxi'zeçxa', Dxus kçxhãaçxha'jnith fxi'ze' i'kwe'sxtxi yu'kxpehwa'jsa.
5 Txãa pa'ga ¿ma'wẽ ũskxtha'w? sũhna meswe, aan Dxus yakh fxi'zena ũsne'kwe. Ĩsxiisamée Dxus luuçx dxihçxa', ¿Jesukristo's ũuste ji'phkwe'? 6 Jesukristo's ũuste ji'phdxihçxa', adxa's ew jiyune'kwe, Dxus luuçxthu jĩna isath we'we' naa pa'ga. 7 Txãasath i'kwe'sx pa'gate Dxus yakh puutx we'we', kĩhwa ewmée yũuna fxi'zekahmen. Adxa' ĩsxiiçxa txã'jĩ'çmeeth, naasáa i'kwe'sx ewçxáa fxi'ze jxthãasçxath txã'jĩ'. Adxa' ĩsxiisa na'wẽ wá'ntka, nawa txãa yu' kĩh yuumeena. 8 Txãa pa'gatey isasa's kxtey nwẽese'jwa'jçxáa ji'pthu. 9 Dxus yakh i'kwe'sx çxhãçxha fxi'zeçte', adxa' weçxa'th. Adx yu' çxhãçxhameekuẽçxawa, Dxus yakh puutx we'wena nes yu'nja, i'kwe'sxa' Dxus yakh çxhãçxha fxi'zeya' ãjãkahn. 10 I'kwe'sx tasxna yuhmey, naa karta's fxi'jçxa yaçte kajaçthu, kxah adx pa'jçxa', i'kwe'sxtxi juuna' yuuwa'j ji'phume'nja. Txã'wẽ juuna' yũuwa'ja's Kristok adxa's neewe'we, i'kwe'sxa' Dxus yakh txutemée jweeíiçxáa çxhãçxha fxi'zewa'ja's ji'phukahn. I'kwe'sxtxi wejx yuuwa kçxhãaçxha'jnath txã'wẽ yũ'.
11 Ãçxha' yakhthẽ'jwe'sxtxi neewe'we'th ewçxáa fxi'zekahn. Kĩhtewa jxpubamée fxi'zewa'jçxá's mpaakwewe. Kvxiisxa'jni myũuwe. Kxteeçxah zhiçxkwe puiimée mfxi'zewe. Aça' ew kaafxi'ze'jsa Dxusa' i'kwe'sx yakh nes yuuna peeygãhna. 12 Ũusuh jxuka yajkxçxa puutx teeçxsa na'wẽçxah yuwe mweeçxawe. 13 Dxusa's jiisa yakhthẽ'jwe'sx jxukaysatx yuwe weçxaakaja'.
14 Dxus yakhçxáa mfxi'zewe. Jesukristo pu'çxhina, Dxus Espiritu yakh txutemée fxi'zekahn.
