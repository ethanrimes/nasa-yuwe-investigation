3
E'zsa Dxus tasxte mjĩisaçxáatha'w
1 Yakhthẽ'jwe'sx, makwe wala jiisa ne'kwe nawa Dxusa's ew jiisa yakh na'wẽ yu' we'weya' ãjameeth, Dxusa's kĩh yuhwa jiimeesa yakh na'wẽçxáath we'weya' ãja. Kristo kaapiya'jni yu's kĩh yuhwa jiimée luuçx le'çxkwe na'wẽçxáa i'kwe. 2 Luuçx le'çxkwesa's ma'wẽta leçxiçxáana puuçu' vxite ũ' yu's puuçmée txã'wẽy i'kwe'sxtxi' kaapiya'jçxawa thẽymeekuẽsaçxáanath kaapiya'jçxa nvxiht, txã'wẽ thẽysa yu'sa' ãjameei'kwe naa pa'ga sa' ãçxpkaçx jiyuya' ãja'çmeei'kwe. 3 Txãasa ãçxpkaçx i'kwe'sx ũuste Dxusa's yu' kĩh yuhwa ji'phmeei'kwe, txãasa i'kwe puutx yahçena puiina txuteeçxa fxi'ze'. Txã'wẽ yũunisa', Dxusa's ũuste ji'phmeesa fxi'zenisa'. 4 Txã'wẽ Adxa' Pablo yakh u'jsath jĩçpkaçxha' vxite' Apolos yakh u'jsath jĩna txã'wẽ yũuçxa', Dxusa's ¿ma'wẽçxáa yuhwa ũuste ji'phmeesa na'wẽ yuh meekwe wakx? 5 Ũus miiyajkxwe: Pablo' ¿kĩh yũusaana? Aça' Apoloswa ¿kĩh yũusaana? Kwe'sxa' naasáa Dxusna selpisaçxáatha'w, Dxus ma'wẽ jxkaathe' txã'wẽy mjĩitha'w teeçxsa na'wẽçxah, txãa pa'ga i'kwe Kristo's jiyuwa'ja's ji'phu. 6 Adxa' nyafxte uuhsaçxáath yu', naapkaçxha' Apolosa' yu'awsak yu'. Nawa Dxusku kbuuçxa'jçxa nuywala. 7 Txã'wẽ uuhsa vxite' yu'awsa txã'wẽ yũusa' kĩhwa jxtuhthesameeta', naasáa Dxus yuuçxáa jxtuhthesa', txã'wẽ kbuuçxa'jçxa nuywalapa'ga. 8 Uuhsa vxite' yu'awsa txãawe'sxa' maa yuhwa jxthaakwesamée, txãa na'wẽçxaçxáata'. Nawa dewewa'j yu'sa' txakweçxaçxáa yu'mée Dxusa' deweena, makwe mjĩite', txã'sçxáan deweena. 9 Dxus mjĩinite kwe'sxa' kxtee mjĩisaçxáatha'w, aça' i'kwe'sxa' Dxus mjĩini kiwe na'wẽsa i'kwe.
Txã'wẽy i'kwe'sxa' yat na'wẽsa i'kwe, aça' txãa yata's txahna pheu'jsa' Dxusa'. 10 Dxus jxkaathe adxa' yat txajxa' jiisa na'wẽ yat txaahwa'ja's kaaçxida'jsaçxáath. Aça' adx takhni kajkwete' vxite mjĩina ũsa' yata's. Nawa ma'wẽ mjĩiwa'ja'swa jxpa'yakx mjĩiwa'j ji'pha' teeçxsa na'wẽçxah. 11 Yat txaahwa'ja's takhna kaaçxida'jni ũsyã'ja' txã' Jesukristo', aça' kim yuhwa vxite fxiy kaaçxida'jwa'j ji'phmeeta'. 12 Na'wẽ kaaçxida'jni kajkwete kĩh yakhwa mjĩitxna: vxyuu beh, vxyuu çxihme, kwet zhiçxkwesa yakh meeçxawa fxtũu, çhĩçh, khĩh txã'wẽsa yakh. 13 Nawa Dxus kastigãywa'j ente ya'pdxi'puçxa ya'jiyuna ma'wẽ mjĩini'swa. Txãa ente' ipx yakh kĩijana, aça' txãa ipxa' kaajiyu'juna mjĩisa ma'wẽ mjĩini'swa. 14 Txãa kaaçxida'jni kajkwete mjĩisa' ew wáa mjĩina ipxte çxhavxywa'jmeesa yakh aça' txã'wẽ ew mjĩipa'ga dewení yuuna. 15 Nawa txãa yatte mjĩisa' ipxte çxhavxywa'jsa yakh wáa mjĩina aça' jxuka çxhavxyte', mjĩipa'ga dewenimée yuuna. Naasáa kxtee mjĩisa yuuçxáa ewte neeyũuna, txãa ipx walajũ ũphna kasehsa na'wẽ.
16 I'kwe'sxíi Dxus ũswa'j yati'kwe, naa pa'ga Dxus Espiritu i'kwe'sx ũuste u'pwa'jsate jiimeewá'kwe. 17 Dxus ũswa'j yata' kĩh pkal yuhwa ji'phmeesa na's maawa Dxus ũswa'j yata's ivxiitute', Dxusa' ivxiituna txã'wẽ yũusa's. Txãa Dxus ũswa'j yata' i'kwe'sxi'kwe.
18 I'kwe'sxa' maa yuhwa jxpubanuwe, wala jiisatha'w sũhna. Maawa naa kiwetewe'sxa's wala jiisa ũsçxa', jiimeekwesa na'wẽçxáa miiyajkxwe. Txã'wẽ yuuçxáa, wala jiisa yuuna pa'jane'kwe. 19 Naa kiwetewe'sxçxáana wala jiyuni', Dxus dxi'pte' kĩh yuhwa seelpimeesa'. Txajíik Dxus librute na'wẽ we'we': Wala yã'j jiitha'w sũhna fxi'zesatxi' ewmeete khẽewa'ja'sku vxitu' Dxusa', txã'wẽ yaakxpa'ga. 20 Txã'wẽy vxite ajkhẽwa na'wẽ fxi'jni ũsa': Wala jiitha'w sũhna fxi'zeni', kĩhte yuhwa seelpimeetewa jiia' Dxusa' jĩ'k. 21 Txãa pa'ga nasaçxáatx jxthaakwe iiwejçx iiwejçx yajkxna fxi'zewa'jme'. Txã'wẽ yaakxkahmeníi kĩhwa jxuka i'kwe'sx jĩiçxaçxáata'. 22 Pablo ma'k yuu, Apolos ma'k yuu, Pedro ma'k yuu, naa kiwe ma'k yuu, ĩtxi fxi'zenisa ma'k yuu, uuwa'j ma'k yuu, naa ensu fxi'zeni ma'k yuu, vxite ensu fxi'zewa'j ma'k yuu, nawa txã' jxuka i'kwe'sx jĩiçxaçxáata'. 23 Naapkaçxha' i'kwe'sx yu' Kristo jĩi i'kwe, aça' Kristo yu' Dxus jĩ'.
