15
Maawa Jesus yakh txutemée fxi'zewa'j
1 Adxíi ũ'tasx na'wẽsath, aça' adx Tata' txãa tasxa's pheu'jsa na'wẽsa'. 2 Ku'ta yũnsameetxi' spẽ'the'k, nawa ku'ta yũnsatxi' sũupi'jna pheu'ju'k jxthaakwe yũnkahn. 3 I'kwe'sxa' pheu'jniyã'j ũsi'kwe adx kaapiya'jni's nwẽese'pha'ga. 4 Adx yakh txutemée meswe, adxwa ma'wẽ i'kwe'sx yakh txutemée ũstka txã'wẽy. I'kwe'sxa' peekx jii i'kwe: ku'ta çxpiteçxa ũssa' yũnu'çme'. Txã'wẽy i'kwe'sxwa adx yakh txuteeçxa ũsçxa', Dxus dxi'pte selpiiya' ãjameene'kwe.
5 Adxa' ũ'tasx na'wẽsath aça' i'kwe'sxa' ũ'tasx ku'ta na'wẽsa i'kwe. Adx yakh txutemée ũsçxa', wala selpiine'kwe. Aça' adxwa i'kwe'sx yakh txutemée fxi'ze'nja. Adx yakhmée yu', i'kwe'sxa' kĩh yuhwa yũuya' ãjameene'kwe. 6 Aça' ũ'tasx ku'ta yũnsameetxi' ma'wẽga spẽethe'thçxa ya'kabu', txã'wẽy ya'yuuna adx tasxu txutesatxi'.
7 I'kwe'sxa' adx yakh txutemée fxi'zeçxa adx kaapiya'jni's peçxkanameeçxa', kĩhwa pẽyine'kwe, aça' ya'ũsuna. 8 Adx kaapiya'jni's nwẽese'jçxa' Dxus dxi'pte wala selpiiya' ãjane'kwe. Aça' txã'wẽ yũute', adx Tata's wala tuuthe'jni yuuna. 9 Adx Tata' adxa's wala peeygãja'k, aça' adxa' txã'wẽythu i'kwe'sxtxi peeygãja'. Aça' adx peeygãanijũ txutemée meswe. 10 Adx jxkaahnitx nwẽese'jçxa', adx peeygãaniteçxáa fxi'zene'kwe, adxwa ma'wẽtka Tata jxkaahnitx nwẽese'jçxa' txajx peeygãaniteçxáa fxi'ze' txã'wẽy.
11 Na'wẽth pta'sx kxah adx ma'wẽtka weçxweçxa yajkxna fxi'ze', i'kwe'sxwa txã'wẽy bagaçxwa weçxweçxa yajkxna fxi'zene'kwe. 12 Adxa' na'wẽth jxkaaja': adx ma'wẽtka i'kwe'sxtxi peeygãja', i'kwe'sxwa txã'wẽy puutx ya'peeygãhna mfxi'zewe. 13 Namiku's nwe'weya' sũhçxa peekx ĩtxi fxi'zeni's ivxiitunisaçxáa peeygãani yaase'. 14 I'kwe'sxa' isa adx namiku dxihçxa', adx jxkaahni's kxtey yuune'kwe. 15 Aça' txã'wẽ yũusatxi' peena yu' selpisáa jĩna kxsusme'nja. Selpisá' txajx patron kĩh yuuya'watewa jiiméek fxi'ze'. Nawa i'kwe'sxa' adx Tata we'wenitxi' jxuka jii i'kwe, txãa pa'ga adxna selpisáa i'kwe jĩna kxsusmée, adx namiku i'kwe jĩnaçxáa kxsuusu'nja. 16 I'kwe'sx khuẽ'y adxa's txhitxhmeei'kwe, wejxwa adxthu i'kwe'sxtxi txhitxh. Sa' i'kwe'sx mteewa u'jçxa Dxus dxi'pte selpiina fxi'zekahnthu neewe'we', aça' i'kwe'sx txã'wẽ Dxus dxi'pte selpiina u'juni' kxul yuumeena. Txã'wẽ yũusatxi' adx Tata' jxuka ũsuna kĩh pẽjxtewa, adxa's pi'kxçxa pẽjxte'. 17 Na'wẽ yũuwa'ja'sthu i'kwe'sxtxi jxkaaja': Puutx ya'peeygãhna mfxi'zewe.
Jesus txhitxnisatxi' naa kiwetewe'sx açeni
18 Naa kiwetewe'sxa' adxa's nyafxkhẽytxi ahçe na' i'kwe'sxtxiwa txã'wẽyçxáa açetxna, nawa nxusmée meswe. 19 I'kwe'sxa' naa kiwetewe'sxwaça', nasa' wedxitxna, txãawe'sx pwe'sx ma'wẽta wedxi' txã'wẽy. Nawa adxa' i'kwe'sxtxi txhitxthu naa kiwejũ, txãa pa'gatx naa kiwetewe'sxa' i'kwe'sxtxi açe', naa kiwetewe'sxmée yã'ji'kwe naa pa'ga. 20 Miiyajkxwe adx na'jĩna we'weni's: Maawa selpisá' txajx patronte jxthaakwesame' jĩni's. Adxa' i'kwe'sxtxi jxpe'jsath na's yuhwa ikhya' pakwe'tx na' i'kwe'sxtxiwa txã'wẽyçxáa ikhya' pakweetxna. Sa' adx kaapiya'jni's nwẽese'jsa', i'kwe'sx kaapiya'jni'swa nwẽese'jetxna. 21 Adx pa'gaçxáa i'kwe'sxtxi pkxuutxna, adxna kaahsa's jiyumeeçxa.
22 Dxus jxkaahni's adx pta'sxya' kĩhmeewaça', txãawe'sxa' yuuwemeesa yuutxna. Nawa ãçxha' adx pta'sxni's jiyutx na' yuuwemeetha'w jĩya' ãjameetxna. 23 Adxna açesa' adx Tatanawa açesata'. 24 Txãawe'sx ksxavxysu kim yuhwa vxit-ya' ãjanimeesa's adx kaavxya'ja'jmeewaça', yuuwemeesa yuutxna. Nawa makwe txã'wẽ adx ãjani's kaavxya'ja'the uyçxawa, adxna açe'tx. Aça' adxna açesayã'j yu' adx Tatanawa açesata'. 25 Nawa txãawe'sx leyte na'wẽ fxi'jni ũsa': adxna ahçetx kĩhwa yuuwemeetewa jĩ'k txã' kxtey yuukahntxi txã'wẽ yũ'.
26 Nawa i'kwe'sxtxi pu'çxwa'jsa Dxus Espiritu isa ew kaajiyu'jwa'jsa's adx Tata tasxu kaja'nja txã' adx kimtewa ew kaajiyu'juna. 27 Aça' i'kwe'sxwa txã'wẽy adx kimtewa pta'sxine'kwe, i'kwe'sxa' nyafxíi i'kwe adx yakh ũssa yu' naa pa'ga.
