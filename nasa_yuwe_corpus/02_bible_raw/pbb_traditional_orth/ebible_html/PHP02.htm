Kristo' ãhmeekwesa na'wẽ yuuçxawa thaakwe walasa yuuna pa'jni
1 I'kwe'sxtxi na'wẽth yu'kxpeje': Kristo peeygãanisa Dxus Espiritu's ũuste ji'phdxihçxa', txajx kvxiisxa'jni kçxhãaçxha'jni mfxi'zewe, 2 teeçxsa na'wẽçxah puutx ya'peeygãhna, teeçxsa na'wẽçxaçxáa yajkxna. Aça' txã'wẽ fxi'zeçta' jĩni's jiyuçxa', adxa' wala weçxa'nja.
3 Iiwejçx yajkxna peekx wẽt fxi'zewa'jçxá's yaakxwa'jme', wejxwa iiwejçx yajkxmée vxitesatx jxthaakwe tuhthesa' sũhna txãawe'sxtxi ewçxáa yũuna mfxi'zewe. 4 Txã'wẽ peekx wẽt fxi'zewa'jçxá's yajkxmée, wejxwa vxitesatx pu'çxna mfxi'zewe ew fxi'zewa'ja's txãawe'sxwa jiyukahn.
5 Kristo' na'wẽk yũuya' yajkx txã'sna i'kwe'sx mji'phuwe: 6 Kristo' Dxusíiçxáak yu', nawa Dxus txakwe seena' wala jxtuhthesath sũhwa'j yu'sa' ji'phumée, 7 txã'wẽ yaakxwa'ja's jxuka nvxihtçxa kwe'sx ãhmeekwesa na'wẽ yuuçxak selpiiya' yajkx. 8 Txãasak ãhmeekwesa na'wẽ yuuna pa'jçxa nxus nxus kluuste uuwa'ja'swa ji'phu, Dxusna nwẽese'jçxa. 9 Txã'wẽ yũupa'gak Dxusa' cielun pe'jna u'jçxa maatewa jxthaakwe tuhthesa vxitçxa ki'p jxkaahwa'jsa. 10 Aça' cielute ũssa vxite' naa kiwete ũssa vxite' mtee mteewe'sxwa jxuka Jesus tasxteçxaçxáa peejxũkweçxa 11 na'jĩtxna: Jesukristo yuuçxáa maatewa jxthaakwe jxkaahwa'jsa' jĩna Dxusa's weçxaatxna.
Dxusa's jiisa' naa kiwete eena' kweethna fxi'zewa'ja'
12 Adx yakhthẽ'jwe'sx yaakxnisatx na'wẽth yu'kxpeje': i'kwe'sx yakh adx kxtee ũsçxa kaapiya'jni's wa'lmée i'kwe jxuka kxtey nwẽese'je'. Txãasa ãçxha' jweeíiçxáa çxhãçxha Dxusa's yajkxna meswe, jxpubamée fxi'zewa'ja's pa'pçxuna, makwe adx yu' jxu'the ũstewa. 13 Txãa pa'gatey txã'wẽ ew yũuna fxi'zewa'ja's Dxusku kaaũusuthe'je', txajx jxthãasni's kxtey yuuna fxi'zekahn.
14 Kĩhtewa jxuka ewçxáa myũuwe nuywe'wena puiimée. 15 Txã'wẽ ewçxáa yũute', kim yuhwa i'kwe'sxtxi yuuwesa ewmeesata' jĩya' ãjãmeetxna, wejxwa Dxus luuçx yuh yu'ta' yuuwemeesa jĩnaçxáa we'wetxna, makwe naa kiwete nasa ewmeesa ksxavxyte ũstewa. Aça' i'kwe'sxa' eena' kweethsa na'wẽ mfxi'zewe txãawe'sx ksxavxyte, 16 Dxus yuwe ĩtxĩ kaafxi'ze'jwa'ja's mtee mteewa pta'sxna. Aça' naa kiwete Kristo kĩhte', adxa' kxulmeetka pa'pçxuna mjĩi sũhna wẽt wẽt yaaki'nja i'kwe'sx txã'wẽ ew yũuna fxi'zepa'ga. 17 Txãasath ũukhmée mjĩ', makwe adx ĩtxĩ fxi'zeni's ivxiituwa'j ma'k yuu i'kwe'sx pa'gate Dxusna selpiipa'ga, nawa txã'sa' ũukhmée weçxana kxtey yuuya' ũsthu. Txã'wẽ adx weçxani'sthu i'kwe'sx jxukaysatx pta'sxi', 18 adx na'wẽy ũukhmée weçxana ũskahn.
Timoteo vxite' Epafxrodito yakh kaahni
19 I'kwe'sx ma'wẽ fxi'zena ũstewa jiyuçxa weçxana ũsya', Dxus pu'çxte' Timoteo's dud kaja'nja i'kwe'sxtxi thegya' sũju'th. 20 Txã'wẽme', adx na'wẽy i'kwe'sx pa'gate pa'pçxusa vxite yu' ji'phmeeth. 21 Vxite yu' ũsçxawa, peekx wẽt fxi'zewa'jçxá'stxi yaaki', Kristo Jesus jxthãasni yu'sa' yajkxmée. 22 Nawa Timoteo' txã'wẽ yu'çme'. I'kwe'sxa' jiiyã'ji'kwe ma'wẽga wala selpíi Dxus yuwe's adx yakh pta'sx pu'çxna, peekx luuçx ma'wẽ neya's pu'çxhikx txã'wẽk yũu. 23 Txãa pa'ga adxa's kĩh yũuya'watewa jiyuna'wa, Timoteo's dud kaja'nja i'kwe'sxtxi pta'sxya'. 24 Meeçxa' adx yuu i'kwe'sxtxi thegya' yuhwaça', sũhçxa Dxusçxá'sthu neewe'we'.
25 Yakhthẽ'j Epafxrodito's i'kwe'sxa' kaahne'kwe adx peejinitx ktũ'se'jçxa aça' ayte ũsa', adxa's pu'çxna pxthaa yuuna. Aça' txã'swa kaja'nja sũjũ'th. 26 Txã'wẽ txajx ãçã'ni's i'kwe'sxwa jiyutx jĩni's wẽsẽ'jçxa', txã' i'kwe'sx yakh wala puutx uy wẽje'k, txãasak wala kuh yaaki'. 27 Isa ãçã'na weteçxa', uuwa'theçxáa yã'kh ũsu'. Nawa Dxusa' txã'sa' pu'çxku. Sa' txã'sçxáana pu'çxmée, adxnawa txã'wẽyku pu'çx, na'wẽ nxusna ũsthu txãate jxthaakwe nxussa pa'khamén. 28 Txãa pa'ga txã'sa' dud kaja'nja sũju'th, txãa yakh i'kwe'sx puutx uyçxa' weçxana ũskahn. Aça' i'kwe'sx weçxana ũste', adxwa nxusmée weçxana ũsu'nja. 29 Txãa yakhthẽ'ja's wala weçxana mpa'gawe, sa' txãa na'wẽysatxi's bagaçxtewa mpeeygãhwe. 30 Txãa yakhthẽ'ja' Kristo's selpiipa'gaçxáa txajx ĩtxĩ fxi'zeni's ivxiitu'dxijk, txã'wẽ i'kwe'sx yu' adxa's pu'çxya' ãjãmeetewa, txãa yu' adxa's selpiina ũsçxak txã'wẽ u'dxih.
