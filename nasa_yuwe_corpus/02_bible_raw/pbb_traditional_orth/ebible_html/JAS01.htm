SANTIAGO KARTA
1
Israel ji'j mteewa jxuka ũssatx Santiago fxi'jni
1 Israel nasa doce ji'jsa naa kiwete msuuwa jxuka ũssatx weçxana fxi'jaçthu Jesukristo vxite' Dxusna selpisáa Santiago yaasesa.
Dxus yakh sxiigmée fxi'zewa'ja's Dxus íiçxáak kaajiyu'ju'
2 Adx yakhthẽ'jwe'sx, nxusmée meswe weçxweçxa yajkxna ma'wẽsawa thẽysa vxyaatewa. 3 Mjiyuwe: Dxusa's jweeíiçxáa çxhãçxha yajkxya' piyakahníiçxáak txã'wẽ thẽysa' vxya'. 4 Aça' thẽysa vxyaatewa ũukhnuwe, txãa thẽysa' i'kwe'sx kĩhte yuhwa sxiigmée jxuka Dxus yakh çxhãçxha fxi'zewa'ja's kaapiya'jsa'.
5 Maawa Dxus yakh fxi'zewa'ja's sxiiguuççxa', Dxusa'síiçxáa mpẽjxwe, aça' txã' wa'lmée maatxwa jxuka kaajiyu'juna i'kwe'sx pẽynite thaakwe. 6 Nawa kuh jwed yajkxmée ũsyujuna sũhna yuuçxáa mpẽjxwe. Txã'wẽme', kuh jwed yaakxsa' tee jwedteçxáa ũsmée ĩkh wala's wejxaa kẽese'jni na'wẽ yuutxna. 7 Aça' txã'wẽ tee jwedçxáa yaakxsame' Dxusa' ũsuna sũhwa'j ji'phme'. 8 Maawa Dxusa' pasmée wáanakx sũhna e'z jwed yajkxna pẽysa' bagaçxte yuhwa Dxus yakhçxáa fxi'zeya' yu' ãjameena.
9 Maa yakhthẽ'jwa kĩhwa ji'phmeesaçxawa weçxweçxa yajkxna fxi'zewa'j ji'pha', Dxusa' pu'çxhina sũhna. 10 Aça' txã'wẽy ji'phsawa txajx ji'phuni pçuwa'j en pa'thewa, wẽtwẽt yaakxwa'j ji'pha', txãa pa'gatey naa kiwete ji'phsa' kitewa ma'wẽga txaçxmeyna udxi' txã'wẽysaçxá'. 11 Sek açxaate', jxũth tasxa' udxi'k, naapkaçxha' kitewa ubu'k, aça' zhiçxkwesa' kxtee pçu'k. Txã'wẽy naa kiwete ji'phsawa txajx ji'phuni yakh pçuwa'jsaçxá'.
Ewmée yũuya' yaakxnisa's Dxusa' ũsu'çme'
12 Ewmeete kweete'jwa'jsa vxyaatewa, txã'sa' nwẽese'jsame' wala yuh wẽtwẽt yaakxwa'j ji'pha'. Txãa pa'gatey txã'wẽ yũusa' bagaçxte yuhwa pçumée ĩtxi fxi'zewa'ja's Dxus ũsya' neeyũuk txã'sa' jxpa'gawa'jsa' Dxusna nwẽese'pha'ga. 13 Maawa ewmeete weteçxa', Dxus ne' txã'wẽte wetewa'ja's vxit sũhwa'jme'. Dxusa' ewmeete wetewa'ja's bagaçxte yuhwa ũsmeena. Txã' ewmée yũusame'. 14 Wejxwa peekx ũus ewmée yaakxni's nwẽese'pha'gaçxáatx ewmeete wetena pa'ja'. 15 Na'wẽ ewmée ũus yaakxnik pkalte kweete'je', aça' txãa pkalku jweeíiçxáa walaana u'jçxa nmehte' uuwa'jçxáa vxitna pa'ja'.
16 Yakhthẽ'jwe'sx mjiyuwe: 17 Na'wẽ Dxusa' cielute eena' kweethsatx jxuka vxitku sa' txã' ãçxpkaçx le'çxkweçxáa yuhwa yu'pthehmée ũsa', aça' txajx tasxuçxaçxáa yuusata' kĩhwa ewsa yu' jxuka. 18 Txãak kwe'sxtxi peeygãhçxa txajx yuwe isasa's pta'sxçxa ewte fxi'zewa'ja's ũs, kwe'sx nyafxte txãa yakh utxasáa yuukahn.
Dxus yuwe ma'wẽ jxkaathe' kxtey yuusaaçxáa Dxus yakh fxi'zesa'
19 Txãa pa'ga adx yakhthẽ'jwe'sx kĩhwa pta'sxni ewsa yu'sa' bagaçxtewa wa'lmée jxpa'gaya'çxáa meswe. Nawa we'wewa'j yu'sa' ũus yajkxçxa jxpa'yajkx mwe'wewe, sa' ũusçxawa'j yu'sa' wejx wala mpa'yajkxwe. 20 Txã'wẽme', ũusçxanisa' Dxus dxi'pte ewmeesa', aça' Dxusa' txã'wẽsa's jxpa'ga'çme'. 21 Txãa pa'ga kĩhwa ewmeesa nakwe wala pehnaya' duda' txãa yu'sa' jxuka nvxihtçxa, Dxus yuwe ewsa pta'sxni yuuk txã'sçxáana wa'lmée ũuste myu'akhwe. Dxus pta'sxni' i'kwe'sx ũusa's nwe'weya' ãjawa'jsa'.
22 Nawa Dxus yuwe ma'wẽ jxkaathe' txã'sa' kxtey myuuwe. Wẽse'jsaçxáa yu' yuunuwe. I'kwe'sxa' jxpubanuwe. Ĩ'neeçxáa wẽse'jnisa' kxulsa'. 23 Maawa txã'wẽ Dxus pta'sxni's kxtey yuumée ĩ'neeçxáa wẽse'jsa', peekx dxi'pa's speejute thegçxa 24 khũçxte uyçxawa txãa dudtey khukhmée u'jyã'jçxa' ma'wẽ peçxkanakx txã'wẽysa'. 25 Nawa wẽse'jçxa peçxkanamée, Dxus ley ewte nvxiit-sa jxkaahni's kxtey nwẽese'jsa', jxpubamée wẽt fxi'zena Dxus jxkaahni's nwẽese'jna.
26 Maawa Dxus dxi'pte ewsath sũhçxawa ma'wẽwa we'weni's jxpa'yakxmeeçxa', peekíiçxáa jxpubasáa yuuna. Aça' Dxus dxi'pte ew fxi'zesath sũhni' kxul yuh yuuna. 27 Dxus dxi'pte kĩhte yuhwa jxpubamée ew fxi'zesa' na'wẽ yũuwa'ja': Nmi' ji'phmée puuplesatx pu'çxna nxuste fxi'zesatx kvxiisxa'jna luuçx wakçxahtxwa txã'wẽy yũuwa'ja', sa' naa kiwete ewmeesa peekx ũuste khẽewe'sxte wala jxpa'yakxwa'ja'.
