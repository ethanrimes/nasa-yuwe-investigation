Nasa ma'wẽsatxwa txãa na'wẽçxaçxáa ew yũuwa'ja'
1 Adx yakhthẽ'jwe'sx i'kwe'sxa' Jesukristo txakwe ewsana ũuste jxpa'gayã'jçxa', nasa maatxwa puutx ja'daçxah txãa na'wẽçxaçxáa ew yũuwa'j ji'phi'kwe. 2 Meeçxawa na'wẽ wáa yuune'kwe: I'kwe'sx pkhaakheni yatte teeçx piçthẽ'j ropa wala ewsa athçxa, vxyuu bee sultxhikha paatçxa u'kana, naapkaçxha' vxitewa pa'jana kĩhwa ji'phmée ropa ĩisxkwesa athçxa. 3 Aça' txãa ropa ewsa ĩijxũusa's ew jxpa'gaçxa kaçxni ewsate kaçx kajane'kwe. Nawa kĩhwa ji'phmée pxthaakuẽesa's yu': Ayte myuju meeçxa' kxtee kiwete mkaaçx jĩne'kwe. 4 Txã'wẽ yũuçxa', puutx ja'daçxah txãa na'wẽçxaçxáa ew yũusamée, vxite yu'sa' açeyaakxsaçxa txã'wẽ yuune'kwe.
5 Adx yakhthẽ'jwe'sx ew mwẽese'jwe: Naa kiwete makwe kĩhwa ji'phmée pxthaakuẽesaçxawa txãawe'sxa' Dxusa's yu' ma'wẽçxáa yuhwa peçxkanamée ũuste yajkxna fxi'zeçxa Dxus jxkaahna ũsnitewe'sxa's Dxusíi ũsya' neeyũuk Dxusna yaakxsatxi' txã'sna jxpa'gawa'jsata'. 6 Txã'wẽtewa, i'kwe'sx yu' txãa ji'phmée pxthaakuẽesatxi' weeçxçxa ji'phsaçxáatx i'kwe ew we'we'. Nawa ji'phsaíiçxáa i'kwe'sxtxi pkxuuçxa ¿khuẽs tasxsu kuutxa'jsamée wáatxna? 7 Txãawe'sxíiçxáa Kristo yase's ¿açewe'wesamée wáatxna?
8 Dxus ley maatewa thaakwesa we'weçxa': Vxite nasatxwa peeygãawa'ja' idx ma'wẽga ya'peeygãja' txã'wẽyçxáa jĩ'k. Aça' txã'sa' kxtey nwẽese'jna fxi'zeçxa', ewsaçxah txã'wẽ yuune'kwe. 9 Nawa maatxwa jxuka txãa na'wẽçxaçxáa ew yũumeeçxa', pkalte weteene'kwe, sa' Dxus jxkaahni leya's yuuwesa yuune'kwe, leya's jxuka nwẽese'pha'gamée. 10 Maawa Dxus jxkaahni leya's ew yuh nwẽese'jçxawa teeçx ahte le'çxkuẽçxáawa jxpubaana aça' txãa pa'gaçxáa Dxus jxkaahni ley makwetewa jxukak yuuwesa yu'. 11 Miiyajkxwe wakx Dxus leya' na'wẽk we'we': Ptamsayã'ja' vxitesa yakh pkal yuuwa'jme' jĩ'k. Sa' nasanawa ikhwa'jme' jĩnak jxkaaja'. Txã'wẽ yã'the', nasa yu's ikhmeeçxawa, vxitesa yakh yu' pkal yuuwáana aça' txãa pa'gaçxáa Dxus jxkaahni ley makwetewa jxuka nwẽese'jsamée yuuna. 12 Txãa pa'ga i'kwe'sxa' we'wewa'j ma'k yuu, meeçxa' ũus yaakxni yakhçxáa ma'k yuu, nawa wala jxpa'yakxmeete', i'kwe'sxtxi nwe'wesa leya' kastigaĩna. 13 Vxitetx peeygãhmée fxi'zesatxi' txã'wẽy Dxuswa txãawe'sxtxi' peeygãhmée kastigaĩna. Nawa vxitetx peeygãhna fxi'zesatxi' Dxuswa peeygãhçxa kastigaĩmeena.
Dxusa's yaakxsath jĩsa' ãate kaavxya'ja'jwa'ja'
14 Adx yakhthẽ'jwe'sx miiyajkxwe: Maawa Dxus yakh fxi'zesath jĩçxawa ew yũuna fxi'zeni yakh kaavxya'ja'jmeete', txã'wẽ yuwejuçxáa we'weniçxáa ¿seelpimanx? 15 Na'wẽ ũus yaakiga: Maa yakhthẽ'jwa ropa ji'phmée txã'wẽy ũ'wa'jwa peejxsa ũsuna, 16 aça' txã'wẽsa's i'kwe'sxa' uyçxa': Pxthaaga yuuna fxi'ze'. Nawa ãçxha' wẽemée kumamée ew mfxi'ze jĩçxawa, txajx kakwete peejini's kĩhçxáawa kaatu'jmeeçxa yuwejuçxáa txã'jĩni' ¿kĩhçxá' selpiima'k? 17 Txã'wẽ Dxus yakh fxi'zesath jĩsa' ãate kaavxya'ja'jwa'ja'. Txã'wẽme', yuwejuçxáa txã'jĩnisa' kxul yuja'.
18 Nawa na'jĩwáatxna: Idxa' Dxus yakh fxi'zesaçxa', ¿ma'wẽ kaajiyu'juki'g Dxus yakh yuh fxi'zesan sũjkahn? Aça' kĩhwa yũumeeçxawa mkaajiyu'j aan ãjane'ga. Aça' adxa' ew yũuna pu'çxnisa yakhçxáa Dxus yakh fxi'zeni's ãate kaavxya'ja'jya' ãja'nja jĩtxna. 19 Idxa' teeçx Dxusçxáa cielute ũstewa txã'sa' ew jiine'ga ũuste. Txã'wẽ yũuni' ew yuja'. Nawa eçxthẽ'jwa Dxus ũste ew jiita' idx na'wẽy. Txã'wẽçxaíitx ũukhna yaya'. 20 ¿Ma'wẽtega jiyu'çmée? Yuwejuçxáa Dxus yakh fxi'zesath jĩni' kxul yuh yuuna, Dxus yakh fxi'zeni's ãate kaavxya'ja'jmeete yu'. 21 Kwe'sx yaçgathẽ'j Abrahana' Dxusna yaakxni's ãate kaavxya'ja'jna txajx nçxi'k Isaakha's altalte ikhya'wak yu'. Txã'wẽ Dxusna yaakxni's ãate kaavxya'ja'pha'ga, Abrahana's Dxusa' yuuwemeesak vxit. 22 Ãçxha' ¿jiyugá'? Abrahana' ãate kaavxya'ja'kh Dxusna yaakxni's sa' yuwejuçxáa Dxusna yaaki'th jĩmée, Dxusa's ũusuh jxuka yajkxçxa ãate kaavxya'ja'kh. 23 Abrahana' Dxusa's ũusuh jxuka yajkxçxa kxtey yuupa'ga, Dxus librute na'wẽk fxi'jni neeyũu: Abrahana' Dxus dxi'pte ewtek neeyũu jĩna. Sa' txã'wẽy Abrahana' Dxus namiku' jĩnik neeyũu.
24 Ãçxha' jiyui'kwe, yuwejuçxáa Dxusa's yaaki'th jĩmée, Dxusna yaakiççxa' ãate kaavxya'ja'jwa'ja'. 25 U'y kazx yuusáa Rahabwa Dxusna yaakxni's ãate kaavxya'ja'kh na'wẽ yũuna: Txajx yatte piçthẽ'j peethegu'na pa'jsatx jxpa'gaçxa', txãa piçthẽ'jtx nwe'weçxa vxitee dxi'jsuk kaah txãa u'ya'. Aça' txã'wẽ ew yũupa'ga txã'sa' Dxus dxi'pte ewtek neeyũu. 26 Kwe'sx kakwewa ma'wẽga ũus ji'phmeeçxa' uuníi yu', txã'wẽy Dxusna yaaki'th jĩnisa' seelpimeesa', ew yũuna pu'çxnisa yakh ãate kaavxya'ja'jmeete yu'.
