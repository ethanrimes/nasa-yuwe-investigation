10
Piisxáa kxuuphni's kaaja'da'jna pta'sxni
1 Isath we'we', maawa piisxáa ka'ga'jni vxitxtey u'kamée, vxite ahte vxitxtemée u'kasa' txã' pesweeta'. 2 Nawa vxitxte u'kasa' piisxáa-thegsaçxa txã'wẽ yuuna. 3 Aça' kxte vxitxte thegsa' vxitxa's phaade'k u'kakahn. Aça' piisxá' jiyu'tx jxpe'jsa we'weni's, txajũ' teeçxteeçxçxah kxsusna pa'yaçxa kxuuphnijũ kutxi'ji'k. 4 Sa' jxuka kasxisxçxa', piisxáa-thegsa' yaçtek u'jwe', aça' piisxa' txajx e'ste wa'lmeetx u'jwe', jxpe'jsa pa'yani's jiyu'ne'ta naa pa'ga. 5 Nawa nasa fxiysa pa'yate yu', u'jmée wejxwa ũphna u'jwe'tx, nasa fxiysa pa'yate jiyuçxatx txã'wẽ ũphu'.
6 Jesusa' naa ejemplo's pta'sxnak kaapiya'j, nawa nasa' jiyumeeta' txã'wẽ pta'sxni's.
Jesusa' ew jxpe'jsa'
7 Nasa jiyumeete', Jesusa' na'jĩnak ki'kin pta'sx:
—Isath we'we': Adxíi piisxáa u'kani vxitx na'wẽsath. 8 Adx pa'jmeyna yaçte pa'jsa' jxukay ĩsxiisa pesweeçxáatx yu'. Aça' piisxá' ũukhçxa utxaameeta'. 9 Adxíi vxitx na'wẽsath, aça' maawa adx tasxte u'kasa', nwe'weni yuutxna. Txajũyã'ja' ki' kasehn ki' u'kan txã'wẽ yuuna ũsçxawa, sã'jĩni nes yuutxna.
10 Ewmeesa' pesweena ikhna jxuka pebaya'çxáatx pa'ja', naapkaçxha' adxa' bagaçxte yuhwa pçumée ĩtxi fxi'zekahnthu yuwé'. 11 Adxa' piisxaatx ew jxpe'jsath. Aça' ew jxpe'jsa' piisxaatx peeygãhçxa nwe'wepa'gaçxáa txajx ĩtxi fxi'zeni's ivxiituya' ũsa'. 12 Nawa vxyuu pa'gaçxáa piisxaatx thegna ũssa' yu'kh eçx yuhte uyna'wa, piisxaatx thegmée ũphna u'jwe'tx, piisxáa-thegsa meena sa' piisxa' txãa jĩiwa meetxna, txãa pa'gatx txã'wẽ yũ'. Aça' txã'wẽ yũute', yu'kh eçxa' piisxaatx mazwa ikhe'k, naapkaçxha' ĩtxisa yu' ma'wẽwa ũphu'tx. 13 Txã'wẽ vxyuu pa'gaçxáa thegna ũsna, txãasane' ũphna u'jwe' piisxaatxi' thegmée.
14-15 Adxa' piisxaatx ew jxpe'jsath. Adx Tatawa ma'wẽ adxa's ew jiina aça' adxwa ew jiith Tata's, txã'wẽy adxwa piisxaatxi' ew jiith, aça' adx piisxaawa adxa's jiita'. Adx piisxaatx wedxçxa nwe'weya' adx ĩtxi fxi'zeni's ivxiituya'wath. 16 Txã'wẽy piisxáa vxite ji'pthu ayte kxuuphnimeesa txãatxwa nuypa'ja'nja. Aça' txãawe'sxwa adx pa'yani's nwẽese'jetxna. Txã'wẽ yũute', teeçx ahteçxaçxáa jxuka pkhakhni neeyũutxna teeçx jxpe'jsa yakhçxáa.
17 Txã'wẽ adx ĩtxi fxi'zeni's ya'ivxiituya'wath, nawa ĩtxi yuuwa'ja's ki' jxpa'gaya'wath, txãa pa'gak Tata' adxa's wedxi'. 18 Adx ĩtxi fxi'zeni's kim yuhwa kusa'jya' ãjameena. Adx yuuth peekíi ya'dukhe'. Sa' adx ĩtxi fxi'zeni's ya'dukhçxa uuya'wa ãja'nja sa' ki' ĩtxi yuuwa'ja'swa ki' jxpa'gaya' ãja'nja. Txã'wẽ yũukahnku adx Tata' adxa's kaah.
19 Txã'wẽ we'weni's judiuwe'sx wẽse'jçxa, kuh jwedtxi yajkxna neeyũu. 20 Maaíi yu' na'wẽtx ũusçxawe'we':
—¿Kĩjxa'kwe nwẽese'je' eçxthẽ'j yakh ũsçxa luuku we'weni's? jĩ'tx. 21 Naapkaçxha' vxite' na'jĩ'tx:
—Eçxthẽ'j yakh ũsçxa', kim yuhwa na'wẽ zhiçxkwe kaapiya'jya' ãjãmeena. Eçxthẽ'jçxa', txã'wẽ uyya' ãjasameetx kaauyu'jya' ¿ãjãma'k?
Judiuwe'sx Jesusa's açeni
22 Dxuus yata's txahna jxã'jni's anxu iisatx fxiesta'ja' Jerusalen çxhabte. Sa' txãa fxiestatetx ũsu' nus ente. 23 Naapkaçxha' Jesusa' dxuus yat pwa' Salomon jĩi jĩnisuk pekũhna u'ju'. 24 Aça' judiuwe'sxa' txajx tasxte pkhaakheçxa na'wẽtx pẽjx:
—¿Bagaçxpkaçx kwe'sxtxi kaypuba'jna ũsya'waga? Idxa' Dxus tasxu yuusa Kristoçxa', teehwed mpeeta'sx jĩte', 25 Jesusa' na'jĩnak pas:
—Pta'sxyã'th, nawa adx we'weni's jxpa'ga'çmeei'kwe. Adx Tata jxkaathe', na'wẽ kĩhwa mjĩ'th, aça' adx mjĩini' ãate kaajiyu'ju'tx adx kimtewa. 26 Adx piisxáa meei'kwe jĩ'th na', isa i'kwe'sxa' txã'wẽsa yuji'kwe. Txãasa i'kwe adxa's jxpa'gaya' wa'lu'. 27 Adx piisxaatx jiith, aça' txãawe'sxa' adx pa'yani'swa wa'lmée nwẽese'je'tx, sa' adx yakh u'jwe'tx. 28 Txãasath txãawe'sxtxi' pçumée ĩtxi fxi'zewa'ja's peesu'. Aça' kim yuhwa adx kusejũ' kusa'jmeena, sa' bagaçxte yuhwa vxitumeetxna. 29 Adx Tata maatewa jxthaakwesak adx kusete dukh, aça' kim yuhwa kusa'jya' ãjameena adx Tata kusejũ'. 30 Tata adx yakha' txãa na'wẽçxaçxáatha'w.
31 Txã'jĩte', judiuwe'sxa' Jesusa's ki' kwet wãatatajxa'watx yu'. 32 Aça' Jesusa' we'wek:
—Adx Tata pu'çxte i'kwe'sx dxi'pte mjĩi ewsaçxaçxáa wala kuh kaavxya'ja'th. Aça' adx mjĩi ewsa ¿maaga i'kwe'sxtxi ewmeena' thegu' açkwe adxa's kwet wãatatah wẽje'? jĩte', 33 judiuwe'sx pasçxa' na'jĩtx:
—Ew yũupa'ga txã'wẽ kwet wãatatajxa'wameetha'w. Nasaçxáag, naa kxahg Dxusthu jĩna Dxusa's weeçx we'we', txãa pa'ga txã'wẽ yũuya'watha'w.
34 Txajũ' Jesusa' na'wẽk we'we:
—I'kwe'sx leyte i'kwe'sxtxi we'weçxa': Dxus i'kwe ¿jĩ'çmeena'? 35 Jiitha'w Dxus librute fxi'jni's maawa weeçx we'wewa'jme'. Maawa Dxus yuwe's jxpa'gasayã'ja', Dxus i'kwe jĩnak we'we Dxusa'. 36 Nawa adxa' txã'wẽsaçxáameeth. Adxa' Dxus yuu txhitxhçxa naa kiwete kaahnisath, aça' adxa's ¿ma'wẽ Dxusa's weeçx we'we'k Dxus Nçxi'kthu jĩna jĩya' ãjaki'kwe? 37 Meeçxa' adx Tata mjĩi kaahnimeesaçxáatx wáa mjĩina ũsne'tka aça' adx we'wenitxi' jxpa'ganuwe. 38 Nawa adx Tata mjĩi kaahnisatx yuh mjĩite', adx yu's jxpa'gameeçxawa, adx we'weniçxáatxwa miipa'gawe. Txã'wẽ yũuçxaçxáa jiyune'kwe, adx Tata' adx yakh ũsa', aça' adxa' Tata yakh ũstewa.
39 Txã'jĩte', Jesusa's preesu'jçxa jxũna u'j wẽetx. Nawa preesu'jmeyna, kasehna u'kh.
40 Txajũ' Jesusa' Jordan yu'wala kuten u'jçxa', Juan nyafxíi bautisãyna ũsu'k kxteek ũsu'. 41 Aça' nasa wala kuh thegya' pa'jçxa', na'wẽtx we'we:
—Juana' kim yuhwa ãjanimeesatx kaavxya'ja'jmeeçxawa, pta'sxku naa piçthẽ'j pa'jçxa ma'wẽ yũuwa'ja'swa, aça' txã' ĩsxiinimée jxuka kxtey yuuk jĩ'tx.
42 Txajũ' kxtee ũssa nasa' wala kuhsa Jesus kimtewa jiyutx.
