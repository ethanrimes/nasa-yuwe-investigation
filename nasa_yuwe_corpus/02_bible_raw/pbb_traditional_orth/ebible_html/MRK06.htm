6
Jesusa' Nasaretna u'jni
(Mt 13.53-58; Lk 4.16-30)
1 Jesusa' txãa çxhabu kasehçxa', txajx u'pni çxhab Nasaretnak sxawedna u'j txãa yakh u'jusawe'sx yakh. 2 Sa' sapatu jxkaase ente' judiuwe'sx pkhaakhenitek kaapiya'jna ũsu', aça' nasa jxukaysa wẽsẽ'jçxa', kuh yajkxna na'jĩtx:
—Naa piçthẽ'ja' ¿mteega txakwe wala kĩjxa'wa piya? ¿Kim kaapiya'theki'k txakwe wala kĩjkĩh yũuya'wa ãjã'? 3 Naaíi kwe'sx jiyunisa karpintero' Maria nçxi'k. Sa' txajx nyakhtxiwa jiitha'w, Jakobo, Jose, Judas, Simon txãawe'sxta'. Aça' txajx npe'sxwe'sxwa naa çxhabtey u'pta'. Txã'wẽ kwe'sx jiyunisa' jĩçxatx Jesus kaapiya'jni's nwẽesẽ'jya' wa'l.
4 Txajũ' Jesusa' na'wẽk we'we txãa nasatx:
—Mteewe'sxwa Dxus yuwe pta'sxsa yu'sa' wa'lmeetx jxpa'ga'. Nawa peekx kiwetewe'sx yu' jxpa'ga'çmeeta' vxite' peekx nwe'sx, peekx yattewe'sx.
5 Txã'jĩçxa' kxteea' mazkuẽçxáa nuykatxhiçxaçxáa jwee yu' kĩhwa yũume'. 6 Sa' Jesusa' nxusnak neeyũu, txã'wẽ txajx kiwetewe'sx nwẽesẽ'jmeete.
Txajũ' Jesusa' vxite çxhabsuk pta'sxya' kaseh.
Docesatx Dxus yuwe's pta'sxya' kaahni
(Mt 10.5-15; Lk 9.1-6)
7 Sa' txãa yakh u'jusa docesatx pa'yaçxa', eçx luuku vxit-satx nasa ũusu kutxi'jya' ãjãwa'ja's ũsçxa', e'z e'zku kaah. 8 Nawa khãabuçxá's atçxa kĩh yuhwa tu'sméek u'khaah, ya'ja, vxyu, kaame txã'wẽsatx. 9 Sa' pelgatxiçxáa paatçxa teeçx kmiisa yakhçxáak u'khaah. 10 Sa' na'wẽ yũuwa'ja'sku pta'sx: Maa yatte paadejxa' pa'jyã'jçxa', kxteeçxáa paadehna ũsune'kwe txajũ ki' kasehna u'phkaçx. 11 Sa' mteeíi yu' i'kwe'sx pta'sxni's wẽsẽ'j wẽemeeçxa yat pki'jmeete', txãajũ kasejene'kwe i'kwe'sx çxidate kiwe tuhda's sakweekweçxa, kxah yuuwesa yuutxna. Isath we'we': Dxus kastigãywa'j en ãhte', txãawe'sxa' Sodoma Gomoçxa çxhabtewe'sxte jxthaakwe kastigo's knayuutxna txã'wẽ i'kwe'sxtxi jxpa'gaya' wa'lpa'ga.
12 Txã'wẽ Jesus jxkaahte', txãawe'sxa' kasehtx Dxus yuwe's pta'sxna, pkaltxi nvxihtçxa Dxus tasxte misxawedwe jĩna. 13 Sa' eçx luuku vxit-satxwa nasa ũusu kutxi'jna maazkuẽe ãçã'satxwa aceite dxikthekhẽ awna nuykatxhitx.
Juan Bautista uuníi
(Mt 14.1-12; Lk 9.7-9)
14 Txã'wẽ Jesus kĩjkĩhwa ew yũuni's ne'jwe'sx Herodes jiyuçxa':
—Juan Bautistane' uuçxawa ki' ĩtxĩ yuu, txãasane' kĩjkĩh yũuya'wa ãjã' jĩte', 15 vxite nasa we'weçxa':
—Naa piçthẽ'ja' Dxus yuwe pta'sxsa Eliasna jĩ'tx. Naapkaçxha' vxite we'weçxa':
—Txãniiteywa ma'wẽta Dxus yuwe pta'sxsa kaseje', txã'wẽysana jĩnatx ũsu'.
16 Nasa txã'wẽ we'wete', Herodeswa we'weçxa':
—Juan Bautista txhikha's na'wẽ kaaspẽ'the'th txãane' ki' ĩtxĩ yuuçxa kiite jĩk.
17 Herodesa' Juana's na'wẽ yuuçxaga kiikhe'j: Herodesa' Juana's kadenaju tudçxa karcelte ktxaaja'khu'ga, Herodias ahçeçxa ewmée yuwe ũspa'ga. Txãa Herodiasa' Fxelipe nyuuk yu', aça' Fxelipe yu' Herodes nyakhíiçxáak yu'. Nawa Herodesa' Fxelipe nyu Herodiasa's kusa'jçxa txajx nyu na'wẽk jxpe'j. 18 Txãa pa'ga ku'ga Juana' Herodesa's na'jĩna yu'kxpeh: Iidx yakhthẽ'j nyu's kusa'jçxa' iidx nyu na'wẽ jxpe'jni' ewme' jĩni's 19 Herodias jiyuçxa', Juana's ahçeçxa ikh wẽeçxawa ãjã'çmeeku'ga. 20 Juana's Herodias ikhya' ãjãkahmén, Herodesa' karcelteku'ga ktxaaja'j nwe'wena. Juana' Dxus dxi'pte kĩhte yuhwa jxpubamée wala ew fxi'zesatewa Herodesa' jiine' yu', txãasane' ũukhçxa nwe'we, sa' Juan pta'sxni's Herodesa' kxtey yuuçmeeçxawa wa'lmeeku'ga weçxana wẽsẽ'je'.
21 Herodesa' txajx upxni en anxu ãa iisak fxiesta vxitu'. Txãasak teeçx ente' fxiesta ki'p txajx jxkaahni npiiçthẽ'jsa vxite' Galileate jxtuhthesatx pi'kxçxa. Txãa fxiestatene' Herodiasa' Juana's ewmée yũuwa'ja's uy. 22 Txajũ' Herodias niisa' txãa fxiestate pa'jçxa' wala zhiçxkwe ku'jne' pi'kxnasa dxi'pte. Aça' ne'jwe'sx Herodes uyçxa' na'jĩku'ga txãa kna'sa's:
—Ijçxa kĩh pẽjxtewa, adxa' ũsu'nja. 23 Dxus uyte, içxa pẽyni's kĩhnawa ũsuçxá'nja, meeçxa' adx jxkaahna ũsni kiwe's pẽjxyuhtewa pxãhn txuhdeçxa ũsu'nja jĩte', 24 txãa kna'sa' kasehna u'jçxa, txajx njĩ'ja'sku'ga paapẽjx:
—¿Kĩh pẽyi'ki'th? jĩna. Aça' njĩ'j pasçxa':
—Juan Bautista dxikthea's mpẽyçxi jĩnaku'ga jxkaah. 25 Txajũ' txãa kna'sa' Herodes tasxte ki' kũhçxa':
—Ãçxhíi Juan Bautista dxikthea's plaatute akhçxa mah jĩte', 26 ne'jwe'sx Herodesa': Kĩh pẽjxtewa ũsu'nja jĩna nasaa dxi'pte jurãyyã'jçxa', ĩsxiiya' ãjãmeeku'ga. 27 Txajũ' Herodesa' dudtey teeçx soldau's kaakhu'ga Juan dxikthea's nikxya'. 28 Aça' txãa soldau' karcelna u'jçxa', Juan txhikha's spẽ'thçxa, plaatute akhçxa jxũna kũjku'ga txãa kna'sa's ũsya'. Aça' txãa yu' jxpa'gaçxa', txajx njĩ'ja'sku'ga dukh. 29 Txã'wẽ yũute', Juan yakh u'jusawe'sx jiyuçxa', txãa uuni kakwe's kiisçxa pedaaku'ta. Txã'wẽ yũuni'sne' Herodesa' yajkx.
Cinko mil nasatx Jesus sã'jĩni
(Mt 14.13-21; Lk 9.10-17; Jn 6.1-14)
30 Txã'wẽ e'z e'z jxkaahnisawe'sxa' Jesus tasxte ki' sxawedna pa'jçxa', txãawe'sx ma'wẽ kaapiya'jna kĩhçxa yũuçxawa Jesusa's jxuka pta'sxtxi. 31 Txã'wẽ nasa txakwe wala pa'jna ũste', txãawe'sxa' ũ'wa'j en yuhwa uyméetx fxi'zena ũsu'. Txãa pa'gak Jesus we'weçxa':
—Mejkawe, dudte jxkaaseya' nasaa yatmeesu jĩ'.
32 Txajũ' Jesusa' txãa yakh u'jusawe'sxçxáatx pe'jçxa, barkote khẽeçxa u'kh, nasaa yatmeesu pa'jçxa jxkaaseya'. 33 Txã'wẽ Jesus u'jweçte nasa wala uyçxa', mtee pa'jya' u'jweçtewa jiyutx. Txãasatx txãawe'sx khuẽ'y yaçte pa'j Jesus pa'jwa'the maa çxhabuwe'sxwa wuwuuçxáa u'jçxa. 34 Aça' Jesus barkoju kasejeççxa', uyku nasa wala kuh pkhaakhena ũste. Sa' txãawe'sxtxi's peeygãjk txã'wẽ piisxáa namu ji'phmeesa na'wẽ txãawe'sxçxáa ũste. Txajũ' Dxus yuwe's wala kaapiya'kh.
35 Kxtee kaapiya'jna ũsiyna kusuçte', txãa yakh u'jusawe'sxa' txajx tasxte utxaaçxa na'wẽtx we'we:
—Aysu nasaa yatmeete ũsiyna, kusuça'. 36 Aça' nasatxi' mkaah, kxah yat-su meeçxawa çxhabkuẽ utxasu ũsta' kxsũ pa'jçxa ũ'wa'j weyuutxna. Txã'wẽme' ayte' kĩhwa ji'phmeeta' jĩte', 37 Jesus pasçxa':
—I'kwe'sx mpuuçwe jĩnak jxkaah.
Aça' txãawe'sxa' na'wẽtx pas:
—Kwe'sxa' doscientos peso pan weyya' u'jçxa puuçuwá'nja'w jĩte', 38 Jesusa' pẽjxku:
—¿Maz pan ji'phkwe? Me'jwe thegya' jĩna.
Aça' pan uyçxa', pta'sxtxi:
—Tahç pan, e'z wedxçxáatha'w uy jĩna.
39 Txajũ' Jesusa' nasatxi' txaksũ kaçxkaakh fxiifxii txuteeçxa. 40 Aça' cien ãh vxite' cinkwenta kxaztxi fxiifxii txuteeçxa kaçx. 41 Naapkaçxha' Jesusa' tahç pan e'z wedxtxi uweçxa, cielute pagayçxa Dxusa's weçxana'wa pantxi pe'ltedeçxa txãa yakh u'jusawe'sxtxi' ũsku, nasatx jxuka ũsu'skahn. Sa' wedxtxiwa txã'wẽyku ũsu's jxukaysatx. 42 Txã'wẽ yũute', jxukaysa ũ'çxa txiktxi neeyũu. 43 Txajũ' txãa pan vxite' wedx pe'ltedenisa neeyũute' pkhakhçxa', doce kanastate utatx pkhakh. 44 Aça' nasa yu' piçthẽ'jçxáatx isani cinko mil ãhtx yu'.
Ĩkh kajkwega Jesus u'juni
(Mt 14.22-27; Jn 6.16-21)
45 Txajũ' Jesusa' txãa yakh u'jusawe'sxtxi barkote kaateka'jçxa yaçte kaakh Beçaidan. Txã'wẽ yũuçxa' txãa yu' kxtee neeyũuk nasa txuteena u'jsatx weçxana. 46 Sa' nasa jxuka txuteena u'jweçpkaçxha', Dxus yakh puutx we'weya' u'kh thã'the.
47 Jxuka çxhi'dxna jxã'jaçte', barko' ĩkh pxãjũy u'jweçku yu', Nawa Jesus yu', ĩkh kutetee ũsíiçxáak yu'. 48 Sa' khikhyna sẽjeçte', Jesusa' uyku Beçaidan u'jsawe'sxtxi's wejxa yupte, barko's kaajxa' çxhãçxhamée ĩkhsu u'jute.
Txajũ', Jesusa' txãawe'sxtxi kpajkxya' ĩkhsu u'jçxa, txãawe'sx pukasu utxa sẽhçxa, barko's phyahçna skhẽuuçku yu'. 49 Aça' barkote u'jsawe'sxa' txã'wẽ ĩkhsu u'jweçte uyçxa', eçxa'stha'w uy sũhçxa sustxi pebée. 50 Txã'sa' jxukaysatx uyçxa ũukh. Naapkaçxha' Jesusa' we'wek:
—Ũukhnuwe. Adxthu jĩna.
51 Txajũ' Jesusa' barkote khẽjeçpkaçxha', wejxa' yujuk. Aça' txãawe'sxa' iiméh wala ũukh yajkxna kuh we'wenatx neeyũu. 52 Txã'wẽ Dxus Nçxi'kçxa kĩjxa'wa ãjãwa'jsaçxak kuhmée pan yakh nasa txaazkuẽesatx sã'jĩ' sũhwa'j yu'sa' ji'phmeene'ta yu', txãasatx txakwe wala kuh yajkxna neeyũu.
Genesarettewe'sxtxi Jesus nuykatxhini
(Mt 14.34-36)
53 Txajũ' Genesaret kiwete pesahçxa', barko's ĩkh pukatetx tudçxa txah. 54 Aça' barkoju sa'jaçpkaçxha', txãa kiwesuwe'sx nasa' jiyutx Jesus pa'jni's. 55 Sa' txãa kiwesu ãçã'satx jxuka pkhakhçxa yakhna pa'jtx Jesus mtee ũstewa. 56 Txajũ' Jesusa' çxhabna ma'k u'j, meeçxa' yat-su ma'k pa'jya' u'j, meeçxa' dxi'the ma'k u'jweç yuu nawa txajx skhẽwwa'jsutx ãçã'satx khikhe'. Txã'wẽ yũuçxatx skhẽuuçte' wala pxthaa we'we', txajx atxh vxiçteçxáawa ãçã'satx khya'dxi'khan. Aça' maawa txajx atxh vxiçteçxáawa jxa'dxsa', jxuka katxhiçxatx neeyũ'.
