9
Yakhthẽ'jwe'sxtxi pu'çxwa'j vxyu pkhakhni
1 Dxus yakh fxi'zesa yakhthẽ'jwe'sxtxi pu'çxya' vxyuu pkhakhwa'ja's i'kwe'sxtxi kaayaki'jna fxi'jwa'j peejxmeena sũju'th, 2 txãa pa'gatey i'kwe'sxa' kĩhtewa wa'lsameetewa jiith. Txãasath Macedonia kiwesuwe'sxtxi'sa' weçxana na'jĩna pta'sx: Akayasuwe'sxa' jũ'na anxutey pu'çxya' ih ũsta' jĩth. Aça' Macedonia kiwesuwe'sx maazkuẽesawa i'kwe'sx pu'çxya' yaakxni's jiyuçxa', txãawe'sxwa pu'çxya' yajkxtxi. 3 Txã'wẽtewa naa yakhthẽ'jwe'sxtxi' neewe'weçthu, kxah adx we'weçxa': Korintotewe'sxa' pu'çxya' ih ũsta' jĩth txã' ĩsxiini yuumeena. I'kwe'sxa' kxtey yuukahnthu txã'jĩ'. 4 Txã'wẽme', Macedoniajũwe'sx maaíia' adx yakh ja'datey yuusa' i'kwe'sx kxtey yuumée ũsiyna kpaakitxna, aça' wala thaame yuhna i'kwe'sx txakwe wala pu'çxya' ũsta' jĩnisa txã'wẽ kxtey yuumeete'. 5 Txãa sũhçxath naa yakhthẽ'jwe'sxtxi yaçte kaajxa' yajkx i'kwe'sx tasxna, aça' txãawe'sx pa'jçxa' na'wẽ pu'çxwa'j pkhakhya' yajkx i'kwe txã'sa' pkhakh pu'çxna jxã'khan. Kxah i'kwe'sx wala pkhakhya' neeyũui'kwe txã'sa' ũçxhi'dxmée kãaja'jane'kwe, makwe peesweçxtewa.
6 I'kwe'sxa' peekx jii i'kwe: walamée mjĩite', ũ'wa wala meek kaseje'. Nawa wala mjĩite', ũ'wa txã'wẽy walak kaseje'. 7 Teeçxsa na'wẽçxah i'kwe'sx ũuste makwe peesweçxte', txã'sçxáa kxtey yuuwa'ja'. Wa'lna txã'wẽ yũuwa'jme', txãa pa'gatey Dxusa' peeygãja'k ũuste weçxana txã'wẽ peessa's. 8 Txã'wẽ yũute', Dxusa' peejimée ũsna nes yuuna i'kwe'sx peejini yu'sa' jxuka. Txajíiçxáa i'kwe'sxa' vxitesatxwa ũçxhi'dxmée wala pu'çxya' ãjãne'kwe. 9 Txã'sna we'weçxak Dxus librute na'jĩ':
Ji'phmeesatx wa'lmée weçxana pu'çxsa's txã'wẽ ew yũupa'ga,
Dxusa' peejxmée ũsna nes yuuna jĩ'k.
10 Uuhwa'j fxiwa's Dxusku ũsu', sa' txãaíiçxáak kbuuçxa'jna nuywalá'. Txãa pa'ga i'kwe'sxa' ũ'wa'ja's peejxmée jxuka uyçxa', vxitesatx peeswa'ja'swa wala uyuune'kwe. 11 Txã'wẽ yũute', i'kwe'sxa' kĩjkĩhwa ji'phsa yuuçxa wa'lmée pu'çxya' ãjane'kwe. Aça' i'kwe'sx ũçxhi'dxmée peesya' pkhakhni's kwe'sx jxũna u'the', jxpa'gasa yakhthẽ'jwe'sxa' Dxusa's weçxaatxna. 12 Na'wẽ yakhthẽ'jwe'sxtxi pu'çxwa'j kajaçtha'w txã' txãawe'sx peejiniçxá's selpiwa'jsame'. Txã'wẽ yũupa'ga txãawe'sxa' Dxusa's wala weçxakahn txã'wẽ pu'çxhiçtha'w. 13 Na'wẽ pu'çxnisa's txãawe'sxa' jxpa'gaçxa', Kristo jxkaahni's nwẽese'jsa yuh yu'ta sũhna jiyutxna, txãasa Dxusa's tuuthe'jna wala weçxaatxna, txã'wẽ i'kwe'sx wa'lmée peesya' pkhakhçxa txãawe'sxtxi peesna vxitesatxwa peespa'ga. 14 Dxus pu'çxte i'kwe'sxa' txã'wẽ ew yũupa'ga, txãawe'sxa' iiméh wala weçxana Dxus yakh puutx we'wetxna i'kwe'sx pa'gate. 15 Dxusa's iiméh wala weçxa'tha'w txajx Nçxi'ka's yuu kwe'sxtxi peespa'ga.
