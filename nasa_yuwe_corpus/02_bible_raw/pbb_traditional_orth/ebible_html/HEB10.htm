10
1 Moises leya' naasáa thaakwe ewsa vxyaaya' ũsu'tx txãatx kaajiyu'jsaçxáatx yu' txã' isa ewsa yã'j yu'çmeeta', txãasa na'wẽ Dxus tasxte utxaaya' aanxu isa tajkx ikhna nestxi yu' txãawe'sxtxi' pkal ji'phmeesa vxit-ya' bagaçxteçxáa yuhwa ãjawa'jsa meek yu' leya'. 2 Txãa leya' nasatx pkal ji'phmeesa vxit-ya' ãjasaawaça', na'wẽ pkalpa'ga maazkuẽe uswa tajkx ikhna nestxi yu' txã'wẽ yũuna nes yuuwa'j peejxmeene' yu'. Txãa pa'gatey pkalpa'ga tee dxi'j tajkx ikhçxa kabnijũyã'j yu', pkalsatha'w sũhwa'j peena yu' ũusuthemeena. 3 Nawa txã'wẽ yũupkaçxte, aanxu isa pkalsatha'w sũhna nesku ya'yũ', 4 txãa tajkx ikhnisa klaa ee vxite' kapla ee txã'wẽsa' pkaltxi khukhu'çmeeta' naa pa'ga.
5 Txãa pa'gak Kristo naa kiwete pa'jaççxa', na'jĩna we'we Dxusa's:
Tajkx ikhçxa kabni's jxpa'gameeçxa, adx kakwe yakh uuwa'ja'sgu vxit.
6 Pkaltxi khukhya' tajkx ikhçxa kabnisatx weçxa'çmeeg.
7 Txahth na'jĩ: Dxus, iidx jxthãasniçxá's kxtey yuuya' yuhth,
Adx fxi'zewa'ja's ma'wẽ fxi'jni ũsna txã'sa' kxtey yuuya' jĩk.
8 Na'wẽ Kristo we'weçxa': Pkaltxi khukhya' tajkx ikhçxa kabnisatxi's weçxa'çmeeg, (txã'wẽ yũuwa'ja's makwe ley jxkaahnisatewa), 9 txãa pa'ga: Dxus, iidx jxthãasniçxá's kxtey yuuya' yuhth jĩk Kristo'. Isa ew jiyute yu', naa tajkx ikhna ũsni's ivxiituçxa, txãa pa'gate vxite uuwa'jsath nvxiitu' jĩçxane' txã'jĩ'. 10 Jesukristo' Dxus jxthãasni's kxtey yuuçxa txajx kakwe's peekíi ya'dukhçxa kluuste uuk jxukaysa pa'gate tee dxi'jçxáa. Txãa pa'gak Dxusa' kwe'sxtxi's txãa jĩi vxit.
11 Pkalsawe'sx ju'gu we'we pu'çxsa judiuwe'sxa' txã'wẽ yuh een isa maazkuẽe uswa tajkx ikhçxa altalte kabna nestxi yu' jxkaasewa'jmée, makwe txã'wẽ yũunisa' bagaçxte yuhwa pkaltxi' khukhwa'jsameetewa. 12 Nawa Jesukristo' pkalpa'ga tee dxi'j yuuçxáa kluuste uuni yakhçxáa jxuka jxã'jnik yuu txãaçxáa bagaçxtewa selpiwa'jsa. Txãasak cielute Dxus pukate kaçx. 13 Sa' ãçxha' kxtee ũsa', txã'sna açesa jxukaysa txã'sçxaçxáana nwẽese'jwa'ja's Dxus vxitna jxã'phkaçx. 14 Txãa pa'gatey tee dxi'j kluuste uuníi yakhçxáa jxuka jxã'jnik yuu txajx nasa yuuwa'jsawe'sx jĩi bagaçxtewa selpiwa'jsa. 15 Txã'wẽ Kristo tee dxi'j kluuste uuniçxáa bagaçxtewa selpiwa'jsateíiçxáak Dxus Espirituwa na'jĩna we'we:
16 Txãa en skhẽwniijũ' na'wẽ yũuya' puutx we'we'nja jĩ'k Dxusa':
Adx jxkaahnitxi's txãawe'sx ũuste kxu'akhe'je'nja peçxkanakahmén.
17 Sa' txã'wẽ yũunijũ', txãawe'sx ewmée yũuna fxi'zeni pkaltxi's bagaçxteçxáa yuhwa yajkxme'nja jĩ'k.
18 Txã'wẽ pkaltxi's jxuka peltunãynijũyã'ja', peena yu' pkalpa'ga tajkx ikhçxa kabna ũswa'j peejxme'.
Dxus tasxte utxaawa'ja's ji'ptha'w
19 Txãa pa'ga, yakhthẽ'jwe'sx, ãçxha' kĩh pkal yuhwamée Dxus yuuçxáa ũsnite u'kawa'ja's ji'ptha'w, txã'wẽ Jesukristo' ee pkawçxa 20 Dxus tasxte u'kawa'j dxi'j u'sesa's phaadek. Kxtee Dxus ũsnite' kim yuhwa u'kakahmén txudenik ũsu', nawa txãa yuuk kluuste uuçxa kxtee txudeni's phaade kwe'sx u'kakahn. 21 Txãasa Jesusa' Dxus tasxte we'we pu'çxna ũsa' Dxus luuçx jxukaysa ju'gu. 22 Txãa pa'ga Kristo' pu'çxyujuna sũhna ũusuh jxuka yajkxçxa Dxus tasxte ũukhmée utxaaga, txãa pa'gatey Kristo' kwe'sx ũuste pkaltxi' jxuka yaatek, aça' kwe'sx kakwewa bautismote akhnik yuu, yuuwemeesatha'w sũhnaçxáa yaakxkahn. 23 Ãçxha' Dxus tasxte utxaawa'ja's jiiyã'tha'w na' Dxus yakh txutemée txãa yakh yuuçxáa çxhãçxha fxi'zega. Txãa pa'gatey Dxusa' kwe'sxtxi bagaçxte yuhwa pçumée ĩtxi fxi'zewa'ja's ũsya' puutx we'wek txã'sa' kxtey yuuna. 24 Sa' ew yũuna peeygãhna fxi'zewa'ja's ji'phuya' puutx ya'pu'çxhiga kxteeçxah. 25 Vxite na'wẽtx kwe'sx pkhaakheçte pa'ja'çmée txã'wẽ yũumée, wejxwa teeçxsa na'wẽçxah kxteeçxah puutx kçxhãaçxha'jna fxi'zega, na'wẽ Kristo kĩiwa'j enwa utxaaçyã'ja' naa pa'ga.
26 Txã'wẽ ewsa's ãate pta'sxni wala ew jiiçxa txã'sa' ahçena txute'nja'w, aça' txã'wẽ yũusa's peena yu' peltunãywa'jmée yuuna. 27 Naasáa iiméh seena' ũukhũukhsaçxáak ũythasu', Dxus kastigo ipx seena'sa jxuka ivxiituwa'jsa Dxusna açesawe'sxtxi. 28 Ma'wẽga Moises leyçxáa yuhwa txã'sna nwẽese'jsameetxi' e'z meeçxawa tekh nasa txã'wẽ yũuni's jiisatx pẽjxçxa isa dxihte', peeygãawa'jmée ikhwa'jçxá's vxitu'. 29 Aça' Dxus Nçxi'ka's ahçeçxa weeçxina, sa' txajx eea's pkawçxa uuni'swa kĩhte yuhwa seelpimeesa na'wẽ weeçxina, sa' txã'wẽy Dxus Espiritu peeygãasa'swa açesa', wejx seena' kastigãyni yu'nja'w sũhwa'ja's mji'phuwe, txã'wẽ Jesukristo eea' Dxus puutx we'weni's kxtey yuuwa'ja's vxitwa'jsa' txãa eeíiçxáa i'kwe'sx pkaltxiwa peltunãysa' na's txã'wẽ açepa'ga. 30 Dxusa' na'jĩnak we'we txã'sa' mpa'yajkxwe: Adxçxáa kastigãywa'jsath, txã'sa' adx jiine'tka jĩ'k. Sa' ki' we'weçxa': Adx jxpe'jni nasatxi's kastigaĩ'nja jĩ'k. 31 Txãa pa'ga seena' yuh ũukhũukha', Dxus ĩtxisa kusete kastigãywa'j wetena pa'the'.
32 Nawa txã'wẽ yũuya'mée i'kwe'sxa' nyafxiitey fxi'zeni's miiyajkxwe: nyafxçxah Dxus yuwe's jxpa'gaççxa', wala pxthaa yuui'kwe txakwe wala thẽysa pa'thewa. 33 Sa' i'kwe'sx maaíia' wẽesxuna nasaa ksxavxyte pãpani i'kwe yuu, aça' vxitewa txã'wẽy nxussawe'sx yakh ja'daçxah nxussa i'kwe yuu. 34 I'kwe'sxa' karcelte ũssawe'sx pa'gate peeygãawa'ja's ji'phui'kwe, sa' i'kwe'sx ji'phunitx kusa'jaçte', ũusaçxamée weçxanai'kwe neeyũu. Cielute bagaçxteçxáa yuhwa pçuwa'jsamée thaakwe ewsa's jiiçxai'kwe txã'wẽ yũu. 35 Txã'wẽsa's jiiyã'jçxa', peçxkananuwe, txãa pa'gatey txã'wẽçxáa yajkxna fxi'zepa'ga txã'wẽ seena' wala ewsa's jxpa'gawa'jsai'kwe. 36 Dxus jxthãasni's kxtey yuuya' nxuswa'ja's wa'lmée jxpa'gawa'j ji'phi'kwe. Txã'wẽ yũuçxa', Dxus peesni seena' wala ewsa's jxpa'gane'kwe. 37 Txã'wẽme', Dxus librute na'wẽk we'we':
Wejx jxthẽeyuumée dud yuh kĩijana
Txãa kĩiwa'jsa' txaçxhimeena.
38 Aça' Dxusçxáana yajkxna fxi'zesa' ewte neeyũuna.
Nawa txã'wẽ yũumée txuteete', txãa yakha' wẽt fxi'zeme'nja jĩ'k.
39 Nawa na'wẽ txuteeçxa vxituwa'the ũsta' txãatewe'sxmeetha'w kwe'sxa', wejxwa kwe'sx ũus vxitukahmén Dxusa'sçxáa yajkxna fxi'zesatewe'sxtha'w.
