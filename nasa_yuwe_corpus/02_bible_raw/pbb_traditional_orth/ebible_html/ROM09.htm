9
Dxus Israelwe'sxtxi txhitxhni
1 Adx ji'j judiuwe'sxtxi iiméh wala peeygãhna bagaçxtewa adx ũuste nxusna yuuçxáath fxi'ze'. 2 I'kwe'sx pa'gate adxa' Kristo yakh ũswa'ja's ivxiitute', i'kwe'sx jxukaysa ewte neeyũune'jwaça', adxa' peeygãaweete kxtey yu'nja sũju'th. 3 Adxa' i'kwe'sxtxi txã'wẽth wala peeygãja' jĩçxa' ĩsxiiçxa txã'jĩçmeeth, txãa pa'gatey Kristo yakh fxi'zesaçxa' Dxus Espiritu kaaũusuthe'jniçxá'sthu we'we'. 4 Adx ji'jwe'sxa' Dxus txhitxhnisata', naa pa'ga Dxus luuçxta'. Txãapa'gak Dxusa' txãawe'sx ksxavxyte pi'kxna ũsu' eena' kweethna. Sa' ma'wẽçxah pu'çxwa'ja'swa puutx we'wek. Leynawa ũsku. Dxuus yatte ma'wẽ yũuwa'ja'swa pta'sxku. Sa' kiwe's jxuka yamuwa'ja'swa puutx we'wek. 5 Txãawe'sxa' nyafxte Dxus txhitxhnisa Abrahan ji'tha', aça' txãawe'sx ji'thek Kristo upxhna ũsu' nasaa kakwe yakh. Txãa Kristo' Dxusíiçxáak yu', bagaçxtewa tuuthe'jni yuuwa'jsa.
6 Israelwe'sxtxi Dxusa' nwe'weya' puutx we'wek txã'sa' kxtey yuumée yu' yuume'. Israel ji'j judiuwe'sxa' maaíi yu' Dxus luuçx yu'çmeeta', txãa pa'gaçxáak maaíi yuutxi' kxtey yuumée. 7 Txã'wẽy Abrahan ji'jwa jxukay yu' Dxus txhitxhnisa yu'çmeeta', txajíik Dxusíiwa na'jĩna ãate pta'sx: Iidx nçxi'k Isaakte upxsaçxáa iidx ji'j adx txhitxhnisa yuutxna jĩ'k. 8 Aça' Abrahan ji'tha'w naa pa'ga Dxus txhitxhnisatha'w sũhwa'ja's ji'phmeeta' jxukay yu', naasáa Dxus puutx we'wenite upxsaçxáa Abrahan ji'tha'. 9 Txãa pa'gatey Dxusa' na'jĩnak Abrahan yakh puutx we'we: En ãhte', sxawedu'nja, aça' Sara' teeçx nçxi'k ji'phuna jĩk.
10 Txã'wẽ Isaak upxwa'ja'sçxáa we'wemée, Rebeka nçxi'k e'zsa upxwa'ja'swa we'wek. Txãawe'sx neya' Isaakku yu', kwe'sx yaçgawe'sx. 11 Sa' txãa e'z nçxi'ksa upxyuumeyna, Dxusa' txhitxhku txãawe'sx ma'wẽ yũuna fxi'zeni's uymeyçxawa. 12 Txãasak Dxusa' na'wẽ we'we Rebeka's: Nyakh yaçtewe'sxa' e'stewe'sxa's selpisáa yuuna jĩnak we'we. Ew jiyute yu', Dxusa' ew yũusaçxáatx txhitxhwa'jsa yu'çme', maatxwa txajx wedxni yuutxi' txhitxhwa'jsaçxáak yu' txã'wẽ yũuwa'ja's kaajiyu'jnak makwe judiu ji'thewa pxakh e'zíi yuutxi' txhitxhmée, teeçxsaçxáana txhitxh. 13 Aça' Dxus librutewa txã'wẽyçxáa we'wena fxi'jni ũsa': Jakoba's peeygãhth, nawa Esaua's peeygãhmeeth jĩ'k.
14 Dxusa' ¿ewmeesaçxa ma'k txã'wẽ ma'wẽsatxwa txhitxhi'? Ewmeesa yu'çme'. 15 Txã'wẽmeeçxaíik Moisesa's na'jĩna we'we: Adxa' ma'wẽsanawa peeygãjaçxá'nja, sa' txã'wẽy ma'wẽsanawa pu'çxhiçxá'nja jĩ'k. 16 Txã'wẽ kwe'sx kĩhçxáawa pa'pçxuçxa Dxus peeygãani's ya'jypa'ga'çme'. Kwe'sx kakweju kĩhwa pa'pçxumeetewa Dxusa' peeygãjaçxáak maatxwa. 17 Dxusa' ma'wẽsanawa peeygãjaçxáana, sa' txã'wẽy wa'lwa'jsa yu'sa' ma'wẽsanawa kxtee nvxiituçxáana. 18 Txãasaíi Egipto kiwete jxkaahsa's Dxus librute pta'sxi'k na'jĩna: Idxa's jxkaahwa'jsath vxit, adx kĩjxa'wa jxuka ãjani's iidxpa'ga nasa jiyukahn, sa' adxa's kimtewa naa kiwete jxuka jiyukahnthu idxa's txã'wẽ yũu jĩ'k.
19 Txã'jĩte', na'wẽ yaakine'ga: Dxusíi ne' ma'wẽ yũuwa'ja'swa vxitu', aça' kwe'sxa' yuuwemée ne'tka'w sũjune'ga. 20 Nawa ũus miiyajkx: Kwe'sx ma'wẽ yuuwa'jsatewa Dxusa' jiiçxane' txã'wẽ yũ', aça' idxa' txã'wẽ yaakxwa'j ji'phmeeg. Kiwe mitxha' ¿Kĩjxa'ga adxa's txã'wẽ umu'? jĩsaate jiimeetha'w. 21 Mitxh umsa' ma'wẽ mitxha's umweçxtewa txajx wedxni yu'sa' vxituçxáana, txãa kiwe's íiçxáa ewte selpiwa'jsa, vxite' ewmeete selpiwa'jsa.
22 Dxuswa nasatxi' txã'wẽyku vxit. Sa' maaíitxi's wa'lpa'ga kastigo seena'sate ũswa'jsa vxitçxawa, peeygãhçxa makwe txaçxwa ũythasku dud yu' kastigaĩmée. 23 Sa' vxite wa'lsameetxi's ẽe walate seena' zhiçxkwesa's jxpa'gawa'jsak vxit, txã'sna jxpa'gawa'jsatxi' nyafxíi enteyku txhitxh. Aça' kxteewe'sxa' kwe'sxtha'w txajx peeygãanisa. 24 Dxusa' maatxwa txhitxhwa'jsaçxá', txãasaíik maaíitxi' judiu ji'jtxwa txhitxh, sa' judiu ji'jmée vxite nasatxwa txhitxhku. 25 Txã'wẽ maatxwa txhitxhwa'ja's Oseas librute na'jĩnak we'we':
Adx txhitxhnimeesatewa, adx nasa vxitu'nja.
Sa' adx peeygãanisameetewa, adx peeygãanisa vxitu'nja jĩ'k.
26 Sa' nyafxíi we'weçxa': Adx luuçxmée i'kwe jĩçxawa, txãa e'su': Dxus ĩtxisa luuçx yuune'kwe jĩna we'wena jĩ'k.
27 Nawa Israelwe'sxtxi we'weçxa' na'jĩ'k Isaiasa': Israelwe'sx ji'ja' kwet muse na'wẽ seena' kuhsaçxawa, kuhméeçxáa ewte neeyũutxna. 28 Ewsa' kuhméeçxáa yuukahníi wa'lsatxi's dud ivxiituna Dxusa' jĩ'k. 29 Txã'wẽ yũuwa'ja's we'weçxak Isaiasa' na'jĩ':
Dxus kĩhtewa jxuka ãjasa judiu ji'jtx mazkuẽ nvxihtmeewaça',
Sodoma vxite' Gomoçxa çxhab na'wẽ jxukate vxituwa'jsa ne'tka'w yu' jĩ'k.
Yuuwemeesa yuuya' Kristoçxá's jxpa'gani
30 Ãçxha' na'wẽ yũuni's miiyajkxwe: Judiu ji'jmée vxite nasa' yuuwemeesa yuuwa'ja's pakwesameeçxawa, Kristo's ũuste wa'lmée jxpa'gasa yuupa'gaçxáa yuuwemeesatx yuuna pa'j. 31 Naapkaçxha' Israelwe'sxa' yuuwemeesa yuuna pa'jya' sũhçxa leya'sçxáatx kxtey yuuya' yajkx. Nawa leya'sçxáa yajkxçxa', yuuwemeesa yuuya' ãjameeta'. 32 ¿Kĩh yuuçxata ãjamée? Yuuwemeesa yuuya' Kristo's ũusuh jxuka yajkxçxa jxpa'gameeta' naa pa'gatx ãjamée. Kristo's ũusuh jxuka yaakipkaçxte, leya'sçxáatx yajkxna ũsu', txãasatx wala jxpuba. 33 Txã'wẽ jxpubawa'ja's Dxus librute na'wẽ pta'sxna fxi'jni':
Adxa' Jerusalen çxhabte kwet ki'puçthu, maaíitxi' kaypuba'jçxa pẽtxwa'jsa.
Nawa maawa txã'sa' jxpubasamée yu', nxuste neeyũumeetxna jĩ'k.
