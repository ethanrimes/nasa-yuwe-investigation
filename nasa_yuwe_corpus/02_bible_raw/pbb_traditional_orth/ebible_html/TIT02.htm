Dxus yuwe ma'wẽ jxkaathe' txã'sçxáana kaapiya'jwa'ja'
1 Nawa idxa' Dxus yuwe ma'wẽ jxkaathe', txã'wẽyçxáa kaapiya'jna mes. 2 Txã'wẽme' piçthẽ'j thẽ'jsawe'sxa' kĩhtewa jxuka jxpa'yakx fxi'zewa'ja's piyawa'j ji'phta', kĩhsuwa kxtey yuuna, ũus yajkxçxa jxpa'yakx we'wena, Dxus yuuçxá's ũusuh jxuka yajkxna, peeygãawa'ja's peçxkanamée, makwe thẽysa pa'thewa txãatxi' nxusmée fxi'zena txã'wẽ piyawa'j ji'phta'. 3 Txã'wẽy u'y thẽ'jsawe'sxwa peekíi wala jxpa'yakx zhiçxkwe fxi'zewa'j ji'phta'. Isameesaçxá's wẽse'jçxa nuywe'wena fxi'zemée, tũunawa fxi'zewa'jsameeta', ewsaçxá's kaapiya'jna fxi'zewa'jsata'. 4 Kxah kna'sa u'se iimi'sawa txãawe'sx nmi'tx peeygãhna fxi'zeya' piyaatxna, txã'wẽy peekx luuçxtxi peeygãhna, 5 peekíi wala jxpa'yakx kĩhtewa jxuka ewçxáa fxi'zena, txã'wẽy yattewa jxpa'yakx fxi'zena peekx nmi'tx nwẽese'jna, kxah kim yuhwa Dxus yuwe's açewe'wemeetxna.
6 Txã'wẽy piçtakxwe'sxtxiwa myu'kxpeh ew zhiçxkwe fxi'zekahn. 7 Sa' kĩhtewa jxuka idxa' ew mfxi'ze, kxah txãawe'sxwa iidx fxi'zeni's uyçxa', txã'wẽy yũukahn. Kaapiya'jni'swa le'çxkwe yuhwa fxiymée jxuka isasa yuuçxá's mpeeta'sx. 8 Ew yuuçxáa yu'kxpehna mfxi'ze, kxah kim yuhwa i'kwe'sxtxi açesa ewmée nuywe'wewa'ja's uymeeçxa', thaameetxna.
9 Selpisaatxwa myu'kxpeh, txãawe'sx patron jxkaahnitx kĩhtewa jxuka nwẽese'jna wa'lna ũusçxa pasmée ewçxáa yuukahn, 10 kĩh yuhnawa pesweemée ew yuuçxáa fxi'zekahn, kxah txãawe'sx ew fxi'zeni's uyçxa', nwe'wesa Dxus yuwe' wala zhiçxkwesa yu'ga jĩnaçxáa we'wetxna.
11 Dxusa' txajx peeygãani's kaavxya'ja'kh txã' maatxwa jxuka ewte nvxiitwa'jsa'. 12 Dxus peeygãani' ewmeesa's nvxihtna naa kiwete pkalte fxi'zewa'jçxá's yaakxni'swa nvxihtçxa, peekíi wala jxpa'yakx fxi'zena Dxus yakh txutemée txã's yuuçxáa selpiina fxi'zewa'ja'sku jxkaaja'. 13 Txã'wẽy Dxus peeygãani' kwe'sxtxi nwe'wesa Dxus Jesukristo jxtuhthesa kĩjxa' neeyũuk txãa en ãhpkaçx weçxana ũythasna ũswa'ja'sku jxkaaja'. 14 Txãa Jesukristo' kĩh ewmeejũwa kwe'sxtxi nwe'weçxa jxuka pkaltxi yaateçxa jxukate txãa jĩi vxit-ya' kwe'sx pa'gate uuk. Ewsa yuuçxá's jxuka kxtey yuu jxthãasçxak txã'wẽ yũu.
15 Txã'wẽ yũuwa'ja's kaapiya'jwa'j ji'phgu, kĩh yũuwa'ja'swa ũukhmée kçxhãaçxha'jna, ewmée yũukahmén yu'kxpehna. Idxa's kim yuhwa ahçena weeçxwa'j ji'phmeeta'.
