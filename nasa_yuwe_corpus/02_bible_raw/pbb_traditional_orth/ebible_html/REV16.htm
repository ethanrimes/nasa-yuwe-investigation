16
Siete kopa Dxus kastigo's ji'phsa
1 Txajũ' siete angeleswe'sxtxi na'jĩna sus we'weni wẽse'th Dxus ũsnijũ:
—Dxus kaahni kastigo seena'satx siete kopate ji'phi'kwe txãatx me'jwe pkawya' kiwete jĩte', 2 Dxus angel nyafxtewe'sxa' u'jçxa txãa kopatewe'sxa's kiwete pkawku. Aça' txã'wẽ yũute', nasa jxukaysa eçx seena' kazxsa vxite' txãa eçx na'wẽy piisani yuuk txãatx neewe'wesawe'sxtxi's kakwete wã'jy seena' akasa pa'kh.
3 Dxus angel e'stewe'sxa' txãa kopatewe'sxa's martek aw, aça' txãa mara' nasaa ee na'wẽçxaçxáak yuu jxuka. Txã'wẽ yũute', txãa marte ũssa ĩtxĩsa jxuka uutx.
4 Txajũ' Dxus angel tekhtewe'sxa' txãa kopatewe'sxa's yu'walasu vxite' yu'le'çxkuẽsu kxsũuk aw, aça' yu' ma'wẽsawa jxuka eeçxaçxáatx yuu. 5 Txajũ' Dxus angel yu'a's thegsa' na'jĩna we'weni'sthu wẽse'j: Dxus, nyafxíiwa idxgu yu' sa' ãçxwa txãaíiçxáag, Dxus atesa. Txãasag txã'wẽ kastigaĩ'. 6 Iidx yuwe's pta'sxpa'ga ahçeçxa idx yakh fxi'zesawe'sxtxi ee pkawtxi, txãa pa'gag txã'wẽ yũusatxi's ka'tuwa ee yusu'. Txãawe'sxa' kastigo's jxpa'gawa'j ji'ph yuhta' jĩ'k.
7 Naapkaçxha' Dxus altaluh na'jĩna vxite we'weni'swa wẽse'th:
—Dxus kĩhtewa jxuka ãjãsa idxa' peekxçxáa kastigaĩmée, isag kastigaĩ' jĩnik pthũuse.
8 Txajũ' Dxus angel pahztewe'sxa' txãa kopatewe'sxa's sektek aw. Txã'wẽ yũute', seka' nasatx iiméh wala açxa kweethkahnku txã'wẽ yuu. 9 Aça' jxukaysa iiméh seena' wala çxhavxytxi. Nawa pkala's nvxihtçxa Dxus tasxte yu' sxawedmeeta' txã'sna neewe'weya'. Txã'wẽ Dxusna yaakipkaçxte, jweeíiçxáatx txã'sna açewe'we' txã'wẽ seena' kastigãyya' ãjãsa's.
10 Txajũ' Dxus angel tahçtewe'sxa' txãa kopatewe'sxa's eçx seena' kazx ũukhũukhsa jxkaahna ũsnitek aw, aça' txãa jxkaahna ũsni kiwe' çxhi'dxteçxáak yuu. Txã'wẽ seena' yuute', nasa' ũusçxaniite thune's ya'wa'ki'tx kastigo txakwe seena'sa pa'pha'ga. 11 Makwe txã'wẽ yuutewa, txãawe'sx ewmée yũuna fxi'zeni yu'sa' weh yuhwa ivxiitu'çmeeta'. Txã'wẽ wã'jy seena' akasa pa'thewa, jweeíiçxáatx cielute Dxusa's açewe'we'.
12 Txajũ' Dxus angel seistewe'sxa' txãa kopatewe'sxa's Eufxrates yu'walatek aw. Aça' txã'wẽ yũute', txãa yu'wala' uhdxku jxkaahsawe'sx sek kãani ju'gu yuusa uykhẽwya' ãjãkahn.
13 Txajũ' na'wẽsath vxite uy: Eçxthẽ'j yuwejũ vxite' eçx seena' kazxsa Kristo's açewe'wesa yuwejũ vxite' eçx kazxsa Dxus yuwe pta'sxsa na'wẽ kĩhwa kaavxya'ja'jçxa kaypuba'jsa yuwejũ tekh eçxtxi kaseh sap na'wẽsa. 14-16 Txãa eçx sap na'wẽsa yu' kĩjkĩhwa seena'satx kaavxya'ja'jya' ãjã'tx, sa' txãawe'sxa' naa kiwete jxkaahsa jxukaysatx pkhakhya' u'jtx, nmeh ente Dxus txakwe çxhãçxhasa yakh puiiya' sũhçxa. Sa' jxkaahsawe'sx jxukaysatx pkhakhtxi hebreo yuwete Armagedón jĩ'tx teeçx ahte kxtee. Kristo we'weçxa': Yajkxmée ũsiyna, peswée na'wẽçxáa pa'ja'nja, aça' txãa ente thaamena ũsya'mée, Dxus yakh txutemée fxi'zesa' wala yuh wẽtwẽt neeyũutxna jĩ'k.
17 Txajũ' Dxus angel sietetewe'sxa' txãa kopatewe'sxa's ẽesuk pkaw, naapkaçxha' Dxus ũsni cielujũ na'jĩnisak sus we'weni pthũuse txajx jxkaahna ũsnijũ:
—Na' Dxus kastigo nmehtewe'sxa' jĩ'k.
18 Txajũ' kpi'sx kwehnena we'weni seena' susku, aça' ehd iiméh seena' çxhãçxha u'kh, naa kiwe yuuiyna bagaçxte yuhwa txã'wẽ yu' ehd çxhãçxha u'jweçmeene'. 19 Txã'wẽ ehd u'pha'ga naa kiwete çxhab jxuka pçuutx. Aça' çxhab iiméh seena' walasawa tekh ahsutx upe. Aça' Dxusa' yajkxku Babilonia çxhab wala's jxukate kastigãyya', seena' ewmée yũupa'ga. 20 Txajũ' mar pxãhsu kiwe u'psa vxite' vxiç wala jxuka vxitutx. 21 Naapkaçxha' cielujũ kwetuba nasaa kajkwekhẽ ũsxitx seena' walasa pahz açxoba ãh duhsa. Aça' txã'wẽ seena' yũute', nasa yu' jweeíiçxáatx Dxusa's açewe'we', txã'wẽ kastigo seena'sa's kaapha'ga.
