Jesukristo jxkaahnisawe'sx mjĩini
1 Txã'wẽyã'the', kwe'sxtxi' naa sũhnaçxáa mfxi'zewe: Kwe'sx ewte neeyũuwa'ja's Dxusa' txãniitey pheu'jçxa ji'phu'k kim yuhwa jiyunimeesa txã'sna pta'sxkahn neewe'weçxa nvxihtte Kristo tasxte selpisaatxna sũhnaçxáa mfxi'zewe. 2 Txã'wẽ mjĩiwa'ja's jxpa'gasa' ma'wẽ mjĩiwa'ja'swa jxuka kxtey yuuwa'j ji'pha'. 3 Adx mjĩini's i'kwe'sxa' ma'wẽwa nuywe'wene'kwe. Nawa i'kwe'sx we'wenitxi' adxa' le'çxkweçxáa yuhwa yaakxwa'j ji'phmeeth. Adxíiçxáa yuhwa adx mjĩini' ewa' sũju'çmeeth. 4 Nawa adx ũuste yu' ewme' sũhna yu'çme'. Aça' adxa' ew mjĩisath jĩçxa txã'wẽ we'weçmeeth. Jesukristo yuuçxáa adx ma'wẽ mjĩisatewa we'wewa'j ji'pha'. 5 Txãa pa'ga mjĩini's we'wewa'j en ãhmeyna yu', i'kwe'sxa' nuywe'wenuwe. Mũythaswe Jesukristo kĩhpkaçx. Txãa yuuçxáa ew ma'k yuu ewmée ma'k yuu nawa kaajiyu'juna makwe jiyunimeesa ũusteçxáa yaakxnisa sũthni yuu ma'k nawa jxuka. Txã'wẽ yũunijũ ya'jiyuna nasaa iisa ma'wẽ yũusaaçxawa.
6 Yakhthẽ'jwe'sx, txã'wẽ nuywe'wena fxi'zeni'sthu yu'kxpeje', Apolos fxi'zeni vxite' adx fxi'zeni txãatx thegçxa txã'wẽy fxi'zekahn. Txã'wẽ yuuçxáa, i'kwe'sxa' jxpubamée fxi'zene'kwe Dxus yuwe ma'wẽ jxkaathe' txã'sçxáa kxtey yuuna. Sa' kim yuhwa txã'wẽ teeçxsaçxá's weeçx yajkxna wedxçxa', vxite yu's ahçena fxi'zemeene'kwe. 7 Idxa's ¿kimga jxthaakwe walasa vxit? Txã'wẽ Dxus peesnimeesa ¿kĩhçxá' ji'phga'? Txã'wẽ kĩhwa jxuka Dxus peesniçxá's ji'phçxa', ¿ma'wẽçxaga iiwejçx yaaki', idxçxáa pakweçxa uysáa na'wẽ?
8 I'kwe'sxa' kĩhwa peejxmée ji'phsa na'wẽ yã'ji'kwe yaaki'. Kwe'sx yuutxi' e'skhẽ nvxihtçxa, jxkaahsa na'wẽ i'kwe yaaki'. Wejxwa jxkaahsaíi yuuwaça' ewna, kxah i'kwe'sx jxkaahnite kwe'sxwa ih ũsya' ãja'nja'w sũju'th. 9 Kwe'sxa' Jesus jxkaahnisatha'w nawa Dxusa' nmejkhẽ ne' nvxiht sũju'th, naasáa ikhwa'j nvxiitnisa na'wẽçxáatha'w. Kwe'sx pxthaa yuuçxa uuçte', angeleswe'sx vxite' nasawa thegkahn nasa walaa ksxavxyte nvxiitni na'wẽ ũstha'w. 10 Kristo kaapiya'jni's pta'sxpa'gaçxáa kwe'sxtxi' kĩhtewa jiimeesa luuku we'wesata' jĩ'tx, naapkaçxha' i'kwe'sx yu' Kristo kaapiya'jni's sxiigmée jxuka jiisa na'wẽ i'kwe yaaki'. Kwe'sxa' ãhmeekwesatha'w, naapkaçxha' i'kwe'sx yu' jxtuhthesa i'kwe. Kwe'sxtxi' açe'tx nasa', nawa i'kwe'sx yuutxi' peeygãja'tx. 11 Ãçxpkaçx pxthaa yuunatha'w fxi'ze', nasa ewmée nuywe'weni, wẽena, yũ'wẽena, athwa'j ropa ji'phmée, yatwa ji'phmeetha'w, 12 sa' kuseju mjĩina wahtxnatha'w fxi'ze'. Nasa' kwe'sxtxi wẽesxutewa, ewçxáatha'w pasu'. Kwe'sxtxi pkxuuna pãpaçtewa, wantãyçxa iideewe'çmeetha'w. 13 Kwe'sxtxi açewe'weçtewa, ka'tu' peeygãhnatha'w yu'kxpeje'. Naa kiwete kxtã' seelpimeesa na'wẽtha'w fxi'ze', kazxsa's wãataani na'wẽtha'w yahçena fxi'ze' ãçxpkaçx.
14 I'kwe'sx thaamekahn txã'wẽ fxi'jaçmeeth, wejxwa adx luuçx wedxnisa na'wẽ yu'kxpehnaçxáath fxi'ja'. 15 Kristo jxkaahni's kaapiya'jsa diez mil ma'kwe ji'ph yuu, nawa ney na'wẽsa yu' kuhmée ji'phi'kwe. Txã'wẽ Kristo Jesus yakh fxi'zewa'ja's kaapiya'pha'ga i'kwe'sx ney na'wẽsa' adxthu. 16 Txã'wẽth i'kwe'sxtxi neewe'we', adx na'wẽy fxi'zekahn.
17 Txãasa kajaçthu Timoteo adx nçxi'k wedxni na'wẽsa Kristo yakh txutemée fxi'zesa's. Txã' i'kwe'sxtxi kaayaki'jina adx ma'wẽ ne'tka Kristo yakh fxi'ze txã'sna. Sa' txã'wẽy mteeçxah Dxusa's jiisa pkhaakhena ũstxna kxsũ pekũhçxa', ma'wẽ ne'tka kaapiya'ja' txã'snaíiçxáa kaayaki'jina. 18 I'kwe'sx maaíi yu' wala çxhãçxha iiwejçx yajkxna ũsi'kwe, Pablo' bagaçx yuhwa pa'jmeena jĩna. 19 Nawa Dxus ma'wẽ jxthãaste', dud yuwe'nja puutx uyya', txã'wẽ iiwejçx we'wena ũssa kĩh ma'tx yuu aan çxhãçxha we'wena ũsçxa', txã'wẽy Dxus yakhwa çxhãçxha fxi'zena ũstxna. 20 Dxus yakh fxi'zenisa' yuwejuçxáa iiwejçx we'wena fxi'zenimeesa', wejxwa Dxus ma'wẽ jxkaathe', kxtey yuuna fxi'zewa'ja'. 21 I'kwe'sxtxi adx thegya' pa'jçxa' ¿ma'wẽkwe yuu jxthãasu'? ¿Peçxuukwe jxthãasukwe', meeçxa' peeygãhnakwe pa'j jxthãasu'á'?
