5
Ji'phsawe'sx mpa'yajkxwe
1 Ãçxha' wala ji'phsawe'sx mwẽese'jwe: I'kwe'sx ji'phuni pçuute seena' yuh nxussa pa'jana txã'sna yajkxçxa weyna mpebeewe. 2 I'kwe'sx ji'phunisa' kĩh yuhwa seelpime' jxuka wa'wani', aça' i'kwe'sx ropa ewsawa txã'wẽy jxuka wes ũ'ni'. 3 I'kwe'sx vxyuu beh vxyuu çxihmewa jxuka iz ũ'ni', aça' txãa vxyu iz ũ'nisaíiçxáa i'kwe'sxtxi' seena' wala ewmée nxus kiwete akhwa'jsa'. Txãa vxyu' i'kwe'sxtxi ivxiituya'wa' ipx na'wẽ. Naa nmeh ensu' i'kwe'sx ji'phuni's iiméh wala jxaaw i'kwe. 4 I'kwe'sx uuthasxte mjĩisa yuul pẽyni's Dxus cielute jxkaahna ũssa' wẽse'kh, aça' i'kwe'sx piuntxi yuulunisa txã'wẽ i'kwe'sxtxi ewmeete nvxiht-ya'wa'. 5 I'kwe'sxa' naa kiwete ji'phunisa yakh wala wẽt weçxana fxi'ze'i'kwe tajkx na'wẽ niisxna txãa pa'ga ikhwa'j en pa'jya'wa'. 6 Nasa ewmeesata' jĩçxa i'kwe'sxa' ewteçxah ikhe'i'kwe yuuwemeesatewa, aça' txãawe'sxa' kxulteçxáa uuwa'ja's ji'phu'tx.
Thẽysa pa'thewa nxusmée fxi'zeni, txã'wẽy ãça'sa pa'gatewa Dxus yakh puutx we'wena
7 Txã'wẽ pkxuuçtewa, yakhthẽ'jwe'sx nxusmée çxhãçxha miiyajkxwe, kwe'sxtxi jxpe'jsa Jesukristo naa kiwete kĩhpkaçx. Ma'wẽga mjĩisawa uhçxa' kuçxwa'jmée ũythasu', nus en skhẽwte', ũ'a's wala pkhakhe'nja sũhna. 8 I'kwe'sxwa txã'wẽy kuçx yajkxmée çxhãçxha yajkxna mũythaswe. Txã'wẽme', kwe'sxtxi jxpe'jsa Jesukristo kĩiwa'ja' utxaaça'. 9 Yakhthẽ'jwe'sx, puutx ũusçxawe'wena fxi'zenuwe, Dxusa' i'kwe'sxtxi ewmeete nvxiitkahmen. Txã'wẽme', yuwee ku'le'sa khuẽs pa'jwa'j en utxa yã'j ũsa'. Txã' Dxusa'. 10 Txãniitey Dxus yuwe's pta'sxsawe'sxwa ma'wẽta pxthaa yuuna fxi'zeçxawa Dxusçxáana yajkxna fxi'ze', txã'wẽy thẽysa nxuswa'j vxyaatewa, Dxusçxáana yajkxna mfxi'zewe yakhthẽ'jwe'sx. 11 Txã'wẽ pxthaa nxuste fxi'zesatxi' Dxus yakh wẽt fxi'zesata' jĩ'tha'w. Job pxthaa nxuste wantãyna fxi'zete', nmehte' Dxusa' wala peeygãhçxa pu'çxni's iiwẽse'ji'kwe i'kwe'sxa'. Dxusa' peeygãasa', txãasa pu'çxya' ũsa'.
12 Adx yakhthẽ'jwe'sx, wala mpa'yajkxwe. Isasa's jiiçxa' jiith jĩwa'jçxá', sa' jiimeeçxa' jiimeeth jĩwa'jçxá'. Txã'wẽ Dxus uyte jiimeeth jĩwa'jme', sa' cielu kiwe txã'wẽsatxwa kxsusna pta'sxwa'jme', Dxusa' ewmeete nvxiitkahmen.
13 I'kwe'sx pwe'sx maawa nxuste fxi'zena ũsçxa', Dxusçxáana neewe'wena meswe. Maawa wẽt fxi'zena ũsçxa', Dxusa's weçxana memna meswe. 14 Maawa akate uuya' ũsçxa', ancianowe'sxtxi mpa'yawe, aça' txãawe'sxa' Dxus kusete neewe'weçxa aceite ki'putxna. 15 Txã'wẽ Dxusa' pu'çxhina sũhçxa oraĩte', ãça'sa pkala' peltunãyni yuute, ãça'sa' ewte neeyũuna, txajũ' Dxusa' nuykatxhina. 16 Dxus dxi'pte ãhmée yũuni's puutx ya'mpeeta'sxwe, aça' Dxusa's yakhthẽ'jwe'sx neewe'wete', ãça'sa' katxhine'kwe. Dxusna nwẽese'jsa txã'wẽ ũusuh jxuka Dxusna yajkxçxa neewe'weni', kĩhtewa jxuka pu'çxwa'jsa'. 17 Ma'wẽga Dxus yuwe pta'sxsa Eliaswa kwe'sx na'wẽysaçxáaçxawa, nus pa'khamen Dxusna ũusuh jxuka yajkxçxa neewe'we, aça' nus pa'jme' tekh anxu y medio ãhpkaçx. 18 Txajũ' ki'kin Dxusna neewe'wek nus kĩikahn, aça' naa kiwete ki' nus pa'the', uuthasx çxhãçxhatx.
19-20 Adx yakhthẽ'jwe'sx mjiyuwe: I'kwe'sx maawa Dxus dxi'pte jxpubate', jxpubasame' txãa jxpubasa's ewmée dxi'ju kaasxwedu'juna. Sa' txã'wẽ yũuçxa' jxpubasá ũus jxukate vxituwa'j yã'j ũstewa, nwe'wesa yuuna, sa' maazkuẽe pkaltxiwa kaapeltunaĩ'jsa yuuna.
