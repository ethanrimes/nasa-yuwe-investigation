3
Juan Bautista nasa u'pnimée kiwesu Dxus yuwe's pta'sxni
(Mt 3.1-12; Mr 1.1-8; Jn 1.19-28)
1 Tiberio Cesar Romate jxkaahna u'pni kince anxusu' Poncio Pilato' Judeate ne'jwe'sxku yu' aça' Herodesa' Galileate ne'jwe'sxku yu'. Naapkaçxha' txajx nyakh Fxelipe' Iturea vxite' Trakonitete ne'jwe'sxku yu'. Aça' Lisaniasa' Abiliniate ne'jwe'sxku yu'. 2 Naapkaçxha' sacerdote npiiçthẽ'jwe'sx yu' Anas vxite' Kaifxas txãawe'sxtxi yu'. Txãn ensuk Dxusa' txajx yuwe's nasa u'pnimeesu pta'sxya' u'khan pa'ya Zakarias nçxi'k Juana's. 3 Aça' Juana' Jordan yu'wala utxasu msuu msuuwa jxuka pekũjk nasatx pta'sxna:
—I'kwe'sx pkaltxi nvxihtçxa Dxus tasxte sxawedçxa yu'te mkhẽewe ya'bautisãyna, i'kwe'sxtxi Dxus peltunãykahn jĩ'k. 4 Dxus yuwe pta'sxsa Isaias na'jĩna fxi'jni' kxtey yuuk:
Nasa u'pnimée kiwesu sus we'wenik pthũuse' na'jĩna:
Dxus pa'jwa'ja's mpheu'jwe,
Txã' thẽymée pa'khan.
5 Kiwe kafx u'psuwa jxuka yuutawa'j yuuna,
Sa' thã'j wala vxite' vxiç ma'k yuu nawa jxuka uukwe'jni yuutxna.
Dxi'j ta'gusawa ku'le'jni yuutxna.
Dxi'j ewmeesa's pheu'jni yuutxna.
6 Aça' nasa jxukaysa uyuutxna nwe'wewa'jsa Dxus kaahnisa's jĩna fxi'jni'.
7 Juan tasxte ya'bautisãyya' pa'jsatxi' na'jĩnak we'we:
—I'kwe'sxa' ul weesweesa ji'ji'kwe, txãa pa'ga kastigo seena'sa yuja' aça' txajũ iyuwa'ja's ¿kimga pta'sx? 8 Txã'wẽ kastigo seena'saju iyuwa'ja's jiyuyã'jçxa', ewçxáa yũuna mfxi'zewe, kxah maawa uysa': Naawe'sxa' pkaltxi nvxihtçxa Dxus tasxte sxawed yuhta sũjũtxna. Txã'jĩte', kwe'sxa' Abrahan ji'tha'w sũhna iiwejçx yajkxna fxi'zewa'jme'. Na'jĩ'th na': I'kwe'sxçxáamée naa kwet yuhtxwa Abrahan ji'j vxit-ya' ãjãna Dxusa'. 9 Txã'wẽ ewçxáa yũuna fxi'zemeete', ama' ih yã'j ũsa' fxtũu tasxtxi spẽ'thya'. Maa fxtũu tasxwa ew yũnmeena aça' spẽ'thçxa ipxte kabwa'jçxáa yuuna jĩnak yu'kxpeh.
10 Txã'jĩte' nasa' paapẽjxtxi:
—¿Kĩhçxa yũuna ewçxáa fxi'zekxtha'w? jĩte', 11 Juan pasçxa':
—E'z atxh ji'phçxa', ji'phmeesa'sa' teeçx ũswa'ja'. Aça' ũ'niwa jxthaakwe ji'phçxa', vxite ji'phmeesa's puuçwa'ja' jĩk.
12 Txã'wẽy gobierno jĩi vxyu pkhakhsawe'sxwa yu'te ya'bautisãyya' utxaaçxa', Juana's na'wẽtx paapẽjx:
—Mpeeta'sx, kwe'sxa' ¿ma'wẽ yũuna fxi'zekxtha'w? jĩte', 13 Juan pasçxa':
—Ley makwene' vxyu's pẽykaja' txã'sçxáana mpẽjxwe, ĩsxĩiçxa thaakwe pẽjxmée jĩk.
14 Txajũ' soldauwe'sxwa maaíi yũ' txã'wẽy pẽjxtxi:
—Kwe'sxa' ¿ma'wẽ yũuna fxi'zekxtha'w? jĩte', txãawe'sxtxiwa pta'sxku Juana':
—Maa nasa yuhtxwa juuna' we'weçxa txãawe'sx jĩitx kusa'jnuwe kĩh yuhnawa. Sa' kĩh yuhwa yuuwemeetewa ĩsxĩiçxa pãatxhĩ'khna fxi'zenuwe. Sa' i'kwe'sxtxi makwe dewetewa txãa yakhçxáa weçxana mfxi'zewe.
15 Txã'wẽ we'wena ũste', nasa wẽsẽ'jsa yu' wala yajkxna ũsu'tx: Meeçxa' ¿naa wáa Kristona? sũhna. 16 Aça' Juana' jxukaysatx pta'sxku:
—Adxa' bautisãyna naasáa yu'teçxáath akhe'. Nawa adx e'suh vxite yuja', txã' Dxus Espiritu ipx na'wẽsa's i'kwe'sx ũuste kxu'akhe'jya' ãjãwa'jsa'. Aça' adxa' txajx pelgatxi wesçxáa yuhtxwa khihbxa' ãjãwa'jmeesath. 17 Txã' yujxa' skuutxh kweejxa'jni plaatu's kusete at yã'jçxa ũsa'. Sa' kweejxa'jçxa skuutxha's jxaawuuna, nawa tama'sa' ipx bagaçxte yuhwa ũçxhawa'jmeesate kabuna jĩk.
18 Txã'wẽk wala kuh jwed yu'kxpehna Dxus yuwe's pta'sxi' Juana'. 19 Sa' ne'jwe'sx Herodesa'swa yu'kxpejk txã'wẽ nyakh Fxelipe nyu Herodiasa's kusa'jçxa, txãa yakh nyu na'wẽ fxi'zeçxa vxite' kĩhwa ewmeeçxáak yũuna ũsu'. Aça' txãa pa'gak txã'wẽ yũuwa'jme' jĩna yu'kxpeh Juana'. 20 Nawa Herodesa' nwẽesẽ'jepkaçxte jweeíiçxáak thaakwe ewmée yũu, Juana's karcelte kaakxuupha'jna.
Jesus ya'bautisãyni
(Mt 3.13-17; Mr 1.9-11)
21 Txã'wẽ yũumeyna Juana' nasatx yu'te bautisãyna ũste', Jesuswa pa'jçxa yu'te ya'bautisãyku. Sa' oraĩçpkaçxha', cielu' phaatek. 22 Aça' Dxus Espiritu' Jesus kajkwete tub çxihme na'wẽsak vxyaana kĩh. Naapkaçxha' cieluju na'wẽ we'wenik pthũuse:
—Idxa' adx Nçxi'k wala wedxnisag, aça' iidxpa'ga wala weçxana ũsthu jĩni.
