8
Dxus ĩtximeesatx weçxaya' tajkx ikhni's pta'sxni
1 Ãçxha' Dxus ĩtximeesa ũsta' txãatx weçxaawa'j tajkx ikhnisa's pta'sxya'wath. Txãa çxiçxa' kĩhwa suwwa'jsameetewa txã'sa' ew jiitha'w kwe'sxa'. Nawa txã'wẽ jiith sũhnisa' weeçx we'wewa'jçxáak kaseje', aça' puutx ya'peeygãhna Dxus yakh çxhãçxha fxi'zewa'j yu' ya'ji'phu'çme'. 2 Maawa txã'sçxáana jiiçxa jiith sũhna fxi'zesa', kĩhwa jiimeeta' ew jiisa na'wẽ yu'. 3 Nawa txã'wẽ yũumée, Dxusa'sçxáa ũusuh jxuka yaakxsa's Dxusa' jiia' txã'wẽ yũusa's.
4 Txã'wẽ dxus ĩtximeesatx weçxaya' tajkx ikhnisa çxiçxa's ũ'te ¿ewmée dxihmanx? Ew ũus miiyajkxwe. Txãa dxus ĩtximeesa' naa kiwete kĩh yuhwa meesata', naasáa teeçx Dxus yuuçxáa ĩtxisa ũsa', peena vxite yu' meeta'. 5 Dxus yaasesa naa kiwete vxite' cielute ũswáatxna txã'wẽ yaakxsa yakh yu'. 6 Nawa kwe'sx yakh yu', teeçx Dxus yuuçxáa ũsa' kwe'sx Tata cielute ũssa. Txãak kĩh yuhwa peejxmée jxuka taki'jçxa nvxiht, aça' kwe'sxa' txãa pa'gaçxáatha'w ĩtxi fxi'zeya' ãja'. Txã'wẽy kwe'sxtxi jxpe'jsawa teeçxçxáa ũsa', txã' Jesukristo'. Kĩhwa jxuka txajx thegna jxpe'pha'gaçxáa ũsta', txãa pa'ga kwe'sxwa txajx jxpe'jnisa íiçxáatha'w.
7 Aça' txãa yakh ũssa yakha' çxiçx txã'wẽsa's ũ'tewa, kĩh yuhwa suwwa'jsameetewa txã'sa' jiimeeta' jxuka yu'. Txãasatx naa kiwete dxus ĩtximeesatx nyafxíi neewe'wesawe'sx Dxusa's açxamée ew jiisa' çxiçx txã'wẽsa's ũ'te', ewmeena sũju'. 8 Nawa kwe'sx ũ'ni' Dxus dxi'pte ewsa vxitwa'jsame'. Aça' txã'wẽ ũ'çxa' ewsawa yuume'nja'w, sa' ewmeesawa yuume'nja'w. 9 Txã'wẽtewa, mpa'yajkxwe, i'kwe'sx txã'wẽ kĩhnawa ũ'ya' ãja'nja'w sũhçxa txãa çxiçxa's ũ'pa'gaçxáa Dxusa's açxamée ew jiisatx pkalte kweete'jwe'sxte. 10 Meeçxawa na'wẽ wáa yuune'ga: dxus ĩtximeesatx weçxanisa çxiçx txweyni's wáa ũ'ya' kaçxhiine'ga, aça' Dxusa's açxamée ew jiisa uyçxa', txãawa txã'wẽy ũ'wena. 11 Aça' iidxpa'gaçxáa Dxusa's açxamée ew jiisa iidx yakhthẽ'j ũusa' vxituna, txajx pa'gate makwe Kristo uutewa. 12 Aça' Dxusa's açxamée ew jiisa yakhthẽ'jtx pkalte kweete'jçxa', idxwa Kristo dxi'pte yuuwesa yuune'ga. 13 Txãa pa'ga adx yakhthẽ'j pkalte wetewe'sxte çxiçx txã'wẽsa's bagaçxte yuhwa ũ'me'nja pkalte kweete'jya'mée.
