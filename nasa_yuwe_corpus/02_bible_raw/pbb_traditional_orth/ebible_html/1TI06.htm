6
Dxus yakh fxi'zenisa' naa kiwete ji'phunite thaakwe walasa'
1 Selpiisá' i'kwe'sx patron yakh wala jxpa'yakx nwẽese'jna mfxi'zewe, Dxusa's açewe'weni yuukahmén vxite' kwe'sx kaapiya'jni'swa txã'wẽy ewmée we'wekahmén. 2 Sa' selpisaawe'sx i'kwe'sx patron maaíia' Dxusa's jiisa wáa yuuna aça' txã'wẽ kwe'sx yakhthẽ'j íiçxá' sũhçxaçxáa le'çxkwe nwẽese'jmée fxi'zewa'j ji'phmeeta', wejxwa jweeíiçxáa ew selpiwa'j ji'phta', txãa pa'gatey Dxusna jiisatxíiçxáa wejx ew pu'çxna selpiwa'ja' peeygãhna. Txã'wẽ yũuwa'ja's pta'sxna mkaapiya'j.
3 Maaíi yu' kwe'sxtxi jxpe'jsa Jesukristo kaapiya'jni'smée, fxiy wáa pta'sxina kwe'sx kaapiya'jni'swamée, 4 sa' txã' ũuste iiwejçx yaakxsa kĩhwa jiimeesaçxa txã'wẽ yuuna. Naasáa txã' luuku na'wẽ kĩhwa seelpimeesuçxáa we'wena fxi'zesa yuuna aça' txã'wẽ yũupa'gak vxitetx ewmée yũuwa'j kaseje': açeyaakxni, puiini, wẽesxuni, nasa ewsatx ewmeesata' sũhna fxi'zeni, 5 puiiwa'j pçuwa'jmeek kaseje', txã'wẽ nasa Dxusa's jiimeesawe'sx yakh. Sa' ewmée kaapiya'jsawe'sxa' Dxus yakh fxi'zeni jĩnisa' vxyuu knaynisana sũju'tx. Txã'wẽsa yakha' pkhaakhenu. 6 Txã'wẽ Dxus yakh fxi'zenisa' naa kiwete iiméh wala ji'phunisate jxthaakwe walasa' kĩhwa ji'phmeeçxawa wẽt fxi'zesa yakh yu'. 7 Txãa pa'gatey kwe'sxa' naa kiwete kĩhwa ji'phmeetha'w pa'j na' kĩhwa ji'phmée kaseje'nja'w. 8 Txã'wẽ kĩhwa ji'phmeeçxawa ũ'wa'j vxite' athwa'j ropa txã'wẽsatx ji'phçxaçxáa weçxana fxi'zewa'j ji'ptha'w. 9 Nawa txã'wẽ yaakxsame' maaíi yu' wala ji'phu wẽeniçxá's yaakxsa' ewmeetetx wete', ewmée yũuwa'jçxá's yaakxpa'ga. Sa' txã'wẽ yaakxnisa' nasatx ewmeete kaaivxitu'jsaçxá'. 10 Txã'wẽ vxyuçxá's wejx thaakwe yaakxpa'gaíiçxáak kĩhtewa jxuka ewmée yũuwa'jçxaçxáa kaseje'. Txãa pa'ga maaíi yu' vxyuu ji'phu wẽeniçxá's yaakxsa' Dxus yakh fxi'zenijũ txuteeçxa wala nxusteçxáa fxi'zena ũsta' peekíi.
Dxus yuuçxá's ũuste peçxkanamée yaakxwa'ja's mji'phu
11 Nawa idxa' Dxusna nwẽese'jsag naa pa'ga txãa ewmeejũ jxuka txuteeçxa kĩhte yuhwa jxpubamée Dxusçxáana yajkxna vxitetx peeygãhna thẽysa pa'thewa txãatxi' yajkxmée ũusuh jxuka manvxiht ewmeesa's. 12 Dxus yakh txutemée fxi'zewa'ja'sçxáa çxhãçxha mpa'pçxu, bagaçxte yuhwa pçumée ĩtxi fxi'zewa'ja'sçxáa yajkxna. Txã'wẽ yuukahníiçxáak Dxusa' pa'ya, aça' idxa' jxukaysa dxi'pte Dxus yakhçxáa fxi'ze'nja jĩnag we'we. 13 Dxusa' na'wẽ ĩtxi fxi'zewa'ja's peesu'k kĩjkĩhçxah ũsna txãatx jxuka vxite' Jesukristowa Dxus Nçxi'kthu jĩna ãate pta'sxku Poncio Pilato tasxte txã'wẽ yuusaawe'sx dxi'pte ãçxha' idxa's jxkaajaçthu: 14 Adx ma'wẽ jxkaahni's kxtey nwẽese'jene'ga kĩhte yuhwa jxpubana yu'pthehmée kwe'sxtxi jxpe'jsa Jesukristo kĩhpkaçx. 15 Jesukristo kĩiwa'j en ãhte', Dxusa' Jesukristo's kaavxya'ja'jana, txãa pa'gatey Dxus yuuçxáa kĩhtewa jxuka ãjawa'jsa txãa yuuçxá', sa' Maa jxkaahsatewa jxthaakwe jxtuhthesa', Maatewa jxthaakwe çxhãçxhasa. 16 Txãa yuuçxáa uuwa'jsame', eena' kweethnite kim yuhwa utxaaya' ãjanimeete ũssa. Txã'sa' maa piçthẽ'j yuhwa iiuymeetxna sa' ãçxpkaçx iiuymeeta'. Txakwe seena' walasa txãa yuuçxá's tuuthe'jni yuuwa'ja', bagaçxte yuhwa pçuwa'jmeesa's.
17 Naa kiwete ji'phsatxi' mwe'we, ji'phuwa'jçxá's pa'pçxuna iiwejçx yajkxna fxi'zekahmén. Txã'wẽme' ji'phuniçxá's yaakxni' wala kxulsata', wejxwa Dxusa'sçxáa yaakitxna, txãa pa'gatey txã' ĩtxisa', kwe'sx kĩhçxah peejini'swa peejxmée jxuka ũssa. 18 Ew yũuwa'jçxá's mkaah, kxah txãawe'sx ew yũuniçxá's ji'phsa yuutxna, sa' txãawe'sx ji'phuni's vxitetx jxtxuhdeya'wa piyaatxna. 19 Txã'wẽ yũuçxa' cielute jxpa'gawa'ja's jxaawsa yuuçxa, bagaçxte yuhwa pçumée ĩtxi fxi'zewa'ja's jxpa'gasa yuutxna.
Timoteo jxpa'yajkx fxi'zewa'ja's neewe'weni
20 Timoteo, idxna neewe'weni's mpa'yajkx. We'weni kĩhwa seelpimeesatxi' wẽse'jnu, sa' Dxus yuwete jxthaakwe jiisatha'w jĩna weeçxsatxwa nwẽese'jnu. 21 Txã'wẽ yũumeeçxaíi maaíi yu' txã'jĩna we'wesa yakh u'jsa' jxpubana ũsta' Dxus yakh fxi'zenijũ. Txã'wẽ yũukahmén, Dxusa' idxa's pu'çxhina.
