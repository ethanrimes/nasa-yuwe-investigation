18
Babilonia vxituni
1 Txãa e'su' Dxus angel cielujũ kĩjaçteth uy seena' juuna' jxkaahwa'jsa, naa kiwete zhiçxkwe eena' kweethna. 2 Sa' na'jĩnak sus we'we:
—Babilonia çxhab txakwe çxhãçxhasaçxawa, Dxus kastigo's jxpa'gawa'the yã'j ũsa'. Sa' ãçxha' eçxthẽ'j jxkaahni espiritu ewmeesa vxite' vxiçxakwe kazxsaçxaçxáa ũswa'j yuuna pa'kh. 3 Txã'wẽ yũukahníi maa çxhabwa jxukaysa txãa pkal kazxsateçxaçxáatx fxi'zena ũsu'. Aça' naa kiwete jxkaahsa jxukaysawe'sx pkal kazx yũuwa'ja's yuuçxáatx piya. Txã'wẽy txweysawe'sxwa wala ji'phsa yuutx, pkalte fxi'zewa'jçxá's txweyna jĩ'k.
4 Txajũ' cielujũ na'jĩna we'weni wẽse'th:
—Adx nasa yã'ja' txãa çxhabu mkaasehwe, txãa pkalsawe'sx yakh ũsya'mée, kxah txãa çxhabte kastigo' i'kwe'sxtxi's kpajkxmeena. 5 Na'jĩ'th na': txãawe'sx pkala' iiméh seena' walani' cielute kãhpkaçx, kxajk Dxusa' txãa çxhaba's kastigãyya' yajkx txã'wẽ kazxsapa'ga. 6 Txãawa vxitetx ma'wẽga ewmée yũu txã'wẽy ewmée myũuwe ka'tuwa. Sa' txãawa ma'wẽga vxitetx juuna' yũu, txãate e'z us thaakwe juuna' myũuwe. 7 Pkxuuna makxũusu'jwe, txãawa makwega iiwejçx yajkxna pkalte fxi'zena ũsu' txãatey ãh. Sa' txã' na'wẽk yaaki' txajx ũuste: Ayte jxkaahsa na'wẽ kaçxçxa u'pthu. Nmi' ji'phmeesameeth, sa' pxthaa yuuna fxi'zeme'nja sũhnak iiwejçx yaaki'. 8 Txãa pa'ga teeçx enteçxáa kastigo pa'jatxna na'wẽsa: nxuswa'j, wẽjẽ, uu en, sa' ipxte kabni yuuna. Txãa çxhaba's Dxus çxhãçxhasa' jxuka ivxiituna.
9 Aça' naa kiwete jxkaahsawe'sx txãa çxhabte kazx pkal yũuna fxi'zesa' wala nxus we'wena ũ'netxna, txãa çxhavxyni aja's uyçxa. 10 Sa' txã'wẽ kastigãyni's ũukhçxa, jxu'juçxáa thegutxna na'jĩna:
—Seena' yuh pxthaa yuuk, Babilonia çxhab txakwe seena' çxhãçxhasa. Naa dudte jxuka vxitunik yuu jĩtxna.
11 Naa kiwete txweyna fxi'zesawe'sxwa txãa çxhaba's yajkxçxa wala pxthaa we'wena ũ'netxna. Txã'wẽ yũukahníi ãçxyã'ja' peena yu' kim yuhwa weymeetxna makwe tũ'sna pa'thewa: 12 vxyuu beh, vxyuu çxihme, kwet zhiçxkwe wala pa'gasa, tela zhiçxkwesa iiméh pa'gasa, fxtũu wẽt putasá, marfxil zhiçxkwesa, fxtũu ewsa wala pa'gasa, bronce yaasesa, hieçxo yaasesa, marmól yaasesa, kĩjkĩhwa txã'wẽ zhiçxkwe vxitnisa. 13 Vxite' ũ'nisate wẽt pẽethẽsa, kabte wẽt putasá, wẽt putakahn kakwete sxũsxnisa, vxino, aceite, skuutxh ũ'we ewsa. Vxite' tajkx ktũ'se'jnisa, piisxáa, jiba, kaçxetilxa jiba wezxnisa. Vxite' nasa txweynisa kĩh yuhnawa ya'txweymeena. 14 Sa' txãa çxhaba's na'jĩtxna:
—¡Txakwe wala weçxana fxi'zeni's ji'phméek neeyũu, sa' txakwe zhiçxkwe ji'phuni'swa jxukate ivxiituk! jĩtxna.
15 Txãa çxhabte kĩjkĩhwa txweyçxa wala ji'phsa yuusaawe'sxa' jxu'juçxáa thegna neeyũutxna kastigãyni's ũukhçxa. Sa' wala nxus we'wena ũ'netxna 16 na'jĩna:
—¡Txãa çxhab txakwe walasa seena' yuh yuuk! Nyafxíi yu' u'y na'wẽk zhiçxkwe iijxũçxa fxi'ze', ropa iiméh pa'gasa, vxyuu beh ki'pna yuunisa, kwet zhiçxkwesa iiméh pa'gasaçxaçxáatx txã'wẽsatxku iijxũ'. 17 Nawa txãa ji'phunisa naa dudte jxuka pçuuk jĩtxna. Txã'wẽy barko namuwe'sx, vxite' kxtee mjĩisawe'sx jxukaysa jxu'juçxáa thegna neeyũutxna. 18 Sa' txãa çxhab çxhavxyna sxi'tasa aja's uyçxa', na'jĩna sus we'wetxna:
—Mtee yuhwa naa çxhab na'wẽy walasa yu' meea' jĩna, 19 nxusweete dxikthekhẽ kiwe ya'fxihbna sus ũ'nena na'jĩtxna:
—Seena' nxusnxus knayku çxhab txakwe walasa txajx ji'phuni yakh wala ji'phsa vxitku barko ji'phsa yuutxi' jxukaysatx. Nawa naa dudte jxuka vxitunik yuu jĩtxna.
20 Nawa cielute ũssawe'sx wala yuh mweeçxawe txãa çxhaba's kastigãyni's uyçxa, Jesus jxkaahnisawe'sx vxite' Dxus yuwe pta'sxsawe'sx, Dxus yakh fxi'zesawe'sx jxukaysa. Txãa pa'gatey Dxusa' txãa çxhaba's kastigãyyã'kh, i'kwe'sxtxi ewmée yũupa'ga jĩnik pthũuse.
21 Txajũ' Dxus angel teeçxsa' seena' wala çxhãçxhasa ũ'khni kwet na'wẽsa's kiisçxa, markhẽk wãatah na'jĩna:
—Babilonia çxhab txakwe walasa txã'wẽ wãataaní yuuna, aça' txã'sa' bagaçxte yuhwa peena yu' uymeetxna. 22 Aça' txãa çxhab klxiikhunsu tukani músika arpa, kuvxy, trompeta txã'wẽsa pthũusemeetxna. Sa' txãa çxhabte kĩh mjĩisa yuhwamée yuutxna, sa' ũ'khniçxáa yuhwa pthũusemeena. 23 Kxtee pki'taniçxáa yuhwa kweethmeena, sa' boda fxiesta vxitna susniwa peena yu' pthũusemeena. Txãa çxhaba' txweyna ũssawe'sx wala yuh çxhãçxhasatx ji'phu'k, sa' naa kiwetewe'sxtxi jxuka kaypuba'kh dxijxuuçxa. Nawa ãçxha' kĩh yuhwa ji'phmée yuuna jĩ'k. 24 Txã'wẽ yuukahníi Dxus yuwe pta'sxsawe'sx vxite' Dxus yakh fxi'zesawe'sx vxite' naa kiwete mtee mteewa ikhnisawe'sx eea' jxuka txãa çxhabteçxaçxáak ũs yu'.
