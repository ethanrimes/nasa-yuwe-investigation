2
Jesus upxni
(Mt 1.18-25)
1 Txãn ensu' Romate jxkaahsa Augusto Cesara' txajx jxkaahni kiwesu nyafxçxah nasatx jxuka eçte ãsxkahn jxkaakh, 2 Siriate u'psa ne'jwe'sx Cirenio yaasesa's. 3 Aça' jxukaysatx u'jwa'j ji'phu' txãawe'sx upxni çxhabte, eçte ãsxkahn. 4 Txãa pa'gak Jose' Nasaret çxhab Galilea kiweju kasehna u'j Judea kiwen Belen çxhab Davxid upxnite, Jose' Davxid ji'kh yu' naa pa'ga. 5 Belenna u'jweççxa', txajx nyu yuuwa'jsa Maria yakhku u'j. Nawa Maria' nasak ji'ph yu'. 6 Aça' Belente ũsíyna, Maria's ãçã'wa'j en ãhte', 7 kxtee ji'phuk nyafx nçxi'kkuẽ's. Sa' paadeewa'j yat ãhmeete', klaa sã'jĩni yattek luuçxkuẽ's yapçxa khikh.
Dxus angel piisxáa-thegsawe'sxtxi we'weni
8 Belen utxasu kuswa dehméetx piisxáa-thegsa u'p yu'. 9 Naapkaçxha' Dxus angel teeçx vxyaak txãawe'sx u'pnisu jxuka eena' kweethna. Aça' txãawe'sxa' wala ũukhtxi. 10 Nawa Dxus angela' na'wẽk we'we:
—Ũukhnuwe, yuwe ewsa jxũna yuhth, txã'sa' maawa jxuka wala weçxaatxna. 11 Ãçxha' Davxid çxhabte teeçx upxku i'kwe'sxtxi nwe'wewa'jsa txã' Dxus kaahni Kristo'. 12 Aça' thegya' u'jçxa', uyuune'kwe luuçxkuẽ's yapçxa klaa sã'jĩni yatga khikhni ũste jĩçpkaçxha', 13 txãa dudtey txãa angel yakh wala kuh angel cielujũwe'sx vxyaatx, Dxusna weçxana:
14 ¡Dxus ẽe walatewe'sxa' seena' yuh ew yuuk!
Naa kiwete nasa wẽtwẽt fxi'zewa'ja's ũsku peeygãhçxa jĩ'tx.
15 Txajũ' angeleswe'sxa' cielun ki' sxawedna u'jweçpkaçxha', piisxáa-thegsawe'sxa' puutx we'wetx:
—Belenna u'jwega Dxus pta'sxni's thegya' kĩh yũutewa uyu'nja'w jĩna.
16 Txajũ' wala dud u'jtx Belenna, sa' Mariawe'sxtxi' uytxi txã'wẽ luuçxkuẽ's klaa sã'jĩni yatga khikhçxa u'pte. 17 Sa' luuçxa's uyçxa', Dxus angel ma'wẽ we'weni'swa jxuka pta'sxtxi Mariawe'sxtxi. 18 Txajũ' vxitetxwa txã'wẽçxaçxáa pta'sxte', wala kuh we'wena neeyũutx. 19 Nawa Maria yu', txã'wẽ yũuni's jxuka peçxkanamée ũusteçxáak yajkxna fxi'ze'.
20 Txajũ' piisxáa-thegsawe'sxa' Dxus angel ma'wẽ pta'sxtewa txã' kxtey yuutewa uyçxa', Dxusa's wala weçxana sxawedna u'jtx.
Jesus le'çxkuẽyna Dxus yatna jxũna u'jni
21 Upxnijũ teeçx ki'sute' luuçxkuẽ's cirkuncidãyya' u'jçxa txãa entey JESUS yasetx, Maria nasa ji'phumeyna Dxus angel ma'wẽne' yase's pta'sx txã'wẽytxi yase.
22 Moises leya' nasate ãçã'satx ma'wẽne' ki'skaja' aça' Maria' txãa entxi kxtey yuuçxa', Jose yakh luuçxkuẽ's Jerusalen çxhabna jxũna u'jtx, dxuus yatte Dxusna weçxana neewe'weya'. 23 Dxus leyte we'weçxa': Nyafxte piçkuẽ yũute', Dxus jĩi nvxiitwa'ja' jĩ'k txãa pa'gatx Dxusna neewe'weya' u'j. 24 Vxite ahte Dxus leyte we'weçxa': e'z tub çxihme meeçxawa tub luuçx jxũna u'jçxa dxuus yatte ikhwa'ja' jĩ'k aça' txã'wẽy yũuya' u'jtx.
25 Txãn ensu' Jerusalen çxhabte' teeçx piçthẽ'j Simeon yaasesak u'pu'. Txãa piçthẽ'ja' Dxusna yuuçxáak neewe'wena fxi'zesa yu', ¿bagaçx Israelwe'sxtxi nwe'wewa'jsa pa'jakx? sũhna ũythãsna. Aça' Dxus Espiritu' Simeona's kaajiyu'kh 26 Nwe'wewa'jsa Dxus kaahni Kristo's uumey uywa'ja's. 27 Sa' Dxus Espiritu' Simeona's kaayaki'kh dxuus yatna u'jwa'ja's, aça' u'jçxa' uyku txã'wẽ Jesus njĩ'jwe'sxa' Dxus ley jxkaahni's kxtey yuuya' Jesusa's nuypa'jaçte. 28 Aça' Simeona' txãa luuçxkuẽ's yakhçxa na'jĩnak Dxusna weçxa:
29 Idxa' adx yakh puutx we'weni's kxtey yuug na' ãçxhã' adx uuwa'ja's ũsune'ga weçxana uukahn.
30 Nwe'wesa's nasa jxukaysa dxi'pte kaag.
31 Aça' txã'sa' adx yafxju yuu uythu.
32 Txãaíi Israel nasa meetxwa eena' kweethwa'jsa' na' Israelwe'sx yuutxi' jweeíiçxáa weçxweçxa kaayaki'jwa'jsa' jĩ'k.
33 Simeona' txãa luuçxkuẽ's txã'wẽ we'wete', Jose ptamwe'sxa' kuhtx yajkxna neeyũu. 34 Aça' Simeona' bedekĩyçxa', Jesus njĩ'j Maria's pta'sxku:
—Mwẽesẽ'çx, naa luuçxkuẽ' Israelwe'sxtxi wala kçxhãaçxha'jwa'jsa' nawa txã'sna jiyusamée yu' wala jxpubawa'jsata'. Mpa'yajkxwe wakx txã'sa' wala açetxna. 35 Aça' maaçxah txã'wẽ açewa'jsatxna aça' maaçxah txã'wẽ jxpa'gawa'jsatewa txã'wẽ yũunisa içxa ũusuy we'thu yãjãni na'wẽ yuuna jĩk.
36 Kxteea' Fxanwel niisa, Aser ji'kh ũsu' wala thẽ'khuẽsa Ana yaasesa. Txã' Dxusna selpisaak yu' sa' wala kna'satey iimi'k nawa siete anxute' nmi'a' uuk. 37 Ãçxha' nmi' uuni oçxenta y kuatro anxu', nawa dxuus yatte pu'vxitumée een isak Dxusna selpiina, orãyna nes yu'. 38 Sa' Ana' utxaana kũhçxa, Dxusna weçxaçxa' pta'sxku Israelwe'sxtxi nwe'wewa'jsa's ũythãsna ũssawe'sxtxi's: Naa luuçx txã'wẽ yũuwa'jsa' jĩna.
Nasaretna sxawedni
39 Txajũ' Dxus jxkaahni leya's kxtey yuuçxa', Galilea kiwesu txãawe'sx çxhab Nasaretnay Maria Josewe'sxa' sxawedtxi. 40 Aça' luuçxkuẽ' walaana u'kh Dxus yuwe's jweeíiçxáa piyana, txã'sa' Dxusíi pu'çxhi'ne' naa pa'ga.
Jesus doce anxu ji'phçxa Jerusalenna u'jni
41 Jesus njĩ'jwe'sxa' anxu iisatx Jerusalen çxhabna Paskua fxiestan u'jwe'. 42 Sa' Jesus doce anxu ji'phpkaçxha', Jerusalen çxhabna fxiestate ki' u'jtx, ma'wẽne'ta anxu iisa u'jwe' txã'wẽy. 43 Aça' fxiesta pçuute', Maria Jose yakh sxawedna u'jtx. Sa' txãawe'sx jiyunimeete Jesusa' Jerusalentee neeyũuk. 44 Nawa njĩ'jwe'sx yu', Jesusa' nasa wala ksxavxyte yuhna sũhnaçxáatx teen uta dxi'the u'j. 45 Txajũ' nwe'sx ksxavxysu jxuka pakweçxawa uymeeçxa', Jerusalen çxhabnay pakweya' sxawedtxi. 46 Sa' tekh ensu' dxuus yatte ley kaapiya'jsawe'sx ksxavxyte wẽsẽ'jna paapẽjxna u'ptetx uy. 47 Txã'wẽ luuçxkuẽçxáaçxawa txakwe wala leya's jiiçxa ew paste', jxukaysa kxtee wẽsẽ'jna ũssa' wala kuh yaaki'tx. 48 Txajũ' njĩ'jwe'sx uyçxa', kuhtx yajkx sa' njĩ'ja' na'jĩk:
—Idxa' ¿kĩjxa'ga pta'sxmée txã'wẽ neeyũu? Kwe'sxa' wala nxusna pakwetha'w jĩte', 49 Jesus pasçxa':
—Adxa' Adx Tata jxthãasniçxá's selpiina u'pthu aça' ¿kĩjxa'kwe adxa's pakwé'? jĩte', 50 txajx pasni'sa' jiyuya' ãjãmeeta'.
51 Txajũ' Jesusa' neywe'sx yakh Nasaretnay sxawedna u'jçxa txãawe'sxtxi nwẽesẽ'jnak fxi'ze. Aça' txajx njĩ'ja' ma'wẽ we'weni'swa jiyumeeçxawa txajx ũuste peçxkana yu' meek yajkxna fxi'ze'. 52 Txajũ' Jesusa' walaana u'kh Dxus yuwe's jweeíiçxáa piyana, sa' Dxus dxi'pte ew yũuçxak fxi'ze, nasaa dxi'ptewa txã'wẽy.
