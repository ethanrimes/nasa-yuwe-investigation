11
1 Adxwa ma'wẽtka Kristo fxi'zeni's thegçxa fxi'ze', txã'wẽy i'kwe'sxwa adx fxi'zeni's thegçxa mfxi'zewe.
U'ya' kultote jxpa'yakxwa'j ji'pha'
2 Yakhthẽ'jwe'sxtxi weçxa'th adxa's peçxkanamée yajkxçxa adx kaapiya'jnitxi' kxtey yuuna fxi'zepa'ga. 3 Nawa na'wẽsa'swa mjiyuwe: Kristo' Dxusa's nwẽese'jsa', aça' piçthẽ'jwe'sxa' Kristo's nwẽese'jwa'jsata', aça' u'ywe'sxa' nmi'tx nwẽese'jwa'jsata'. 4 Txã'wẽ yũuwa'jsaçxah maa piçthẽ'jwa çxua'a's ki'pmée kultote orãyna Dxus yuwe's pta'sxna ũsçxa', Kristo's weeçxsa yuuni'. 5 Txã'wẽy maa u'ywa dxikthe's ya'pathmée orãyna Dxus yuwe's pta'sxna ũsçxa', txajx nmi'a's weeçxsa yuuni'. Txã'wẽ ya'pathmeete', dxkhasa's kwaaka'jni na'wẽsa'. 6 U'ya' txã'wẽ ya'patheçmée yã'jçxa', teehwed dxkhasa's tee kwaaka'j. Nawa u'y txã'wẽ dxkhasa's kwaaka'the' thaameweçxte', tee ya'path txajx dxikthe's. 7 Piçthẽ'ja' dxikthe's ya'pathwa'jsame', txãa pa'gatey Dxus wedxçxak txãa na'wẽysa vxit, txajx jxkaahnitx nwẽese'jwa'jsa. Naapkaçxha' u'ya' piçthẽ'j jxkaahnitx nwẽese'jwa'jsa'. 8 Dxusa' piçthẽ'ja's taki'jiççxa', u'y kakwejuwe'sxa's kutxi'jçxa pheu'jme', wejxwa piçthẽ'j kakwejuwe'sxa's kutxi'jçxak u'ya's pheu'j. 9 Piçthẽ'ja' pu'çxwa'jsa peejxku yu', txãa pa'gaçxáak u'ya' pheu'jni yuu. U'y txã'wẽ pu'çxwa'jsa peejxte piçthẽ'ja's pheu'jni yuume'. 10 Txãa pa'ga u'ya' piçthẽ'ja's nwẽese'jwa'jsa' jĩni dxikthete ya'pathnisa ji'phuwa'ja'. Txã'wẽ ya'pathwa'jsaçxah kxtey yuumeete', Dxus angeleswe'sx thegna ũsta'. 11-12 Txã'wẽtewa, ew ũus yajkxte yu', maa yuhwa thaakwesameeta', txãa pa'gatey piçthẽ'jwaçwa u'y kakwetemée peekxçxáa yu' naa kiwete upxya' ãja'çme', txã'wẽy u'ywa piçthẽ'j ji'phmée peekxçxáa yu' naa kiwete upxya' ãja'çme'. Isa yuja' u'ya' piçthẽ'j kakwejuwe'sxa'sku pheu'jni yuu, txã'wẽy piçthẽ'jwa u'y kakwetek upxya' ãja'. Txãasa maa yuhwa thaakwesamée, e'zíi Dxus pheu'jniçxaçxáata'.
13 Txã'wẽ' naa pa'ga i'kwe'sx pwe'sx ũus miiyajkxwe, aan ewna txã'wẽ Dxusa's orãiççxa u'ya' dxikthe's ya'pathmeete. 14 Piçthẽ'ja' peekx yuu dxkhas jxu'j ji'phuwa'jmeesaçxa txã'wẽ khyu'ja'the', thaame yuja'. 15 Nawa u'y txã'wẽ dxkhas jxu'j ji'phte', zhiçxkwesa'. Txãa pa'gatey dxikthe's aphwa'jíiçxáak dxkhas jxu'j nvxiitni yuu. 16 Nawa maaíi yu' dxikthe's ya'pathwa'ja's jxkaahni's wa'lsa ũsçxa', peekx ũus yaakxwa'j ji'phta'. Txã'wẽme' kwe'sx vxite' Dxus luuçx msuuwe'sx maa yuhwa jiimeeta' dxikthe's ya'pathmée Dxusa's orãyni yu's.
Ewmée yũuya'çxáa Santa Cena's ũ'ya' pkhaakheni
17 Na'wẽsana pta'sxna fxi'jaççxa', i'kwe'sxa' ew yũusa i'kwe jĩna weçxaya' ãja'çmeeth. I'kwe'sxa' Dxusa's weçxaya' pkhaakheni' selpiipkaçxte, wejx suwsaçxáana sũju'th. 18 I'kwe'sxa' Dxusa's weçxaya' pkhaakheççxa', bagaçxtewa i'kwe'sx pwe'sx puutx fxiifxii txuteeya'çxáatx pkhaakhe' jĩ'tx aça' txã'wẽ pta'sxni' isa yuhna sũju'th. 19 Pkhaakhesa i'kwe'sx pwe'sx txã'wẽ fxiifxii txuteni' ew yuhna, txajíiçxáa mtee pkhaakhesa wejx Dxus yakh ew fxi'zesatewa ya'jiyuna. 20 Nawa Jesukristo mesatewe'sxa'stha'w jxpa'ga' jĩççxa txã'wẽ fxiifxii txuteeçxa ũsni' txã' Dxus jxthãasnime'. 21 Txãa pa'gatey i'kwe'sxa' ih pkhaakheçxa ja'daçxah ũ'wa'j en ãjaçpkaçxha', puutx fxii txuteeçxa maaíi yu' yaçte ũ'we'i'kwe, vxite jĩi yu' nvxihtmée. Aça' vxiteçxáa txikpkaçx meeçxawa tũunus tudxte', vxite yu' ũ'wa'j ji'phmeetx wẽena neeyũ'. 22 Txã'wẽçxáa yũuççxa', i'kwe'sx yat-suywa ũ'wa'jna. Txã'wẽme', vxitee ũ'wa'j yu' nvxihtmeeçxa', kxũusu'ju'i'kwe, aça' puutx yaaçeniçxáak kaseje'. I'kwe'sx txã'wẽ yũuna ũste', adxa' i'kwe'sxtxi' ew yũuna ũsi'kwe jĩna ¿weçxaya' ãjama'th? Na'wẽçxáa yũute', weçxaya' ãjame'nja.
Santa Cena's ũ'wa'ja's Kristok kaapiya'jçxa nvxiht
23 Adxa' i'kwe'sxtxi' kaapiya'th nawa Jesukristo adxa's ma'wẽ kaapiya'the' txã'wẽyçxáath kaapiya'j txã' na'wẽ': Jesusa's ikhkahn dukhwa'j kus ãjaçte', txãa kusuçte pana's kusete jxpa'gak. 24 Sa' Dxusa's weçxana orãyçxa', pe'ltedeçxa na'jĩk: Mẽ'we. Naa pana' adx kakwe's i'kwe'sx pa'gate pe'ltedenisa na'wẽsa'. Txã'wẽ myũuwe adx pxthaa yuuçxa uuni's bagaçxtewa yajkxya'. 25 Ũ'na jxã'jçxa', txã'wẽy waso's jxpa'gaçxa na'jĩk: Adx eea's pkawçxa yuuçxáa i'kwe'sxtxi nwe'wewa'ja's Dxusa' u'se pheu'jçxa nvxihtku. Aça' naa wasotewe'sxa' adx ee i'kwe'sx pa'gate uwusáa na'wẽsa'. Txã'wẽ tudx iisa adx pxthaa yuuçxa ee pkawni's bagaçxtewa kaayaki'jwa'jsa' jĩnak kaapiya'j. 26 Txã'wẽ yã'the', bagaçxtewa naa pana's ũ' iisa, naa wasotewe'sxnawa tudx iisa i'kwe Jesukristo uuni's yajkxna nes yu', txãa sxawedna kĩhpkaçx.
Santa Cena's jxpa'gaya'waçxa' na'wẽ yũuwa'ja'
27 Txã'wẽ maawa Jesukristo kaapiya'jni's kĩhwameesa na'wẽ Dxusa's yajkxmée maa sũhmée wáa pana's ũ'na wasotewe'sxnawa tudxiitxna aça' txã'wẽ yũusa' pkalte wetesá yuutxna, txã'wẽ Jesukristo kakwe vxite' txajx ee na'wẽsa's kĩhtewa seelpimeesa na'wẽçxáa yũupa'ga. 28 Txãa pa'ga ũus miiyajkxwe teeçxsa na'wẽçxah pan vxite' wasotewe'sx txãatx jxpa'gamey. 29 Txã'wẽme' nxuste wetewa'ja's peekíi pakwesáa yuutxna, txã'wẽ Jesukristo kakwe vxite' txajx ee na'wẽsa txãatx kĩhtewa seelpimeesa na'wẽ maa sũhmée jxpa'gapa'ga. 30 Txã'wẽ jxpa'yakxpa'gamée i'kwe'sx ksxavxytewe'sx maaíi yu' ãça'na çxhãçxhamée ũsta', naapkaçxha' vxite' uuna ũsta'. 31 Peekíi wala jxpa'yakxna fxi'zewaça', txã'wẽ nxuste wetewa'j en pa'jmeena. 32 Nawa Dxusa' kwe'sxtxi peeygãhçxak kastigo kaja', ki'kin txã'sna pakwete', bagaçxte yuhwa Dxus yakh puutx uywa'jmeesawe'sx yakh kaajxa'mée.
33 Na'wẽ' aça' ãçxha' adx yakhthẽ'jwe'sx, Jesukristo mesatewe'sxa's ũ'ya' pkhaakheççxa', teeçxsa na'wẽçxah puutx ya'peeygãhna ja'daçxah ũ'ya' mũythaswe. 34 Maaíi yu' wala wẽesa ũsçxa', txajx yatte ũ'wena, txã'wẽ puutx ya'peeygãhmée pkhaakhete', Dxus kastigãywe'sxte. Vxite yuwe ũsta' txãatxi's adx pa'jçxa pheu'ju'nja.
