16
Yultumu patron jĩ's ewmée jxpe'jsa
1 Txajũ' Jesusa' na'jĩnak kwentu'j txãa yakh u'jusawe'sxtxi:
—Teeçx patron wala ji'phsa' teeçx yultumu ji'phu'k. Aça' txãa yultumu ewmée yũuni's pta'sxtxi: Iidx yultumu' iidx ji'phuni's wala psuwna ũsa' jĩte', 2 txãa patrona' txajx yultumu's pa'yaçxa na'jĩk: Adx ji'phunitxi' wala psuwna ũsa' jĩ'tx sa' idxa' ¿kĩh yũuna ũsga? Txã'wẽ yã'jçxa', idxna neewe'weni adx ji'phuni's jxuka ki' mdukh adxna. Idxa' peena yu' adx yultumu selpiiya' ãjãmeene'ga jĩte', 3 yultumu' ũus yajkxya' kaçxku: Ãçxha' ¿kĩh yũuki'th adx mjĩia's patron kusa'the'? Adxa' uuthasxte mjĩiya' yu' jiimeeth sa' lmusxnu thãasna u'jute yu' thaameyuhna. 4 Aça' patron tasxu kasehnijũ maawa txãawe'sx yat-su pe'jna sã'jĩkahn na'wẽ yũuya'k yajkx: 5 Patrona's yuulsatx teeçx teeçxçxah pa'yaçxa, nyafxte utxaasa's pẽjxku: Idxa' ¿maz yuulga adx patrona's? jĩte', 6 Cien taçxo aceite yuulthu jĩk. Aça' yultumu' na'jĩk: Iidx yuuluni eça' ayga ũsa' txã'sa' dud yuh khũkhçxa, yuula's cinkwentaçxáa mki'p jĩk. 7 Txajũ' vxite'sku pẽjx: Idxa' ¿makwe yuulga? jĩte', cien açxoba skuutxh yuulthu jĩk. Aça' yultumu' na'jĩk: Iidx yuuluní eça' ayga ũsa' txã'sa' dud yuh khũkhçxa, yuula's oçxentaçxáa mki'p jĩk. 8 Txajũ' txãa yultumu ewmeesa' txã'wẽ dudtey yũuni's patrona' jiyuçxa': Ma'wẽ fxi'zewa'ja'swa dud yuh ũus yaaki'k jĩk. Aça' naa kiwete nasa pkalsa' Dxus luuçxte jxthaakwetx dud ũus yaaki' txãawe'sx ma'wẽ puutx we'wewa'ja'swa.
9 Aça' adxa' i'kwe'sxtxi' yu'kxpeje'th txã'wẽy yũukahn, naa kiwete ji'phuni's psuwmée jxpa'yakx mkseelpi'jwe namiku vxitna. Kxah naa kiwete ji'phunisa pçuutewa, vxite kiwe pçuwa'jmeete i'kwe'sxtxi jxpa'gawa'jsa ji'phune'kwe.
10 Maawa le'çxkuẽçxáa ji'phçxawa jxpa'yakx jxpe'jsa', walasa'swa txã'wẽy jxpa'yakx jxpe'jena. Aça' maawa le'çxkuẽçxáatewa jxpa'yakxmée psuwna jxpe'jsa', walasa'swa txã'wẽyçxáa jxpa'yakxmée psuwuuna. 11 Txã'wẽ naa kiwete ji'phuni'sçxáa yuhwa jxpa'yakxmée psuwna fxi'zeçte', i'kwe'sxtxi' ¿kim neewe'weçxa nvxiitukx pçuwa'jmée ji'phuwa'ja's? 12 Peekx jĩimée vxite jĩi yuhtxwa jxpa'yakxmée psuwna fxi'zeçte', ¿kim ũsukx i'kwe'sx jĩi yuuwa'ja's?
13 Maa selpisáa yuhwa e'z patrontxi selpiiya' ãjãmeena. Txã'wẽ vxiteçxá's wedxna fxi'zeçxa', vxite patron yu'sa' açena. Sa' teeçxsa jxkaahniçxá's kxtey yuuçxa', vxite patron jxkaahni yu'sa' kxtey yuuya' ãjãmeena. Txã'wẽy Dxusna selpiina naa kiwete ji'phuni'swa pa'pçxuna e'z ahtey yajkxna fxi'zeya' ãjãmeena jĩte', 14 fxariseowe'sxa' naa kiwete ji'phuniçxá's wejx pa'pçxuna fxi'zesa wẽsẽ'jçxa', Jesusa's weeçx we'wetx. 15 Aça' Jesusa' na'jĩk fxariseowe'sxtxi':
—I'kwe'sxa' naa kiwete nasaa dxi'pte yu' wala ewsa na'wẽ fxi'ze'i'kwe. Nawa Dxusa' i'kwe'sx ũus yaakxnitxi' jxuka jiia'. Naa kiwete nasaçxáa i'kwe'sxtxi' ewsata' jĩte', Dxus yu' txã'jĩmeena.
Dxus jxkaahni ley skhẽwwa'jsamée
16 Moises ley vxite' Dxus yuwe pta'sxsawe'sx kaapiya'jni' Juan Bautista entepkaçxtxi ũsu'. Kxajuk yuwe u'sesa' Dxus jxkaahwa'j ena's pta'sxni yuu, aça' maawa Dxus jxkaahnitewe'sx yuuya' wala pa'pçxu'tx. 17 Makwe cielu pçuuna, kiwewa pçuuna, nawa Dxus jxkaahni ley yu' weh yuhwa yu'pthehwa'jsame' bagaçxte yuhwa jĩk.
Ptamsa txuteewa'jme's Jesus kaapiya'jni
(Mt 19.1-12; Mr 10.1-12)
18 Maa piçthẽ'jwa peekx nyu's wãatãhçxa vxite yakh ptamuuçxa', txã' pkal yuuna. Aça' maa piçthẽ'jwa txãa u'y wãatãanisa yakh ptamuuçxa', txãawa pkal íiçxáa yuuna.
Teeçx ji'phsa vxite' puuple Lasaro
19 Txajũ' Jesusa' vxite kwentu na'wẽsak pta'sx:
—Teeçx piçthẽ'j wala ji'phsa u'pu'k. Sa' ropa ewsa yuuçxáa athçxa een isak wala weçxana fxiesta vxitu'. 20 Txãa ji'phsa yat vxitxte teeçx puuple wã'jyçxáa uta ji'phsa Lasaro yaasesak kiwete kaçxçxa u'pu'. 21 Txãa puuple yu' wẽeçxa ji'phsa mesa tasxsu kĩh ũ' pe'laçxáawa ũsxisatx ũ'wẽn ũythasna u'pte', alku' kũhçxa txãa wã'jytxi teçxi'tx. 22 Nawa teeçx ente' txãa puuple' uuk. Aça' angeleswe'sxa' jxũna u'jtx wẽt ũsnite Abrahan yakh ũskahn. Txãa e'su' ji'phsawa uuk, aça' txã'sa' pedaatx. 23 Aça' ji'phsa' uusaawe'sx ũsni ksxavxyte pxthaa yaaçxana ũsçxa', pesayçxa jxu'the uyku Lasaro yu' Abrahan yakh wẽt weçxana u'pte. 24 Txajũ' na'jĩnak sus we'we: Tata Abrahan, adxa's meen peeygãh. Seena' yuh wala pa'ga yuuna ũsthu naa ipx wala açxate. Aça' Lasaro's mkaah txajx kuse vxiççxáanawa yu'te tuupha'jçxa adx thunete tuupha'jya' yuukahn jĩte', 25 Abrahan pasçxa': Nçxi'k, miiyajkx. Idxa' nee ĩtxĩ u'juy yu', wala yuh wẽt weçxana fxi'zesa yu'g. Naapkaçxha' Lasaro yu' wala pxthaa yuuna nxuste fxi'zek. Nawa ãçxha' ayte wẽt weçxana ũsa', naapkaçxha' idxa' wala yuh pxthaa yuuna ũsgu. 26 Pxthaag we'we' nawa penxa thkafx wala txuhdena ũsa', aça' ayjxuwe'sx kxtee pesah wẽeçxawa ãjãmeetxna, sa' txajuwe'sxwa ayte pesajxa' ãjãmeetxna jĩte', 27 çxhavxysáa pasçxa': Txã'wẽyã'the', Tata Abrahan, idxa'sthu pxthaa we'we', aça' Lasaro's meen kaah adx tata yatna, 28 kxte' tahç nyakh ji'pthu txãawe'sxtxi pta'sxya' adx ayte nakwe wala pxthaa yuuna ũsthu kxtee yuukahmén jĩte', 29 Abrahan pasçxa': Txãawe'sxa' Moises vxite' Dxus yuwe pta'sxsawe'sx fxi'jnitx ji'phta' na' txãatx wẽsẽ'jetxna jĩte', 30 çxhavxysáa pasçxa': Tata Abrahan, txã'wẽ yuhta'. Nawa maaçxáawa uusaawe'sx ksxavxyu ĩtxĩ yuuçxa txãawe'sxtxi' pta'sxya' u'jwaça', pkaltxi nvxihtçxa Dxus tasxte ki' sxaweduutxna jĩk. 31 Aça' Abrahan pasçxa': Moises vxite' Dxus yuwe pta'sxsa txãawe'sxtxi nwẽesẽ'jmeeyã'jçxa', makwe uusaawe'sx ksxavxyu ki' ĩtxĩ yuuçxa pta'sxtewa, kxul yuhna jĩk.
