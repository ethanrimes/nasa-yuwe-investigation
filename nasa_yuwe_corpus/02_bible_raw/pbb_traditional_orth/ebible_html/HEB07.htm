7
Jesusa' Melkisedek na'wẽsa'
1 Naa Melkisedekha' Salem çxhabte jxkaahsak yu', nawa txãaíiçxáa pkalsawe'sx ju'gu we'we pu'çxsak yu'. Aça' teeçx ente' Abrahana' gweçxate puiina ũsçxa', naa kiwete jxkaahsa ewmeesatx knayçxa sxawedna pa'jaçte', Melkisedekha' pu'thegya' kasehçxa bedicion akhte', 2 Abrahana' diesmo dukhku makwene' dukhwa'j ji'phu' txãa yu's jxuka. (Hebreo yuwete' Melkisedek jĩnisa' Ew Jxkaahsa jĩnisa', aça' Salem jĩnisa' wẽt fxi'zeni jĩnisak yu'. Txãasa Melkisedekha' wẽt fxi'zenite ew jxkaahsa' jĩnisa'.) 3 Sa' txajx ney txajx njĩ'j txajx yaçgawe'sx txãatxi's ya'jiyu'çme', txajx upxhni en txajx uuni txã'wẽsaçxa yuhtxwa. Txãasa Dxus Nçxi'k pkalsawe'sx ju'gu we'we pu'çxna nes yuusa na'wẽysak yu' Melkisedekha'.
4 Ãçxha' ew mjiyuwe: Melkisedekha' iiméh wala jxtuhthesa yu'k. Txajíik kwe'sx yaçgawe'sx Abrahan yuhwa diesmo's txã'sna dukh gweçxate puiiçxa txajx jĩitxi's ki' kusa'jni's. 5 Moises leya' naasáa Levxi ji'j yuuçxáatxku jxkaaja' txãawe'sx nwe'sx diesmotx jxpa'gawa'jsa. 6 Txã'wẽtewa, Melkisedekha' Levxi ji'jmeeçxawa Abrahan dukhni diesmo's jxpa'gak, sa' Abrahana' makwe Dxus txhitxhnisatewa Melkisedekha' bedekĩyku. 7 Bedekĩysa' bedekĩynisate jxthaakwe walasa' txã'sa' txã'wẽme' jĩya' ãjameetxna kim yuhwa. 8 Ãçxh naa ensu kwe'sx pwe'sx dukhni diesmo's jxpa'gasa' nasa uuwa'jsaçxáata'. Nawa Dxus librute Melkisedekha's we'weçxa': Bagaçxte yuhwa uumée ĩtxi ũssa jĩna we'weç na'wẽk pta'sxi': 9 Levxi ji'jwe'sx jxukaysa na'wẽ diesmo's jxpa'ga'tx txãawe'sxwa diesmo's dukhsatx yuu Melkisedekna. Ew jiyute yu', txãawe'sx jxukaysa pa'gate Abrahanku dukh. 10 Txã'wẽk yuu jĩkahníi Abrahana' Melkisedek yakh puutx uyçxa diesmo's dukheçte', Levxi ji'j yuuwa'jsa' Abrahan kakweju kasehmeytxi yu'.
11 Ley nvxiitnisa Aaron ji'j levxiwe'sxçxáa txã'wẽ nasa pkalsatx Dxus tasxte kuutxa'jya' ãjawaça', vxite kaahwa'j peejxmeene' yu', Melkisedekwa ma'wẽga Aaron ji'thewe'sxmée yu' txã'wẽysa. 12 Txã'wẽ pkalsawe'sx ju'gu we'we pu'çxsa levxiwe'sxtxi kutxi'jçxa', leynawa phu'se'jwa'ja'. 13 Na'wẽ vxite kaahwa'ja's we'we'k txã' kwe'sxtxi jxpe'jsa Jesukristo's we'weçxak txã'jĩ'. Jesukristo' Israel nwe'sxçxawa, Aaron ji'thewe'sx meek yu'. 14 Judá ji'thewe'sxku yu' txã'sa' ew jiitha'w. Aça' Judá ji'thewe'sxa' kim yuhwa pkalsawe'sx ju'gu we'we pu'çxwa'ja's ji'phmeeta'. Txajíik Moisesa' txã'wẽ yũuwa'ja's Judá ji'thewe'sxtxi' maa yuhtxwa kxsusmée.
15 Aytek isa ew ya'jiyu' leya'swa phu'se'jwa'ja's. Txã'wẽ phu'se'khaníi pkalsawe'sx ju'gu we'we pu'çxwa'jsa' ley nvxiitnisamée, Melkisedek na'wẽsak yu'. 16 Txã' ley txhitxhçxa nvxiitnisamée, bagaçxteçxáa yuhwa pçumée ĩtxi fxi'zewa'jsak yu'. 17 Txajíik Jesusa's we'weçxa na'jĩ Dxusa':
Idxa' bagaçxtewa pkalsawe'sx ju'gu we'we pu'çxna nes yuune'ga,
Melkisedek na'wẽsa jĩ'k.
18-19 Txajíik Moises leya' nasa pkalsatx Dxus tasxte kuutxa'jya' ãjamée ma'wẽçxáa yuhwa, txãa pa'gak txãa leya's phu'se'jni yuu. Aça' ãçxha' txãa leyte jxthaakwe ewsa ji'ptha'w txãa yakha' Dxus tasxte utxaaya' ãja'tha'w.
20 Dxus tasxte kuutxa'jya' ãjawa'ja's Dxusa' nvxiituççxa' juraĩmée yu' nvxihtme'. 21 Dxus tasxte kuutxa'jya' ãjawa'jsa jĩnisa nyafxiiwe'sxtxi' Dxusa' juraĩmeek nvxiht. Nawa Jesusa's txã'wẽ yuuwa'jsa nvxiituççxa', jurãyçxak nvxiht. Txajíik Dxus librute na'jĩna we'we':
Idxa' bagaçxtewa pkalsawe'sx ju'gu we'we pu'çxna nes yuune'ga jĩnak Dxusa' txajx we'weni's jurãy na' yu'pthehmeena.
22 Txãa pa'ga Jesusa' jxthaakwe ew puutx we'weçxa nvxiitnisa'. 23 Pkalsawe'sx ju'gu we'we pu'çxsa nyafxiiwe'sxa' wala kuh yu'tx, txãa pa'gatey uunatx u'jwe'. Txãasatx teeçxçxáa yu' u'pxa' ãja'çmée. 24 Nawa Jesusa' uuwa'jsame', txãasa pkalsawe'sx ju'gu we'we pu'çxna ũswa'ja's vxitetx nvxihtmeena. 25 Sa' txã'sna pi'kxçxa Dxus tasxte utxaawa'ja's pakwesáa yuutxi's nwe'weçxa, bagaçxte yuhwa vxituwa'jmeete nvxiht-ya' ãjana, txãa pa'gatey txã' bagaçxte yuhwa uumée ĩtxi nes yuuwa'jsa', pkalsawe'sx ju'gu we'we pu'çxna.
26 Pkalsawe'sx ju'gu Dxus tasxte we'we pu'çxsa' Jesus na'wẽsa ewsatha'w peejx yu'. Txã' kĩh pkal yuhwa ji'phmeesa, bagaçxte yuhwa ewmée yũuna fxi'zesamée, naa kiwetewe'sx na'wẽsa meek yu'. Txãasak Dxus pukate kãhpkaçx jxũna u'jnisa yuu. 27 Pkalsawe'sx ju'gu we'we pu'çxwa'jsa nyafxiiwe'sxa' na'wẽtx een isa tajkx ikhçxa Dxus altalte kabu' txã'wẽ yũuwa'jsa meek yu'. Txãawe'sxa' nyafxte' peekx pkalpa'ga khuẽ'y txã'wẽ tajkx kabçxatx nasaa pkalpa'gawa txã'wẽy yũ'. Nawa Jesusa' een isa txã'wẽ tajkx ikhme', txãa yuuk uu tee dxi'jçxáa jxukaysa pa'gate. 28 Pkalsawe'sx ju'gu we'we pu'çxwa'jsatx Moises ley nvxiituççxa', naa kiwete nasa ãhmeekwesaçxáatxku nvxiitu'. Nawa ley skhẽwnite Dxus jurãyçxa txã'wẽ yũuwa'jsa nvxiituççxa', txajx Nçxi'k iiméh thaakwe ewsa'sku nvxiht bagaçxtewa nes yuuwa'jsa.
