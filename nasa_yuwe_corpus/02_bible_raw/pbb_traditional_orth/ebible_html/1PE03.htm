3
Ptamsa' na'wẽ fxi'zewa'ja'
1 U'ywe'sx iimi'sawa, i'kwe'sx nmi'tx kxtey nwẽese'jna mfxi'zewe. Txã'wẽ yũute', i'kwe'sx nmi' maaíia' Dxus yuwe's wẽse'jya' wa'lsawa 2 i'kwe'sx ew fxi'zeni's uyçxa', Dxusa's jiyuna pa'jatxna, Dxusa's mpaakwe jĩna we'wemeetewa. 3 I'kwe'sxa' peekx kakwe ãakhẽwe'sxçxá's txakwe wala zhiçxkwesa ki'pna pheu'jwa'j ji'phmée i'kwe, 4 wejxwa ũuste txã'wẽ zhiçxkwe mpheu'jwe, ew yũuwa'jçxá's yajkxna, vxitesa yakh puiimée fxi'zewa'ja'sçxáa pakwena. Txãaçxáa thaakwe ewsa' Dxus dxi'pte. 5 Nyafxíi entey u'ywe'sxa' txãawe'sx ũustetx txã'wẽ zhiçxkwe pheu'ju' Dxusçxáana neewe'wena txãawe'sx nmi'tx nwẽese'jna. 6 Sara' txã'wẽsak yu'. Abrahana's ũ'kwe's jxpe'jsa' jĩnak nwẽese'je', aça' i'kwe'sxwa txãa na'wẽysa yuune'kwe, txã'wẽy ew nwẽese'jçxa yu', ãhmeeth yũu sũhna ũukh yajkxna fxi'zemeene'kwe.
7 Txã'wẽy nmi'we'sxwa i'kwe'sx nyuu yakh jxpa'yakx ewçxáa mfxi'zewe, u'ya' wejxkuẽ' çxhãçxhameekweta' txãa pa'ga. Sa' txã'wẽçxáamée, i'kwe'sx na'wẽyçxáa Dxus yakh fxi'zesata' txãa pa'ga txã'wẽ ew mfxi'zewe, Dxus yakh puutx we'weni' kxul yuukahmen.
Ew yũupa'ga nxus knaysáa
8 Jxukaysa i'kwe'sxa' ewçxáa yũuna mfxi'zewe, vxitesatxwa teeçxsa na'wẽçxah peeygãhna. Weeçx yajkxmée zhiçxkwe puutx we'wena yakhthẽ'j pwe'sx na'wẽ mfxi'zewe. 9 Ewmée yũusa's ka'tuwa txã'wẽy ewmée yũuwa'jme', sa' wẽesxute', txã'wẽy ka'tuwa wẽesxuwa'jme', wejxwa txã'wẽ yũusatxi' ka'tu yu' ewçxáa myũuwe. Txã'wẽ ewçxáa yũukahn íiçxáak Dxusa' i'kwe'sxtxi pa'yaçxa txakwe wala ew yũu. 10 Txã'wẽ yũuwa'ja's na'wẽk pta'sxi' Dxus librute:
Maawa ewçxáa wẽt fxi'ze wẽeçxa',
Peekx thune's ewmée we'wekahmen jxpa'yakxçxa
I'kwe'sx yuweju ĩsxiinuwe.
11 Ewmeejũ txuteeçxa, ewçxáa yũuna
Puiimée fxi'zewa'jçxá's pakweçxa uu entepkaçx txã'wẽ yuuçxáa fxi'zewa'ja'.
12 Txãa pa'gatey Dxusa' jxpe'je'k ew yũusatxi',
Sa' Dxusa's pakwen neewe'weni's wẽse'je'k.
Nawa ewmée yũusatxi' txã'wẽ yũ'çme' jĩ'k.
13 Bagaçxtewa i'kwe'sxa' ewçxáa yũuçte', ¿kim txã'wẽ ka'tu' ewmée yũukx? 14 Ew yũuna fxi'zepa'ga nxuswa'j en ji'phuçxawa, weçxana wẽtwẽtçxáa meswe. Kim yuhtxwa ũukhnuwe kuh yajkxna, 15 wejxwa i'kwe'sx ũuste jxkaahsa Kristoçxá's weçxana mneewe'wewe. Na'wẽ i'kwe'sxtxi kaapiya'jni's jii i'kwe txã'sna paapẽysatxi's pasya' thaamemée meswe bagaçxtewa, sa' ũusçxa we'wemée ewçxáa mpaaswe. 16 Txã'wẽ ew yũuçxa mfxi'zewe, i'kwe'sx ũuste yuuwemeetha'w sũhwa'jçxáa ũusuthekahn, kxah Kristo yakh ew fxi'zesatx e'sga ewmée nuywe'wesawe'sxa' thaameetxna, txã'wẽ i'kwe'sxtxi ewmée nuywe'weçxa'. 17 Wejxwa ewmée yũuçxa nxus knayupkaçxte, ew yũupa'ga nxus knayni wejx ewa', Dxus yuu txã'wẽ jxthãas yã'the'.
18 Kristowa txã'wẽyku pxthaa yuuçxa uu, tee dxi'jçxáa jxukaysa pa'gate. Txã' ewsak yu', nawa kwe'sx ewmeesa pa'gate pxthaa yuuk Dxus tasxte nuypa'jya'. Txã' isa uuyujk kakwe yu', nawa ũus yu' uume'. 19 Txãasak u'j uusaawe'sx ũus preesu ũsu'tx kxtee pta'sxya'. 20 Txãawe'sxa' nyafxíi Noé ente' nwẽese'jsameetx yu' Dxusa's. Txãa ente' Noea' barko's pheu'ju'çpkaçxha', Dxusa' wala yuh ũythasku txãawe'sxtxi' nwe'weya'. Nawa oçxo nasaçxáatx yu'ju nwe'wenisa yuu. 21 Noé ente yu'a' ãçxh yu'te ya'bautisaĩ'tha'w ewmeejũ jxukate txuteeya' sũhçxa txã'wẽsak yu'. Nawa bautismo' pkala's yaate'çme', naasáa Dxus dxi'pte peena yu' pkalte fxi'zeya'mée sũhçxaçxáak ya'bautisãynisa yu'. Aça' Jesukristo uuçxa ki' ĩtxi yuunisa yuuçxáak kwe'sxtxi' nwe'we. 22 Ãçxha' txã' cielun u'jçxa Dxus pukate jxkaahna ũsa', aça' txã'sa' angeleswe'sx ma'wẽsawa txã'sçxaçxáanatx nwẽese'je'.
