7
Israelita ji'jwe'sx iisawa'jniitx yuu
1 Txãa e'su' pahz angeleswe'sxtxith uy, txãawe'sxa' naa kiwe peeku'the puutx pahz ahtetx yujuçxa ũsu', naa kiwete pahz ajũ wejxa kasehsatx ka'ga'jmée yupna, naa kiwete, marte, yu'khsu mtee yuhwa wejxakahmen. 2 Sa' vxite angel uythu txãa yu' sek kãani ju'guk yuh yu', Dxus ĩtxĩsa selxo's atçxa. Txãa Dxus angela' susku we'we pahz angeleswe'sx ũsu'tx naa kiwe vxite' mar txãatx suwwa'jsatx na'jĩna:
3 —Naa kiwe, mar, yu'kh mtee yuhwa ãçxhíi yu' suwnuwe, Dxusna selpisaawe'sx dxikthesu kwe'sx iisawa'jmeyna yu' jĩ'k.
4 Txajũ' wẽse'th Israelita ji'jwe'sx iisawa'jnisa jxuka yu' ciento kuarenta y kuatro miltxi yu'. 5 Judá ji'j, doce mil; Rubén ji'j, doce mil; Gad ji'j, doce mil; 6 Aser ji'j, doce mil; Nefxtalí ji'j, doce mil; Manasés ji'j, doce mil; 7 Simeón ji'j, doce mil; Levxí ji'j, doce mil; Isakar ji'j, doce mil; 8 Zabulón ji'j, doce mil; José ji'j, doce mil; Benjamín ji'j, doce mil; txãawe'sxtxi jxukaçxah yu' txazkuẽe yu'.
Maa nasawa Dxus nwe'wenisawe'sx
9 Txãa e'su' ki' thegçxa' nasa iiméh wala kuh uythu. Txãawe'sxa' maa nasa ma'wẽsa majũwe'sxwa jxukatx yu', yuwe fxiyfxiyçxah we'wesa. Sa' Piisxakuẽ jĩnisa jxkaahna u'pni peeku'jsutx yujuçxa ũsu'. Txãawe'sxtxi' kim yuhwa isaya' ãjãmeeta', iiméh wala kuh yu'tx txãa pa'ga. Sa' ropa çxihmesa athçxa kusesu yu' lamus eçnatx yaat yu'. 10 Txãawe'sx jxukaysa na'jĩnatx sus we'we':
Kwe'sx Dxus jxkaahna u'pa' vxite' Piisxakuẽ jĩnisa txãawe'sx yuuçxáatx ewte neeyũuwa'ja's ũs jĩ'tx.
11 Aça' Dxus jxkaahna ũsni peeku'jsu vxite' Dxus luuçxtxi jxkaahsawe'sx peeku'jsu vxite' Dxus vxitni ĩtxĩsa pahz ũsu'tx txãa peeku'jsu angeleswe'sx yujuçxa ũssa' jxkaahna ũsni ju'gu kiwete pa'j kĩiçhuçxa Dxusa's na'jĩnatx neewe'we':
12 Weçxana tuuthe'jwa'ja' kwe'sx Dxusa's.
Txãaçxáa kĩhtewa jxuka jiisa'.
Txã' bagaçxtewa çxhãçxhasa jxtuhthe jxkaajxa' ãjãwa'jsa' jĩ'tx.
13 Txajũ' Dxus luuçxtxi jxkaahsa teeçxsa' na'jĩnak adxa's pẽjx:
—Na'wẽ ropa çxihme athçxa ũsta' txãawe'sxa' ¿kimtxna, sa' majũ yuusatxna? jĩte':
14 —Txãawe'sxtxi' idxíiçxáa jiine'ga jĩnath pas. Aça' txã' na'wẽk pta'sx:
—Txãawe'sxa' iiméh seena' wala nxuste skhẽwsaata', sa' txãawe'sx athni ropatxi's thethçxa Piisxakuẽ jĩnisa ikhni yuuk txajx eete yaatesata'. 15 Txãasa Dxus jxkaahna ũsni tasxte ũsta', en ma'k yuu meeçxa' kus ma'k yuu nawa bagaçxtewa selpiina nes yuuwa'jsata'. Aça' kxtee jxkaahna u'psa yakh ũssa' kĩhte yuhwa peejxmée fxi'zetxna. 16 Txãa pa'ga txãawe'sxa' ãçxha' pxthaa yuumeetxna wẽena yũ'wẽena. Sa' sektewa çxhavxyna yaaçxana fxi'zemeetxna, 17 txãa pa'gatey Piisxakuẽ jĩnisa jxkaahna ũsni ksxavxyte ũsa' txajx jxpe'jni yuutxna. Sa' yu' bagaçxte yuhwa pçuwa'jmeesa's yusni yuutxna. Aça' Dxusíi txãawe'sx ũ'neni yafx yu'txi's khukhuna jĩ'k.
