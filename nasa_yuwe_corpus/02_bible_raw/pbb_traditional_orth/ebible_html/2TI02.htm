2
Idxa' soldau ew nwẽese'jsa na'wẽ myuu
1 Adx nçxi'k na'wẽsa idxa' Kristo Jesus pu'çxni's jxpa'gaçxa Dxus yakh çxhãçxha mfxi'ze. 2 Nasa maazkuẽesa ksxavxyte adx ma'wẽne'tka pta'sx txãa pta'sxni'síiçxáa mkaapiya'j Dxus yakh ew fxi'zesatx, kxah txãawe'sxwa vxitetx txã'wẽyçxáa kaapiya'jatxna.
3 Idxa' Jesukristoçxá's nwẽese'jsa yã'jçxa', nxuswa'j en thẽysa pa'thewa txãatxi' ũukhmée çxhãçxha myajkx. 4 Soldauwa ma'wẽga vxitesu yu' nwẽese'jmée soldau npiiçthẽ'j jxkaahniçxá's nwẽese'je' txã'wẽy myũu. 5 Txã'wẽy knayya' pweesa'sawa ma'wẽ jxkaathe' kxtey nwẽese'jna pa'pçxumeeçxa yu', kĩh yuhwa knaymeena. 6 Mjĩisawa nyafxte' mjĩiwa'ja's khuẽ'y çxhãçxha mjĩiwa'j ji'pha' uuthasx thẽ'the' ũ'wa'j uyya'. 7 Adx we'wenitxi's jiyuya' ũus myajkx, aça' Jesukristo' kaajiyu'juna jxuka.
8 Ãçxha' Jesukristo' jxkaahsa Davxid ji'j uuçxawa ki' ĩtxi yuuk txã'sna myajkx. Adxa' txã'snath pta'sxi' ewte nvxiitwa'jsa' jĩna. 9 Txã'wẽ Kristo yuwe ewsa's pta'sxpa'ga yuuçxáa preesu ũsthu yuuwesa na'wẽ tudni pxthaa yuuna. Nawa Dxus yuwe yu's tud-ya' ãjameetxna. 10 Txã'wẽ Dxus yuwe's yup-ya' ãjawa'jme' açíiçxáath adx nxuswa'ja's ũukhmée Dxus yuwe's pta'sxi' Dxus txhitxnisa nwe'wewa'j ũstxna txãawe'sxtxi peeygãhçxa, kxah Kristo Jesus yakh utxaapa'ga txãawe'sxwa ji'phutxna pçuwa'jmée weçxana ewte ũswa'ja's.
11 Na'wẽ yũuwa'ja's pta'sxnisa' ĩsxiinisame':
Kristo's ũuste jxpa'gaçxa uuçxa', txãa na'wẽy ĩtxi fxi'ze'nja'w.
12 Makwe nxuste fxi'zewa'ja's ji'phuçxawa, cielute txãa yakh jxkaahwa'j yu'sa' ivxiitume'nja'w.
Txã'wẽ yũumée wa'luwá'nja'w aça' txãawa kwe'sxtxi' jxpa'gameena.
13 Meeçxa' kwe'sxa' txãa yakh kxtey yuumeewá'nja'w, nawa txã' txajx puutx we'weni's kxtey yuuna nes yuuna.
Txãa pa'gatey Dxusa' txajx puutx we'weni's ĩsxiiwa'jsame'.
Kĩhte yuhwa sxiigmée fxi'zewa'jçxá's pa'pçxuni
14 Yakhthẽ'jwe'sxtxi yu'kxpehni's mkaayaki'j Dxus dxi'pte, kxah selpiwa'jmeesu we'weniçxá's ũusçxa pasna fxi'zemeetxna. Txã'wẽsa' selpi'çme', naasáa wẽse'jsatx kaypuba'jsaçxá'. 15 Dxus dxi'pte kĩhte yuhwa sxiigmée wala ew fxi'zewa'jçxá's mpa'pçxu, Dxus yuwe's jxpubamée pta'sxna, mjĩisawa ma'wẽga ew mjĩiçxa' thaame'çmée txã'wẽy myũu. 16 Selpiwa'jmeesuçxáa we'wena kaapiya'jnitx mpa'yajkx. Txã'wẽme' txã'wẽ kaapiya'jsa' jweeíiçxáatx een isa jxpubana ewmeete wetena u'jwe'. 17 Naapkaçxha' txãawe'sx kaapiya'jni yu' jweeíiçxáa mteewa jxuka suwna u'jwena. Himeneo vxite' Fxiletowe'sxa' txã'wẽ yũusatx yu'. 18 Txãasatx isa pta'sxnijũ yu' jxpubaçxa uusaawe'sx ĩtxi yuuwa'j ena' skhẽwyã'kh jĩna kaypuba'jna ũsta' Dxus yakh fxi'zesatx maaíitxi's. 19 Nawa maaçxah txãa jĩitewa ew jiyukahn Dxusíi na'jĩna we'weçxak nvxiht: Dxusa' jiia' maaçxah txãa jĩitewa jĩ'k. Sa': Jxukaysa Dxus yakh fxi'zesa' ewmeejũ jxuka txutewa'jsata' jĩ'k.
20 Miiyajkx: Teeçx yatte' iijxũnisa vxyuu beh vxyuu çxihme txã'wẽ ewsaçxáa ũsmeeta'. Kxteea' açxamée selpisáa fxtũu, kiwe mitxhwa ũsta'. Nawa iijxũni ewsa' ewsaçxá's selpiwa'jsa', aça' açxamée ewsa' ewmeesuçxáa selpiwa'jsata'. 21 Txã'wẽ Dxus kusete ew selpi wẽeçxa', ewmeejũ jxuka txuteeçxa bagaçxte yuhwa pkalte wetemée Dxus yakh yuuçxáa fxi'zewa'ja'. Txã'wẽ yuuçxáa selpiiya' ya'ãjana kĩhtewa ew yũuwa'ja's.
22 Idxa' piçtakxwe'sx ewmée yajkxna fxi'zenijũ txuteeçxa kĩhte yuhwa jxpubamée ewçxáa mfxi'ze, Dxusçxáana yajkxna, maa yakhwa ewçxáa fxi'zena peeygãhna Dxusa's ũusuh jxuka neewe'wesawe'sx yakh utxaaçxa. 23 Yuwe kĩhtewa selpiwa'jmeesatxi' nwẽese'jnu, yuwe txã'wẽsatxi' jiiyã'jg puiiwa'jçxáak kaseje'. 24 Txãa pa'gatey Dxusa's selpisá' puiisu fxi'zewa'jme', wejxwa jxukaysa yakh ewçxáa yũuna fxi'zewa'ja'. Sa' ew kaapiya'jsa yuuwa'ja', ewmée nuywe'wenitx yajkxmée. 25 Txã'wẽ ũusuh jxuka peeygãhna açesatx yu'kxpehwa'ja', Dxusa' txãawe'sx ewmée yaakxnitx yu'pthehçxa ewsa's kaajiyu'jwaça' sũhna. 26 Kxah eçxthẽ'j kusete fxi'zeni's jiyuçxa', txãajũ iyuya' ãjatxna. Txã'wẽme' eçxthẽ'ja' ma'wẽwa suwya' ũsa'.
