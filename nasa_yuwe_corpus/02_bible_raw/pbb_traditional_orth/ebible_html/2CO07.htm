1 Dxusa' txã'wẽ ew fxi'zewa'ja'sku jxkaaja'. Txãa pa'ga, yakhthẽ'jwe'sx, wala jxpa'yakx fxi'zewa'j ji'ptha'w, kwe'sx kakwe ma'k yuu meeçxa' kwe'sx ũus ma'k yuu nawa suwwa'jsa yu'sa' ka'ga'jwa'j ji'phmeetha'w. Dxusçxáana yajkxna jxpa'yakx fxi'zewa'j ji'ptha'w.
Korintotewe'sx ewmée yũunijũ sxawedni
2 Adxa's açeyaakxnuwe. Adxa' kim yuhtxwa ĩsxiina kaypuba'jna ewmée yũuna ũsmeeth. 3 Aça' i'kwe'sxçxáa yuuwesa i'kwe jĩçxa txã'jĩ'çmeeth. Txã'wẽ yajkxmeeçxaíith adx peeygãanisa i'kwe, txãasa adx yakh ja'daçxah fxi'zena txutemey uuwa'jsatha'w jĩnath we'we. 4 Makwe seena' nxuswa'j ente ũsçxawa, i'kwe'sxtxi yajkxçxa weçxanaçxáath fxi'ze', weh yuhwa ewmée yu' yajkxmée.
5 Macedonia kiwete pa'jíi le'çxkuẽçxáa yuhwa jxkaasewa'j ji'phumeeth. Kĩhwa jxukak thẽysaçxaçxáa vxyaa, adx pekũhnisu yu' mteewa jxuka. Açesawe'sx yakh pdxi'puwa'jwa ji'phuth, sa' ũustewa ũukh yaakxwa'j kasejk. 6 Nawa Tito pa'pha'ga Dxusa' adxa's kvxiisxa'kh, nxusna ũstewa. 7 Nawa Tito yakh puutx uypa'gaçxáa adxa's txã'wẽ weçxa en pa'jme'. Tito' adx tasxte seena' wala weçxana pa'kh, i'kwe'sx ew yũuya' yaakxpa'ga. Sa' pta'sxku txakwe wala ãhmée yũuçxawa idx yakh weçxaya' puutx uy wẽje'tx jĩnisak wejx wala kvxiisxa'j.
8 ¿Kĩjxa'ki'th i'kwe'sxtxi juuna' we'wena txã'wẽ karta fxi'j? sũhna wala kuh yajkxthu txãa ensu yu'. Nawa ãçxha' txã'wẽ yaaki'çmeeth. Nyafxíi yu' txã'wẽ yaakxkahníi txãa karta' i'kwe'sxtxi wala kxũusu'kh maz en yu'. Txagaçxhíiçxáa juuna' we'weni's kuh yajkxçxa', ãçxha' adxa' weçxa'th. 9 Nawa i'kwe'sxtxi kxũusu'jçxa weeçx yajkxna txã'wẽ weçxaçmeeth, wejxwa txãa juuna' we'wenisa' i'kwe'sxtxi wala selpiik, ewmée yũuniijũ kaasxwedu'jna, txãa pa'gaçxáath weçxá'. Aça' i'kwe'sxa' txãa nxus en pa'pha'ga ũusçxa yajkxmée Dxus dxi'pte ew fxi'zewa'jçxá's i'kwe kxtey yuu. Txãa pa'gath adxa' kimnawa açesameeth sũju'. 10 Txãa pa'gatey ãhmée ne'tka'w yuu sũhna wa'lmée Dxus yakh ew fxi'zewa'ja's pakwete', ewmée yũuni's nvxihtçxa ewte neeyũuwa'ja'sku ya'jypa'ga'. Txãa pa'ga nxuswa'j entxi' açewa'jme'. Nawa ãhmée ne'tka'w yuu sũhwa'ja's yajkxmeete', vxituwa'jçxáak kaseje'. 11 I'kwe'sxa' Dxusçxáana yajkxna ne'kwe txãa nxusnisa's ahçemée jxpa'ga. Aça' txã'wẽ yũupa'ga ma'wẽga ew fxi'zewa'j kaseh txã'sa' miiyajkxwe naata': Ãhmée ne'tka'w yuu sũhna ũukhçxa yuwe's pheu'jya' yajkx i'kwe, txãa ewmée yũusa's kastigãywa'ja's puutx we'wena. Txã'wẽ yũuçxa', adx yakh puutx uyya' yajkx i'kwe. Txãasa i'kwe yuuwemeesa yuu. 12 Adxa' txãa karta's fxi'jçxa', txãa ewmée yũusa's açeyajkxna txã'wẽ yũumeeth, sa' ewmée yũute nxussa'swa yajkxna txã'wẽ fxi'jmeeth, naasáa i'kwe'sxtxi' wala yuh yaaki'th Dxus dxi'pte txã'sa' i'kwe'sx jiyukahnçxáath txã'wẽ fxi'j. 13 Txãasath i'kwe'sx kxtey yuute' wala weçxawa'ja's jxpa'ga.
Nawa na'wẽ weçxawa'ja's ji'phuth txãajũ jweeíiçxáath wala weçxawa'j ji'phu i'kwe'sxa' Tito's wala ewçxáa jxpa'gaçxa ewçxáa ne'kwe jxpe'jna ũsu' aça' weçxanak sxawed naa pa'ga. 14 Adxa' Tito's kajaççxa' i'kwe'sxtxi we'wena: Wala ewsata' jĩth txã' ĩsxiini kasehme'. I'kwe'sxa' adx yaakxni's kxtey i'kwe yuu, Tito's ew jxpa'gana. Txãasa adx we'weni' bagaçxtewa isasaçxaçxáata'. 15 I'kwe'sxa' ma'wẽne'kwe ewkuẽçxáa jxpa'gaçxa txajx we'weni's kxtey nwẽese'j txã'wẽ ew yũuni's yajkxçxak Tito' jweeíiçxáa i'kwe'sxtxi's wala weçxá'. 16 I'kwe'sxtxi's kĩhte yuhwa ewmée yaakxwa'j ji'phmeeth, txãasath weçxá'.
