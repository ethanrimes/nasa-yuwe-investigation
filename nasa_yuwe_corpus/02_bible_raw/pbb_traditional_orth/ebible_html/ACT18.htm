18
Korinto çxhabte Pablo pa'jni
1 Txãa e'su', Pablo' Atenasuh kasehçxa Korinto çxhabnak u'j. 2 Kxte' teeçx judiu Akila yaasesa yakhku puutx uy, Ponto kiwejuwe'sx. Akila txajx nyu Priscila yã'jçxa u'seçxáa Italiaju pa'jçxatx kxte ũsu', Romate jxkaahsa Klaudio' judiuwe'sxtxi txajũ jxuka kutxi'pha'ga. Aça' Pablo' txãawe'sx yatna u'jçxa Akilawe'sx yattek yuju. 3 Txãawe'sx na'wẽy tultu kaççxak txweyna fxi'zesa yu', txãasak kxte neeyũu txãawe'sx yakh mjĩina. 4 Sa' jxkaase ensu' judiuwe'sx pkhaakheni yatna u'jçxa, kxte judiuwe'sx vxite' judiumeesatxwa Dxus yuwe's pta'sxi'k Dxus jĩi knayya'.
5 Silas Timoteo yakh Macedonia kiweju pa'the', Pablo' vxite mjĩia's nvxihtçxa, Dxus yuwe pta'sxwa'the yuuçxáak yuju een isa, Jesusa' Dxus tasxuh yuusa Kristo yuh yu'k jĩna judiuwe'sxtxi kaajiyu'jna. 6 Nawa txãawe'sxa' txã'wẽ pta'sxni's jxpeejxmeetha'w jĩçxa ahçena weeçxwa'ja's uytxi. Aça' Pablo':
—Adxa' yuuwemeeth, i'kwe'sxtxi pta'sx yã'th. Aça' yuuwesa' i'kwe'sxi'kwe peekx uuwa'ja's pakwena ũspa'ga jĩna txajx athni ropa's ya'tatak. Sa' txã'wẽ yũupa'ga ãçxha' u'jweçthu judiu ji'jmée vxite nasatx pta'sxya' jĩk.
7 Txã'jĩna'wa judiuwe'sx pkhaakheni yatuh kasehna'wa, teeçx piçthẽ'j Justo yaasesa yatnak u'j, judiuwe'sx pkhaakheni yat pukateyçxáa. Txãa piçthẽ'ja' Dxusna neewe'wena fxi'zesak yu'. 8 Txajũ' judiuwe'sx pkhaakheni yattewe'sx npiiçthẽ'jsa Krispo' txajx ũuste Jesusa's nwẽese'jya' yajkxku petx txajx yattewe'sx yã'jçxa jxuka. Txã'wẽy Korintotewe'sxwa wala kuhsa Kristo's jiyuçxa ũuste jxpa'gatx yu'te ya'bautisãyna.
9 Txãa e'su' Jesusa' teeçx kuste na'jĩnak vxyaaçxa we'we Pablo's:
—Weh yuhwa ũukhmée Dxus yuwe's mpeeta'sx. Txã'wẽme', naa çxhabte adxna nwẽese'jwa'jsa wala ũs íiçxáata'. 10 Txãa pa'ga adxa' idx yakh pi'kxna ũsthu, aça' kim yuhwa idxa's ikhya' yu' ãjameetxna.
11 Txã'jĩyã'the', Pablo' Korinto çxhabte txãawe'sx ksxavxyte Dxus yuwe's kaapiya'jna ũsu'k anxu y medio ãhpkaçx.
12 Nawa Akaya kiwete ne'jwe'sx Galión ensu' judiuwe'sxa' Pablo's ahçeçxa khuẽs tasxte jxũna u'jtx. 13 Sa' na'jĩnatx pta'sx ne'jwe'sxa's:
—Dxusa's nwẽese'jwa'ja's ley jxkaaja'k txãa jxkaahnijũ kasehçxa Dxusa's fxiy nwẽese'jwa'ja'sku kaapiya'ja' naa piçthẽ'ja' jĩte', 14 Pablo pasya'wagaçxha', ne'jwe'sx Galión khuẽ'y judiuwe'sxtxi we'wek:
—Yuwe ewmeesa's adxa's pãatxhĩ'kheçwaça', pu'çxhiwá'nja i'kwe'sx pãatxhĩ'khni's. 15 Nawa i'kwe'sx pwe'sx fxi'zeni leyçxá' aça' txã'sa' i'kwe'sx pwe'sx mpheu'jwe. Adxa' txãasu' thegu ji'phmeeth jĩçxa, 16 txajũ eka kutxi'kh. 17 Aça' judiuwe'sx pkhaakheni yatte npiiçthẽ'jsa Sostenesa's uweçxa, pãpatx griegowe'sx jxukaysa ne'jwe'sx dxi'pte. Nawa Galiona' kuçx ma'jĩme'.
Pablo Dxus yuwe's pta'sxya' ki' kasejk naa yakha' tekh us
18 Txã'wẽtewa, Pablo' wala kuh en ũsu'k kxte. Txãa e'suk kxte yakhthẽ'jwe'sxçxáatx nvxihtçxa, Siria kiwen u'jwa'j ji'phu barkote khẽeçxa Priscila Akila yã'jçxa. Txãa dxi'j u'jweççxa', Cenkrea çxhabte pa'jçxa barkote khẽemey txajx dxkhasa's jxuka ktxiidxi'k. Dxus yakh yuuçxáa fxi'zewa'ja's Dxus yakh puutx we'wenak txã'wẽ yũu. 19 Txajũ' Efxeso çxhabte pa'jçxa', Pablo' Priscila Akilawe'sxtxi nvxihtna'wa, judiuwe'sx pkhaakheni yatna u'kh, kxte judiuwe'sxtxi Dxus yuwe's kaajiyu'jya'. 20 Aça' txãawe'sxa' wala pxthaa we'wetx kuh en ũskahn. Nawa Pablo' kxtey yuumée, 21 txajũ ki' kasejk na'jĩçxa:
—Jerusalente fxiesta u'puya'wa' kxte ũswa'j ji'pthu. Nawa ki' sxawedu'nja i'kwe'sxtxi thegya', Dxus ma'wẽ jxthãaste' jĩna we'weçxa, Efxeso çxhabuh barkote khẽeçxa u'kh. 22 Sa' Cesarea çxhabte pa'jçxa', Jerusalennak u'j kxte pkhaakhesawe'sxtxi weçxaya'. Txajũ' Antiokía çxhabnak u'j. 23 Kxte maz en ũsçxaçxáa, txajũ ki' kasejk Galacia Fxrigia kiwesu nyafxíi pta'sxnisu jxuka pekũjxa', Dxus yakh fxi'zesawe'sxtxi kçxhãaçxha'jna.
Efxeso çxhabte Apolos Dxus yuwe's pta'sxni
24 Txãa ensu' teeçx judiu Apolos yaasesa Alejadría çxhabuwe'sx pa'kh Efxeso çxhabte, Dxus libru Moises fxi'jni's ew jiisa kaapiya'jya' sxiigmeesa. 25 Sa' Jesukristo kimtewa pta'sxni jiik yu', txãasak Juan yu'te bautisãyniçxáana jiiçxawa txajx jiyuni yu'sa' jxpubamée kaapiya'ja'. 26 Apolosa' judiuwe'sx pkhaakheni yatte u'kaçxa sxiigmeek pta'sxi'. Aça' Priscila Akilawe'sx jiyuçxa', txãa Apolos txã'sçxáa fxii pe'jna u'jçxa, jweeíiçxáatx ew pta'sx Jesukristo yakh fxi'zewa'ja's jxuka. 27 Aça' Apolosa' Akaya kiwen u'jweçte', yakhthẽ'jwe'sxa' u'jwa'ja's pu'çxçxa teeçx karta fxi'jtx Akayate yakhthẽ'jwe'sx wa'lmée jxpa'gakahn. Txã'wẽ Akayate pa'jçxa', wala pu'çxku Dxus peeygãhçxa nwe'wenisatx na'wẽ yũuna: 28 Jesusa' Dxus tasxuh yuusa Kristo yuh yu'k, i'kwe'sx ũythasni txã'sna i'kwe judiuwe'sx jxpuba jĩnak pta'sxi', Dxus librute ma'wẽ fxi'jni ũsna txãa yakh kaaja'da'jna.
