3
Ma'wẽsa anciano yũuwa'ja'swa pta'sxni
1 Isath we'we': Maawa dxuus yatte jxkaahwa'ja's jxpa'gasa anciano' mjĩi ewsa's jxpa'gasa yuuna. 2 Nawa kxtee jxkaahwa'ja's jxpa'gasa' kĩhte yuhwa jxpubamée wala ew fxi'zesa yuuwa'ja', ãhmée yũute yu'kxpehni fxi'zewa'jsame'. Sa' peekx nyuu yakhçxáa fxi'zesa yuuwa'ja'; kĩhtewa jxuka jxpa'yakx fxi'zesa yuuwa'ja', sa' zhiçxkwe juuna' yuumée fxi'zesa yuuwa'ja'; paadeesatx yat pki'jna fxi'zesa yuuwa'ja', sa' ewsa's ma'wẽ kaapiya'jwa'ja'swa jxuka jiisa yuuwa'ja'. 3 Tũuna fxi'zesa yuuwa'jme' puii jxpakwena, vxyu knaywa'jçxá's yajkxna fxi'zesa yuuwa'jme', wejxwa vxyu's yajkxmée peeygãhna pu'çxna ewçxáa yũuna fxi'zesa yuuwa'ja'. 4 Peekx yattewe'sxtxi ew zhiçxkwe jxpe'jsa yuuwa'ja', sa' peekx luuçxwa nwẽese'jna ew fxi'zekahn kaapiya'jsa yuuwa'ja'. 5 Txã'wẽ peekx yattewe'sx yuhtxwa ew jxpe'jya' ãjameeçxa', ¿ma'wẽ Dxus luuçx yuutxi' jxpe'jya' ãjakx? 6 Dxuus yatte jxkaahsa' Kristo's u'se jxpa'gasa yuuwa'jme'. Txã'wẽme', wala iiwejçx yajkxçxa ewmeete weteena eçxthẽ'j weteniitey. 7 Nasa Dxusa's jiimeesa ksxavxysuwa wala ew fxi'zesa yuuwa'ja', txã'wẽ eçxthẽ'j kusete weteya'mée, nasa ewmée we'wewe'sxte.
Ma'wẽsa diákono yũuwa'ja'swa pta'sxni
8 Txã'wẽy dxuus yatte pkhaakhesatx theg pu'çxsa diakonowa Dxus dxi'pte wala ew fxi'zesa yuuwa'ja'. Kĩh yũuya' puutx we'weçxawa ĩsxiimée kxtey yuusáa yuuwa'ja'. Tũumée fxi'zesa yuuwa'ja', vxyuu knaywa'jçxá's wejx pa'pçxuna fxi'zesa yuuwa'jme'. 9 Dxus yakh fxi'zewa'ja's ew jiisa yuuna sa' ũustewa jxpubamée ũsthu sũhwa'ja's ji'phusa yuuwa'ja'. 10 Ma'wẽsatewa jiyuya' nyafxte maz en nvxihtçxa, kĩhtewa ewçxáa fxi'zeni's uyçxa txã'wẽ dxuus yatte pkhaakhesatx theg pu'çxwa'jsa nvxiitwa'ja'. 11 Txã'wẽy u'ywa dxuus yatte selpiina fxi'zesa' Dxus dxi'pte wala ew fxi'zesa yuuwa'ja', vxitesatx nuywe'wemée, kĩhtewa jxuka jxpa'yakx ewçxáa fxi'zesa yuuwa'ja'. 12 Dxuus yatte pkhaakhesatx theg pu'çxsa diakono' peekx nyuu yakhçxáa fxi'zesa yuuwa'ja', sa' peekx yattewe'sxtxi ew jxpe'jsa yuuwa'ja'. 13 Txã'wẽ diakonowe'sxa' txãawe'sx mjĩ's ew mjĩiçxa', tuhthetx fxi'ze', sa' Kristo Jesus yakh fxi'zewa'ja's thaamemée pta'sxwa'ja's uyu'tx.
Kwe'sx ewte neeyũuwa'ja's kim yuhnawa kaajiyu'jnimeek ũsu'
14 Idx yakh puutx uyya' dud yuwe'nja sũju'th, nawa meeçxa' jxthẽeyuuwá'nja sũhçxa naa karta's fxi'jaçthu, 15 kxah Dxus yakh fxi'zesa yakh ew fxi'zeya' ma'wẽ yũuwa'ja'swa jiyune'ga. Txãawe'sxa' Dxus ĩtxisa luuçxta', txãasatx isa ewsa's nvxihtmée txãate yuuçxáa fxi'ze'. 16 Kwe'sx ewte neeyũuwa'ja's kim yuhwa jiyunimeek ũsu' txã'sa' Dxusa' kaavxya'ja'kh txã' na'wẽ seena' walasa':
Dxusa' nasaa kakwe yakhku vxyaa,
Sa' Dxus Espiritu we'weçxa': Txã' kĩh pkal yuhwa ji'phmeesa' jĩnik yuu.
Aça' Dxus angeleswe'sxwa uyniisak yuu.
Sa' mtee mteewa jxuka pta'sxni yuute',
Naa kiwete msuuwe'sxwa maaíi yu' txãawe'sx ũuste jxpa'gatx.
Txajũ' cielute ki' jxpa'ganik yuu.
