6
1 Kristo's jiiyã'jsa' luuçxwe'sx i'kwe'sx neywe'sxtxi nwẽese'jna mfxi'zewe, Kristo' txã'wẽk jxkaaja' naa pa'ga. 2 Txã'wẽ kxtey nwẽese'jsatxi's Dxusa' wala txaçx kaafxi'ze'jya' neeyũuk txã'sa' Moises leyte na'jĩna pta'sxna fxi'jçxa nvxiitni ũsa': Peekx neywe'sxtxi nwẽese'jçxa', 3 wala ew fxi'zene'ga naa kiwete wala txaçx jĩ'k.
4 Neywe'sxwa i'kwe'sx luuçxtxi zhiçxkwe ewçxáa miipe'jwe, Kristo's jiyuwa'ja'sçxáa kaapiya'jna.
5 Selpisaawe'sxwa naa kiwete i'kwe'sx patronwe'sxtxi wa'lmée mwẽese'jwe, Kristo yu's nwẽese'je'ç na'wẽ yajkxna. 6 Patronwe'sx thegna ũsteçxáa txã'wẽ kxtey yuuwa'jme', wejxwa Kristo's nwẽese'je'ç na'wẽ yajkxna kxtey yuuwa'ja', ũuste weçxana. 7 I'kwe'sx mjĩia's wa'l yajkxmée Kristo's selpiiçthu sũhna ew mjĩiwa'ja', nasatx selpiiçthu sũhmée. 8 I'kwe'sxa' jiiyã'ji'kwe patron ji'phsa ma'k yuu patron ji'phmeesa ma'k yuu nawa txã'wẽ yũusatxi' Dxus dewewa'jsa' teeçxsa na'wẽçxah ma'wẽ mjĩisatewa. 9 Patronwe'sxwa i'kwe'sxtxi selpisaatx juuna' yũumée miipe'jwe, txãa pa'gatey jii i'kwe selpisaawe'sx vxite' patronwe'sxwa jxukaysa Kristo cielute ũssa teeçx yuuçxáana nwẽese'jsai'kwe. Txãa pa'gatey Kristo dxi'pte' maa yuhwa thaakwe walasa meeta'.
Ewmeesa's deewewa'ja's jxpa'gani
10 Ãçxha', yakhthẽ'jwe'sx, çxhãçxha mfxi'zewe Kristo yakh txutemée, aça' txã' pu'çxna nes yuuna bagaçxtewa kçxhãaçxha'jna. 11 Txãa pa'gatey ewmeesa yakh puiiwa'ja's Dxusa' jxuka ũsku, aça' txãa yakh puiiçxa', eçxthẽ'j ewmée yũunite wetemeene'kwe. 12 Txã'wẽme', naa kiwete nasaa pwe'sx yakh puiina ũsmeetha'w. Naa kiwete eçxthẽ'j ma'wẽ seena' ewmée kazx txãaçxaçxáa jxkaahna ũsna txãa yakh kwe'sxa' pdxi'puçxa puiina ũstha'w. 13 Txãa pa'ga ewmeesa yakh puiiwa'j Dxus ũsni's jxuka mpa'gawe, ewmeesa yakh pdxi'puçxa ũukhmée çxhãçxha deeweya'. Txã'wẽ yũuçxa', ewmeesa ma'wẽsa'swa i'kwe'sx yuh knayuune'kwe. 14 Txãasa ãçxha' ũukhmée çxhãçxha meswe, Dxus yuwe isasaçxá's ũuste ew yu'akhçxa kxtey nwẽese'jna. Ew yũuwa'j yuuçxá's ũuste jxuka mji'phuwe. 15 Sa' Dxus yuwe ewte nvxiitwa'jsa's mteewa pta'sxya' u'jya'çxáa meswe. 16 Aça' Dxusa' kĩhtewa jxuka pu'çxhina sũhwa'j yuuçxá'sa' weh yuhwa peçxkananuwe. Txã'wẽ yaakxni yakha' eçxthẽ'j makwe seena' yuuçtewa, jxuka knayuune'kwe.
17 Dxus nwe'wenijũyã'ja' txãajũ weh yuhwa txutenuwe, sa' Dxus Espiritu kaapiya'jni Dxus yuwe'swa weh yuhwa peçxkananuwe. 18 Txã'wẽçxáa yũumée, Dxus Espiritu's pi'kxçxa Dxus yakh yuuçxáa bagaçxtewa puutx we'wena meswe, çxhãçxha fxi'zewa'ja's pẽjxna. Sa' Dxus yakh puutx we'wewa'ja's bagaçxte yuhwa peçxkanamée meswe, Dxus luuçx jxukaysa pa'gatewa Dxusa's mneewe'wewe. 19 Sa' adx pa'gatewa Dxusa's neewe'wena meswe, kxah Kristo's ma'wẽ ũuste jxpa'gawa'ja's kim yuhwa jiyunimeesa's sxiigmée pta'sxya' ãjakahn. 20 Txã'sna pta'sxwa'jsa íiçxáak Dxusa' adxa's nvxiht, aça' txãa pa'ga íiçxáa ãçxha' preesu ũsthu. Aça' i'kwe'sxa' Dxusa's neewe'wena meswe, txajx yuwe's ũukhmée pta'sxkahn.
Yuwe weçxaa kaahni
21-22 Kwe'sx yakhthẽ'j Tíkiko bagaçxtewa Dxus yakh txutemée pta'sx pu'çxsa txã'sa' kajaçthu, adx ma'wẽne'tka fxi'ze' txã'sa' jxuka pta'sxkahn. Aça' adx ma'wẽ fxi'zena kĩhçxah yũuna ũstewa jiyuçxa', i'kwe'sx maaíi yu' nxusna ũsçxawa çxhãçxha yaakine'kwe.
23 Dxus kwe'sx Tata vxite' kwe'sxtxi nwe'wesa Jesukristo txãawe'sxa' i'kwe'sxtxi pu'çxhitxna, kĩhtewa ewçxáa puutx ya'peeygãhna Dxusa's yuuçxáa yajkxna fxi'zekahn. 24 Sa' maawa kwe'sxtxi nwe'wesa Jesukristo's yaakxsa yuutxi's Dxusa' pu'çxhina jxukaysatx.
