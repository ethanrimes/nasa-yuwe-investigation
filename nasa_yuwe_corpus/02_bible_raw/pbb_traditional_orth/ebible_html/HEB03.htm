3
Jesusçxáa Moiseste thaakwesa'
1 Yakhthẽ'jwe'sx i'kwe'sxa' Dxus pa'yanisa i'kwe yuu txãa jĩi yuuwa'jsa, txãa pa'ga wala ew mjiyuwe Kristo Jesusa's. Txã' Dxus kaahnisa', pkalsawe'sx pa'gate Dxus tasxte we'wewa'jsa, sa' Dxus yakh kwe'sx fxi'zewa'ja's pta'sxsa'. 2 Jesusa' kĩhtewa jxuka kxtey yuuk ma'wẽ yũuwa'ja's ne' Dxus jxkaah txã'sa' jxuka. Moiseswa ma'wẽga Dxus luuçxtxi jxpe'jwa'ja's jxuka kxtey yuu, txã'wẽyku Jesuswa kxtey yuu. 3 Nawa Jesusçxáa Moiseste thaakwe jxtuhthesa', ma'wẽga yat namuçxáa jxtuhthe yu' aça' yatte selpiisáa yu' jxtuhthe meek yu', txã'wẽy Jesusçxáa jxtuhthesa'. 4 Yata's kimçxáawa txahte yujk yat yuuna pa'ja', nawa namu yu' Dxusa'. 5 Moisesa' selpiisaaçxáaçxawa txã'wẽk kĩhte yuhwa peejxmée jxuka kxtey yuu Dxus luuçxtxi jxpe'jna, nawa txajx selpiini yu' e'su Dxus kĩh yũuya'watewa txãatx kaajiyu'jsaçxáak yu'. 6 Naapkaçxha' Kristo' selpiisamée Dxus Nçxi'k yuuk yu', txãasa Dxus luuçxa' kwe'sxtha'w txãatx kĩhte yuhwa peejxmée jxpe'jwa'jsa', kwe'sx yuu uu entepkaçx txutemée txã's yuuçxáa weçxana fxi'zete yu'.
Dxus luuçx, Dxus tasxte jxkaasena ũswa'j
7 Txãa pa'ga txãa yakhçxáa fxi'zewa'ja's na'wẽk pta'sxi' Dxus Espiritu':
Ãçxh Dxus we'weni's wẽse'jyã'jçxa',
8 I'kwe'sx ũuste wa'l yaakxnuwe. I'kwe'sx yaçgawe'sxwa na'wẽtx wa'lna ũsu' txã'wẽ yũunuwe.
Txã'wẽ wa'lçxa txãawe'sxa' Dxusa's kvxissatx na'wẽ yuu nasa u'pnimée kiwete.
9 I'kwe'sx yaçgawe'sxa' kxtee ũsçxa',
Txakwe kuarenta anxupkaçx txãawe'sxtxi adx kĩjkĩhtewa ew yũuna pu'çxni's uyçxawa,
Adxa's wa'ltxi.
10 Txãa pa'gath txãa nasa yakh wẽt fxi'zeya' ãjameeçxa na'jĩna we'we:
Adx kaapiya'jni's wa'lçxa, txãawe'sx ũuste jxpubanaçxáatx fxi'ze'.
11 Aça' txã'wẽ yũupa'ga, txãawe'sxa' adx yakh jxkaasena ũswa'the u'kameetxna jĩnath jurãy jĩ'k.
12 Txãa pa'ga, yakhthẽ'jwe'sx, wala mpa'yajkxwe, txã'wẽ Dxus we'weni' weh yuhwa isameesana sũhna ewmée yajkxçxa Dxus ĩtxisa tasxu txuteeya'mée. 13 Txã'wẽ yũumée, jweeíiçxáa çxhãçxha yaakxwa'ja's een isa puutx pu'çxna mfxi'zewe, kxah i'kwe'sxtxi' maa yuhtxwa pkala' kaypuba'jmeena, aça' i'kwe'sx ũuste ewmée yaakxwa'ja's ji'phumeene'kwe. 14 Nyafxíiwa ma'wẽtka'w Kristo yakh jxpubamée fxi'ze txã'wẽesa nmeh entepkaçx fxi'zeçxa', txãa yakh ũswa'ja's ji'phu'nja'w.
15 Txã'wẽ wa'lkahmen na'wẽk we'we':
Ãçxh Dxus we'weni's wẽse'jyã'jçxa',
I'kwe'sx ũuste wa'l yaakxnuwe. I'kwe'sx yaçgawe'sxwa na'wẽtx wa'lna ũsu' txã'wẽ yũunuwe jĩ'k.
16 ¿Kimta txã'wẽ Dxus yuwe's wẽse'jçxawa e'su yã'ja' wa'lna ũsu'? Egipto kiwejũ Moises kutxi'kh txãawe'sx jxukaysatx txã'wẽ yũu. 17 Dxusa' ¿kim yakhga kuarenta anxupkaçx pxũuskwete fxi'zena ũsu'? Pkalte wetepa'ga nasa u'pnimée kiwete uutx txãawe'sx yakhku txã'wẽ pxũuskwete fxi'zena ũsu'. 18 Dxusa' ¿kimtxiga txã'wẽ adx yakh jxkaasena ũsya' u'kameetxna jĩna we'we? Nwẽese'jsameetxku txã'jĩna we'we. 19 Dxus we'weni's nwẽese'jmeeçxa', u'kaya' ãjameeta' txã'sa' ãçxha' jiyutha'w.
