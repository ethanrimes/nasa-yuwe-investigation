1 KORINTIOS
Korintotewe'sx Dxus yakh fxi'zesawe'sxtxi Pablo fxi'jni
Jesukristo pu'çxpa'ga Dxusa' kĩh yuhwa peejxmée jxukak pees
Dxus yakh fxi'zesawe'sx puutx puiina txuteni
Nwe'weya' ãjawa'ja's Dxus pheu'jçxa nvxiitnisa
Kristo kluuskhẽ uuk txajx kaapiya'jni's pta'sxni
Dxus pheu'jnitxi' txajx Espiritu yakh íiçxáak ya'jiyu'
E'zsa Dxus tasxte mjĩisaçxáatha'w
Jesukristo jxkaahnisawe'sx mjĩini
Peekx njĩ'j npaasa yakh íiçxáa nyu na'wẽ fxi'zesa
I'kwe'sx yuuwe yuuni's Dxusa's jiimeesa khuẽs tasxsu paatxhĩ'khni
Peekx kakwe's pkalte kaafxi'ze'jwa'jme'
Ptamunisu yu'kxpehni
Dxus ĩtximeesatx weçxaya' tajkx ikhni's pta'sxni
Jesukristo jxkaahnisa' ũ'wa'ja's jxuka pẽywa'j ji'pha'
Dxus ĩtximeesatx neewe'wekahmen yu'kxpehni
Kĩhnawa ũ'ya' ãjawa'jsatha'w, nawa jxpa'yakxwa'ja' vxitetx peeygãhçxa
U'ya' kultote jxpa'yakxwa'j ji'pha'
Ewmée yũuya'çxáa Santa Cena's ũ'ya' pkhaakheni
Santa Cena's ũ'wa'ja's Kristok kaapiya'jçxa nvxiht
Santa Cena's jxpa'gaya'waçxa' na'wẽ yũuwa'ja'
Dxusna selpiiwa'ja's Dxus Espiritu' nasa iisa fxiyfxiyçxah ũsni
Jxukaysa teeçxsa kakwetewe'sxçxaçxáatha'w
Peeygãanisa yuuçxáa kĩhtewa thaakwe walasa'
Yuwe fxiysaju we'weni' nasatxi' selpi'çme'
Kĩh yũuççxawa teeçx teeçxçxah we'wewa'ja'
Jesukristo ki' ĩtxi yuuni
Uusaawe'sx ki' ĩtxi yuuwa'j
¿Uusáa ki' ĩtxi yuuwa'ja' ma'wẽkx?
