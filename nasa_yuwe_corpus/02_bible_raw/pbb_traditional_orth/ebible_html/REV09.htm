9
1 Txajũ' Dxus angel tahçtewe'sxwa txajx trompeta's tujkak, naapkaçxha' uythu cieluju estrelxa naa kiwete weteeçte. Aça' txãa wetesa estrelxa's lxavxi's ũsnik yuu, kafx seena' dxiisatewe'sxna. 2 Txajũ' txãa kafx seena' dxiisa's phadek, naapkaçxha' orno walate ah na'wẽk kaseh, sa' txãa aja' jxuka nuyçxhi'dxku sekna vxite' ẽesu jxuka. 3 Txãa ahte yu' khã'sxtxi kaseh naa kiwete jxuka fxuhna. Aça' txãa khã'sx yu' yãjaya' ãjãwa'jsatx yu', usmitx ma'wẽta aka yãja' txã'wẽ. 4 Nawa naa kiwete fxtũ tasx, jxũth tasx çẽysa yu'sa' kĩh yuhnawa suwwa'jsameetx yu', naasáa Dxus selxo's dxikthesu maa yuhwa ji'phmeesaçxaçxá'stxi yãjawa'jsa yu'. 5 Nawa nasatx ikhwa'jsa yu' yu'çmeeta', naasáa tahç a'te ãhpkaçx seena' wala kaaka'jwa'jsaçxáatx yu'. Sa' txãa akasa yu' usmitx yãjani na'wẽ seena' aka yuuwa'jsatx yu'. 6 Txãa ensu' nasa' uuwa'ja's pakweçxawa, uymeetxna. Makwe uuwa'ja's pakwetewa, txãawe'sxtxi' uu en pa'jmeena.
7 Txãa khã'sxa' jibatx gweçxate puiiya' u'jweççxa pheu'jnisa na'wẽtx yu'. Dxikthesu yu' vxyuu bee korona na'wẽsatx ji'phu', sa' dxi'pa' nasaa dxi'p na'wẽtx yu'. 8 U'y na'wẽtx ya'iidxkhas yu', sa' txãawe'sx ki'tha' león ki'th na'wẽsatx ji'phu'. 9 Txãawe'sx kakwe yu' çam na'wẽsaçxaçxá'stxi ya'iiphuph yu', sa' txãawe'sx fxujaçte susni yu' kaçxetilxa jiba wezxnisa kuhsa u'jweççxa ma'wẽ susukx txã'wẽtx susu'. 10 Sa' txãawe'sx mez yu' usmitx mez na'wẽtx yãjawa'j ji'phu', nasatx yãjaçxa tahç a'te uta kaaka'jwa'jsa. 11 Txãa khã'sxa' teeçx ne'jwe'sx ji'phu'tx, kafx seena' dxiite ũssa. Txãa ne'jwe'sxa' hebreo yuwete yu' Abadonku yaase yu', sa' griego yuwete' Apolionku yaase yu' meeçxawa Ivxiitusa jĩnisa.
12 Txajũ' Juan we'weçxa':
—Nyafxte seena' ũukhũukhsa yu' skhẽwku, nawa e'zíi peejxta' jĩ'k.
13 Txajũ' Dxus angel seistewe'sxwa txajx trompeta's tujkak, naapkaçxha' Dxus tasxte vxyuu bee altal pahz vxiçsu we'weni pthũusek. 14 Sa' Dxus angel seistewe'sx trompeta ji'phu'k txã'sa' na'jĩnak jxkaahni yuu:
—Pahz angeleswe'sx tudni ũsta' Eufxrates yu'wala pukate txãawe'sxtxi' mitxhwete jĩna.
15 Txajũ' txãa angeleswe'sxtxi'sa' txhwetenitx yuu, nasa naa kiwetewe'sxtxi puupxãhn ãatuw ikhkahn. Txã'wẽ yũuwa'jsa ne'ta pheu'jni ũsu', kĩh ora, maa ente, kĩh a'te, maa anxute txã'wẽ yuuwa'jsaçxawa txãa en ãhtewe'sx. 16 Txajũ' txãa jiba seena'su a'jsatx jiyuth. Txãawe'sxa' seena' wala kuh yu'tx, dos cientos milxones.
17 Na'wẽ jiba seena'satx uythu, aça' txãa jibate a'jsa yu' puiiwa'j txãawe'sx peçxusu ya'phuphnisa ji'phu'tx tekh jwed, teeçxsa' beh, vxite' çẽy, vxite' lemsa. Aça' jiba yu' león na'wẽtx ya'iidxiktheh yu', sa' yuwesu yu' ipx vxite' ah vxite' asufxre txã'wẽsatx kasehna ũsu'. 18 Aça' naa kiwete nasa puupxãhn ãatuwtxi uu, txã'wẽ jiba yuwesu tekh jwed kasehçxa ikhpa'ga. 19 Txãa jiba' yuweju vxite' mezuhtx ewmée yũuwa'ja's ji'phu'. Txãawe'sx meza' ul na'wẽtx yu' dxikthé ji'ph, txãasatx txãaju ikhya' ãjã'.
20 Nawa txãa ewmeesa vxyaatx txãate uusaamée nasa yu' txãawe'sx ewmée yũuna fxi'zeni's nvxihtmeeta'. Eçxthẽ'j jxkaahni'swa peçxkanamée nwẽese'jna nestxi yu'. Sa' vxyuu beh, vxyuu çxihme, çam, kwet, fxtũ, txãatxtxi nasa na'wẽ piisaçxa neewe'wena nes yu'. Txãa piisanisa' uysaawameeta', sa' wẽse'jsawameeta', u'juya'wa ãja'çmeeta'. 21 Sa' ikhna fxi'zeni'swa nvxihtmeeta', sa' dxijxuuna, kazx pkal yuuna, pesweena txã'wẽ yũunisatxwa nvxihtmeeta'.
