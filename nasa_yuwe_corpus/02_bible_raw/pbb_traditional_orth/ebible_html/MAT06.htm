6
Jxpa'yakx ew yũuwa'ja'
1 I'kwe'sx ew yũuni's nasa uyçxa ew we'we jxthãasçxa mjĩini's wala mpa'yajkxwe. Txã'wẽ ew we'we jxthãasçxaçxáa ew yũuna fxi'zete', iidx Tata cielute ũssa' kĩh yuhwa dewemeena. 2 Txãa pa'ga peejxsatx kĩhçxáawa peesu'ççxa', nasa uykahn sũhçxa nasa ksxavxyte txã'wẽ yũunu. Ãateçxáa ew we'wesawe'sxçxáatx txã'wẽ yũ' judiuwe'sx pkhaakheni yat-su vxite' klxiikhunsu, nasa' uyçxa' ew yuh yũ'k jĩna tuuthe'j jxthãasçxatx txãawe'sxa' txã'wẽ yũ'. Nawa adxa' isath we'we': Txã'wẽ yũuçxa', nasaa pwe'sx tuuthe'jniçxáatx fxi'ze'. 3 Nawa peejxsatx kĩhçxáawa peesu'ççxa', kim yuhwa jiyunimeete paaçte txã'wẽ myũu. 4 Aça' paaçte txã'wẽ yũuçte iidx Tata uyçxa', idxa's deweena.
Dxus yakh na'wẽ puutx we'wewa'ja'
(Lk 11.2-4)
5 I'kwe'sxa' Dxus yakh puutx we'weççxa', ãateçxáa ew we'wesawe'sx na'wẽ yũunuwe. Txãawe'sxa' judiuwe'sx pkhaakheni yat-su vxite' klxiikhunsu yujuçxatx Dxus yakh puutx we'wena pekũju' nasa uykahn sũhçxa. Isath we'we', txã'wẽ yũuçxa' nasaa tuuthe'jniçxáatx fxi'ze'. 6 Nawa idxa' Dxus yakh puutx we'weççxa', yat dxiite u'kaçxa vxitxa's aphçxa kim yuhwa uynimeete mpuutxwe'we. Aça' txã'wẽ paaçte puutx we'wete', iidx Tata cielute ũssa' deweena.
7 Dxus yakh puutx we'weççxa', lxisasáa na'wẽ txã'snaíiçxáa kuh us peenana ũswa'jme'. Dxusa's jiimeesaçxáatx txã'wẽ yũ', wala we'wete', Dxusa' wẽse'jena sũhçxa. 8 Txãawe'sx na'wẽy yũunuwe. Txã'wẽ yũumeetewa, i'kwe'sx Tata' jiiyã'ja' i'kwe'sx peejini's pẽjxumeyna. 9 Txãa pa'ga i'kwe'sxa' na'wẽçxáa Dxus yakh puutx we'wena na'jĩne'kwe:
Kwe'sx Tata cielute ũssa
Idxa' bagaçxtewa jxtuhthe nes yuune'ga.
10 Iidx jxkaahwa'j ena'sçxáatha'w ũythasu',
Naa kiwete idx ma'wẽne'ga jxthãasu' txã'snaçxáa kxtey tee yuu cielute na'wẽy.
11 Kwe'sx ũ'wa'j een isa peejini's meen pees.
12 Kwe'sx ewmée yũuni's meen peltunaĩ, kwe'sxwa ma'wẽtka'w peltunãy kwe'sxtxi ewmée yũusatx txã'wẽy.
13 Ewmeete wetekahmén, meen nwe'we.
Txãa pa'gatey idx yuuçxáa kĩhtewa jxuka ãjawa'jsag, sa' bagaçxtewa idx yuuçxáa jxkaahna nes yuuwa'jsag.
14 Txã'wẽ i'kwe'sxwa ewmée yũusatx peltunãyte', i'kwe'sx Tata cielute ũssawa peltunaĩna i'kwe'sxtxi. 15 Nawa ewmée yũusatx peltunaĩmeete', i'kwe'sx Tatawa peltunaĩmeena i'kwe'sxtxi.
Dxus yakh puutx we'weya' yũunani's kaapiya'jni
16 I'kwe'sxa' yũunaççxa', maa nasawa uykahn sũhçxa ãate kaavxya'ja'jwa'jme'. Ãateçxáa ewtha'w jĩsaçxáatx txã'wẽ yũ' nasa jiyukahn. Isath we'we': Txã'wẽ yũuçxa' nasaa tuuthe'jniçxáatx fxi'ze'. 17 Nawa idx txã'wẽ yũunaççxa', dxi'pna pçxhĩ'çxçxa dxkhasnawa ew mkid, 18 kxah idx yũunani's ãate kaavxya'ja'jmeete', nasa jiyumeetxna, naasáa iidx Tata yuuçxáa jiyuçxa idxa's deweena.
Cielute ji'phuwa'jçxá's mpa'pçxuwe
(Lk 12.33-34)
19 Naa kiwete kĩjkĩhwa ji'phuwa'ja's pa'pçxunuwe. Txã'wẽme', wes ũ'we'tx, aça' izwa suwu'tx. Naapkaçxha' peswé' pa'jçxa ji'phuni's kutxi'ji'tx. 20 Wejxwa cielute ji'phuwa'jçxá's wala mpa'pçxuwe. Kxteea' weswa ũ'meena, aça' izwa suwmeena, pesweewa kutxi'jmeena. 21 Txãa pa'gatey mtee jxaawni ji'phçxa', kxte yuuçxáak wejx jxthaakwe yajkxna ya'fxi'ze'.
Yafxa' kakwe's ewte kaafxi'ze'jsata'
(Lk 11.34-36)
22 Yafxa' kakwe's ewte kaafxi'ze'jsata'. Txã'wẽ iidx yafxa' ewsaçxá's thegsate', iidx kakwe' ewte sxiigmée fxi'zena. 23 Nawa iidx yafxa' ewmeesaçxá's thegsate', iidx kakwe' jxuka ewmeete çxhi'dxteçxáa fxi'zena. Txã'wẽ eena'te ew kaafxi'ze'jwa'jsa yuu ewmeete çxhi'dxtewe'sxa'sçxáa thegsate', bagaçxte yuhwa eena'te fxi'zewa'ja's ya'ji'phumeena.
Dxusna yajkxna vxite' naa kiwete ji'phuni'swa yajkxna fxi'zeni
(Lk 16.13)
24 Kim yuhwa e'z patrontxi selpiiya' ãjameena. Txã'wẽ vxiteçxá's wedxna fxi'zeçxa', vxite patron yu'sa' açena. Sa' teeçxsa jxkaahniçxá's kxtey yuuçxa', vxite patron jxkaahni yu'sa' kxtey yuuya' ãjameena. Txã'wẽy naa kiwete ji'phuni's pa'pçxuna Dxusnawa selpiina yuuya' ya'ãja'çme'.
Kwe'sx peejini's Dxusa' jxuka pu'çxhina
(Lk 12.22-31)
25 Na'wẽth yu'kxpeje': I'kwe'sxa' kĩh yuhwa kuh yajkxmée mfxi'zewe, ¿kĩh ũ'wekxtha'w? sa' ¿kĩh athekxtha'w? sũhna. Txãa pa'gatey ũ'nisate jxthaakwe peejxsa ĩtxi fxi'zeni's ji'phyã'ji'kwe, sa' athnisate jxthaakwe peejxsa kakwe'swa ji'phyã'ji'kwe naa pa'ga. 26 Mthegwe ẽesu fxuusa vxiçxakwetx. Txãawe'sxa' uja'çmeeta', sa' ũ' pkhakhe'çwameeta', sa' tee jwedte jxaawu'çwameeta'. Txã'wẽtewa i'kwe'sx Tata cielute ũssa' txãa vxiçxakwetxi' peejxmeek sã'jĩ'. I'kwe'sxa' txãa vxiçxakwete jxthaakwe pa'gasa i'kwe, aça' wejx txã'wẽ sã'jĩna. 27 Txã'wẽ wala pa'pçxuçxa' ¿i'kwe'sx maaçxá' peena medio metro thaakwe walaaya' ãja'kwe'?
28 ¿Kĩjxa'çxáakwe txakwe pa'pçxu' i'kwe'sx athwa'j ropaçxá's? Mthegwe kite tasxtxi. Txãawe'sxa' mjĩ'çmeeta', sa' khaswa pudu'çmeeta', nawa ma'wẽta zhiçxkwe walá'. 29 Na'jĩ'th na': Jxkaahsa Salomon yuhwa txãa kite tasx na'wẽ yu' zhiçxkwe iijxũme' makwe wala ji'phsaçxawa. 30 Txã'wẽ kite tasxa' ãçxhíi yu' zhiçxkwe ũsçxawa, kuskusa' ipxte kabwa'jçxáak yu', nawa txã'wẽsa'swa Dxusa' wala zhiçxkwe pheu'ju'k na' i'kwe'sxtxi' wejx thaakwe ew pheu'juna. Txãa sũjxa' ãja'çmeene'kwe. 31 Txãa pa'ga i'kwe'sxa' kĩh yuhwa kuh yajkxmée mfxi'zewe, ¿kĩh ũ'wekxtha'w? sa' ¿kĩh athekxtha'w? sũhna. 32 Dxusna jiimeesaçxáatx txã'wẽ kuh yajkxna fxi'ze'. Nawa i'kwe'sxa' cielute Tata ji'phi'kwe txã' jxuka jiia' i'kwe'sx peejini's. 33 Txãa pa'ga i'kwe'sxa' Dxus yakh fxi'zewa'ja's khuẽ'y wejx thaakwe mpa'pçxuwe, txajũyã'ja' kĩhwa thẽymée vxyaana. 34 Ãçxha' jiyui'kwe na' kuskuskhẽwe'sxa's ũythasna pa'pçxunuwe. Txã'wẽme', kuskuskhẽ' pa'pçxuwa'j thaakwe thẽysa pa'jana een isa fxiyfxiysa.
