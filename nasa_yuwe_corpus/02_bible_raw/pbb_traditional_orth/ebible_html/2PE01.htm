SAN PEDRO KARTA E'SU FXI'JNI
1
Dxus yakh fxi'zesatx Pedro fxi'jni
1 Jesukristo's selpisáa Simon Pedro yaaseth, txajx jxkaahnisa. Txãasa naa karta's fxi'jaçthu, i'kwe'sxwa kwe'sx na'wẽyçxáa i'kwe Dxusa's yaakxwa'ja's ji'phu naa pa'ga. Txã'wẽ yuukahníi kwe'sxtxi nwe'wesa Jesukristo nwe'wek. 2 Dxusa's jiyui'kwe sa' kwe'sxtxi jxpe'jsa Jesusa'swa jiyupa'ga, wala ew mfxi'zewe.
Dxus ma'wẽsate' txãa na'wẽysa yuuna pa'jya' na'wẽsatx mpaakwewe
3 Txãa pa'gatey kwe'sxtxi pa'yasa's jiyupa'ga txã' jxuka ũsku kwe'sx kĩh peejini'swa, sa' kĩh pkal yuhwa ji'phmée fxi'zewa'ja'swa txã'wẽy. 4 Txã'wẽ Dxusa' kĩhte yuhwa sxiigmée pu'çxwa'ja's ji'phçxa íiçxáak kwe'sxtxi's nwe'weya' puutx we'we. Txã'wẽ yũute', i'kwe'sxa' naa kiwete jxuka ewmeesa kazx yajkxna fxi'zenijũ txuteeçxa, txãa ma'wẽsate' txãa na'wẽysa íiçxáa yuuna pa'khanku txã'wẽ yũu. 5 Txãa pa'ga Dxusa' pu'çxhina sũhwa'ja's ji'phçxa', jweeíiçxáa ew fxi'zewa'ja's mji'phuwe. Sa' ew fxi'zewa'ja's ji'phuçxa', Dxusa's jweeíiçxáa ew jiyuwa'ja's mpaakwewe. 6 Dxusa's ew jiyuwa'ja's ji'phuçxa', peekx kakwe ewmée yũuya' yaakxni's nwẽese'jmée fxi'zewa'ja's mji'phuwe. Peekx kakwe's nwẽese'jmée fxi'zewa'ja's ji'phuçxa', nxuswa'j pa'thewa çxhãçxha fxi'zewa'ja's mpaakwewe. Sa' txã'wẽsana ji'phuçxa', Dxusa'sçxáa neewe'wena fxi'zewa'ja's mpaakwewe. 7 Dxusa'sçxáa neewe'wena fxi'zewa'ja's ji'phuçxa', yakhthẽ'jwe'sx yakh ewçxáa wẽt fxi'zewa'ja's mpaakwewe. Txã'wẽsa's ji'phuçxa', peeygãawa'ja's mji'phuwe.
8 Txã'wẽsatx i'kwe'sxa' jxpa'gaçxa jxuka nwẽese'jçxa', kwe'sxtxi jxpe'jsa Jesukristo's kxulteçxáa jiyuçxa en ivxiit-sa yuumeene'kwe. 9 Nawa maawa txã'wẽsatx ji'phmeesa' uyya' ãjasamée na'wẽsa yuuna, sa' Dxusa' pkaltxi's peltunãytewa peçxkanasa yuuna. 10 Txãa pa'ga Dxusa' i'kwe'sxtxi txhitxhçxa pa'yayã'the', txã'wẽsatx i'kwe'sx ũuste bagaçxtewa ji'phuwa'jçxá's mpa'pçxuwe. Txã'wẽ yũuçxa' bagaçxte yuhwa Dxus tasxu txutemeene'kwe. 11 Txã'wẽ yũusatxi' thẽymée ũsni yuuna Jesukristo kwe'sxtxi nwe'wesa tasxte u'kawa'ja's. Kxteea' bagaçxte yuhwa pçuwa'jsame'.
12 I'kwe'sxa' txã'wẽ yũuwa'ja's ew jiiçxa ãçxh naa ensu isa pta'sxnijũ txutemée ũsne'kwe, nawa adxa' txã'wẽ yũuwa'ja's kaayaki'jna nes yu'nja. 13 Adx uumeyçxáawa i'kwe'sxtxi' yu'kxpehna nes yuuni ewna sũju'th peçxkanawe'sxte. 14 Txã'wẽme', txaçxmeyna adx uuwa'ja's kaajiyu'kh kwe'sxtxi jxpe'jsa Jesukristo'. 15 Txãa pa'ga nee uumey txã'wẽ yũuwa'ja's yu'kxpehna nes yu'nja, kxah adx uutewa i'kwe'sx ũuste neeyũute', peçxkanamée kxtey yuuna nes yuune'kwe.
Jesukristo txakwe seena' walasa's uysáa
16 Kwe'sxtxi jxpe'jsa Jesukristo kĩhtewa jxuka ãjasa sxawedna kĩiwa'ja's pta'sxtha'w txã' ũus yajkxçxa ĩsxiinisameek yu'. Txãa pa'gatey kwe'sx yafxju yuu uytha'w txakwe seena' zhiçxkwe walasa yuuçxa vxyaate. 17 Txã'wẽ Dxus kwe'sx Tata' Jesukristo's weçxana zhiçxkwe eena' kweethni'stha'w uy, sa' Dxusa' na'wẽk we'we: Na' adx Nçxi'k wedxnisa', txãa pa'ga wala weçxana ũsthu jĩ'k. 18 Cielujũ txã'jĩna we'weni's kwe'sx yuutha'w wẽse'j, thã'j vxiçte Dxus vxyaak kxte kwe'sxtxi jxpe'jsa Jesukristo yakh ũsçxa'.
19 Txã'wẽçxáamée, vxite ji'ptha'w txãak wejx ãate pta'sxi'. Txã' e'su Dxus ma'wẽ yũuwa'ja'swa dxiikhẽy pta'sxna fxi'jçxa nvxiitnisa'. Txãa pta'sxnisa's i'kwe'sxa' nwẽese'jçxa', ew yũusáa yuune'kwe. Txãaíi kus çxhi'dxtewa eena' kweethsa', khikhiiçte estrelxa na'wẽsa i'kwe'sx ũuste pki'tapkaçx. 20 Nawa i'kwe'sxa' ew mjiyuwe: Dxus yuwe dxiikhẽy pta'sxna fxi'jçxa nvxiitnisa' maa yuhwa peekx ũusuhçxáa yajkxçxa fxi'jnisa yuumeeta'. 21 Txãa pa'gatey Dxus kĩh yũuya'watewa dxiikhẽy pta'sxsawe'sxa' Dxus yakh fxi'zesatx yu', sa' peekx ũusuhçxáa yajkxçxa we'wemée, Dxus Espiritu kwe'we'thetx txã'wẽ fxi'jna ũsu'.
