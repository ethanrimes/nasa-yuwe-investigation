11
E'z piçthẽ'j Dxus yuwe's pta'sxya' kaahnisa
1 Txajũ' isani khĩh ũsçxa na'wẽk jxkaah:
—Dxuus yat vxite' altal txãa makwetewa jiyuya' miisa, sa' Dxusna neewe'weya' kxtee u'kasa maazkuẽetewa jiyuya' miisa. 2 Nawa Dxus yatte patxu ekajuwe'sx yu'sa' isanu. Txã'wẽme' txajuwe'sxa's vxite nasatxku ũsni yuu, aça' txãawe'sxa' jxuka suwuutxna Dxus çxhab atesa's kuarenta y dos a'te ãhpkaçx. 3 Naapkaçxha' adxna selpisáa e'z kaja'nja, adx yuwe's mil doscientos sesenta en ãhpkaçx pta'sxna ũskahn. Txãawe'sxa' nasa uute nxusna athnisa ropa khũçxsa ji'ph yuutxna.
4 Txãa e'zsawe'sxa' e'z oliwo fxtũ tasx ũsu'tx txã'wẽsata', vxite' bela txaahni e'z ũsu'tx naa kiwete Dxus tasxte txã'wẽsaíiçxáata'. 5 Txãawe'sxtxi' maaíi yu' pkxuu wẽjẽwáatxna, nawa txãawe'sx yuwesu ipx kasehçxa jxuka kabuna açesawe'sxtxi's. Aça' txã'wẽ yũute', maawa pkxuu wẽesa' uu en ji'phutxna. 6 Aça' txãa selpisaawe'sxa' cielu's aphya' ãjãwa'jsata', Dxus yuwe's pta'sxwa'j en pçuupkaçx nus pa'khamen. Sa' yu'a'swa eeçxaçxáa vxit-ya' ãjãçxa seena' wala kxũusu'jwa'jsata' naa kiwete kĩh weewa nuypa'jna, txãawe'sxtxi ma'wẽ yũuweçxtewa txã'wẽ yũutxna. 7 Nawa txãawe'sx mjĩia's jxã'jaçpkaçxha', eçx iiméh seena'sa kafx seena' dxiijũ kãhçxa txãawe'sxtxi puiiçxa knayçxa ikhena. 8 Txãawe'sx uuníi kakwe's na'wẽ kwe'sxtxi jxpe'jsa kluuste uuk txãa çxhab wala klxiikhunsu wãataani yuutxna. Txãa çxhaba's Sodoma vxite' Egipto na'wẽsa' jĩ'tx. 9 Aça' tekh en ẽepxãh ãhpkaçx nasa maa çxhabuwe'sx, ma'wẽsa fxiyfxiy we'wesa, maa kiwejuwe'sxwa jxukasa txãa uuníi kakwetxi's uyuutxna, nawa kaapeda'j yu' meetxna. 10 Txajũ' nasa naa kiwetewe'sxa' seena' wala weçxaatxna txã'wẽ uupa'ga, sa' txã'wẽ weçxaweete txãawe'sx ji'phuni's puutx peesutxna. Txãawe'sx yakh yu' iiméh seena' wala ewmeesa na'wẽsa yuutxna txãa e'z piçthẽ'jsa Dxus yuwe's pta'sxna ũssawe'sxa', txãa pa'ga uute' wala weçxaatxna. 11 Nawa uuníi tekh en ẽepxãh skhẽwniijũ', Dxusa' ĩtxĩ vxitku aça' ki' kiitetx. Txã'wẽ yũute', iiméh seena' wala ũukhtxi jxukaysa uyna ũssawe'sxa'. 12 Txajũ' txãa e'z selpisaawe'sxa' wẽse'jtx seena' sus we'weni cielujũ na'jĩna:
—Ayte cielute ẽete myuhwe jĩçpkaçxha', tãaph pxãhte u'jçxa cielute tejkatx, txãawe'sxtxi açesawe'sx uyna ũste. 13 Txã'wẽ yuuçpkaçxha', seena' wala ehd u'jçxa txãa çxhaba's ma'wẽ yu' suwçxa ejx jxũna u'kh, aça' siete mil nasatx uu txãa ehd u'pha'ga. Txã'wẽ yũute', nasa neeyũusa' iiméh seena' wala ũukhçxa Dxus cielute ũssa's pxthaa we'wena tuuthe'jtx.
14 Txajũ' Juan we'weçxa': Seena' ũukhũukhsa e'ztewe'sx yu' skhẽwku, nawa jxthẽeyuumeena tekhtewe'sxwa jĩ'k.
Trompeta sietetewe'sx
15 Txajũ' Dxus angel sietetewe'sxwa txajx trompeta's tujkak, aça' seena' sus we'wenisa pthũuse'tx cielujũ na'jĩna:
Naa kiwete jxkaahna ũsnisa' jxuka kwe'sx Tata vxite' txajx Txhitxhnisa Kristo txãawe'sx jĩiçxaçxáatx yuuna pa'j.
Aça' ãçxha' txãaçxaçxáa jxkaajana bagaçxtewa jĩnatx we'we.
16 Txajũ' Dxus luuçxtxi jxkaahsa weintikuatrosawe'sx Dxus jxkaahna ũsnite ũssa' kiwete kĩiçhuçxa Dxusa's neewe'wetx na'jĩna:
17 Kwe'sxtxi jxpe'jsa idxa's weçxa'tha'w, Dxus iiméh seena' walasag.
Idxa' nyafxíiwa idxíiçxáag yu', sa' ãçxwa txãaíiçxáag, sa' bagaçxtewa idx yuuçxáa nes yuuwa'jsag.
Txãa pa'gatey kĩhtewa jxuka ãjãwa'ja's idxçxaçxáa ji'phgu,
Txãasa ãçxha' jxkaahna ũsgu.
18 Naa kiwete nasa' idxa's ahçetx, nawa txãawe'sxtxi kastigãywa'j ena' ãjk.
Aça' txã'wẽy uusaawe'sxtxiwa kastigaĩne'ga.
Sa' walasa ma'tx yuu meeçxa' le'çxkwesa ma'tx yuu nawa idx yakh txutemée fxi'zeçxa iidx yase's açewe'wesameewe'sxtxi' deweene'ga ew yũuna iidx yuwe's pta'sxna selpiina fxi'zepa'ga.
Naapkaçxha' naa kiwete suwna ũssawe'sxtxi's ka'tu' ivxiitune'ga jĩnatx we'we.
19 Txajũ' cielute Dxus ũsni phatek, aça' na'wẽ Dxusa' nasa yakh puutx we'wek txãa puutx we'weni's fxi'jçxa kajate jxaawni yuuk txãa kaja' vxyaak. Naapkaçxha' kpi'sx kwehneçxa seena' susku kwetuba seena' wala ãsxna, aça' ehdwa u'kh.
