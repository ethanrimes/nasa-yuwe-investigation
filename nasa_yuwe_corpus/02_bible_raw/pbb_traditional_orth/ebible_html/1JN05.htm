5
Dxus luuçxa' ewmeesa's nwẽese'jmée jxuka knayu'tx
1 Maawa Jesusa' Dxus tasxu yuusa Kristona sũhnaçxáa ũuste yaakxsa', Dxus luuçxçxa txã'wẽ yaakina. Dxusnawa peeygãjaççxa', Dxus luuçxtxiwa peeygãhna fxi'zewa'ja'. 2 Dxusa's peeygãhna txajx jxkaahnitxi' nwẽese'jçxa', Dxus luuçxtxiwa peeygãasaçxa txã'wẽ yu'nja'w. 3 Dxusna peeygãani txã'wẽ txajx jxkaahnitx nwẽese'jnisa'. Txajx jxkaahni' jxthaakwe yu' thẽysameeta'. 4 Maawa Dxus luuçxa' naa kiwete ewmeesa's nwẽese'jmée jxuka knayu'tx. Jesukristo's ũusuh jxuka yaakxpa'ga yuuçxáak naa kiwete ewmeesa's ya'knayu'. 5 Maawa Jesusa' Dxus Nçxi'kna sũhna ũusuh jxuka yaakxsa yuuçxáa, naa kiwete ewmeesa's nwẽese'jmée knayuutxna.
Jesukristo' Dxus nçxi'ktewa kaajiyu'jsa' naata'
6 Jesukristo naa kiwete kĩisa' Dxus Nçxi'ktewa kaajiyu'jsa' naata': Yu'te bautisãyni vxite' kluuste uuni. Nawa yu'te bautisãynijũçxáa yu' açxaméek ew ya'jiyu', naasáa kluuste uuniisa yakh txãa e'z jwedsa yakh yuuçxáak isa ew ya'jiyu' Dxus Nçxi'ktewa. Txã'sa' Dxus Espirituk txã'jĩ'. Txajx we'weni' isasata'. 7 Jesukristo' Dxus Nçxi'ktewa jiisa cielute' tekhta': Dxusa', vxite' Jesukristo íiçxá', vxite' Dxus Espiritu'. Aça' txãa tekhsa' txãa na'wẽçxaçxáatx we'we'. 8 Txã'wẽy Jesukristo' Dxus Nçxi'ktewa jiisa naa kiwete' tekhta': Dxus Espiritu, vxite' yu'te bautisãynisa vxite' kluuste uuniisa. Aça' txãa tekhsa' txãa na'wẽçxaçxáatx we'we'. 9 Piçthẽ'jwe'sx pta'sxniçxáa yuhnawa isa yuh we'wene' sũhnatha'w jxpa'ga'. Aça' Dxus we'weni yu' wejx jxthaakwe isasa yuhna sũhna jxpa'gawa'j ji'ptha'w, txãa pa'gatey Dxus yuuk we'weçxa': Jesukristo' Adx Nçxi'ka' jĩ' naa pa'ga. 10 Jesukristo' Dxus Nçxi'ka' sũhna fxi'zesa', Dxus Nçxi'ka's ũuste jxpa'gasaçxa txã'wẽ yaakina. Nawa Dxus we'weçxa': Jesukristo' Adx Nçxi'ka' jĩni's maawa jxpa'gasame', Dxusa' ĩsxiija'k jĩsa yuuna, txã'wẽ Dxusa' isane' we'we' sũhmeeçxa. 11 Jesukristo' Dxus Nçxi'ka' jĩna we'weçxa', Dxusa' kwe'sxtxi' pçumée ĩtxi fxi'zewa'ja's peesku jĩnisa', txãa yuuçxáa pçumée ĩtxi fxi'zewa'ja's ji'phsa' naa pa'ga. 12 Txãa pa'ga maawa Dxus Nçxi'ka's ũuste jxpa'gasa', pçumée ĩtxi fxi'zewa'ja's ji'phsa yuutxna. Nawa Dxus Nçxi'ka's ũuste jxpa'gasame', pçumée ĩtxi fxi'zewa'ja's ji'phmeesa yuutxna.
Pkal perdon ji'phmeesawa ũsta'
13 Dxus Nçxi'ka's ũuste jxpa'gasa', pçumée ĩtxi fxi'zewa'ja's ji'phne'tka'w sũhna jiyukahnthu i'kwe'sxtxi pta'sxna fxi'ja'. 14 Aça' txã'wẽ yaakxsatxi' Dxusa' pasu'k txajx jxthãasniçxá's pxthaa we'wena pẽjxte' txã'sa' ew jiitha'w. 15 Kwe'sx pxthaa we'weni's Dxusa' wẽse'jna ũstewa ma'wẽ ew jiitka'w txã'wẽy kwe'sx pẽyni's jxpa'gawa'ja'swa ew jiitha'w.
16 Yakhthẽ'j pkalte fxi'zena ũstewa txãa pkala' perdon ji'phte uyçxa', maawa Dxusa's neewe'wewa'ja' txãa pkalsa pa'gate, aça' yakhthẽ'j txã'wẽ fxi'zesa's Dxusa' ĩtxi fxi'zewa'ja's peesuna. Nawa pkal perdon ji'phmeesa ũsta' txã'wẽsate fxi'zena ũste yu', Dxusa's neewe'wewa'ja's jxkaja'çmeeth. 17 Kĩhwa ewmée yũuni' pkalçxaçxáata', nawa perdon ji'phta'.
18 Kwe'sxa' jiitha'w maawa Dxus luuçx yã'ja' peena yu' pkalte fxi'ze'çme'. Txã'wẽ yũuwe'sxte Dxus Nçxi'ka' txajx kusetek jxaawu' eçxthẽ'j suwkahmén. Txãa pa'gak pkalte fxi'ze'çmée.
19 Kwe'sxa' Dxus luuçx yuhtha'w, nawa naa kiwete nasa jxukaysa Dxusna yajkxmée fxi'zesa' eçxthẽ'j kusetewe'sxta' txã'sa' jiitha'w.
20 Jiitha'w Dxus Nçxi'k naa kiwete kĩhçxa', Dxus ĩtxisa's jiyuwa'ja's kwe'sxtxi ũsku. Txãa pa'ga Dxus ĩtxisa Nçxi'k Jesukristo yakh fxi'zena ũstha'w. Txãa Dxus ĩtxisa' bagaçxte yuhwa pçumée ĩtxi fxi'zewa'ja's kwe'sxtxi peessa'. 21 Adx luuçxwe'sx, mpa'yajkxwe dxusmeeçxá's yaakxni's.
