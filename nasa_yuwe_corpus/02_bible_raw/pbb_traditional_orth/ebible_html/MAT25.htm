Diez kna'sa yakh kaaja'da'jni
1 Dxus jxkaahwa'j en ãhte', na'wẽ yuuna: Diez kna'sawa ma'wẽta lampara aceite awniisa's atçxa boda yatna u'j novxio's jxpa'gaya', txã'wẽy yuutxna. 2 Txãa kna'sawe'sx tahçsa' jxpa'yakxmeeçxa pheu'jmeetx u'j, sa' vxite tahçsa' jxpa'yakxçxa jxuka pheu'jçxatx u'j. 3 Tahçsa' ew ũus yajkxmeeçxa', lampara yu'sa' jxũçxa u'jçxawa aceite yu' jxũmeetx u'j pçuute ki' awwa'j. 4 Nawa vxite tahçsa' aceite jxũçxatx u'j lamparatewe'sx pçuute' ki' awwa'j. 5 Novxio pa'jya' jxthẽeyuute', txãawe'sxtxi wala deewẽe yuute' dehtx. 6 Txajũ' kuspxãh yuuçte', pthũusek na'jĩna sus we'weni: Novxio yujxã'ja', aça' jxpa'gaya' myuhwe jĩni. 7 Naapkaçxha' kna'sawe'sx kxazíi kiiteçxa lampara's pheu'jtx. 8 Aça' tahçsa jxpa'yakxmée u'jsawe'sxa' vxite tahçsatx aceite thãastxi: Le'çxkuẽçxáawa peesune'kwe. Txã'wẽme', kwe'sx pki'tanisa' ũçxhaça' jĩnatx we'we. 9 Nawa kna'sawe'sx jxpa'yakx pheu'jçxa u'jsa' na'wẽtx pas: Ũsme'nja'w. Txã'wẽme', kwe'sx jĩi yuhwa ãhmée ji'ptha'w, aça' i'kwe'sxtxi ũswa'j yu' wejx ãhmeena. Wejx yuuwa me'jwe weyya' jĩ'tx. 10 Txã'wẽ tahçsa jxpa'yakxmeeçxa aceite weyya' u'jweçpkaçxha', ptamusá novxio' pa'kh. Aça' jxpa'yakx ũythãssawe'sxa' boda yatte novxio yakh u'kaçpkaçxha', vxitxa's aphtxi. 11 Txãa e'suyã'jtx vxite kna'sawe'sx kũhçxa: Meen phaade jĩna we'we. 12 Nawa pasçxa': Isa, adxa' i'kwe'sxtxi' jiimeeth jĩk.
13 I'kwe'sxa' jxpa'yajkx meswe. Txã'wẽme', kĩh ente kĩh orate Dxus Nçxi'k Nasa Yuusáa pa'jwa'ja's jiimeei'kwe.
Mjĩiya' ãjawa'jsatx vxyu's neewe'weçxa u'jni yakh kaaja'da'jni
14 Teeçx piçthẽ'jwa ma'wẽga vxite kiwen u'jweççxa', txã'sna selpisaawe'sxtxi pa'yaçxa txajx vxyu's neewe'weçxa ũsçxa u'j, txã'wẽ yũuna Dxus jxkaahwa'j en ãhte'.
15 Txãa piçthẽ'ja' selpisáa teeçxsa's vxyu tahç milku ũs, sa' vxite's e'z milku ũs, sa' vxite's teeçx milku ũs, teeçxsa na'wẽçxah makwesa's sxiigmée iijxũya' ãjawa'jsatewa txakweçxáak ũsçxa u'j. 16 Teeçx selpisáa tahç mil vxyu jxpa'gasa', txãa vxyu's kmãaji'jçxa ki' vxite tahç mil íik ãh knay. 17 Txã'wẽy e'z mil jxpa'gasawa ki' vxite e'z mil íik ãh knay. 18 Nawa teeçx milçxáa jxpa'gasa' patron vxyu's kafxi'jçxa kiwetek pedáa.
19 Patrona' wala txaçxte ki' sxawedna pa'jçxa', txãa selpisaawe'sxtxi vxyu ũsni's ki' pẽjxku. 20 Nyafxte' tahç mil vxyu jxpa'gasa khuẽ'y utxaana kũhçxa ki' vxite tahç mil íik ãh kaasxwedu'j na'jĩna patrona's: Idxa' tahç milgu ũs, aça' ki' vxite tahç mil íith knay kmãaji'jçxa jĩte', 21 patrona' na'wẽk we'we: Idxa' vxyu'sa' psuwmeeg wala ew kmãaji'j. Txã'wẽ le'çxkuẽçxáasanawa psuwmée kmãaji'pha'ga, wejx walasanawa neewe'weçxa iidx kusete nvxiitu'nja. Ãçxha' me'ka adx yakh weçxana ũsya' jĩk. 22 Txajũ' vxite selpisáa e'z mil jxpa'gasawa kũjk na'jĩna patrona's: Idxa' e'z milçxáag ũs, nawa txã'sa' kmãaji'jçxa ki' vxite e'z mil íith ãh knay jĩte', 23 patrona' na'wẽk pas: Idxa' vxyu'sa' psuwmeeg wala ew kmãaji'j. Txã'wẽ le'çxkweçxáasanawa psuwmée ewgu kmãaji'pha'ga ãçxha' txãate jxthaakwe walasanawa nvxiitu'nja iidx kusete. Me'ka adx yakh weçxana ũsya' jĩk. 24 Txajũ' teeçx milçxáa jxpa'gasawa kũjk patrona's na'jĩna: Idxa' wala weesweetewa jiith, sa' iidx uuthasxmeesuwa jxukag pkhakhe' kĩhwa uhmeeçxawa. 25 Txãa pa'ga ũukhçxa iidx vxyu's jxaawthu kiwete pedaaçxa. Nawa ãçxha' dukheçthu idx makwene'ga ũs txã'sa' jĩte', 26 patrona' na'wẽk pas: Idxa' seelpimée watxkuẽg. Adxa' txã'wẽ juuna'çxa kĩhwa uhmeeçxawa vxite jĩitx pkhakhsatewa jiith jĩ'g na', 27 ¿mjĩtega adx vxyu's bankoga jxaawmée txãate jxthaakwekuẽ nuypehnaçxa kaasxwedu'jya'? jĩçxa', 28 kxtee ũssatx na'wẽk jxkaah: Txãa piçthẽ'j vxyu mil ji'phsa's kusa'jçxa diez mil ji'phsa's meswe. 29 Txãa pa'gatey ji'phsa's jweeíiçxáa jxthaakwe ũswa'ja' wala ji'phukahn. Nawa ji'phmeesa's jxuka kusa'jwa'ja' le'çxkuẽçxáa ji'phuni'swa. 30 Sa' txãa piçthẽ'j seelpimeesa's uweçxa ekaju çxhi'dx walate makhwe. Kxte' seena' yuh pebeena ki'thtxi ksuusu'jna ũswa'ja' jĩk.
Naa kiwetewe'sxtxi jxuka ewsatx fxii txudeni
31 Dxus Nçxi'k Nasa Yuusa' jxkaahsa yuuçxa angeleswe'sx ksxavxyte kĩhçxa txajx jxkaahwa'the kaçxhiina. 32 Txajũ' nasa' naa kiwete mtee mteewe'sxwa jxuka pkhaakhetxna txajx tasxte, aça' teeçxsa na'wẽçxah txhitxhçxa txudena, piisxáa-thegsawa ma'wẽga fxii txudé' piisxáa vxite' kaplatx txã'wẽy. 33 Sa' piisxaatxi' paçu ju'gu txahçxa', kaplatxi' jebu ju'gu txajana. 34 Txã'wẽ yũuçxa jxkaahsa' we'wena paçu ju'gu ũssatxi' na'jĩna: Adx Tata peeygãanisa, i'kwe'sxa' myuhwe txã'wẽ naa kiwe's vxitiyna i'kwe'sx jxkaahwa'j pheu'jni ũsa' txã'sna jxpa'gaya'. 35 Txãa pa'gatey i'kwe'sxa' adxa's sã'jĩ i'kwe wẽena u'jute'. Sa' yũ'wẽena u'jute', yusi'kwe. Nasa ekajuwe'sx na'wẽ u'jute', deewa'j pki'ji'kwe. 36 Athwa'j ropa ji'phmée u'jute', ropa ũsi'kwe. Uuya' ũste', thegya' pa'ji'kwe. Karcelte ũste', kxteewa thegya' pa'ji'kwe jĩte', 37 nasa ew yũusa' na'jĩtxna: Idxa' ¿bagaçx wẽena u'jutetka'w uyçxa ũ' puuç? ¿Bagaçx yũ'wẽena u'jutetka'w tudxwa'j ũs? 38 Idxa' ¿bagaçx txã'wẽ ekajuwe'sx na'wẽ u'jute uyçxatka'w deewa'j pki'j? ¿Bagaçx txã'wẽ ropa ji'phmée u'jutetka'w ropawa ũs? 39 ¿Bagaçx ãça'na ũstetka'w thegya' pa'j? ¿Bagaçx karcelte ũstetka'w kxteewa thegya' pa'j? jĩtxna. 40 Aça' jxkaahsa' na'wẽ pasuna: Isath we'we', adx yakhthẽ'jwe'sx makwe wala pxthaa ãhmeekwesaçxawa ayte ũsta' txãatx txã'wẽ yũusa' adxna pu'çxsaçxa i'kwe txã'wẽ yũu jĩna.
41 Txajũ' jebu ju'gu ũssatxi' jxkaahsa' na'jĩna we'wena: Adx tasxu mtxuteewe, i'kwe'sxa' ewmeetewe'sx yã'ji'kwe. Txãa pa'ga eçxthẽ'j jĩi eçxthẽ'j angeleswe'sx jĩi pheu'jni ũsa' infxierno ipx wala kxtee me'jwe jĩna jxkaajana. 42 Txã'wẽ adx wẽena u'jutewa, puuçmeei'kwe. Yũ'wẽena u'jutewa tudxwa'j ũsmeei'kwe. 43 Nasa ekajuwe'sx na'wẽ u'jutewa, deewa'j pki'jmeei'kwe. Ropa ji'phmée u'jutewa, athwa'j ũsmeei'kwe. Ãça'na ũstewa, thegya' pa'jmeei'kwe. Sa' karcelte ũstewa, kxteewa thegya' pa'jmeei'kwe jĩte', 44 txãawe'sxa' na'wẽ pasutxna: Idxa' ¿bagaçx wẽena u'jutetka'w puuçmée? ¿Bagaçx yũ'wẽena u'jutetka'w tudxwa'j ũsmée? ¿Bagaçx ekajuwe'sx na'wẽ u'jutetka'w deewa'j pki'jmée? ¿Bagaçx ropa ji'phmée u'jutetka'w athwa'j ũsmée? ¿Bagaçx ãça'na ũstetka'w thegya' pa'jmée? ¿Bagaçx karcelte ũstetka'w kxteewa thegya' pa'jmée? jĩtxna. 45 Aça' jxkaahsa' na'wẽ pasuna: Isath we'we', naa nasa pxthaa ãhmeekuẽsatx kĩhte yuhwa pu'çxmeei'kwe na' adxna pu'çxsameeçxai'kwe txã'wẽ yuu jĩna. 46 Txãa pa'ga ew yũusame' infxierno ipx pçuwa'jmeete khẽeya' u'jwetxna, naapkaçxha' ew yũusawe'sxa' bagaçxtewa pçuwa'jmée ĩtxi fxi'zewa'ja's ji'phutxna.
