14
Çu'vxy weete ãça'sa's nuykatxhini
1 Teeçx sapatu jxkaase ente' Jesusa' fxariseo npiiçthẽ'j yattek ũ'ya' u'j. Aça' vxite fxariseowe'sx yu' peethegu'natx ũsu'. 2 Txãa yatte' teeçx piçthẽ'j çu'vxy weete ãçã'sak ũsu'. 3 Aça' Jesusa' na'jĩnak pẽjx ley kaapiya'jsawe'sx vxite' fxariseowe'sxtxi:
—Sapatu jxkaase entewa ãçã'sa's nuykatxhite ¿ewmanx? jĩte', 4 txãawe'sxa' pasmée sxuuna'tx neeyũu. Aça' Jesusa' txãa ãçã'sa's pa'yaçxa nuykatxhiçxak kaah. 5 Sa' fxariseowe'sxtxi' na'jĩk:
—I'kwe'sx jiba meeçxawa i'kwe'sx kla kafxte khẽeçxa u'pte', ¿dudtey kutxi'jmeema'kwe jxkaase entewa?
6 Txã'jĩte', weh yuhwa pasya' ãjãmeeta'.
Boda fxiestate pi'kxte walasath sũhna pa'jsa
7 Fxiestate pi'kxnisa' kaçxni ewsaçxáatx txhitxhiçte uyçxa', Jesusa' na'wẽk yu'kxpeh:
8 —Boda fxiestate i'kwe'sxtxi pi'kxte', kaçxní ewsu yu' kaçxnuwe. Txã'wẽme' vxite i'kwe'sxte jxthaakwe jxtuhthesa wáa pa'jana, 9 aça' fxiesta namu' i'kwe'sxtxi kiisuna: Ayte' naajx kaçxwa'jçxá' jĩçxa. Aça' wala thaamena u'jwene'kwe kaçxní açxamée ewkhẽ kaçxya'. 10 Fxiestate i'kwe'sxtxi pi'kxte', wejxwa nmeh puzakhẽ mkaaçxwe, kxah i'kwe'sxtxi pi'kxsa uyçxa': Namiku, ay yaçkhẽ wejx ewkhẽ myuh kaçxya' jĩte', wala weçxana yaçkhẽ kaçxya' u'jwene'kwe. 11 Maawa walasath sũhna iiwejçx iiwejçx yaakxsa' thaamewa'jçxáa ji'phuna. Nawa ãhmeekwesa na'wẽçxáa yajkxna fxi'zesa' jxtuhthe wálasa yuuna.
12 Sa' fxiesta namu pi'kxsa'swa na'jĩk Jesusa':
—Idxa' sã'jĩya' pi'kiççxa', iidx namikuwe'sxtxi yakhthẽ'jwe'sxtxi pukakhẽ npe'sxtxi meeçxawa utxasuwe'sx vxyu ji'phsatx pi'kxnu. Txã'wẽme' txãawe'sxwa kaasxwedu'ju'tha'w jĩna idxa's txã'wẽy sã'jĩya' pi'kitxna. 13 Aça' sã'jĩya' fxiesta vxituççxa', wejxwa puuple ãhmeekwesa legsa uysamée txã'wẽsaçxáatx mpi'kx sã'jĩya'. 14 Aça' txãawe'sxa' ka'tu' txã'wẽy kaasxwedu'jya' ãjãmeetxna idxa's. Nawa weçxana mneeyũu, txãa pa'gatey Dxusa' idxa's deweena Dxus luuçx ki' ĩtxĩ yuu ente' jĩk.
Fxiestate pi'kxte wa'lsa
15 Txã'wẽ pta'sxni's wẽsẽ'jçxa', mesate u'psa teeçxsa' na'wẽk we'we Jesusa's:
—Dxus jxkaahna ũsnite u'kaçxa ũ'sa yakha' wala yuh weçxa yuhna jĩte', 16 Jesusa' na'wẽk kwentu'j:
—Teeçx piçthẽ'j fxiesta wala vxituççxa', nasa seena' wala kuh pi'kxku sã'jĩya'. 17 Sa' ũ'wa'j ora ãjãçte', teeçx selpisa's pa'yaya' kaakh: Ũ'ya' myuhwe, jxuka pheu'jniyã'j ũsa' jĩna pa'yakahn. 18 Nawa jxukaysa wa'lçxa na'wẽçxáatx pas: Nyafxtewe'sx pasçxa': Kiweth wey, sa' thegya' u'jwe ji'phthu. Aça' tee peltunaĩ jĩk. 19 Aça' vxite we'weçxa': Diez novxilxoth wey, sa' thegya' u'jweçthu. Naa pa'ga tee peltunaĩ jĩk. 20 Aça' tekhtewe'sxa' na'wẽk pas: Ãçxçxáatha'w ptamúu, sa' yujxa' tuduume'nja jĩk. 21 Txajũ' selpisá' sxawedna u'jçxa txajx patrona's jxuka pta'sxku. Aça' patrona' wala ũusaçxaçxa txã's selpisa's na'jĩnak jxkaah: Dudtey plaasasu klxiikhunsu ki' u'jçxa puuple ãhmeekwesa legsa yafx puuple uysamée txã'wẽsatx pi'kxçxa ayte pe'jna yuweene'ga jĩk. 22 Aça' selpisá' u'jçxa ki' sxawedna pa'jçxa': Iidx jxkaahni's kxtey yuuth, aça' pa'jtx. Nawa kaçxwa'j yu' nee ũsiyçxáata' jĩte', 23 patrona' selpisá's na'jĩk: Ãçxha' çxhab ekaju jxu'juwe'sx vxite' dxi'j u'jsatxwa pi'kxçxa ayte ka'ga'jane'kwe makwe wa'luçtewa ãadxijimée, adx yatte utapkaçx. 24 Nawa nyafxte pi'kxnisa wa'ltxi txãawe'sx yuutxi' kaapa'ja'jme'nja adx yatte puuçya' jĩk. Txã'jĩnak kwentu'j Jesusa'.
Jesus yakh fxi'zewa'j thẽya'
25 Txajũ' Jesus e'ste nasa wala u'jweç yu'tx, aça' txãawe'sxtxi yu'thegçxa na'jĩk:
26 —Maawa adx yakh yuusá' txajx ney, njĩ'j, nyu, luuçx, nyakhwe'sx, npe'sxwe'sxtxi nvxihtçxa txã'wẽy iiwejçx yajkxna fxi'zeni's uu entepkaçx nvxihtmeeçxa yu', adx yakh fxi'zeya' ãjãmeena. 27 Sa' makwe thẽyte uupçxãhtewa txãatxi' yajkxmée, adx yakh yuhmeeçxa yu' adx yakh fxi'zeya' ãjãmeena. 28 I'kwe'sxa' yat wálasa txajxa'waçxa', jxuka jxã'jwa'j kĩjkĩhwa ãh ũstxmanx sũhna kaçxçxa ¿yajkxmeema'kwe? 29 Txã'wẽ yũumeete', kiwete pxãhn pa'j mjĩiçxaçxáa nvxihtte', maawa uysa' npeevxysxikatxna: 30 Jxã'jya' ãjãme' jĩna.
31 Txã'wẽy maa npiiçthẽ'jwa vxite npiiçthẽ'j yakh puiiya'waçxa', kaçxçxa ũus yajkxmeema'k: Naa diez mil soldau yakhçxáa weinte mil soldautx ¿knayma'th? sũhna. 32 Sa' ewupçxãhmeete', vxite npiiçthẽ'j utxameyna, yuwe kajana kuçx puiimée ewteçxah neeyũuya'. 33 Aça' txã'wẽy i'kwe'sxwa dxiikhẽy jxuka ũus yajkxçxa i'kwe'sx ji'phuni's nvxihtmeeçxa yu', adx yakh fxi'zeya' ãjãmeene'kwe.
Nega seelpimeesa
(Mt 5.13; Mr 9.50)
34 Nega' selpi'k, nawa jxuka sxũukãhçxa' ¿ma'wẽ selpiikx? 35 Kiwete sxaakwe'jwa'jçxáa yuhwa selpi'çme', pubwa'jçxáak yu'. Jiyuwa'jsa ũsçxa', jiyune'kwe jĩk.
