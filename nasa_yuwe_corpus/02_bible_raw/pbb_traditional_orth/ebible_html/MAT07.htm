7
Jiimeeçxa' nuywe'wewa'jme'
(Lk 6.37-38, 41-42)
1 Ma'wẽsatewa jiimeeçxa', ewmeesata' jĩna nuywe'wewa'jme', Dxus dxi'pte i'kwe'sxwa ewmeesa yuuya'mée. 2 Txã'wẽme', vxitesatx nuywe'wete', Dxusa' i'kwe'sxtxiwa ewmeesata' jĩçxa kastigaĩna nuywe'wepa'ga. I'kwe'sx ma'wẽ juuna' yũute', Dxuswa txã'wẽyçxáa juuna' kastigaĩna. 3 Iidx yakhthẽ'j yafxte kxtã' le'çxkuẽesa khẽeçxa ũste ¿ma'wẽ uyuki'g iidx yafxte yu' fxtũ pe'la yuu walasa khẽeçxa ũste txã's khuẽ'y kutxi'jmeeçxa'? 4 Sa' ¿ma'wẽ iidx yakhthẽ'ja's kxtã' le'çxkuẽsa iidx yafxte ji'phgu txã'sa' kutxi'jith jĩna we'weki'g, txã'wẽ iidx yafxte yu' fxtũ pe'la walasa's ji'phçxa'? 5 Ãakheçxáa ew we'wena u'jusa, iidx yafxte fxtũ pe'la walasa's khuẽ'y mkuutxi'j. Txã'wẽ yũuçxa iidx yakhthẽ'j yafxte kxtã' le'çxkuẽsa's ew uyçxa kutxi'jya' ãjane'ga.
6 Dxus yuwe's alkutx ũswa'jme', sa' kuçxitxwa ũswa'jme'. Txã'wẽme', jxuka wa'khi'khi'jçxa ũusaçxayã'jçxa', ũssa i'kwe'sxtxi íiçxáa wa'kxya' kũjutxna.
Dxusa's pẽyni vxitxte tu'kakana
(Lk 11.9-13; 6.31)
7 Dxusa's mpẽjxwe, aça' ũsuna, sa' pakweçxa', uyuune'kwe. Vxitxte mtu'kakawe, aça' phaadena. 8 Txã'wẽ maawa pẽysa' jxpa'gana, aça' pakwesá' uyuuna. Vxitxte tu'kakate', phaadeni yuuna.
9 I'kwe'sx pwe'sx maaçxa' i'kwe'sx luuçx pan thãaste', ¿kweta's ũsma'kwe? 10 Meeçxa' wedx thãaste', ¿ula's ũsma'kwe? 11 I'kwe'sxa' Dxus dxi'pte ewmeesaçxawa, i'kwe'sx luuçxtxi' ewsaçxáa i'kwe peesu', aça' i'kwe'sx Tata cielute ũssa' wejx thaakwe ewsa's peesuna thãassatxi'.
12 Bagaçxtewa i'kwe'sxtxi maa nasawa ewçxáa yũuna fxi'ze jxthãasçxa', i'kwe'sxwa txã'wẽy maa nasatxwa ewçxáa yũuna mfxi'zewe. Txãa pa'gatey Moises ley vxite' Dxus yuwe pta'sxsawe'sx fxi'jniwa txã'wẽçxaçxáatx jxkaaja'.
Vxitx pteezsa
(Lk 13.24)
13 Vxitx pteezte me'kawe. Txã'wẽme', vxituwa'the u'kawa'j vxitxa' wala tape', aça' dxi'jwa txã'wẽy tape'. Kxteea' wala kuhsa u'kana ũsta'. 14 Naapkaçxha' vxitumée Dxus tasxte pa'jwa'j vxitxa' pteeza', aça' dxi'jwa txã'wẽy pteeza'. Kxtee yu' kuhmeeçxáa u'kana ũsta'.
Nasa ma'wẽ yuuçtewa mpa'yajkxwe jiyuya'
(Lk 6.43-44)
15 Dxus jxkaahnisatha'w jĩna ĩsxiisatxi' mpa'yajkxwe. Txãawe'sxa' i'kwe'sx tasxte yu' piisxáa kakwe yakhtxi vxya', nawa ũuste yu' yu'kh eçx weeswée na'wẽsata'. 16 I'kwe'sxa' txãawe'sx fxi'zeni's jiyune'kwe. Miiyajkxwe: Uwa's çhũçh tasxte ya'ude'çme', sa' brewa's khãas tasxte ya'ude'çme'. 17 Txã'wẽ fxtũu tasx ewsa' ewsaçxáak yũnu', aça' fxtũu tasx ewmeesa' ewmeesaíiçxáak yũnu'. 18 Fxtũu tasx ewsa' ũ' ewmeesa's yũnya' ãjameena. Txã'wẽy fxtũu tasx ewmeesa' ũ' ewsa's yũnya' ãjameena. 19 Maa fxtũu tasxwa ewmée yũnsa's spẽ'thçxa ipxte ya'kabu'k. 20 Txãa pa'ga txãawe'sx fxi'zeni's uyçxa jiyune'kwe ma'wẽsatewa.
Jxukaysa yu' cielute u'kameetxna
(Lk 13.25-27)
21 Yuwejuçxáa adxa's ew we'wesa jxuka yu' cielute Dxus jxkaahna ũsnite u'kawa'jsameeta', naasáa adx Tata jxthãasni's kxtey yuusaçxáa cielute u'kawa'jsata'. 22 Txãasa cielute u'kawa'j en ãhte', wala yuh maawa adxa's na'jĩtxna: Kwe'sxtxi jxpe'jsa mwẽese'j: Kwe'sxa' iidx yuwe's pta'sxtha'w, sa' iidx yase's kxsusçxa eçxthẽ'jtxwa kutxi'tha'w, sa' kim yuhwa ãjanimeesa'swa kaavxya'ja'tha'w maz uswa jĩtxna. 23 Nawa adxa' na'jĩna pasu'nja: Bagaçxte yuhwa adx jxpe'jnisa yu'çmeei'kwe, naa pa'ga i'kwe'sxtxi' jiimeeth. Ewmée yũuna fxi'zesa' adx tasxtewe'sx meei'kwe.
Dxusa's nwẽese'jsa vxite' Dxusa's nwẽese'jsamée
(Lk 6.47-49; Mr 1.22)
24 Maawa adx jxkaahni's wẽse'jçxa kxtey yuusa', yat txajxa' jiisa na'wẽsa yuuna: Txãa yata's kwette kaaçxida'jçxa txahte', 25 nus pa'jna, wejxa wala kiitena, yu' walaana yuuçxawa, yata's suwme', kwette kaaçxida'jçxa txaahnipa'ga. 26 Nawa maawa adx jxkaahni's wẽse'jçxawa kxtey yuusame', yat txajxa' jiimeesa na'wẽsa yuuna: Txãa yata's kwetmuseteçxáa txahte', 27 nus pa'jna, wejxa wala kiitena, yu' walaana yuute', txãa yata' weteçxa jxuka suweek jĩk.
28 Txajũ' Jesus we'wena jxã'the', nasa' wala kuh yajkxna neeyũutx txajx kaapiya'jni's wẽse'jçxa, 29 txã'wẽ leya's kaapiya'jsawe'sx na'wẽmée, nwẽese'jwa'ja's ũukhnus we'wena kaapiya'pha'ga.
