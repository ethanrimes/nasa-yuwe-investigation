6
Jxkaase entewa skuutxhtxi çxuktedeçxa ũ'ni
(Mt 12.1-8; Mr 2.23-28)
1 Teeçx sapatu jxkaase ente', Jesusa' skuutxh ehsu u'jweçte', txãa yakh u'jusawe'sxa' skuutxhtxi ũhdena nxinxtxi sũupi'jçxa ũ'tx. 2 Aça' fxariseowe'sx maaíi yu' pẽjxtxi:
—I'kwe'sxa' ¿mjĩipa'gakwe mjĩi en meetewa txã'wẽ yũ'? jĩte', 3 Jesusa' na'wẽk pas:
—I'kwe'sxa' Dxus librute iilẽeymeewá'kwe' Davxida' ¿kĩhga yũu? txãa yakh pi'kxna u'jusawe'sx yakh teeçx ente' wẽeçxa', 4 dxuus yatte u'kaçxa kxtee pantxi ũ'k, sa' txãa yakh u'jusawe'sxtxiwa ũsu'sku. Txãa pana' naasáa sacerdotewe'sxçxáa ũ'wa'the jiiçxawa txã'wẽk yũu. 5 Aça' Dxus Nçxi'k Nasa Yuusaawa makwe sapatu jxkaase entewa jxkaahwa'j ji'pha' jĩk.
Teeçx piçthẽ'j paçu kuse udxnisa
(Mt 12.9-14; Mr 3.1-6)
6 Vxite sapatu jxkaase ensu', Jesusa' judiuwe'sx pkhaakhenite u'kaçxa, Dxus yuwe's pta'sxya' takhku. Aça' kxte' teeçx piçthẽ'j paçu kuse udxnisak u'p yu'. 7 Aça' Dxus leya's kaapiya'jsawe'sx vxite' fxariseowe'sxa' Jesusa's peethegu'natx ũsu' txã'wẽ sapatu jxkaase entewa nuykatxhite' pãatxhĩ'khya' sũhçxa. 8 Nawa Jesusa' peethegu'sawe'sx txã'wẽ ũus yaakxni's jiyuçxa', na'jĩk txãa piçthẽ'j kuse udxnisa's:
—Kiiteçxa, nasaa pxãhte myuju jĩte', txãa piçthẽ'ja' nasaa pxãhtek yuju. 9 Aça' Jesusa' nasa kxte ũssatx na'jĩk:
—I'kwe'sx ¿ma'jĩki'kwe? Sapatu jxkaase entewa, pu'çxte ¿ewmanx? meeçxa' ¿pu'çxwa'jmeemanx? Nasa's nwe'wete ¿ewmanx? meeçxa' nwe'wemeete ¿ewmanx? jĩçxa', 10 Jesusa' nasatx thegçxa', txãa ãçã'sa's na'wẽk jxkaah:
—Kuse's mku'le'j jĩçpkaçxha' txã'wẽy yũute', txajx kuse' ewuuk. 11 Nawa vxitesa yu' wala ũusaçxatx, sa' puutx paapẽyi'tx: Jesusa's ¿ma'wẽ yũuçxa ewmeete nvxiitukxtha'w? jĩna.
Docesatx Dxus yuwe's pta'sxya' kaahni
(Mt 10.1-4; Mr 3.13-19)
12 Txãn ensuy Jesusa' Dxusna pẽjxya' u'kh thã'the sa' teeçx kus utak Dxusna pẽjxna pe'te. 13 Sa' khikhyna sẽhte', txã'sna pi'kxna u'jusa kuhsatx pa'yaçxa', doce nasa txhitxhku txajx jxkaahwa'j. 14 Txãawe'sxa' naaçxahtx yu': Simon, txã'sa' Pedro yasek ki'p. Vxite' nyakh Adres, txajũ' Jakobo, Juan, Fxelipe, Bartolomé, 15 Mateo, Tomas, Alfxeo nçxi'k Jakobo, Simon kananista nasa, 16 Jakobo nyakh Judas, vxite' Judas Iskariote, Jesusa's açesawe'sx kusete dukhwa'jsa.
Nasa seena' wala kuhsatx Jesus kaapiya'jni
(Mt 4.23-25)
17 Jesusa' txãa yakh u'jusawe'sx yakh thã'j vxiçu jxsa'jna kĩhçxa', teeçx ahte txaktek yuju nasa wala kuhsawe'sx yakh. Txãa nasa' Judea kiwesuwe'sx, Jerusalen çxhabuwe'sx, vxite' ĩkh wala pukasu Tiro Sidon çxhabsuwe'sxtxi Jesus pta'sxni's wẽsẽ'jya' pa'j, aça' vxite' ãçã'sa Jesus nuykatxhikahntxi pa'j. 18 Sa' eçx luuku vxit-satx ũuste ji'phsawa Jesus kutxi'the', katxhiçxatx neeyũ'. 19 Aça' nasa jxukaysa kxte ũssa' Jesusa's jxa'dxya' wala pa'pçxu'tx, txã'wẽ jxa'dxçxaçxáa maawa katxhiçtetx uyu', txãasatx txã'wẽy yũu wẽjẽ'.
Weçxana fxi'zewa'jsa vxite' nxuste pa'jwa'jsa
(Mt 5.1-12)
20 Txajũ' Jesusa' txãa yakh u'jusawe'sxtxi thegçxa', na'jĩk:
—I'kwe'sxa' kĩhwa ji'phmeeçxawa kuh yajkxmée weçxana mfxi'zewe, txãa pa'gatey Dxus jxkaahna ũsni' i'kwe'sx jĩ' naa pa'ga.
21 I'kwe'sxa' ãçxhíi yu' wẽena ũsçxawa weçxana meswe, txãa pa'gatey wala sã'jĩni yuune'kwe.
I'kwe'sxa' ãçxhíi yu' nxusna ũsçxawa weçxana meswe, txãa pa'gatey sxijkana fxi'zewa'ja's jxpa'gawa'jsai'kwe.
22 I'kwe'sxa' Dxus Nçxi'k Nasa Yuusa's nwẽesẽ'pha'ga ewmeesathas i'kwe'sx yasetx açewe'wena txãawe'sx tasxu kutxi'jna txã'wẽ yũuçtewa, weçxana mfxi'zewe. 23 Txãniitey Dxus yuwe pta'sxsawe'sxtxiwa txã'wẽtx pkxuu na' ãçxwa txã'wẽyçxáa yuutxna. Nawa nxusmée weçxana mfxi'zewe, txãa pa'gatey cielute peesni's jxpa'gawa'j ji'phi'kwe.
24 Nawa ji'phsawe'sx ãçxhíi weçxana fxi'zeni pçunijũ', wala yuh nxuste pa'jane'kwe.
25 I'kwe'sxa' ãçxhí' wẽt txikna ũsi'kwe, nawa e'su pxthaa yuh wẽjẽne'kwe. I'kwe'sxa' ãçxhí' sxijkana ũsi'kwe, nawa e'su nxusna ũ'nene'kwe.
26 I'kwe'sx yu', ãçxhíi yu' maa maa nasawa ewçxáa tuuthe'jni fxi'zeçxawa, e'su nxuste neeyũune'kwe. Nyafxiiteywa Dxus yuwe's pta'sxsatha'w jĩna ĩsxĩina u'jusatxi' txã'wẽtx ewsata' jĩna tuuthe'je'.
Açesatx peeygãawa'ja'
(Mt 5.38-48; 7.12)
27 Nawa ayte wẽsẽ'jna ũssa i'kwe'sxtxi' na'wẽth jxkaaja': Puíi jxpakwesatxi' mpeeygãhwe. Açesatxi' ew yũuna mfxi'zewe. 28 Ewmée nuywe'wesatxi's ka'tu' ewçxáa mwe'wewe. Wẽesxusatxi' txãawe'sx pa'gate Dxusa's mneewe'wewe. 29 Idxa's pukakwetga pehtxate', ka'tuwe'sxa'swa petxakahn mes. Maaí' atxha's kusa'jaçyã'the', puiimée kmiisa yuhnawa mes. 30 Pe'wsa's mes, sa' iidx jĩ's kusa'jçxa', dukhmeetewa pãatxhĩ'khna pẽynu. 31 Maa nasawa i'kwe'sxtxi ewçxáa yũuna fxi'ze jxthãasçxa', i'kwe'sxwa txã'wẽy maa nasatxwa ewçxáa yũuna mfxi'zewe. 32 I'kwe'sxtxi peeygãasaçxáatx ka'tuwa peeygãhçxa', ew yũusatha'w ¿jĩya' ãjãma'kwe? Makwe nasa ewmeesaçxawa txã'wẽ peeygãasaçxáatx peeygãjxa' yu' ãjã'tx aça'. 33 I'kwe'sxtxi ew yũusaçxáatx ka'tuwa ew yũuçxa', i'kwe'sxa' ew yũusatha'w ¿jĩya' ãjãma'kwe? Makwe pkalsaçxawa txã'wẽ ew yũusaçxáatx ew yũuya' yu' ãjã'tx. 34 Sa' i'kwe'sxtxi ki' kaasxwedu'jya' ãjãwa'jsaçxá's pki'jçxa', i'kwe'sxa' pu'çxsatha'w ¿jĩya' ãjãma'kwe? Makwe nasa ewmeesaçxawa, txã'wẽ kaasxwedu'jya' ãjãwa'jsaçxáatx pki'jya' yu' ãjã'tx. 35 Wejxwa i'kwe'sxa' puíi jxpakwesaatx peeygãhna fxi'ze ji'phi'kwe, sa' pki'jçxawa jxthaakwe kaasxwedu'juna sũhmée mfxi'zewe. Txã'wẽ ew yũusaatxi' Dxusa' wala deweena, aça' i'kwe'sxa' cielute Dxus luuçx yuune'kwe, txãa pa'gatey Dxus cielutewe'sxa' txã'sna yaakxsamée makwe ewmeesatewa ka'tu' ewçxáa yũuna ũsa'. 36 I'kwe'sxa' peeygãasa myuuwe, i'kwe'sx Tatawa ma'wẽ peeygãasana txã'wẽy.
Jiimeeçxa' nuywe'wewa'jme'
(Mt 7.1-5)
37 Ma'wẽsatewa jiimeeçxa', ewmeesata' jĩna we'wewa'jme', txã'wẽ yũupa'ga i'kwe'sxwa Dxus dxi'pte ewmeesa yuuya'mée. Vxitetx nuywe'wewa'jme', ka'tuwa nuywe'wekahmén. Ãhmée yũusaatx peltunãywa'ja', Dxuswa i'kwe'sxtxi peltunãykahn. 38 Vxitetx peeswa'ja', ka'tuwa i'kwe'sxtxi Dxusa' peeskahn. Makwe isaçxa vxitetx peeste', ka'tuwa i'kwe'sxtxi Dxusa' txãa isanitey ew uta pu'kisu'sna zuuna' ãsxçxa peesnisa's jxpa'gane'kwe.
39 Teeçx ejemplo kaaja'da'jçxa' na'wẽk pta'sx:
—¿Yafx ji'phmeesa' vxitewa txã'wẽysa's dxi'the pe'jna u'jya' ãjãma'k? ¿E'zíi kafxkhẽ khẽemeema'tx? 40 Piyasá' kaapiya'jsate jxthaakwesame'. Nawa maawa jxuka piyan jxã'jçxa yuuçxáa, kaapiya'jsa na'wẽy yuuna. 41 ¿Ma'wẽçxa iidx yakhthẽ'j yafxte kxtã' le'çxkuẽesa khẽeçxa ũstewa, uyya' ãjãki'g? iidx yafxte yu' fxtũ pe'la yuu walasa khẽeçxa ũste'. 42 ¿Ma'wẽçxa iidx yakhthẽ'ja's pẽyiki'g: Iidx yafxte kxtã'a's kutxi'ji'nja jĩna? iidx yafxte yu', fxtũ pe'la walasa khẽeçxa u'ptewa txãa yu'sa' uymeeçxa'. Ãakhẽçxáa ew we'wena u'jusathẽ'j, iidx yafxte fxtũ pe'la walasa khuẽ'yna mkuutxi'j, sa' txã'wẽ yũuçxaçxáa iidx yakhthẽ'j yafxte kxtã' le'çxkuẽsa's kutxi'jya' ãjãne'ga.
Nasa ma'wẽ yũuçtewa mpa'yajkxwe jiyuya'
(Mt 7.17-20; 12.34-35)
43 Fxtũu tasx ewsa', ewsaíiçxáak yũnu', nawa fxtũu tasx ewmeesa', ewmeesa íiçxáak yũnu'. 44 Fxtũu tasx ma'wẽsatewa yũntek isa ew ya'jiyu'. Brewa's khãas tasxte ya'ude'çme', sa' uwa's çhũçh tasxte ya'ude'çme'. 45 Nasa ewsa' ewçxáatx we'we', ewsa's ũuste ji'phna txãa pa'ga. Nawa nasa ewmeesa', kazxçxáatx we'we', txãa pa'gatey ũuste yuu, kazxsa u'pna aça' we'wenisa' ũusuhne' kaseje' naa pa'ga.
Dxusa's nwẽese'jsa vxite' Dxusa's nwẽese'jsamée
(Mt 7.24-27)
46 I'kwe'sxa' adxa's Jxpe'jsa jĩ'i'kwe sa' ¿kĩh yuuçxakwe adx jxkaahni's kxtey yu'çmée? 47 Maa nasawa adx tasxte utxaaçxa', adx we'weni's nwẽesẽ'jçxa jxkaahni's kxtey yũusa' txã' na'wẽsa': 48 Teeçx piçthẽ'ja' yu'wala pukate kwet walate kaaçxida'jçxa yat txajk. Txajũ' yu' walaaçxa', kxsũ afxihbna skhẽwçxawa yata's kẽesẽ'jme', kwette kaaçxida'jçxa txaahnipa'ga. 49 Nawa maa nasawa adx we'weni's wẽsẽ'jçxawa, adx jxkaahni's kxtey yuumeena txã' teeçx piçthẽ'ja' yu'wala pukate yata's kwetmuse kajkweteçxáa txaahni na'wẽsa'. Yu' walaaçxa', tũçxhaçxa txãa yata's pẽtxçxa jxuka suwku.
