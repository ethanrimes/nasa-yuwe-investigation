12
U'ya's eçx seena'sa ikh wẽeni
1 Txajũ' ẽe walaju na'wẽk seena'sa vxyaa: teeçx u'y txajx kakwete yu' sekçxaçxáa ji'phsa a'tete yujuçxak ũsu', sa' dxikthete yu' teeçx korona doce estrelxa ji'phsa'sku ji'phu'. 2 Txãa u'ya' nasatek ãçã'aç yu', sa' seena' wala pxthaa yakana pebe'k.
3 Txãa e'su' ẽe walaju ki' vxite na'wẽk vxyaa: eçx behsa iiméh seena'sa siete dxikthé ji'phsa, diez kaaçxuk ji'phu', sa' dxikthé iisa teeçx koronak ji'phu'. 4 Txajx mezu yu' estrelxatx puupxãhn ãatuwku suwçxa naa kiwekhẽ pũsx. Sa' txãa eçx seena'sa yu' u'y nasa kasejxa' ũsu'k txãa yaçtek ũythasna yuju, nasa kasejeçpkaçxha' luuçxa's ũ'ya'. 5 Txajũ' txãa u'ya' teeçx luuçx pihçku ji'phu, txãak naa kiwete jxuka jxkaahwa'jsa yu' iiméh wala juuna'. Txajũ' txãa luuçxa's kusa'jçxa, Dxus jxkaahna ũsnitek jxũna u'jni yuu. 6 Aça' txãa u'ya' nasa u'pnimée kiwetek ũphna u'j, txãa jĩi Dxus pheu'jçxa ji'phune' kxtee ũsya'. Kxtee pa'the', mil doscientos sesenta en ãhpkaçx sã'jĩni ũswa'jsa'.
7 Txãa e'su' cielute puiiwa'kh u'pu', aça' Dxus angel npiiçthẽ'jsa Migwela' txajx angeleswe'sx yakh puiitx eçx seena'sa yakh. Aça' eçx seena'sawa txãa yakh ũssawe'sx yakh kasejk puiiya'. 8 Nawa eçxa' knayme', sa' txãawe'sxa' cielute ũswa'j ji'phmeetx neeyũu. 9 Txãa pa'ga eçx seena'sa's ekaju wãataanik yuu, txãaíik txãniitey ul jĩnisa yu', naa kiwetewe'sx jxukaysatx ĩsxĩina kaypuba'jsa Eçxthẽ'kh yaase yu'. Txãa eçxthẽ'j vxite' txãa yakh ũssa jxukaysatx naa kiwekhẽ wãataanitx yuu.
10 Txajũ' cieluju sus we'wena na'jĩni'sthu wẽse'j:
—Ewte neeyũuwa'j en pa'kh, kwe'sx Dxus txãaçxaçxáa jxkaahwa'j en vxite' Dxus txhitxhnisa Kristowa txã'wẽy jxkaahwa'j en. Txãa pa'gatey kwe'sx yakhthẽ'jwe'sxtxi ewmeesata' jĩna pãatxhĩ'khna ũssa's ekaju wãataanik yuu, txãaíik bagaçxtewa pu'vxitumée nes yu' kwe'sxtxi pãatxhĩ'khna. 11 Txã'wẽtewa, kwe'sx yakhthẽ'jwe'sxa' eçx ewmeesa's knaytxi, na'wẽ Piisxakuẽ jĩnisa ikhni uuk txajx eeju vxite' txajx kaapiya'jni's pu'vxitumée pta'sxna fxi'zepa'ga. Txãawe'sxa' Kristo's selpiipa'ga uu en pa'thewa txã'sa' nxusmée uuya'çxáatx ũsu'. 12 Txã'wẽ ewmeesa's knaypa'ga weçxweçxa miiyajkxwe, cielute fxi'zesawe'sx jxuka. Nawa seena' yuh wala ũukhũukha' naa kiwete mteewe'sxwa jxukaysa yakha'. Txã'wẽ ũukhũukh yuukahníi eçxthẽ'ja' i'kwe'sxtxi pkxuuya' kĩjk seena' wala ũusaçxana, sa' kuh enmée ũswa'ja's jiia' txãasa txã'wẽ ũusaçxana ũsa'.
13 Sa' txãa eçx seena'sa' kiwekhẽ wãataani yuuçxa', txãa u'y luuçx ji'phusa's ikhya' pakwek. 14 Nawa txãa u'ya's uuh wala fxu ku'ta na'wẽsa's ũsnik yuu, eçx seena'sa tasxu iyuna u'jçxa nasa u'pnimée kiwete txajx ũswa'the fxuhna u'khan, kxteea' tekh anxu pxãhtepkaçx sã'jĩnik fxi'zewa'jsa yu'. 15 Txajũ' txãa eçxa' yuwesuk yu' nuysẽh, yu'a's seena' wala nuywalaaçxa txãa u'ya's yu' jxũna u'khan. 16 Nawa txãa eçx yuwesu yu'sẽesa's kiwe' jxuka çxazxaçxa txãa u'ya's nwe'wek. 17-18 Txã'wẽ yũute', txãa eçx seena'sa' txãa u'ya's jweeíiçxáak seena' wala ũusaçxa. Sa' txãa u'y ji'jwe'sx yakh puiiya'k u'j Dxus jxkaahnitxi's nwẽese'jna Jesukristo kaapiya'jni's jxpubamée nwẽese'jsawe'sx yakh.
