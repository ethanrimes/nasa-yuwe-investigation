Macedonia vxite' Grecia kiwen Pablo u'jni
1 Yuwe susni pçuute', Pablo' Dxus yakh fxi'zesawe'sxtxi pa'yaçxa wala ew yu'kxpehçxak txãawe'sxtxi weçxaçxa Macedonia kiwen u'j. 2 Txãa kiwesu jxuka pekũjk yakhthẽ'jwe'sx ũukhwe'sxte kçxhãaçxha'jna. Sa' txajũ' Grecia kiwenwa u'kh. 3 Kxte tekh a'te ũsçxa', barkote khẽeçxa Siria kiwen u'jya'wak yu'. Nawa judiuwe'sx ikhya'wata' txãa dxi'the jĩni's jiyuçxaçxáa, barkote u'jmée çxidaçxáa maa dxi'june' pa'j txã'wẽyku sxawedxa' yajkx, Macedonia kiwejuy uykhẽwna. 4 Txãa yakh pi'kxna u'jtx Sópater Bereajuwe'sx, Aristarko vxite' Segudo Tesalonikajuwe'sx vxite' Gayo Derbejuwe'sx vxite' Timoteo vxite' Tíkiko Trófximo txãawe'sxa' Asia kiwetewe'sxtxi yu'. 5 Txãa yakhthẽ'jwe'sxa' dxi'j pxãjũ yaçte u'jçxa Troas çxhabtetx kwe'sxtxi ũythas. 6 Pan lewadura ji'phmée ũ'ni fxiesta skhẽwniitetha'w kwe'sxa' kaseh, Fxilipos çxhabuh barkote khẽeçxa, sa' tahç ente' kpajkxtha'w Troas çxhabte. Kxte siete entha'w ũsu'.
Troas çxhabte Pablo weçxaya' pa'jni
7 Ki'sen ente', Dxus yakh fxi'zesawe'sx yakh pkhaakhetha'w kxteçxah pkhaakheçxa ũ'ya'. Naapkaçxha' Pablo' Dxus yuwe's kaapiya'kh. Txã'wẽ kuskuskhẽ' u'jwe ji'phçxa', txaçx kuspxãhnpkaçxku pta'sxna ũsu', 8 teeçx piesate ẽete wala kuh lampara pki'tanisa ũsu'tx kxtee. 9 Aça' kxte teeçx piçtakx Eutiko yaasesak wentanate kaçxçxa u'pu'. Txã'wẽ Pablo' wala jxu'j we'wete', txãa piçtakxa' dee wẽeçxa dehna u'píi saapajkxku yat ẽe walaju, aça' uuniisa'stxi kiis. 10 Txã'wẽ yũute', Pablo' sa'jçxa, txãa uusa's psxa'dçxa na'wẽk we'we:
—Yakhthẽ'jwe'sx, nxusnuwe, ki' ĩtxi yuuk jĩnak pta'sx. 11 Txajũ' Pablo' ki' ẽe yatte tejkaçxa Santa Cena's puuçku. Txã'wẽ yũuçxa khikhyna sẽhpkaçx pta'sxçxak u'j. 12 Txãa piçtakxa' ki' ĩtxi yuute', pe'jna u'jçxa jxukaysa wala weçxana neeyũutx.
Troas çxhabu Mileto çxhabna u'jni
13 Kwe'sxa' barkote khẽeçxa yaçte u'tha'w Asón çxhabna, Pablo's kxte ũythasya'. Naapkaçxha' txã' çxidajk u'j. 14 Asón çxhabujk kwe'sx yakh barkote khẽe, aça' txajũtha'w Mitilene çxhabna u'j. 15 Txajũ ki' kasehçxa vxite enkhẽ' Kío kiwe pukasutha'w skhẽw. Sa' vxite enkhẽ' Samos kiwetek barko' dudte utxaa. Txajũ ki' u'jçxa', teeçx ahte Trogilio yaasesate pa'jçxatha'w jxkaase. Txajũ' vxite enkhẽ' ki' u'jçxa' Mileto çxhabte pa'tha'w. 16 Pablo' Asia kiwete jxthẽeyuuya'mée, Efxeso çxhabte u'kame', Jerusalente dud pa'jçxa Pentekostes fxiestate ũsya' sũhçxa.
Mileto çxhabu Efxesotewe'sxtxi pa'yani
17 Sa' Mileto çxhabte pa'jçxa', Efxeso çxhabte ancianowe'sxtxi pa'yak na'wẽ we'weya':
18 —Adxa' Asia kiwete nyafxçxah pa'jíi kĩhtka yũuna fxi'ze ãçxpkaçx txã'sa' i'kwe'sxa' jii i'kwe. 19 Judiuwe'sx açepa'ga txakwe wala nxuswa'j en pa'thewa, Jesukristo's een isa selpiina bagaçxtewa i'kwe'sxtxi ũ'ne petx khakh pu'çxna nesthu yu' kim yuhnawa iiwejçx we'wemée. 20 I'kwe'sxtxi selpiwa'jsa Dxus yuwe's pta'sxmée yu' yuumée, nasaa ksxavxysu yat-suwa kaapiya'jna nesthu yu'. 21 Txã'wẽy judiuwe'sx vxite' judiu ji'jmée txãawe'sxtxiwa jxukaysatx pta'sxthu: Dxus tasxte sxawedxa' pkaltxi nvxihtçxa kwe'sxtxi jxpe'jsa Jesukristo'swa ũuste miipa'gawe jĩna. 22 Ãçxha' u'jweçthu Jerusalenna Espiritu kaahni. Nawa jiimée u'jweçthu kxte jwee kĩhçxa yuune'thewa. 23 Naasáa na'wẽ yuuçxáa jiith: maa çxhabte u'thewa Dxus Espiritu' pta'sxna nesku yu' karcelte kxuuphni wala nxuswa'j ji'phgu jĩna. 24 Nawa txã'sa' yaaki'çmeeth, sa' adx uuwa'ja's yuhwa ũukhu'çmeeth. Jesukristo neewe'wek Dxus peeygãani's txã'sna uu entepkaçx weçxana pta'sxna fxi'zewaççxáa, txã'wẽena sũju'th.
25 Ayte i'kwe'sx yakh ũsçxa' Dxus jxkaahwa'j ena's pta'sxthu txã's wẽse'jsa aytewe'sxa' adxa's ãçxhuyã'ja' peena yu' uymeene'kwe sũju'th. 26 Nawa adxa' i'kwe'sxtxi kaapiya'jyã'th na' yuuwemeeth, 27 txãa pa'gatey Dxus jxthãasni's paaçte nvxihtmée jxuka ãateçxaçxáath pta'sx naa pa'ga. 28 Aça' ãçxha' peekíi ya'mpa'yajkxwe, txã'wẽy Jesukristo ee pkawçxa weynisa Dxus luuçxtxiwa ew jxpe'jna, Dxus Espiritu' i'kwe'sxtxik thegwa'jsa nvxiht naa pa'ga. 29 Jiith adx u'the', vxite pa'jatxna Dxus yakh fxi'zesawe'sxtxi ivxiitu wẽena, sxĩ'j weesweewa ma'wẽta piisxaatx ivxiitu' txã'wẽy yũuya'. 30 Jxpa'yakxmeeçxa', i'kwe'sx pwe'sxíiwa kasejene'kwe isasa's pta'sxipkaçxte kaypuba'jwa'jçxá's pta'sxna Dxus yakh fxi'zesatx kaypuba'jna. 31 I'kwe'sxa' miiyajkxwe, adxa' tekh anxuth teeçxsa na'wẽçxah yu'kxpehna ũsu' en petx kus yã'jçxa ũ'ne petx khakh.
32 Ãçxha' yakhthẽ'jwe'sxtxi Dxusa's neewe'weçxa txajx kusete nvxiituçthu txajx kaapiya'jni's peçxkanamée fxi'zekahn, txãa pa'gatey txajx yuwe' kçxhãaçxha'jna weçxana kaafxi'ze'jçxa cielute txajx nwe'wenisa jxukaysa yakh ũswa'ja'swa ũsya' ãjawa'jsa'. 33 Adxa' i'kwe'sx yakh ũsçxa', vxyu, ropa, txã'wẽsa pees jxthãasna fxi'zemeeth kim yuhnawa. 34 I'kwe'sxa' ew jii i'kwe txã'wẽ yũumée, adx kuseju mjĩiçxath kĩh peejini'swa uyu', vxite' adx yakh u'jusawe'sx jĩiwa txã'wẽy. 35 Txã'wẽ vxite jxpeejxsatx pu'çxwa'ja's kaapiya'jnath fxi'ze bagaçxtewa. Txã'wẽ yũukahníik Jesukristowa na'jĩ': Peessa yakh wejx weçxa yuja' jxpa'gasate jxthaakwe jĩ'k.
36 Pablo' we'wena jxã'jçxa', jxukaysa yakh peejxũkweçxa Dxus yakh puutx we'wek. 37 Txajũ' jxukaysa ũ'nena psxa'dçxa Pablo's weçxatx. 38 Txã'wẽ peena yu' uymeene'kwe jĩte', wala nxusna ũsu'tx, sa' pi'kxna u'jtx barkote khẽepkaçx.
