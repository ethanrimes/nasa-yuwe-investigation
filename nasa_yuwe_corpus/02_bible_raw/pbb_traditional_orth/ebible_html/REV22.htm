22
1 Txajũ' Dxus angela' teeçx yu'wala seena' zhiçxkwe çxihme atesa's pta'sxku, bagaçxte yuhwa uumée kaafxi'ze'jwa'jsa's. Sa' txãa yu'a' Dxus jxkaahna ũsni vxite' Piisxakuẽ jĩnisa txãawe'sx jxkaahna ũsnijũk kasehna ũsu'. 2 Çxhabte klxiikhun walasa pxãhte vxite' yu'wala pukate puutx pdxi'p fxtũutasx buçxaçxa walaana kãhtx, bagaçxte yuhwa uumée kaafxi'ze'jwa'jsa. Sa' txãa fxtũutasxa' a'te iisak yũnna nes yu', txãasak anxute' doce weces yũnu', naapkaçxha' eç yu' yu'çek selpi' maa kiwejũ nasatxwa jxuka. 3 Kxteea' ewmeesa yu' kĩh yuhwamée yuuna. Txãa çxhabte' Dxus vxite' Piisxakuẽ jĩnisa txãawe'sx jxkaahna ũsutxna, naapkaçxha' txãawe'sxtxi selpisaawe'sxa' weçxana neewe'wena nes yuutxna. 4 Kxteea' Dxus yakh pdxi'puçxa puutx uyya' ãjãtxna, sa' Dxus yase's txãawe'sx dxikthesu ji'phsa yuutxna. 5 Kxteea' çxhi'dxwa'j enmée yuuna, aça' kxtee fxi'zesa' pki'tawa'jwa peejimeetxna, sa' sek kweethniwa peejimeena, txãa pa'gatey eena' kweethsa' Dxusíiçxá'. Aça' kxtee ũssa' bagaçxtewa weçxana nes yuutxna.
Jesukristo kĩiwa'j en utxa ũsa'
6 Txajũ' Dxus angela' adxa's na'jĩnak we'we:
—Na'wẽ pta'sxnisa' isa kxtey yuuwa'jsata', aça' txãatxi's isasa yuhtxna sũhna wa'lmée jxpa'gawa'ja'. Txãa pa'gatey nyafxíi txajx yuwe's pta'sxsawe'sxtxi kaaũusuthe'kh txãa Dxus íiçxáak ãçxwa txajx angela's kaah, na'wẽ kĩhwa yũuya'wa' jwee yu' jxthẽeyuumée txãatx kaajiyu'khan ãçxh naa ensu selpisaawe'sxtxi.
7 Txajũ' Jesus we'weçxa':
—Txaçxmey yuwe'nja, aça' naa librute Dxus yuwe's fxi'jni ũsta' txãatx kxtey yũusa' seena' weçxana neeyũutxna jĩ'k.
8 Juan yaasesa adxa' txã'wẽsatxthu uyna wẽse'jna yuu. Sa' txã'wẽ uyna wẽse'jçxa', Dxus angel kaavxya'ja'kh txajx tasxte peejxũkweth neewe'weya'. 9 Nawa txãa yu' na'wẽk we'we adxa's:
—Adxa's peejxũkwenu. Adxa' idx na'wẽysaíiçxáath, iidx yakhthẽ'jwe'sxwa ma'wẽta Dxus yuwe's pta'sxna fxi'ze' txã'wẽsath, vxite' Dxus librute fxi'jni ũsa' txã'sna kxtey yuusawe'sx na'wẽysaíiçxáath Dxusna selpisáa. Aça' wejxwa Dxusnaçxáa mneewe'we jĩ'k.
10 Txajũ' ki' we'wek na'jĩna:
—Naa librute Dxus yuwe's fxi'jni ũsa' txã'sa' pta'sxmée yu' yuunu. Txã'wẽme' kxtee we'wenisa kxtey yuuwa'j en ãjxa'wa'. 11 Aça' txãa en ãanijuyã'ja', pta'sxni's nwẽese'jsamée yu' jweeíiçxáa ewmée kazx yuuna fxi'zetxna, naapkaçxha' pta'sxni's nwẽese'jsa' jweeíiçxáa Dxus yakh txutemée çxhãçxha fxi'zetxna.
12 Txajũ' Jesus ki' we'weçxa':
—Txaçxmey yuuyuju'nja. Sa' teeçxsa na'wẽçxah kĩh yũuna fxi'zesatewa txã'wẽ fxi'zepa'ga deweni's jxũçxa yuwe'nja. 13 Adxa' naa kiwe meetey ũsyã'th yu', sa' bagaçxtewa nes yuuwa'jsath. Adxthu nyafxte ũsya' takh, sa' jxuka skhẽwtewa adx íiçxáa neeyũuwa'jsath pçuwa'jsamée.
14 Maawa athni ropa's yaateçxa ji'phsa yakha' wala yuh weçxa yuja', Dxus çxhabte vxitxte u'kaçxa kxtee fxtũutasx bagaçxte yuhwa uumée kaafxi'ze'jwa'jsa's ũ'ya' u'kasa yakha'. 15 Nawa kazx ewmeesawe'sx, dxijxuuna fxi'zesa, kazx pkal yũuna fxi'zesa, nasa ikhsa, Dxus ĩtxĩmeesatx neewe'wesa, kĩhtewa jxuka ĩsxĩiwa'jçxá's yajkxna fxi'zesa txã'wẽ yũusawe'sxa' u'kaya' ãjãmeeçxa ekaju neeyũutxna.
16 Jesus ki' we'weçxa': Adxa' adx tasxte selpisáa angela's kaath, Dxus luuçxwe'sxtxi txã'wẽsa's jxuka pta'sxkahn. Adxa' nyafxtewe'sxthu, sa' jxkaahsa Davxid ji'the kasehsath. Sa' khikhiiçte estrelxa zhiçxkwe eena' kweethsath jĩ'k.
17 Aça' Dxus Espiritu vxite' Piisxakuẽ jĩnisa nyuu txãawe'sxa' pi'ki'tx:
—Myuh jĩna.
Aça' txã'wẽ pi'kxni's wẽse'jsa' jweeju mpa'yawe. Aça' yũ'wẽesa' utxaaçxa mpa'gawe yu' bagaçxteçxáa yuhwa uuwa'jmeete kaafxi'ze'jsa's, kĩh yuhwa vxyuu pa'gamée.
18 Dxus yuwe naa librute fxi'jni ũsa' txã'sna nwẽese'jsa jxukaysatx adxa' na'wẽth yu'kxpeje': Dxus yuwe naa librute fxi'jni ũsa' txãate kĩhçxáawa yutxawáatxna aça' txã'wẽ yũusatxi's na'wẽ naa librute kastigo fxi'jni ũsta' txãatx jxpa'gawa'j vxituna Dxusa'. 19 Meeçxawa Dxus yuwe naa librute fxi'jni ũsa' txã'sna kĩhçxáawa ivxiituwáatxna aça' txã'wẽ yũusatxi's Dxusa' puuçmeena txãa fxtũutasx bagaçxte yuhwa uumée kaafxi'ze'jwa'jsa's. Sa' na'wẽ pta'sxna fxi'jni yuuk txãa çxhabtewa ka'ga'jmeena.
20 Txã'wẽ yũuwa'jsa' na'jĩ'k:
—Txaçxmey yuuyuju'nja jĩte', passa':
—Txã'jĩwaççxáawa, wẽt yuuna. Nawa dud myuh, Jesus.
21 Kwe'sxtxi jxpe'jsa Jesukristo' i'kwe'sx jxukaysatx pu'çxhina.
