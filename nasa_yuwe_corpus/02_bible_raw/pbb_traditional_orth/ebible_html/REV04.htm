4
Dxusa's cielute weçxana ũsni
1 Txãa e'su' ki' thegçxa' uythu cielute vxitx phateçxa ũste. Aça' nyafxíi we'weni na'wẽy we'weni ki' wẽse'th trompeta's seena' sus tukani, sa' na'jĩ'k:
—Ay ẽete myuh, kxtee kaavxya'ja'jya'wath naa e'su kĩhçxa yuune'thewa txãatx.
2 Txã'jĩçpkaçxha' txãa dudtey Dxus Espiritu aphniith yuuna pa'j, sa' uythu cielute jxkaahna ũsnisa's. 3 Txãa jxkaahna ũsnite yu', teeçx kaçxçxa u'pu'k, sa' kxtee u'psa yu' iiméh seena' zhiçxkwe zmeena' kuh jwed khikhyna u'pu'k. Sa' txãa peeku'jsu yu' kxthũus iiméh zhiçxkwe kweethna ũsu'k. 4 Txãa jxkaahna ũsni pukasu yu' vxite jxkaahna ũsni weintikuatro ãhtx ũsu', aça' kxsũ yu' Dxus luuçxtxi jxkaahsa weintikuatrosatx u'pu', ropa çxihme athçxa dxikthesu yu' vxyuu bee korona ji'phsa. 5 Txãa jxkaahna ũsnijũ yu', kpi'sx kweneni na'wẽsak kasehna ũsu' seena' susna, sa' jxkaahna ũsni dxi'pte yu' siete ipx pki'tanitx behna ũsu' txãa yu' Dxus tasxte ũssa siete espiritusawe'sxtxi yu'. 6 Txãa dxi'pteyçxáa mar na'wẽsak zhiçxkwe khikhyna ũsu'.
Txãa jxkaahna ũsni pxãhsu vxite' peeku'jsu yu' Dxus vxitnisa ĩtxĩsa pahztxi ũsu', kakwete jxuka yafxçxaçxáa ji'phsa. 7 Txãa pahzsa nyafxtewe'sxa' león na'wẽk yu'. E'stewe'sxa' tulu na'wẽk yu'. Tekhtewe'sxa' nasa na'wẽk dxi'p ji'phu'. Aça' pahztewe'sxa' uh na'wẽk fxuusa yu'. 8 Txãa Dxus vxitnisa pahzsa' teeçxsa na'wẽçxah seis fxu ku'tatx ji'phu', sa' yafxçxaçxáa kakwete jxukatx ji'phsa yu'. Txãawe'sxa' kus ma'k yuu en ma'k yuu nawa bagaçxte yuhwa wahtxmeetx na'jĩna ũsu':
Dxus iiméh seena' walasa,
Kĩh pkal yuhwa ji'phmée iiméh seena' atesa,
Nyafxíiwa txãak yu', sa' ãçxwa txãaíiçxá',
Sa' txãaíiçxáa bagaçxtewa nes yuuna jĩnatx we'wena nes yu'.
9 Sa' kxtee jxkaahna ũsnite kaçxçxa bagaçxteçxáa yuhwa uumée nes yuuwa'jsa's Dxus vxitnisa txã'wẽ tuuthe'jna weçxaaçpkaçxha', 10 Dxus luuçxtxi jxkaahsa weintikuatrosawe'sxa' bagaçxteçxáa yuhwa uumée nes yuuwa'jsa tasxte peejxũkweçxa txãawe'sx koronatx txajx tasxte ki'pçxa na'jĩnatx weçxana tuuthe'je':
11 Kwe'sx Dxus idx yuuçxáa jxpa'gawa'jsag weçxana tuuthe'jni's,
Txãa pa'gatey idxçxaçxáag kĩhnawa jxuka vxit,
Aça' iidx peeygãhçxa taki'jnisaçxaçxáa ũsta' jĩ'tx.
