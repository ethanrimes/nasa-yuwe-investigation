18
Kim wejx jxthaakwe jxtuhthesakx
(Mr 9.33-37; Lk 9.46-48)
1 Txãa ensu' Jesus yakh u'jusa docesawe'sxa' txajx tasxte utxaaçxa na'wẽtx pẽjx:
—Naa kiwete Dxus jxkaajxa' kĩi ente' ¿maa wejx jxthaakwe walasakxtha'w? jĩte', 2 Jesusa' teeçx luuçxkuẽ's pa'yaçxa txãawe'sx ksxavxyte txahçxa 3 na'wẽk we'we:
—Isath we'we', i'kwe'sxa' iiwejçx yaakxni's nvxihtçxa naa luuçx na'wẽ yuumeeçxa yu', Dxus jxkaahna ũsnite u'kaya' ãjameene'kwe. 4 Txã'wẽ kĩhtewa ãhmeekwesa naa luuçx na'wẽ yuusa yuuçxáa, Dxus jxkaahna ũsnite' maatewa jxthaakwe walasa yuuna. 5 Maa nasawa adxna jxpa'gaççxa ma'wẽ weçxana jxpa'gatx txã'wẽy weçxana naa luuçxkuẽ na'wẽsatx jxpa'gana txã' adxna jxpa'gasa yuuna.
Pkalte kweete'jya'mée jxpa'yakxwa'ja'
(Mr 9.42-48; Lk 17.1-2)
6 Adxna jxpa'gasa ãhmeekwesatx maawa pkalte kweete'jsa's txhikhte skuutxh ũ'khni kweta's tudçxa ĩkh wala dxiikhẽ akhwaççxáa, wejx ewna. 7 Naa kiwete ewmeesa' bagaçxtewa nes yuutxna. Nawa naa kiwete ewmeesa yakha' seena' yuh ũukhũukha'. Sa' maawa txã'wẽ pkalte kweete'jena txãa yu' weh yuhwa iyumeena.
8 Txãa pa'ga iidx kuse ma'k yuu meeçxa' iidx çxida ma'k yuu nawa txã'wẽ pkalte kweete'jpçxãh yuuçte', txã'sa' spẽ'thçxa mwãatah. Txã'wẽ pçuwa'jmée ipx walate e'z kuse e'z çxida ji'ph akhni yuu na'wẽ', wejxwa pçumée ĩtxi fxi'zewa'the çxida ji'phmée legkuẽçxáawa kxtee u'kani wejx ewa'. 9 Iidx yafx txã'wẽ pkalte kweete'jpçxãh yuuçte', kutxi'jçxa mwãatah. Infxierno ipxte e'z yafx ji'ph ũs na'wẽ', wejxwa pçumée ĩtxi fxi'zewa'the teeçx yafx yakhçxáawa u'kani wejx ewa'.
Piisxáa vxitusa yakh kaaja'da'jni
(Lk 15.3-7)
10 Naawe'sx ãhmeekwesa maa yuhtxwa açenuwe. Na'jĩ'th na': naawe'sxtxi pi'kxna u'jusa angeleswe'sxa' cielute adx Tata tasxte nes yuusaata'. 11 Txã'wẽme', Dxus Nçxi'k Nasa Yuusa' vxitusatx nwe'weya'k kĩh.
12 I'kwe'sxwaççxáawa ¿na'wẽ yũumeema'kwe? Cien piisxáa ji'phçxa', teeçxsa' vxitute', nowenta y nwewesatx vxiçsũ nvxihtçxa piisxáa vxitusa's pakweya' ¿u'jmeema'kwe? 13 Sa' uyçxa', vxite vxitusamée nowenta y nwewesate jxthaakwe peeygãhna weçxaane'kwe. 14 Txã'wẽy i'kwe'sx Tata cielute ũssawa teeçxçxáa yuhwa vxitu jxthãasu'çme' naawe'sx ãhmeekwesatewa.
Peekx yakhthẽ'ja's maz us peltunãywa'j yuukx
(Lk 17.3)
15 Iidx yakhthẽ'j idxa's ewmée yũute', txã'sçxáan pa'yaçxa mkaajiyu'j ewmée yũuni's. Iidx we'weni's nwẽese'jewáana aça' iidx yakhthẽ'j yakh kxtee yuwe ewuune'ga. 16 Nawa nwẽese'jmeewáana aça' testigo teeçx meeçxa' e'z txahçxa txãawe'sx dxi'pte we'wene'ga, kxah txãa e'z testigosa' jiyutxna iidx yuwe ewuuya' pa'pçxuni's. 17 Txãa e'z testigo dxi'pte nwẽese'jmeete', dxuus yatte jxkaahsawe'sxtxi mpeeta'sx. Aça' txãawe'sxtxiwa nwẽese'jmeeçxa', yakhthẽ'j na'wẽmée Dxusna jiimeesa na'wẽçxáa nvxiitni yuuna.
18 Isath we'we': I'kwe'sxa' yuwetxi's naa kiwete pheu'the', cielutewa Dxus dxi'pte pheu'jni yuutxna. Nawa i'kwe'sxa' yuwetxi's pheu'jmeete', cielutewa Dxus dxi'pte pheu'jnimée yuutxna.
19 Na'jĩ'th na': I'kwe'sx kĩh yũune'jçxawa e'zsaçxáawa txã'wẽ yuute ewna jĩna puutx we'weçxa, Dxusa's pi'kine'kwe ma'wẽ yuuwa'ja'swa aça' adx Tata cielute ũssa' kxtey yuuna. 20 Txãa pa'gatey e'z ma'tx yuu meeçxa' tekh ma'tx yuu nawa adxa's yajkxna pkhaakheçxa ũste', adxa' txãawe'sx ksxavxyte ũswa'jsath jĩk.
21 Txajũ' Pedro' Jesus tasxte utxaaçxa', na'jĩnak pẽjx:
—Yakhthẽ'j adxa's ewmée yũusa's ¿maz us peltunãywa'j yuukx, meeçxa' sietepkaçxçxáa ewmanx? jĩte', 22 Jesusa' na'wẽk pta'sx:
—Sietepkaçxçxáa ewna jĩya'wameeth. Sietesaçxaçxáa setenta ãhpkaçx peltunãywa'ja' jĩk.
Selpisáa peltunãyya' wa'lsa yakh kaaja'da'jni
23 Txãa pa'gatey Dxus jxkaahwa'j en ãhte', na'wẽ yuuna: Teeçx jxkaahsa' txã'sna selpisaawe'sx yuultxi pẽyiçku yu'. 24 Yuulsatx teeçxsa na'wẽçxah pẽjxna u'jweçpkaçxha', teeçx jxũna kũhtx iiméh seena' wala yuulsana. 25 Txãa selpisa' kĩh yakh yuhwa dewewa'j ji'phmeek yu'. Aça' patrona' txãa yuulsa's txweykaakh txajx nyu, txajx luuçx, txajx ji'phuni yã'jçxa jxuka txãa yuula's dewewa'j. 26 Txajũ' selpisa' patron tasxte peejxũkweçxa pxthaa we'wek: Adxa's meen peeygãh. Txãa yuula's jxuka dukhe'nja jĩte', 27 patrona' peeygãhna yuula's peltunãyçxa ewte nvxihtku. 28 Nawa txãa peltunãynisa' kasehna u'jçxa', vxite selpisáa txã'sna walameekuẽ yuulsa'sku uy, sa' txhikhte uweçxa ikheçku yu': Adxna yuulgu txã'sna mdukh jĩna. 29 Txajũ' txãa yuulusa' peejxũkweçxa na'jĩnak pxthaa we'we: Adxa's meen peeygãh, jxuka dukhe'nja jĩtewa, 30 kxtey yuumée wejxwa karcelna jxũna u'jçxa kxtee txajk txãa yuula's dukhpkaçx. 31 Txã'wẽ yuuçte vxite selpisaawe'sx uyçxa', wala nxustxi. Sa' u'jçxa pta'sxtxi txã'wẽ ewmée yũusáa patrona's. 32 Aça' patrona' ewmée yũusa's pa'yaçxa na'wẽk we'we: Selpisáa peethãhwe, adxa' iidx yuula's jxuka peltunãythu, pxthaag we'we txãa pa'ga. 33 Aça' idxwa txã'wẽy idxna yuulusa's peeygãhna peltunãywa'j ji'phgu, adxwa ma'wẽtka peeygãh idxa's txã'wẽy jĩçxa', 34 txãa patrona' wala ũusaçxaçxa, kastigãykaakh yuula's jxuka dukhpkaçx.
35 Txã'jĩçxa', Jesusa' na'wẽk pta'sx:
—Teeçxsa na'wẽçxah ũusuh jxuka peltunaĩmeewá'kwe i'kwe'sx yakhthẽ'jwe'sxtxi, aça' adx Tata cielute ũssawa txã'wẽy kastigaĩna i'kwe'sxtxi' jĩk.
