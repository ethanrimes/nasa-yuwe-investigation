1 Aça' txã'wẽ juuna' we'weya'çxáa yu' u'jmeete ewna sũhnath yajkx. 2 Adxa's i'kwe'sx yuuçxáa wẽtwẽt kaayaki'jwa'jsa i'kwe naapkaçx txã'wẽ adxa' i'kwe'sxtxi juuna' we'weçxa kxũusu'the', adxa's ¿kim wẽtwẽt kaayaki'jikx? sũhnath yajkx. 3 Txãasath i'kwe'sxtxi kxũusu'jna pa'jya'mée kartaçxáa fxi'jçxa kaah, i'kwe'sx txã'wẽ ãhmée yũuna ũspa'ga adxa' weçxana pa'japkaçxte, nxuswa'jçxáa ji'phu'nja sũhnath yajkx. Naasáa adx weçxana pa'the yuuçxáa i'kwe'sxwa txã'wẽy weçxana ũswa'ja's ji'phune'kwe sũju'th. 4 Iiméh wala kuh yajkxna nxusçxa ũ'nenath karta's fxi'j, nawa i'kwe'sxtxi juuna' yuuçxa kxũusu'jya' sũhna ũusaçxaçxa txã'wẽ yũumeeth. Adxa' i'kwe'sx txã'wẽ ãhmée yũupa'ga iiméh wala peeygãhçxaçxáath txã'wẽ ũ'nena fxi'j.
Kazx pkal yũusa's peltunãyçxa kvxiisxa'khan yu'kxpehni
5 Nawa txã'wẽ ãhmée yũusa' adxçxáana txã'wẽ kxũusu'jme', i'kwe'sxtxiwa jxukaysatxku txã'wẽ kxũusu'j, txã'wẽ kazx yũuçxa. (Txã'wẽ kazxku yũu jĩnaçxáath we'we' jxuka yu' pta'sxya'mée.) 6 I'kwe'sx puutx we'weçxa kastigãywa'ja's vxiti'kwe txã' kxtee pa'jçxáa ewna, jwee yu' thaakwe yuumée. 7 Wejxwa ãçxha' peltunãyçxa mekvxiisxa'jwe, txã'wẽme' wejx thaakwe wala nxusçxa isa jxukate vxituwe'sxte. 8 Txãa pa'gath i'kwe'sxtxi neewe'we' peeygãawa'ja's ki' ji'phukahn txã'wẽ ãhmée yũusa's. 9 Juuna' yuukahn i'kwe'sxtxi fxi'th, nawa adx jxkaahni's aan kxtey nwẽese'jetxna sũhçxaçxáath jiyuya' txã'wẽ yũu. 10 Ãçxha' i'kwe'sxa' txã'wẽ ãhmée yũusa's kĩh yũuni'swa mpeltunaĩwe, adxwa peltunãythu peltunãywa'j ũsu'k txã'sa' Kristo dxi'pte, i'kwe'sx pwe'sx puiimée ew fxi'zekahn. 11 Txã'wẽme', eçxthẽ'ja' wala yuh jiia' kwe'sxtxi kaypuba'jçxa knaywa'ja's txã'sa' jiitha'w.
Troaste Tito's uymeeçxa, Pablo wala nxus yaakxni
12 Kristo nwe'weni's pta'sxya' Troas çxhabte pa'the', Kristo's selpiina ũswa'j en wala yuh ewsa vxyaak. 13 Nawa kwe'sx yakhthẽ'j Tito's uymeeçxa wala kuh yajkxthu, txãasath txajũ kasehçxa Macedonian u'j.
Kristo yakha' kĩhtewa jxukak ewçxáa ya'fxi'ze'
14 Nawa Dxusa' pu'çxhi'k Kristo Jesus yakh fxi'zete', kĩhtewa ewçxáa kaafxi'ze'jna, aça' txajx yuwe's mteewa kaajiyu'jwa'jsatha'w, maa maawa jiyuna jxã'khan. 15 Txãa pa'gatey kwe'sxa' Dxusa's jiiyã'jsa' vxite ewte neeyũuwa'jsa ma'tx yuu meeçxa' vxituwa'jsa ma'tx yuu nawa kaajiyu'jwa'jsatha'w Dxus yakh fxi'zeni's. 16 Txã'wẽ yã'the', ¿kim txã'wẽ Dxus yuwe's kaajiyu'jya' u'jwa'jsa ũstxki? Vxituwa'jsa yakh yu', Dxus yakh fxi'zeni' wala yuh ewmée theguna, naapkaçxha' ewte neeyũuwa'jsa yakha' iiméh seena' zhiçxkwesa yuuna. 17 Maaíia' Dxus yuwe's yu'pthehçxa fxiytxi pta'sxna pekũju'. Nawa kwe'sxa' txã'wẽ yũ'çmeetha'w, sa' Dxus yuwe's vxyuu pa'gawa pta'sxna ũsmeetha'w. Txã'wẽ yũuya'méeíi, kwe'sxa' Dxus kaahnisatha'w, Kristo's selpisáa. Txãasatha'w ĩsxiimée ũusuh jxuka isasaçxá's we'we' Dxus dxi'pte.
