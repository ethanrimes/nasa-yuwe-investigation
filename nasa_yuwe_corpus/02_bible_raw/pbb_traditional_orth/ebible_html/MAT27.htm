27
Jesusa's Pilato kusete dukhni
(Mr 15.1; Lk 23.1-2; Jn 18.28-32)
1 Txajũ' khikhyna sẽjeçte', sacerdote npiiçthẽ'jwe'sx vxite' judiu nthẽ'jsawe'sx txãawe'sxa' puutx we'wetx Jesusa's kiikhe'jya'. 2 Sa' tudçxa jxũna u'jtx ne'jwe'sx Poncio Pilato tasxte kuutxa'jya'.
Judas uuníi
3 Jesusna kuuwe'jsa Judasa' Jesusa's ikhwa'j vxitni's jiyuçxa', iiméh wala nxusku. Txã'wẽ yũumeewaça' ewne' yu' sũhçxa treinta vxyutxi' kaasxwedu'jya' u'kh sacerdotewe'sx vxite' judiu nthẽ'jsawe'sx tasxna na'jĩna:
4 —Adxa' ãhmeeth yũu, txã'wẽ piçthẽ'j kĩhtewa yuuwemeesa's ikhkahn kuuwe'pha'ga jĩte', txãawe'sxa' na'wẽtx pas:
—Kwe'sxa' ¿jiitka'wá'? Txã'wẽ yũusa idx jiine'ga jĩçxa jxpa'gameete', 5 Judasa' txãa vxyu's dxuus yatte wãatahna'wa u'jçxa ya'jythujkku.
6 Txã'wẽ yũute', sacerdotewe'sxa' txãa vxyu's pkhakhçxa na'wẽtx puutx we'we:
—Naa vxyu's ofxreda kajate akhya' ãjame'nja'w. Txã'wẽme', nasa's kiikhe'jni vxyua' naa pa'ga jĩçxa', 7 txãa vxyuju mitxh umsáa kiwe'stxi wey, nasa ekajuwe'sx pa'jçxa uusaatx pedaawa'j. 8 Txãa pa'ga ãçxpkaçx txãa kiwe' ee pkawnisa kiwe' jĩ'tx. 9 Aça' Dxus yuwe pta'sxsa Jeremias we'weni kxtey yuuk. Txã' na'wẽk we'we: Israelwe'sx makwetene'ta txweyya' puutx we'we txãa treinta vxyu's jxpa'gaçxatx 10 mitxh umsáa kiwe's wey, Dxusa' adxa's jxkaakh kiwe weykahn txã'sa' kxtey yuutx jĩnak fxi'j.
Jesusa's Pilato pẽyni
(Mr 15.2-5; Lk 23.3-5; Jn 18.33-38)
11 Jesusa' ne'jwe'sx tasxte jxũna u'jnik yuu, aça' txã' na'jĩnak pẽjx:
—Idx wáa txã'wẽ judiuwe'sxtxi jxkaahsane'ga jĩte', Jesus pasçxa':
—Idxa' isag we'we'. Adxa' txã'wẽsaíith jĩk.
12 Txajũ' sacerdotewe'sx vxite' judiu nthẽ'jsa txãawe'sxa' Jesusa's ewmeesag jĩna we'wete', Jesusa' pasu'çme'. 13 Aça' Pilato' na'wẽk we'we:
—Idxa's na'wẽtx kĩhtewa jxuka ewmeesa' jĩna yuwe ũsu' aça' wẽse'jeçmeewá'ga jĩtewa, 14 Jesusa' kĩhwa pasme'.
Jesusa's ikhwa'j vxitni
(Mr 15.6-20; Lk 23.13-25; Jn 18.38–19.16)
Txã'wẽ pasmeete', ne'jwe'sxa' ma'wẽ yũuya'çxáa yuhwa ãjameeçxa', na'wẽ yũuya'k yajkx. 15 Fxiesta ensu' ne'jwe'sxa' teeçx preesu txhweete'k ma'sna nasa pẽyitxna txã'sna. 16 Aça' txãa fxiesta ensu' teeçx preesu jxtuhthesa ũsu'k Barabas yaasesa. 17 Kxajk Pilato' na'jĩna pẽjx txãawe'sx pkhaakheçxa ũssatx:
—I'kwe'sxa' ¿kimnakwe txhweete jxthãasu'? ¿Barabasna', meeçxa' Kristo jĩ'i'kwe txã'sna wáa kaatxhweete'je'nja?
18 Txã'wẽ Jesusa's ahçeçxa txajx tasxte dukhtewa jiyuçxak txã'jĩ.
19 Pilato' jxkaahna u'pnite u'pte', txajx nyua' na'jĩnaga yuwe kaah: Txãa piçthẽ'j yuuwemeesa yakha' kuçx yuumeena. Txã'wẽme', ãçxh kus iiméh seena' ũukhũukh ksxa'wthu txãa piçthẽ'j ma'wẽ yũuwa'jsatewa.
20 Txã'jĩna pta'sxtewa, sacerdotewe'sx vxite' judiu nthẽ'jsa txãawe'sxa' nasatx knaytxi yu'kxwe'weçxa, txã'wẽ Barabasa's txhweeteçxa Jesusa's kiikhe'jwa'ja's pẽykahn. 21 Txã'wẽtewa, ne'jwe'sxa' ki' pẽjxku na'jĩna:
—Naa e'zsatxi' i'kwe'sxa' ¿ma'snakwe kaatxhweete'j jxthãasu'? jĩte', txãawe'sxa' na'wẽtx pas:
—Barabasna metxhwete jĩte', 22 Pilato we'weçxa':
—Barabasa's txhweeteçxa', naa Jesusa's Kristo jĩ'i'kwe txã'sa' ¿kĩh yũutka? jĩte', jxukaysa pasçxa':
—Kluuste mkaafxuçu'j jĩ'tx.
23 Aça' Pilato' na'wẽk pẽjx:
—¿Kĩhga ewmée yũu? jĩte', txãawe'sxa' ki' sus we'weçxa':
—Kluuste mkaafxuçu'j jĩ'tx.
24 Txajũ' Pilato' kĩhwa yũuya' ãjanimeeteçxáa nasa' jweeíiçxáa seena' wala suste', yu' jxũukaahçxa nasaa dxi'pte txajx kusetx kweeçxak na'jĩna:
—Naa piçthẽ'j kĩhtewa yuuwemeesa's ikhni's adxa' yuuwemeesath. Txã'sa' i'kwe'sx pasune'kwe jĩte', 25 nasa jxukaysa we'weçxa':
—Txã'sa' kwe'sx luuçx yã'jçxa jxuka paswa'jsatha'w jĩ'tx. 26 Txajũ' Pilato' kaatxhweete'kh Barabasa's, nawa Jesusa's kaapeçxukwe'jçxa kluuste fxuçkahn dukhku.
27 Txã'wẽ yũute', soldauwe'sxa' ne'jwe'sx u'pnite ka'ga'jçxa, soldauwe'sx jxukaysatx pa'yaçxa Jesusa's jxuka kxtadçxatx yuju. 28 Txajũ' ksũupi'jçxa atxh behsatx kaathe'j. 29 Sa' çhũçha's korona na'wẽ vxitçxa dxikthete ki'ptxi. Paçu kuseju yu', fxtũu zuzkuẽtx kaatu'j. Txã'wẽ yũuçxatx txajx tasxte peejxũkwe:
—Judiuwe'sxtxi jxkaahsath jĩ'ne'ga jĩna weeçxna. 30 Sa' fxne'sx avxythethna fxtũ's kusa'jçxa, dxikthegatx pekwe'kwe. 31 Txã'wẽ Jesusa's weeçxna jxã'jçxa', txãa atxh behsa's ksũupi'jçxa Jesus ropa's ki' kaathe'jtx. Txã'wẽ yũuçxatx kluuskhẽ fxuçya' jxũna u'j.
Jesusa's kluuste fxuçni
(Mr 15.21-32; Lk 23.26-43; Jn 19.17-27)
32 Txajũ kasehna u'jweççxa', teeçx piçthẽ'j Cirenejũwe'sx Simon yaasesa yakhtxi pu'jykhẽw. Sa' Jesus yakhni kluusa's txã'snatx kxaakhe'j.
33 Txã'wẽ yũuçxatx pa'j teeçx ahte Golgota yaasesate. Txãa yu' Uusá Dxikthé Dxi'thçxáasa jĩni'. 34 Kxteea' vxinote yaasa's awçxatx Jesusa's yus wẽe. Nawa txã'sa' yuwete isaçxaçxáa tudxme'.
35 Kluuskhẽ a'kxçxa', soldauwe'sxa' swerte kaahtx Jesus athni ropa's maa soldau knaytewa jiyuya'. Dxus yuwe pta'sxsa' na'jĩnak we'we txã' kxtey yuuk: Swerte kaahçxa adx ropa's txudeedetx txãawe'sx pwe'sx jĩnak we'we. 36 Txã'wẽ yũuçxatx kxtee thegna kaçx. 37 Sa' txajx dxikthé kakwey fxi'jçxa ki'ptxi kĩh yũusate ikhçxawa na'jĩna pta'sxna: Naaíi Jesus Judiuwe'sxtxi Jxkaahsa' jĩnak fxi'jni yu'.
38 Sa' Jesus pukakhẽ pdxi'ptxi e'z pesweetx ki'pni yuu kluuskhẽ. 39 Aça' kluus tasxuy uykhẽwsa' na'jĩnatx wẽesxu' dxiktheetx kaawũhna:
40 —Dxuus yata's pẽtxçxa tekh ente' ki' kiissathẽ'j, idxa' ya'menwe'we. Dxus Nçxi'k dxihçxa', kluusuh msa'j jĩ'tx.
41 Txã'wẽy sacerdotewe'sx, ley kaapiya'jsawe'sx, fxariseowe'sx, vxite' judiu nthẽ'jsawe'sx txãawe'sx pwe'sx na'jĩnatx weeçx we'we':
42 —Vxite yuutxi' nwe'wek, nawa peekíi yu' ya'nwe'weya' ãja'çme'. Israelwe'sxtxi Jxkaahsa dxihçxa', kluusuh sa'jana aç yuuçxáa ĩsxiija'çmeega sũju'nja'w. 43 Dxus Nçxi'kthu jĩ'k na' Dxusnane' neewe'we', aça' txã'sa' ãçxhíi Dxusa' nwe'wena, nwe'weya' ãjaçxa yu' jĩ'tx. 44 Aça' kluuskhẽ fxuçni ki'pnisa peswée yuhwa wẽesxu pu'çxhi'tx.
Jesus uuníi
(Mr 15.33-41; Lk 23.44-49; Jn 19.28-30)
45 Txajũ' ẽepxãh walajũjx çxhi'dxçxa kusuçte las trestepkaçxku çxhi'dx naa kiwete jxuka. 46 Aça' las tres ãjaçpkaçxha', Jesusa' na'jĩnak sus we'we:
—Eli, Eli, ¿lama sabaktani?—txã' na'jĩni': Adx Dxusgu sa' ¿mjĩi pa'gaga adxa's thegmée nvxiht? jĩni'.
47 Aça' kxtee ũssa' txã'jĩni's wẽse'jçxa', na'wẽtx we'we:
—Dxus yuwe pta'sxsa Eliasa's pa'yaça' jĩ'tx.
48 Naapkaçxha' teeçxsa' wuwuuna u'jçxa esponja's vxino puste akhçxa fxtũu vxiçte ki'pçxa yuwete utxak çxazxakahn. 49 Nawa vxite yu' na'jĩ'tx:
—Kuçx yuunu. Aan Elias pa'jana nwe'weya' jĩ'tx.
50 Txajũ' Jesusa' ki' seena' sus we'weçxa' uuk. 51 Naapkaçxha' dxuus yatte txudenisa liensu pesath a'kxnisa' puupxãjkhẽ sutek kute ẽeju kiwete kĩh. Naapkaçxha' ehdwa çxhãçxha u'the', penxa yuhwa upetx, 52 aça' uusaawe'sx pedaní kafxwa phaatete', Dxusna jxpa'gaçxa uusaawe'sxa' ki' ĩtxi yuutx. 53 Aça' Jesus uuçxawa ki' ĩtxi yuuniijũ' txãawe'sxa' pedaní kafxu kasehçxa Jerusalente u'kate', maazkuẽesawa uytxi.
54 Txajũ' kxtee Jesusna jxpa'yakx thegna ũssawe'sx vxite' soldau kpiitan txãawe'sxa' wala ũukhtxi ehd u'jna vxite' kĩhwa seena' yũuni's uyçxa. Sa' na'wẽtx we'we:
—Isa, naa piçthẽ'ja' Dxus Nçxi'k yuh yu'ga jĩ'tx.
55 Kxteea' kuh u'ytxi ũsu' jxu'ju thegna, txãawe'sxa' Jesusna selpiina u'jusatx yu' Galileajũjx yuusa. 56 Txãawe'sx ksxavxyte' Maria Magdalena, vxite Maria Jakobo Jose njĩ'j, vxite' Zebedeo nyu txãawe'sxtxi yu'.
Jesusa's pedaní
(Mr 15.42-47; Lk 23.50-56; Jn 19.38-42)
57 Kusna kĩjaçte', teeçx piçthẽ'j wala ji'phsa Jose yaasesa Arimatea çxhabuwe'sxku pa'j. Txãawa Jesus yakh u'jusak yu'. 58 Txãa piçthẽ'ja' Pilato tasxte u'jçxa', Jesus kakwe's pẽjxku, aça' Pilato' yuwe kaakh tee dukh jĩna. 59 Txajũ' Josea' Jesus kakwe's spajkxçxa liensu fxinusatek yap. 60 Sa' kwet penxate u'se kafxi'jçxa ji'phu'k peekx kakwe's pedaawa'j txkaa khikhçxa', u'kani kafxte yu' kwet walasaju aphçxak u'j. 61 Kxtee txã'wẽ yũuçte', pedaní kafx dxi'puh utxatx thegna u'pu' Maria Magdalena vxite Mariawa.
Jesusa's pedaní kafxte thegsa
62 Jxkaasena fxi'zeya' jxuka pheu'jna jxã'jnijũ kuskuskhẽ' sacerdote npiiçthẽ'jwe'sx vxite' fxariseo txãawe'sxa' Pilato tasxte u'jçxa na'wẽtx we'we:
63 —¿Wẽse'gá', ne'jwe'sx? Txãa ĩisxihthẽ'ja' nee ĩtxi u'juçxa', uuçxawa tekh ente' ki' ĩtxi yu'nja jĩni'stha'w yajkx. 64 Txãa pa'ga mkaah pedaní kafxte wala ew aphkahn tekh entepkaçx. Txã'wẽ yũumeete', txãa yakh u'jusawe'sxa' kus pa'jçxa kakwe's pesweetxna, sa' e'suyã'ja' na'jĩna we'wetxna nasatx: Uuçxawa ki' ĩtxi yuuga jĩtxna. Aça' txã'wẽ yũuçxa ĩsxiini' nyafxíi ĩsxiinite thaakwesa yuuna jĩte', 65 Pilato' na'wẽk we'we:
—Soldauwe'sx kxtee ũsta' txãatx pe'jçxa me'jwe, sa' pedaní kafxte wala ew maphwe. I'kwe'sxtxi ma'wẽ wejx ew thegte', txã'wẽ myũuwe.
66 Txã'jĩte', u'jçxa pedaní kafxte wala ew aphçxa txãa aphni kajkwete selxo ki'ptxi, sa' jxpa'yakx thegna ũswa'jsa soldauwe'sx txahtx.
