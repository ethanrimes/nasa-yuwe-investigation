11
Dxusa's ũuste yaakxnisa
1 Dxusa's ũuste yaakxsa' yafxju ãate yu' uymeeçxawa, ũuste yu' jiik ya'fxi'ze' kxtey yuuyujuna sũhna ũythasna. 2 Kwe'sx yaçgawe'sxa' txã'wẽ yaakxwa'ja's ji'phutx, txãasatx Dxus dxi'pte ew fxi'zesa yuu.
3 Sa' kwe'sxwa txã'wẽy yaakxwa'ja's ji'ptha'w, txãasatha'w ãçxh na'wẽ kiwe ũsa' txã'sa' Dxusa' txajx we'weni yakhçxáa vxitne' nyafxíi yu' kĩhwa meetewa sũju'.
4 Abela' tajkx ikhçxa kabte', Dxusa' weçxayujuna sũhna ũuste jiiçxak txã'wẽ yũu, Kain jĩite thaakwe ewsa. Aça' txã'wẽ yũunisa' Dxus yakha' ewsane' yu' naa pa'ga Dxusa' jxpa'gak. Aça' Abel yu' uuni', nawa Abela' txã'wẽk Dxusa's ũuste jxpubamée yajkx jĩni yu' ãçxpa'jwa ũsa'.
5 Enokwa txã'wẽ Dxusa's ũuste ew jiiçxa Dxus yakhçxáa fxi'zepa'ga, uumey pe'jna u'jnik yuu cielun. Txãa pa'ga peena yu' uymeeta', txã'sa' Dxusku pe'jna u'j txãa pa'ga. Aça' Dxus librute Enokha's we'weçxa': Nyafxíi uumey Dxus yakh weçxanak fxi'ze' jĩ'k. 6 Nawa ũuste Dxusa's yuuçxáa yaakxwa'ja's ji'phmeete', Dxus yakh weçxana fxi'zeya' ya'ãja'çme', txãa pa'ga txajx tasxte utxaasa' Dxusa' ũsna sa' txã'sna pakwete', nwe'wena sũhwa'ja's ũuste ji'phuwa'ja'.
7 Noewa txã'wẽy Dxus we'weni' kxtey yuuyujuna sũhwa'ja's ũuste jiik yu'. Txãasak kastigo nus pa'jyujuna sũhçxa Dxus jxkaahni's nwẽese'jçxa barko's vxit, txajx yattewe'sxtxi jxuka nwe'weya'. Sa' Dxusa' kxtey yuuyujuna sũhwa'ja's ũuste ji'phupa'ga, Dxusa' kxtey yuute', naa kiwetewe'sxa' ya'kastigãytxi aça' Dxusa' Noea's ewsa' jĩnak we'we.
8 Abrahanwa txã'wẽy Dxusa' kxtey yuuyujuna sũhwa'ja's ũuste jiiçxak, Dxus kiwe ũsya' pa'yate', nwẽese'jçxa kasehna u'j, nawa mtee pa'jwa'ja'sçxáa yuhwa jiimeek yu'. 9 Txã'wẽ Abrahana' Dxus kiwe ũsya' neeyũune' txã'sa' bagaçxteçxáawa kxtey yuuyujuna sũhwa'ja's ũuste jiiçxak maa sũhmée txagaçxhíi yu' pa'jsa na'wẽçxáa tultu txahçxa fxi'zena ũsu'. Aça' Isaak, Jakob txãawe'sxwa txãa kiwe's jxpa'gawa'jsaçxawa, txã'wẽyçxáatx pa'jsa na'wẽ fxi'zena ũsu'. 10 Txãa pa'gatey Abrahana' cielute Dxus vxitni çxhaba's jxpa'gawa'ja'sku ũythasna ũsu', txãasak maa sũhmée fxi'ze'.
11 Abrahana' txã'wẽ Dxus we'weni' kxtey yuuyujuna sũhwa'ja's ji'phupa'gak Sara' txakwe wala thẽ'khwe luuçx ji'phuwa'jsamée yã'jçxawa, luuçx ji'phu. 12 Txãasak Abrahana' txajx ji'j iiméh wala kuh ji'phsa yuu, cielute estrelxa vxite' yu'pukasu kwetmusetx ma'wẽga isate ya'ãja'çmée txã'wẽk kuh ji'phu, txakwe iiméh wala thẽ'khwe yã'jçxawa.
13 Na'wẽ Dxusna yaakxsa nakwe wala kuh kxsustha'w txãawe'sxa' Dxus ũsya' neeyũuk txã'sa' jxpa'gamey uutx, txãasatx e'su txaçxteçxáawa Dxusa' kxtey yuuyujuna sũhna weçxana yaaki'. Sa' txãawe'sxa' naa kiwete tudtewe'sxçxáa ne'tka'w jĩnatx we'we. 14 Txãawe'sxa' txã'wẽ we'weçxa' ew jiyute yu', vxite ahte ũsya' u'jwa'ja's pakwesáa ne'ta yu'. 15 Txãawe'sxa' maa kiweju yuusaaçxawa txã'sna yajkxna ũswaça', txãa kiweníi sxawedwa'j ne'ta yu'. 16 Nawa txãawe'sx ũswa'j thaakwe ewsa's ne'ta pakwesáa yu' cielutewe'sxna, txãasatx sxawedmée. Txã'wẽ sxawedmeeta' txãa pa'gak Dxusa' thaame'çmée txãawe'sx Dxusthu jĩna ya'kxsuswa'ja's, txãasa txãawe'sx ũswa'j pheu'jni ji'pha'.
17 Abrahana' txajx nçxi'k Isaakha's ikhtewa Dxusa' ki' ĩtxi vxituna sũhwa'ja's ũuste jiiçxak ikhya' jxũna u'j. Dxusa' Abrahan kĩh yũutewa jiyuya' sũhçxa txajx nçxi'k teeçxçxáasa's ikh kaathewa, wa'lmée kxtey yuuya'wak yu'. Makwe Dxus txãa yakh puutx we'weçxa': 18 Iidx ji'j Isaaktewe'sx txã'wẽ wala penatxna jĩna we'wetewa, ikhya'wak yu'. 19 Nawa Abrahana' txajx ũuste yu' uutewa Dxusa' ki' ĩtxi vxituna sũhwa'ja's ji'phu'k. Txãasa isa ew yajkxte yu', Abrahan nçxi'ka' uusaawe'sx ksxavxyu ki' ĩtxi yuusáa na'wẽsa'.
20 Isaakwa Dxusa' kxtey yuuna sũhwa'ja's ũuste ew jiiçxak txajx nçxi'k Jakob Esau txãawe'sxtxi' bedicion wala jxpa'gane'kwe jĩna we'we.
21 Jakobwa Dxusa' kxtey yuuna sũhwa'ja's ũuste ew jiiçxak uuçyã'jçxa', José nçxi'ktxi teeçxsa na'wẽçxah jxukaysatx wala bedekĩni yuune'kwe jĩna we'we, sa' Dxusa's neewe'wek kĩiçhuçxa.
22 Josewa Dxus pu'çxte' Israelwe'sxa' Egipto kiwejũ kasejetxna sũhwa'ja's ũuste ew jiiçxak, uuya'wayã'jçxa txãawe'sxtxi neewe'we txajx kakwe's mtee jxũna u'jçxa pedaawa'ja'swa.
23 Moises neywe'sxwa Dxusa' nwe'wena sũhwa'ja's ũuste ew jiiçxatx tekh a'te uta Moisesa's sũth, luuçxa' wala zhiçxkwe yu'ne' txãa pa'ga. Makwe Egipto kiwete jxkaahsa yu' ikh kaathewa ikhmeeta'.
24 Moiseswa Dxusa' pu'çxhina sũhwa'ja's ũuste ew jiiçxak, Egipto kiwete jxkaahsa niisa nçxi'k jĩna kxsusni yuuwa'j yu'sa' wa'l, walaana kãhçxa'. 25 Sa' naa kiwete pkalte weçxana fxi'zepkaçxte, wejxwa ũukhmée Dxus txhitxhnisa yakh ja'daçxah pkxuuni yuuwa'ja'sçxáak yajkx. 26 Txãa pa'gatey cielutewe'sxa's jxpa'gawa'ja'sçxáak yajkxçxa fxi'zena ũsu'. Txãasak Egipto kiwete iiméh seena' wala ji'phuni's yaakipkaçxte, Kristo na'wẽy yaaçeni pkxuuni fxi'zeni's ji'phuya'çxáa yajkx. 27 Dxus pu'çxwa'ja's ũuste ew jiiçxak Moisesa' jxkaahsa ũusçxani yu'sa' ũukhmée Egipto kiwejũ kasehna u'j, sa' Dxus tasxu le'çxkweçxáa yuhwa txuteméek fxi'ze, Dxusa' vxyaasameetewa yafxju uyna ũs na'wẽ yajkxna.
28 Dxusa' nwe'weyujuna sũhwa'ja's ew ũuste jiiçxak Moisesa' Paskua fxiesta's vxitçxa eea's vxitxkhẽ sxũsx kaah, ikhwa'jsa angel sẽhçxawa Israelwe'sx luuçx nthẽ'jsa maa yuhtxwa ikhkahmen.
29 Israelwe'sxwa Dxusa' pu'çxhina sũhwa'ja's ũuste ew jii yuupa'gaçxáak, mar bejuy uykhẽwwa'j dxi'j phaate txãawe'sx uykhẽwkahn. Aça' Egiptowe'sxwa txã'wẽy uykhẽu'nja'w sũhçxawa txãawe'sxa' ĩkhtee uutx yu' afxihbte.
30 Dxusa' pu'çxhina sũhwa'ja's ũuste ew jiiçxatx Israelwe'sxa' Dxus we'weni's nwẽese'jçxa txã'wẽ siete enpkaçx Jeriko çxhabte muralxa peeku'j tadxina ũsu', aça' txã'wẽ nwẽese'pha'gak muralxa' wete.
31 Rahabwa Dxusa' nwe'weyujuna sũhwa'ja's ũuste ew jiiçxa, Israelwe'sx e'zsa kxtee peethegu'jya' pa'jsatx ewku jxpa'ga. Txãa pa'gak uumée, Dxusa's nwẽese'jsameewe'sx vxite yu' uutewa.
32 ¿Kĩh jwee yu' pta'sxitka? En ji'phwaç yu' pta'sxwa'j yu' walaíiçxáa ũsa', Gedeon, Barak, Sansón, Jefxté, Davxid, Samwel, Dxus yuwe pta'sxsawe'sx txãawe'sxtxi we'wewa'j. 33 Txãawe'sxa' Dxusa' pu'çxhina sũhwa'ja's ũuste ew jiiçxatx maaíi yu' gweçxate puiiçxa knay, vxite' ewtxi jxkaahna fxi'ze, vxite' Dxus pu'çxni's jxpa'gatx, vxite' león yuwetxwa aphtxi, 34 vxite' seena' ipx walajuwa çxhavxymeeta', vxite' ikhsa kusejuwa iyutx, vxite' puiiya' çxhãçxhameekwetewa kçxhãaçxha'jnisa yuuçxa, gweçxate iiméh kuhsatxwa knaytxi. 35 Naapkaçxha' u'ywe'sxwa txãawe'sx luuçx uunisatx ki' ĩtxi vxitni jxpa'gatx. Vxite' seena' wala pkxuuni yuuçxa uutx, ki' ĩtxi yuuwa'ja's ji'phuçxa thaakwe ewte fxi'zeya' sũhçxa, nwe'wewa'j yu'sa' jxthãasmeeta'. 36 Naapkaçxha' vxite' weeçxna peçxujkweçxa çam kadenaju tudçxa karcelte txaahnitx yuu. 37 Vxite' kwet wãatatahçxa ikhnitx yuu. Maaíi yu' pxãhsu pẽçnitx yuu. Vxite' iiméh seena' wala kxũusu'jni yuutx. Vxite' kçxilxuh ikhnitx yuu, naapkaçxha' vxite' ahçeçxa neeuni yat ji'phmeetx pekũhnaçxáa ũsu', kĩhwa ji'phmeeçxa piisxáa kha'tx kapla kha'tx txã'wẽsaçxá's pã'çxçxa, nxuste pkxuunitx fxi'zena ũsu'. 38 Txã'wẽ pkxuunisawe'sxa' nasa u'pnimée kiwesuçxáatx fxi'zena ũsu' yu'khsu, kiwe kafxsu. (Nasa txã'wẽ Dxus yakh zhiçxkwe fxi'zesa yakha' naa kiwe' ũsweçxte yuuwa meena.) 39 Dxusa' pu'çxhina sũhwa'ja's ũuste ew jiiçxa txãawe'sx jxukaysa txã'wẽ seena' yũuni yuuçxawa, Dxus ũsya' neeyũuk txãa yu'sa' txagaçxhíi yu' jxpa'gameeta', 40 txãa pa'gatey Dxusa' thaakwe ewsane' ji'phu' ãçxh naa ensu kwe'sx jĩi yã'jçxa. Aça' txãawe'sxa' kwe'sx yakh ja'daçxah jxpa'gawa'jsaçxatx txagaçxhíi yu' jxpa'gamée.
