5
1 Pkalsawe'sx ju'gu we'we pu'çxwa'jsa txhitxhçxa naa kiwete nvxiitnisa' txãa íiçxáa pkalpa'ga tajkx ikhçxa Dxus altalte kabwa'jsa'. 2 Pkalsawe'sx ju'gu we'we pu'çxsa' txãa íiwa pkalte nxuste fxi'zesaçxa', nxuste fxi'zeni ma'wẽtewa jxuka jiia'. Txãasa jiimeekwesa vxituna fxi'zesatx wala peeygãhna ju'gu yuuwa'jsa'. 3 Sa' txãa íiwa ãhmeekwe pkalsaçxa', peekx pkalpa'gawa tajkx ikhçxa Dxus altalte kabwa'j ji'phu'k, vxitesa pa'gatewa txã'wẽy.
4 Pkalsawe'sx ju'gu we'we pu'çxwa'jsa' nasaa pwe'sxçxáa yajkxçxa ki'pnisa yu'çmeeta'. Txãawe'sxtxi' Dxusku txhitxhçxa nvxiitu', Aaronwa ma'wẽga Dxus txhitxhnisa yuu txã'wẽy. 5 Aça' Kristowa txã'wẽy yũunisaíiçxáak yuu. Txã' peekxçxáa yu' pkalsawe'sx ju'gu Dxus tasxte we'wesa yuume'. Txã'sa' Dxusku txhitxhçxa nvxiht. Txajíik Dxus librutewa na'jĩ':
Dxus we'weçxa':
Idxa' adx Nçxi'kgu,
Txãa pa'gath ĩtxi yuuwa'ja's ãçxha' ũs jĩnak we'we'.
6 Sa' vxite ajkhẽwa Dxus librute na'jĩnak we'weni yuu:
Idxa' pkalsawe'sx ju'gu Dxus tasxte we'we pu'çxwa'jsag bagaçxtewa,
Melkisedekwa ma'wẽga yuu txã'wẽysa jĩ'k.
7 Kristo' naa kiwete ũsçxa', txajx uuwa'j en jxuka utxaaçte', Dxus nwe'weya' ãjawa'jsa dxi'pte wala pxthaa sus neewe'wek ũ'nena, aça' Dxusa' wẽse'kh txajx pxthaa we'weni's. 8 Kristo' makwe Dxus Nçxi'kçxawa, iiméh wala pxthaa yuuwa'ja's jiyuçxa, nwẽese'jwa'ja'swa piyak. 9 Txã'wẽ kĩh pxthaa yuunisatewa jxuka jiyuna jxã'jçxak, bagaçxtewa nwe'wena nes yuuwa'jsa yuuna pa'j jxukaysa nwẽese'jsa yuutxi's. 10 Txã'wẽ yũunijũk Dxusa' pkalsawe'sx ju'gu we'we pu'çxwa'jsa vxit, Melkisedek na'wẽysa.
Dxusa's jiiçxa txajũ txuteete' ũukhũukhsa'
11 Na'wẽ pta'sxi'th txã'sa' wala we'wewa'j ji'phíiçxáath, nawa i'kwe'sxa' jiyuya' ãja'çmeei'kwe, aça' wala thẽya' ew pta'sx wẽete yu'. 12 Dxusa's jiyuni txakwe txaçxte', i'kwe'sx yuu kaapiya'jsa yuupkaçxte, nee peejíiçxáa i'kwe Dxus kaapiya'jni thẽymeekwesa's ki' peena ew pta'sxwa'j. I'kwe'sxa' luuçx na'wẽysaçxáa i'kwe, kĩh ũ'a'swa ũ'wepkaçxte, luuçx ũ'ni leçxi'sçxáa i'kwe ũ'we'. 13 Txã'wẽ leçxiçxá's ũ'sa' luuçx le'çxkwe kçxu'çxu'jnisa na'wẽsaçxáata', txãasatx Dxus yakh ew fxi'zewa'j yu'sa' jiyu'çmée. 14 Nawa thẽ'jsa yã'ja' thẽ'jsa ũ'nisatx jxpa'gawa'ja', kĩh ewsatxna vxite' kĩh ewmeesatxna nawa txãatx jxuka jiyuya'.
