13
Bernabé Saulo yakh Dxus yuwe's pta'sxya' nyafxçxah kasehni
1 Antiokía çxhabte pkhaakhena ũssa yakh ũsu'tx Dxus yuwe pta'sxsa vxite' Dxus leya's jiisawa: Bernabé, vxite' khũçx Simon jĩnisa, vxite' Lucio Cirene çxhabuwe'sx, vxite' Manaén Galileate jxkaahsa Herodes yakh ih walaasáa, vxite' Saulo'. 2 Txãawe'sxa' teeçx ente' Dxusna selpiina yũunaana ũste', Dxus Espiritu' na'wẽk we'we:
—Saulo Bernabé yakh mkaahwe adxa's selpikahn, txãawe'sxa' adx pa'yanisata' mjĩiwa'jsa jĩk.
3 Txajũ' yũunaana jxã'jçxa', txãawe'sx dxikthesu peekizeçxa Dxusna neewe'wena' kaahtx.
Bernabewe'sxa' Çxipre kiwete Dxus yuwe's pta'sxni
4 Dxus Espiritu kaathe', Bernabé Saulowe'sxa' Seleucia çxhabna u'jçxa txajũ' barkote khẽeçxa ĩkh pxãhte Çxipre kiwentxi u'j. 5 Txãa kiwete Salamina çxhabte pa'jçxa', judiuwe'sx pkhaakheni yat-su Dxus yuwe's pta'sxya' takhtxi. Sa' Juan Markosnawa iipe'jtx yu' pu'çxwa'jsa. 6 Çxipre kiwe's pekũhna jxã'jaççxa', Pafxos çxhabtetx pa'j. Sa' kxte' teeçx judiu dxijxuusáa Barjesus yaasesa'stxi uy. Txãa piçthẽ'ja' wala ĩsxiisa yu'k Dxus yuwe pta'sxsath jĩna ĩsxiina. 7 Txãa dxijxuusá' kxte ne'jwe'sx wala jiisa Sergio Paulo tasxte mjĩisak yu'. Txãa ne'jwe'sxa' Dxus yuwe's wẽse'j wẽeçxa Bernabewe'sxtxi pa'yak. 8 Nawa txãa dxijxuusáa Elimasa' Bernabewe'sxtxi' kwe'we'j wẽeme', ne'jwe'sxa' Dxus yuwe's jiyukahmen. 9 Txã'wẽ yũute', Dxus Espiritu's ũuste jxuka ji'phsa Saulo Pablo yaasesa' dxijxuusáa dxi'pte ũusçxa thegçxa', na'jĩk:
10 —Ĩsxiina fxi'zesa, ewmeesa, eçxthẽ'j luuçx, ewsa yu'sa' jxuka ahçena fxi'zesa ¿mjĩitega Dxus yuwe ewsa's yupmée fxi'ze'çmée? 11 Txãa pa'ga Dxusa' idxa's ãçxhíi kastigaĩna, aça' uyya' ãjamée fxi'zene'ga naa ensu' jĩçpkaçxha', txãa dudtey uyya' ãjameeçxa' maaçxáatxwa pakwek pu'kisçxa pe'jna u'khan. 12 Txajũ' kxte ne'jwe'sxa' txã'wẽ yũuni's uyçxa', Jesus kaapiya'jni's isa yuhna sũhna ũuste jxpa'gak.
Pablowe'sx Pisidia kiwesu pekũhni
13 Pablo vxite' txãa yakh pi'kxna u'jusa yã'jçxa barkote khẽeçxa Pafxos çxhabu kasehtx Panfxilia kiwete Perge çxhabte pa'jya'. Nawa Juan Markos yu' Perge çxhabte pa'jçxaçxáa ki' sxawedku Jerusalennay. 14 Pablowe'sxa' Perge çxhabu kasehçxa Pisidia kiwete Antiokía çxhabnatx u'j. Kxte pa'jçxa', jxkaase ensu' judiuwe'sx pkhaakheni yatte u'katx. 15 Aça' pkhaakheni yatte npiiçthẽ'ja' Dxus ley vxite' Dxus yuwe pta'sxsa fxi'jni's lẽeyna jxã'jçxa', na'wẽtx we'we:
—Yakhthẽ'jwe'sx, kĩh we'wewa'j ji'phçxa', myu'kxpehwe nasatx ãçxhíi jĩte', 16 Pablo' kiitena'wa kuseju sxuuna' yuuwa'ja's pta'sxna'wa we'wek:
—Israel ji'jwe'sx, vxite nasawa Dxusna nwẽese'jsa, jxpa'yakx mwẽese'jwe: 17 Israelwe'sx Dxusa' kwe'sx yaçgawe'sxtxi txhitxhçxa wala kpeena'kh Egipto kiwete nee ũsiyna, sa' txãa kiwejũ kutxi'kh kĩhwa seena'sa kaavxya'ja'jçxa. 18 Dxusa' nasa u'pnimée kiwete kuarenta anxu wantãyku nasa ãhmée yũuna fxi'ze'çtewa. 19 Sa' Kanaan kiwete nasatx jxuka ivxiituk txãa kiwe's kwe'sx yaçgawe'sxtxi ũsya'. 20 Txajũ' kuatrocientos cinkwenta anxu ãhpkaçx jxkaahwa'jsa ji'phmée kwe'sxtxi thegwa'jsa khuẽsçxáak nvxiht, Dxus yuwe pta'sxsa Samwel entepkaçx. 21 Txajũtx txãawe'sxíi pẽjx teeçx jxkaahwa'jsa, aça' Dxusa' ũsku kuarenta anxu jxkaahwa'jsa Cis nçxi'k Saul yaasesa's. Txãa piçthẽ'ja' Benjamin ji'kh yu'. 22 Txãa e'su' Dxusíi Saula's jxkaahnijũ kutxi'jçxa, Davxida'sku jxkaahwa'jsa ki'p. Sa' na'wẽk we'we: Isaí nçxi'k Davxid yuuçxáa adx jxkaahni's kxtey yuuwa'jsa uythu txã' adx wedxnisa' jĩk. 23 Jesusa' Davxid ji'thewe'sxku yu', aça' txã'snak Dxusa' nvxiht Israelwe'sxtxi nwe'wewa'jsa, nyafxiitey Dxusa' ma'wẽne' ũsya' neeyũu txã'sa' kxtey yuuk. 24 Jesus pa'jmeyna Juana' Israel nasa jxukaysatx pta'sxku: I'kwe'sx pkaltxi jxuka nvxihtçxa Dxus tasxte misxawedwe yu'te ya'bautisãyna jĩna. 25 Sa' Juana' txajx uu en utxaaçte', na'wẽk we'we: Adxa' i'kwe'sx ũythasu'i'kwe txãameeth. Adx e'ste teeçx yuja' txajx pelgatxi wesçxáa yuhnawa adxa' khihbxa' selpimeesath jĩk.
26 Yakhthẽ'jwe'sx Abrahan ji'j, vxite nasawa Dxusna nwẽese'jsa i'kwe'sx ewte neeyũuwa'ja's pta'sxnik yuu. 27 Nawa Jerusalente ũssa vxite' txãawe'sx npiiçthẽ'jsawa jiyumeeta' Jesusa' kimtewa. Sa' Dxus yuwe pta'sxsa fxi'jni'swa jiyumeeta' pkhaakheni yat-su jxkaase een isa lẽeyçxawa. Sa' txãawe'sxíiçxáa Jesusa's ewmeete nvxihtçxa', Dxus yuwe pta'sxsa fxi'jni txãawe'sx lẽeyni's kxtey yuutx. 28 Txã'wẽ Jesus ewmée yũuni's uymeeçxawa Pilato's yuwe ũstxi Jesusa's kiikhe'khan. 29 Txajũ' Dxus librute ma'wẽne' fxi'jni ũsu' txã'sa' jxuka kxtey yuuçxa', Jesusa's kluusu spajkxçxa pedaatx. 30 Nawa Dxusa' uuniijũ ki' ĩtxi vxitku. 31 Aça' Jesusa' wala kuh en vxyaak Galileajuwe'sx Jerusalenna pi'kxna yuusatx. Txãasa txãawe'sxa' Jesus kimtewa pta'sxsata'.
32 Txãa pa'ga kwe'sxwa i'kwe'sxtxi pta'sxiçtha'w nyafxiitey kwe'sx yaçgawe'sxtxi Dxusa' ũsya' neeyũuk txã'sna. 33 Kwe'sxa' yaçgawe'sx ji'tha'w, txãa pa'gak Dxusa' txajx ũsya' neeyũuni's kwe'sx yakh kxtey yuuçxa, Jesusa's uuniijũwa ĩtxi vxit, salmo doste na'wẽ fxi'jni ũsa' txã'wẽy: Idxa' adx nçxi'kgu. Aça' idxa's uuniijũwa ĩtxi vxitthu ãçxha' jĩk. 34 Uute' txãa kakwe' suweekahmen ĩtxi yuuwa'ja's Dxusa' pta'sxku txajx librute na'jĩna: I'kwe'sxtxi peeygãawa'ja's Davxid yakh puutx we'weth txã'sa' kxtey yu'nja jĩk. 35 Txãasak salmo vxite ajkhẽwa na'jĩ': Idxna selpisáa peeygãanisa kakwe's nvxihtmeene'ga suweewe'sxte jĩ'k. 36 Txã'wẽ we'weni' isa kxtey yuuyujk. Txajíik Davxida' txãn ensu' Dxus jxkaahni's kxtey yuuçxa e'su' uuk, aça' txajx kakwe's yaçgawe'sx pedaniitey pedaate', txajx kakwe' kxtee jxuka pçuuk. 37 Nawa Dxusa' Jesusa's uuniijũwa ĩtxi vxitku, aça' txãa kakwe' pçuume'. 38 Ãçxha' yakhthẽ'jwe'sx i'kwe'sxa' mjiyuwe: I'kwe'sx pkaltxi peltunãywa'ja's pta'sxi'tha'w, nawa peltunãywa'jsa' Jesus yuuçxá'. 39 Nyafxíi Moises leya's nwẽese'jçxawa, perdona's uymeei'kwe. Nawa Jesusna yajkxçxa nwẽese'jçxa', perdona's uyuune'kwe maawa. 40 Mpa'yajkxwe, txã'wẽme', nyafxíi Dxus yuwe pta'sxsa na'wẽ we'wetx txã' kxtey yuuwe'sxta':
41 Weeçxsawe'sx, mpa'yajkxwe. I'kwe'sx vxituwa'ja's mũukhwe.
Txã'wẽme', adxíi Dxusthu na',
Txã'wẽ yũuwa'j en ãhte', kĩhwa seena'çxáa yu'nja.
Txã'wẽ yũuwa'ja's pta'sxtewa, i'kwe'sxa' jxpa'gameene'kwe jĩnatx we'we jĩk Pablo'.
42 Txajũ' Pablo' txã'wẽ pta'sxçxa', txãa yakh pi'kxna u'jusa yakh judiuwe'sx pkhaakheni yatu kasejeçte', kxte ũssa judiu ji'jmeeçxawa Pablowe'sxtxi ki' pi'kxtxi vxite jxkaase ente txã'wẽy pta'sxya' yuukahn. 43 Kxte pkhaakheni pçuute', judiuwe'sx wala kuhsa vxite' judiu ji'jmeeçxawa judiuwe'sx kaapiya'jni's jxpa'gasa Dxus yuwe's wala ew nwẽese'jçxa', Pablowe'sx kaapiya'jni's nwẽese'jsatx yuu. Aça' Pablowe'sxa' wala yu'kxpehtx Dxus peeygãanite wa'lmée fxi'zekahn.
44 Vxite ki'su jxkaase ente', çxhabte nasa jxukay pkhaakhetx Dxus yuwe's wẽse'jya'. 45 Nawa txã'wẽ nasa wala pkhaakhete', kxte judiuwe'sxa' wala ũusaçxaçxa Pablowe'sxtxi açewe'wena wẽesxutx. 46 Txã'wẽtewa, Pablowe'sxa' ũukhmeetx na'jĩna we'we:
—Dxus yuwe's nyafxte i'kwe'sx judiu ji'j khuẽ'ytxi pta'sxwa'kh yu', kxahtha'w pta'sx. Nawa jxpa'gameeyã'ji'kwe pçumée ĩtxi fxi'zewa'ja's, aça' ãçxha' u'jweçtha'w judiumée vxite nasatx pta'sxya'. 47 Dxusa' kwe'sxtxi na'wẽk we'wena kaah:
Nasa ksxavxysu eena' kweethwa'jsath nvxiht i'kwe'sxtxi',
Sa' txã'wẽy maa kiwetewa ewsa's pta'sxwa'jsa jĩk.
48 Aça' judiu ji'jmée vxite nasa txã'jĩni's wẽse'jçxa', Dxus yuwe' wala ewsa yu'ga jĩna weçxaçxa maaçxahne'ta bagaçxte yuhwa pçumée ĩtxi fxi'zewa'jsa yu' txãa yu' jxukay Dxus yuwe's jxpa'gatx. 49 Txã'wẽk Dxus yuwe's pta'sxni yuu txãa kiwete jxuka. 50 Nawa txãa çxhabte judiuwe'sxa' nasa jxtuhthesa petx u'y yã'jçxa kxteeçxah puutx we'weçxa, Pablowe'sxtxi pkxuuya' yajkxtxi txãa kiwejũ kutxi'phkaçx. 51 Txã'wẽ yũute', Pablowe'sxa' txãawe'sx çxidate kiwe tuhda's sakweekweçxa u'jtx Ikonio çxhabna. 52 Nawa kxte nasa Dxus yuwe's nwẽese'jsa yu', wala weçxana neeyũutx, Dxus Espiritu's ũuste jxpa'gaçxa.
