17
U'y kazx pkal yũusa's ivxiituni
1 Txajũ' siete angeleswe'sx na'wẽ siete kopa ji'phu'tx txãatewe'sx teeçxsa's uythu, aça' adxa's na'wẽk we'we:
—Ayte myuh, na'wẽ u'y kazx yũuna fxi'zesa mar kajkwete kaçxçxa u'pa' txã'sna kastigãywa'ja's pta'sxya'wath. 2 Maa kiwete jxkaahsawe'sxwa txãa u'y yakhçxaçxáatx kazx pkal yũuna fxi'ze', aça' naa kiwete nasa' txãa kazx fxi'zewa'j yuuçxáanatx piyana ũsu'.
3 Txajũ' adxa' Dxus Espiritu aphniith yuu, aça' Dxus angela' adxa's pe'jna u'kh nasa u'pnimée kiwete. Kxteea' teeçx u'y eçx beh seena' kazxsa kajkwete a'jçxa u'pteth uy. Aça' txãa eçx seena' kazxsa kakwete yu' Dxusna açewe'wena fxi'jnisaçxáa utak ji'phu', sa' siete dxikthé diez kaaçxuk ji'phu'. 4 Txãa u'ya' athni ropa iiméh ew behsak ji'phu', vxyuu beh vxite' kwet zhiçxkwesa vxite' perla iiméh pa'gasa txã'wẽsa atxhku iijxũ yu'. Sa' kusete yu' vxyuu bee kopa ewmée kazxsaçxaçxáa uta ji'phsa'sku ji'phu'. 5 Sa' dxikthete yu' na'wẽk fxi'jni yu': Babilonia çxhab kazx jxtuhthesa kazx yũuwa'ja's kaapiya'jsa jĩnak fxi'jni yu'. 6 Txajũ' jiyuth, txãa u'ya' tũunik yu' Dxus luuçxwe'sx ee vxite' Jesus kaapiya'jni's pta'sxpa'ga ikhni yuutx txãawe'sx ee yakh. Sa' u'y txã'wẽsa's uyçxa', wala ũukhthu.
7 Txajũ' Dxus angela' na'jĩ'k:
—¿Kĩh yũuçxa ũukhna ũsga? Kaajiyu'jya'wath txãa u'y ma'wẽsatewa vxite' txãa eçx seena' kazxsa tũ'sna u'jweça' siete dxikthé ji'phçxa diez kaaçxu ji'pha' txã'sna. 8 Eçx seena' kazxsa's uygu txã' nyafxíi yu' ũsu'k, nawa ãçxha' meea'. Txã'wẽçxawa kafx iiméh seena' dxiisajũ kasejena vxituya'. Aça' naa kiwetewe'sx jxukaysa txãawe'sx yase's bagaçxteçxáa yuhwa uuwa'jmée ĩtxĩ fxi'zewa'j librute fxi'jnimeesa yu' wala yuh ũukhutxna txãa eçx seena' kazxsa ki' vxyaana txã'sna uyçxa.
9 Na'sa' jiyuwa'jsa ũsçxa', mjiyuwe: U'y kaçxçxa u'pa' txãa eçx dxikthé sietesa' siete vxiç walata'. 10 Sa' txãa dxiktheíiçxáa siete jxkaahsata'. Txãa jxkaahsawe'sxa' tahçsa yu' vxituyã'jtx, aça' ãçxha' seistewe'sxa' jxkaahna ũsa', nawa nmehtewe'sx sietesa yu' jxkaajxa' takhmeya'. Nawa txãa nmehtewe'sxa' jwee yu' txaçxmée jxkaajana. 11 Eçx seena' kazxsa nyafxíi yu' ũsu'k nawa ãçxha' meea' txã' jxkaahsa sietesak yu'. Nawa ew jiyute yu', txãaíiçxáa jxkaahsa oçxotewe'sx yuuwa'jsa'. Txãa e'su isa jxukate vxituna.
12 Sa' diez kaaçxu uygu txãa yu' diez jxkaahsawe'sxta'. Nawa txãawe'sxa' jxkaajxa' takhmeyta'. Nawa jxkaahsa yuuwa'ja's jxpa'gatxna wala txaçxmeekuẽçxáa, eçx seena' kazxsa yakh ja'daçxah. 13 Txãa diez jxkaahsawe'sxa' ma'wẽ yũuwa'ja'swa kxazíi ja'daçxah puutx we'weçxa ũsta'. Txãasa eçx seena' kazxsa's kçxhãaçxha'jatxna kxteeçxah jxkaah pu'çxna. 14 Txã'wẽ yũuçxa Piisxakuẽ jĩnisa yakh puiitxna. Nawa Piisxakuẽ jĩnisa yuh pejka knayuuna txãawe'sxtxi', txãa pa'gatey txã' Maatewa Jxthaakwe Çxhãçxhasa', sa' Maa Jxkaahsatewa Jxthaakwe Jxtuhthesa'. Aça' txãa yakh ũssa' Dxus peeygãhçxa nwe'wenisata' txãa yakh txutemée fxi'zesa.
15 Txajũ' Dxus angela' vxite na'wẽsa'swa pta'sxku:
—Na'wẽ u'y kazx pkal yũuna fxi'zesa kaçxçxa u'pni mar uygu txã' naata': maa kiwete çxhabwa jxuka, nasa majũwe'sxwa fxiyfxiyçxah we'wesa txãata'. 16 Sa' eçx seena' kazxsa dxikthesu diez kaaçxu uygu txã' jxkaahsa yuuwa'jsata', sa' u'y kazx pkal yũuna fxi'zesa's ahçeçxa sũpxçxáa wãatajatxna. Txã'wẽ yũuçxa, kakwete çxiçx yu'sa' ũ'çxa ipxte kabutxna. 17 Txãa u'ya's txã'wẽ yũuçxa ivxiitukahn, Dxus ne' jxthãas. Txãa pa'ga txãa jxkaahsawe'sxa' eçx seena' kazxsa's kçxhãaçxha'j pu'çxçxa txã'wẽ yũutxna, Dxus we'weni' kxtey yuukahn. 18 Aça' u'y kaçxçxa u'pte uygu txãa yu' Babilonia çxhab kazx jxtuhthesa', naa kiwete jxkaahsa jxukaysatx txãaçxaçxáa jxkaahna ũssa.
