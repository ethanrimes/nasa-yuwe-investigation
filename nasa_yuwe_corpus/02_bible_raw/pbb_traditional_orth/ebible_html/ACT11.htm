11
Dxus ma'wẽ yũuni'swa Jerusalente pa'jçxa Pedro pta'sxni
1 Jesus jxkaahnisa vxite' yakhthẽ'jwe'sx Dxus yakh fxi'zesa Judea kiwete ũssa' jiyutx txã'wẽ judiu ji'jmée vxite nasawa Dxus yuwe's jxpa'gatx jĩni's. 2 Sa' Pedro Jerusalen çxhabte sxawedna pa'the', kxte judiu cirkuncidãynisawe'sxa' ũusçxawe'wetx Pedro's na'jĩna:
3 —¿Kĩjxa'ga judiumée vxite nasa tasxsu u'jçxa txãawe'sx yakh pkhaakheçxa ũ'we'?
4 Txajũ' Pedro' pta'sxku ma'wẽ yũuni'swa jxuka:
5 —Adxa' Jope çxhabte Dxus yakh puutx we'wena u'pçxa uythu tela wala na'wẽsa puutx pahz vxiçte tudçxa ẽe walakhẽ kaahni kĩjk adx tasxte pa'j. 6 Aça' ew thegte', dxiite yu' niimal yu'kh eçx, ul, vxiçxakwe kazxsak ji'ph yu'. 7 Txajũ' na'jĩni'sthu wẽse'j: Pedro, kiiteçxa ikhçxa mẽ' jĩte', 8 na'wẽth pas: Adxa' bagaçxte yuhwa na'wẽ niimal kazxsa yuutxi' ũ'wa'jsameeth. 9 Txajũ' ki' pthũusek cieluju we'weni: Dxus yaatenisatxi' idxa' kazxsata' jĩwa'j ji'phmeeg jĩk. 10 Txã'wẽ tekh uswa vxyaaçxak ẽe walate ki' pu'kisni yuu. 11 Naapkaçxha' Cesareaju kaahnisa tekh piçthẽ'j pa'jtx adxa's pakwena. 12 Aça' Dxus Espiritu' adxa's u'khaakh txãawe'sx yakh wa'lmée. Txajũ' Jope çxhabtewe'sx seis yakhthẽ'jwe'sx yakh u'jçxa' jxukaysa Kornelio yatte u'katha'w. 13 Aça' txãa piçthẽ'ja' pta'sxku Dxus angel vxyaaçxa na'wẽ we'weni's: Jope çxhabna Simon Pedro yaasesa's pa'yaya' mkaah. 14 Aça' txãa pa'jçxa pta'sxina iidx yatte jxukaysawe'sx Dxus yakh fxi'zewa'ja's. 15 Txã'wẽ pta'sxte', adx we'weçpkaçxha', Dxus Espiritu' txãawe'sx kakwete kĩjk, kwe'sx kakwetewa ma'wẽga nyafxíi kĩh txã'wẽy. 16 Txajũ' yajkxthu kwe'sxtxi jxpe'jsa Jesukristo na'wẽ we'weni's: Juana' yu'teçxáak akhna bautisaĩ'. Nawa i'kwe'sxa' Dxus Espiritute yuu bautisãyni yuune'kwe jĩni's. 17 Kwe'sxa' Jesukristo's nwẽese'pha'ga Dxusa' ma'wẽga txajx Espiritu's ũs, txã'wẽyku txãawe'sxtxiwa ũs. Aça' adxa' ¿ma'wẽ yũuçxa Dxus wedxni's wa'lna ũsuki'th?
18 Txajũ' Jerusalente ũssa yakhthẽ'jwe'sxa' wẽse'jçxa', peena yu' pasmée Dxusçxáanatx na'wẽ tuuthe'j:
—Judiuwe'sxmée vxite nasatxwa Dxusa' ũsne' pkaltxi nvxihtçxa Dxus tasxte pçuwa'jmée fxi'zewa'ja's jĩnatx weçxá'.
Antiokía çxhabte Dxus yakh fxi'zesawe'sx
19 Ãçxha' nyafxíi na'wẽ Estebana's ikhçxa vxite neeyũusáa yuutxi' ahçeçxa pkxuute', txãawe'sxa' ũphna u'jçxa' maaíi yu' Fxenice kiwen, vxite' Çxipre kiwesu, vxite' Antiokía çxhabsu kxsũ pa'jçxa vxite nasa yuutxi' pta'sxmée judiu ji'j pwe'sx yuuçxáatx pta'sxi' Dxus yuwe u'sesa's. 20 Nawa Dxus yakh fxi'zesa Çxipre vxite' Cirene kiwejuwe'sx maaíi yu' Antiokía çxhabte pa'jçxa', judiuwe'sxmeetxwa pta'sxtxi Jesus kaapiya'jni ewte fxi'zewa'ja's. 21 Aça' Dxus pu'çxte', txãawe'sxa' nyafxíi jxpubana fxi'zeni's nvxihtçxa Jesus kaapiya'jni's nwẽese'jtx.
22 Txã'wẽtx Dxusa's nwẽese'j jĩni's Jerusalente pkhaakhena ũssa' jiyuçxa', Bernabea's kaahtx Antiokía çxhabna. 23 Aça' Bernabea' kxte pa'jçxa' wala weçxak Dxus txã'wẽ pu'çxni's uyçxa. Sa' jxukaytxi yu'kxpejk jxpubamée ew Dxus yakh fxi'zekahn. 24 Txãa pa'gatey Bernabea' Dxus Espiritu's txajx ũuste jxuka jxpa'gaçxa Dxus yakh yuuçxáa fxi'zesak yu'. Txajũ' nasatx wala ya'knayku Dxus jĩi.
25 Txã'wẽ yũuçxa', Bernabea' Tarso çxhabna Saulo's pe'jya' u'jçxa', Antiokía çxhabte nuypa'kh. 26 Sa' kxte teeçx anxutx pkhaakhena ũsu' nasa ma'wẽsatxwa jxuka kaapiya'jna. Antiokíatetx nyafxçxah kristiano jĩna kxsusnisa yuu Kristo's nwẽese'jsawe'sxtxi.
27 Txãn ensu' Dxus yuwe pta'sxsawe'sx Jerusalenu yuusa pa'jtx Antiokíate. 28 Teeçxsa Agabo yaasesa' Dxus Espiritu kaajiyu'the', yakhthẽ'jwe'sx ksxavxyte yujuçxa: Judiuwe'sx kiwete jxuka wẽje en pa'jana jĩnak pta'sx. (Txã'wẽ pta'sxni' kxtey yuuk jxkaahsa Klaudio ente.) 29 Txã'wẽ wẽje en pa'jana jĩni's wẽse'jçxa', Dxus yakh fxi'zesa Antiokíatewe'sxa' txãawe'sx ji'phuni yakh pu'çxya' yajkxtxi yakhthẽ'jwe'sx Judea kiwetewe'sxtxi. 30 Txajũ' peeswa'ja's pkhakhçxa kaahtx Bernabé Saulo yakh Judeate ancianowe'sxtxi dukhkahn.
