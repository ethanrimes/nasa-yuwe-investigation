3
Kwe'sxa' Dxus luuçx yã'tha'w
1 Dxus peeygãani' seena' yuh wala' txã'sna miiyajkxwe. Txã'wẽçxaíik adx luuçxwe'sx jĩna kxsusu' kwe'sxtxi'. Aça' kwe'sxa' txajx luuçx yuhtha'w. Txãa pa'gatx naa kiwetewe'sxa' kwe'sxtxi' jiyu'çmée, Dxusa's jiimeeta' naa pa'ga. 2 Adx peeygãanisawe'sx, kwe'sxa' Dxus luuçx yã'tha'w. Nawa Kristo kĩhte', ma'wẽ seena' zhiçxkwe neeyũuwa'jsa ne'tka'w txãa yu'sa' ũus yajkxçxa kpajkxya' yu' ãja'çmeetha'w. Nawa Jesukristo vxyaate', txãa ma'wẽsate' weh yuhwa fxiymée txãa na'wẽy neeyũuwa'j yu'sa' jiitha'w. 3 Aça' maawa txãa na'wẽy yuu wẽesa' pkal ji'phmée fxi'zena, Jesukristowa ma'wẽ kĩh pkal yuhwa ji'phmeesana txã'wẽy.
4 Maawa pkalte fxi'zesa' Dxus jxkaahnitx nwẽese'jmée ũsçxa txã'wẽ yuuna, aça' Dxus jxkaahnitx kxtey yuumée ũsni' pkal íiçxá'. 5 I'kwe'sxa' jii i'kwe Jesukristo' kwe'sx pkaltxi txuhdeya'k naa kiwete kĩh, nawa txãa yu' kĩh pkal yuhwa ji'phmeesak yu'. 6 Txãa pa'ga maawa Jesukristo yakh fxi'zesa' pkalte fxi'ze'çme'. Aça' maawa pkalte fxi'zesa' Jesukristo's iiuymeena, sa' txãa ma'wẽsatewa jiimeesaçxa txã'wẽ yuuna. 7 Adx luuçxwe'sx, mpa'yajkxwe, i'kwe'sxtxi maawa kaypuba'jwe'sxte. Jesukristo na'wẽy ew yũuna fxi'zesa yuuçxáa, ewsa yuuna. 8 Nawa pkalte fxi'zesa' eçxthẽ'j yakh ũssaçxa txã'wẽ yuuna, txãa pa'gatey eçxthẽ'ja' nyafxiitey pkalsak yu'. Txajíiçxáak Dxus Nçxi'ka' eçxthẽ'j mjĩini's ivxiituya' kĩh naa kiwete.
9 Dxus luuçxa' maa yuhwa pkalte fxi'ze'çmeeta', txãa pa'gatey txãawe'sx ũusa's Dxus yu'pthehçxa nvxiitnisata' naa pa'ga. Txãasa pkalte fxi'zewa'jsameeta' Dxus luuçx yã'ja'. 10 Txãa pa'gak ya'jiyu' maa Dxus luuçxtewa. Sa' maa txã'wẽ eçxthẽ'j luuçxtxiwa ya'jiyu'k: Maawa ewmeeçxáa yũuna txajx yakhthẽ'jwe'sxtxi ahçena fxi'zesa' txãawe'sxa' Dxus luuçx meeta'.
Teeçxsa na'wẽçxah puutx ya'peeygãhna fxi'zewa'ja'
11 I'kwe'sxa' Kristo kaapiya'jni's iiwẽse'jne'kwe txã' na'wẽk yu': Teeçxsa na'wẽçxah puutx ya'peeygãhnak fxi'zewa'j yu'. 12 Eçxthẽ'j yakh ũssa Kaina' txajx nyakha's ikhku, nawa kwe'sxa' txãa na'wẽy yuuwa'jsameetha'w. ¿Kĩh yuutega ikh wakx? Kain mjĩini' ewmeek yu', aça' nyakh mjĩini' ewsak yu' txãa pa'ga yuuçxáak ikh.
13 Adx yakhthẽ'jwe'sx, kuh yaakxnuwe naa kiwetewe'sx ahçetewa. 14 Txãa pa'gatey kwe'sxa' vxituwa'ju kasehçxa bagaçxte yuhwa pçumée ĩtxi fxi'zewa'the pa'jsatha'w, txãasa íiçxáatha'w yakhthẽ'jwe'sxtxi peeygãja'. Nawa maawa txajx yakhthẽ'ja's peeygãasame', txãa vxituwa'ju kasehsameeçxa txã'wẽ yuuna. 15 Maawa txãawe'sx yakhthẽ'jwe'sxtxi ahçena fxi'zesa', nasa's ikhsa na'wẽysa íiçxá'. Aça' i'kwe'sxa' jii i'kwe nasaa ikhsa' kim yuhwa pçumée ĩtxi fxi'zewa'ja's ji'phumeetxna. 16 Jesukristo' kwe'sxtxi peeygãhçxa nwe'weya' txajx ĩtxi fxi'zeni's ivxiituk. Txãa txã'wẽ peeygãani yaase'. Aça' kwe'sxwa txã'wẽy kwe'sx yakhthẽ'jtx peeygãhçxa kwe'sx ĩtxi fxi'zeni's ivxiituwa'j ji'ptha'w. 17 Nawa kwe'sx yakhthẽ'j peejiina ũste uyçxawa kĩhçxáawa iijxtxuhdemeeçxa', ¿ma'wẽ Dxus peeygãani's kwe'sx ũuste ji'ptha'w jĩya' ãjakxtha'w? 18 Adx luuçxwe'sx, kwe'sx peeygãani' yuwejuçxáa peeygãa we'wewa'jme'. Isa peeygãjaççxa', ãate kaavxya'ja'jwa'ja' kĩhçxáawa pu'çxna.
Kwe'sx ũuste ewçxáa ũusutheçxa', Dxus dxi'pte jxpubamée ũste txã'wẽ yuuna
19 Txã'wẽ yũuçxa', isa Dxus luuçxçxa txã'wẽ yu'nja'w, sa' Dxus dxi'ptewa jxpubamée ũsne'tka'w sũhwa'ja's ji'phu'nja'w. 20 Nawa kĩhteçxáawa jxpubaaçte', kwe'sx ũusçxáa yuhwa kaayaki'ji'k aça' Dxusa' kwe'sx ũuste jxthaakwe ew jiisa' aça' txajx dxi'pte ĩsxiiya' ãjame'nja'w. 21 Adx peeygãanisawe'sx, kwe'sx ũuste ewçxáa ũusuthena aça' Dxusa' pasuna sũhçxa 22 Dxusa's pẽyi'nja'w aça' txã' jxuka ũsuna txajx jxkaahnitx nwẽese'jçxa Dxus jxthãasni's kxtey yuuna fxi'zesatxi'. 23 Dxusa' na'wẽ yũuwa'ja'sku jxkaaja': txajx Nçxi'k Jesukristo's ũuste jxpa'gaçxa, teeçxsa na'wẽçxah puutx ya'peeygãhnak fxi'zekaah. 24 Dxus jxkaahnitx nwẽese'jsa' Dxus yakh fxi'zesa yuuna, aça' Dxuswa txãawe'sx yakh fxi'zena. Dxusa' kwe'sxtxi txajx Espiritu's peesku, txãa pa'ga kwe'sxa' jiitha'w Dxusa' kwe'sx yakh ũswa'jsatewa.
