12
Kwe'sx ĩtxi fxi'zeni's Dxus kusete dukhni
1 Adx yakhthẽ'jwe'sxtxi neewe'we'th: Dxusa' txakwe wala peeygãapa'ga, ãçxha' i'kwe'sx ĩtxi fxi'zeni petx kakweyã'jçxa jxuka kĩh pkal yuhwa ji'phmée txajx kuseteçxaçxáa ya'mdukhwe, Dxusa's yuuçxáa selpiina fxi'zeya'. Dxusa' txã'wẽ yũuni'sku jxthãasu' naa pa'ga kxtey yuuwa'j ji'ptha'w. 2 Ãçxhũyã'ja' naa kiwetewe'sx na'wẽy yu' fxi'zenuwe, wejxwa i'kwe'sx fxi'zeni i'kwe'sx ũus yaakxni txãatx jxuka myu'pthehwe, kĩhtewa jxuka u'se yuuna pa'jya'. Txã'wẽ yũuçxa Dxus jxthãasni ewsa, txajx dxi'pte zhiçxkwe wẽt fxi'zeniçxá's jiyune'kwe.
3 Dxusa' peeygãhçxa adxa's neewe'wek i'kwe'sxtxi yu'kxpejkahn, txãa pa'ga yu'kxpehwa'j ji'pthu: Adxçxáath thaakwe selpi' sũhna maa yuhwa iiwejçx yaakxwa'j ji'phmée i'kwe, naasáa kĩhte selpiwa'jsa ne' Dxusa' nvxiht txã's yuuçxáana kxtey myũuwe, wala jxpa'yakxna. 4 Nasaa kakwe's miiyajkxwe: Txã' teeçxçxáaçxawa wala kuh jwed ji'pha', sa' fxiyfxiyçxahtx selpi', teeçx kakwetewe'sxçxáa we'sxçxawa. 5 Txã'wẽy kwe'sxwa wala kuhçxawa, jxukaysa Kristo yuuçxá'stha'w selpi', jxukaysa kxteeçxah fxiyfxiyçxah selpiina.
6 Kwe'sx selpiiya' ãjawa'ja's Dxusa' fxiyfxiyçxajk ũs, kĩhteçxah selpiiya' ãjawa'jsa ne'tka'w yu' txãa yu'sa' jxukak ũs. Aça' txãatxi' wala jxpa'yakx iijxũwa'j ji'ptha'w. Meeçxawa Dxusa' txajx yuwe's pta'sxya' ãjawa'ja's ũste', txajx kaajiyu'jniçxá's kxtey yuuwa'j ji'ptha'w. 7 Vxitesatx selpiina fxi'zewa'ja's ũste', txã'snawa kxtey yuuwa'j ji'ptha'w. Sa' maawa kaapiya'jya' ãjasa' txã'sa' kxtey yuuwa'ja'. 8 Nxuste fxi'zesatx puutx pu'çxya' ãjasa' txã'wẽ yũuwa'ja's nvxiitwa'jme'. Peesya' yaakiççxa', kaasxwedu'juna sũhmée txã'wẽ peeswa'ja'. Dxus luuçxtxi jxpe'jsa', wala jxpa'yakx jxpe'jwa'ja'. Peejiina fxi'zesatx pu'çxsa', weçxana pu'çxwa'ja'.
Dxusa's jiisa' na'wẽ yũuwa'ja'
9 Teeçxsa na'wẽçxah puutx ya'peeygãawa'ja's mji'phuwe, ĩsxiimée ũusuh jxuka yajkxna. Ewmeesa's nwẽese'jmée ewsaçxá's kxtey myuuwe. 10 Yakhthẽ'ja's juuna' yuumée wejxwa yakhthẽ'ja' thaakwe walasa' sũhna puutx ya'mpeeygãhwe.
11 Dxusa's selpiwa'ja'sçxáa ũusuh jxuka weçxana mpa'pçxuwe, wa'l yajkxmée.
12 Ewte pa'jwa'ja's jii i'kwe txãa pa'ga weçxana mfxi'zewe. Nxuswa'j en pa'thewa txã'sa' yajkxmée, bagaçxtewa Dxus yakh yuuçxáa puutx we'wena mfxi'zewe.
13 Yakhthẽ'jwe'sx peejiina fxi'zesawe'sxtxi' mpu'çxwe. I'kwe'sx yatte pa'jsatxwa wa'lmée mpa'gawe.
14 I'kwe'sxtxi ahçena pkxuusatx ka'tu yu' ewçxáa myũuwe, wejx yuuwa txãawe'sx pa'gate Dxusa's mneewe'wewe nwe'wekahn.
15 Weçxasáa yakh i'kwe'sxwa weçxaa mpu'çxwe, sa' nxussa yakh i'kwe'sxwa nxus mpu'çxwe.
16 Puutx ja'daçxah ewçxáa mfxi'zewe. Jxthaakwe jiith sũhçxa iiwejçx yajkxmée, ãhmeekwesa na'wẽyçxáa mfxi'zewe.
17 Makwe ewmée yũusaatewa, ka'tu yu' ewmée yuuwa'jme'. Ew yuuwa'jçxá's pa'pçxuwa'ja' jxukaysatx.
18 Jxukaysa yakh ew fxi'zewa'jçxá's mpaakwewe, i'kwe'sxtxi ma'wẽ wejx ew theguna txãa yu'sa' jxuka kxtey yuuna. 19 Yakhthẽ'jwe'sxtxi neewe'we'th i'kwe'sxtxi ewmée yũusaatx ka'tuwa ewmée yũuwa'ja's pakwena fxi'zekahmén. Txã'wẽ yũumée wejxwa idxna ewmée yũusa's kuçx yuunu, Dxus kastigãywa'jsa' naa pa'ga. Txajíik Dxus librutewa na'wẽ we'we': Ewmée yũusatx kastigãywa'jsa' adxthu. Adx jiine'tka jĩ'k. 20 Txã'wẽ ka'tu ewmée yũuwa'ja's yajkxmée na'wẽ myũu: Idxna açesa ũ'wa'j ji'phmée wẽena fxi'zeçte', ũ' mpuuç. Sa' yũ'wẽena fxi'zeçte', tudxwa'jwa mes. Idxa' txã'wẽ yũuçxa', kaythame'jene'ga jĩ'k. 21 Idxna ewmée yũusa's kaaknayu'jnu, wejxwa idx khuẽ'y meknay, ka'tu' ew yũuçxa.
