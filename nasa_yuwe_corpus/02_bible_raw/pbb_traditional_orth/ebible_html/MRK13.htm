13
Dxuus yat pçuwa'ja's Jesus pta'sxni
(Mt 24.1-2; Lk 21.5-6)
1 Txajũ' Jesusa' dxuus yatu kasejeçte', txãa yakh u'jusa teeçxsa' na'jĩnak we'we:
—Mtheg, naa yata' na'wẽ seena' wala txaahni' kwet zhiçxkwesa yakh jĩte', 2 Jesus pasçxa':
—Naa yat wala's wá'ga thegu', aça' txã'wẽ yuja'. Nawa jxuka pçuuna, aça' teeçx kwetçxáa yuhwa kẽese'jnimeesa yu' neeyũumeena jĩk.
Naa kiwe pçuwa'j en ãjaçte seena'sa vxyaawa'j
(Mt 24.3-28; Lk 21.7-24; 17.22-24)
3 Sa' txajũ kasehçxa', Oliwos thã'j dxuus yat dxi'ptek neeyũ' kxteetx u'j. Kxtee pa'jçxa Jesusa' kaçxçxa u'pte', Pedro, Jakobo, Juan, Adres txãawe'sxa' na'wẽtx paaçte pẽjx:
4 —Bagaçx txã'wẽ dxuus yata' pçuwa'jsakx txã'sa' pta'sxwaça', sũju'tha'w. Sa' txã'wẽ yuuwa'j en ãjãçte', ¿kĩhçxa yuukx? txã'snatha'w jiyu wẽjẽ' jĩte', 5 Jesusa' pta'sxku:
—Mpa'yajkxwe kaypuba'jwe'sxte. 6 Txã'wẽme', wala kasejetxna: Adxíi Kristoth jĩna. Txã'wẽ yũuçxa maaíi yuutxi' wala kaypuba'jatxna.
7 Sa' i'kwe'sxa' wẽsẽ'jene'kwe gweçxa u'pa' sa' aysuwa gweçxa pa'jana jĩni's, nawa ũukhmeene'kwe. Txã'wẽ kxtey yuuyujuna, nawa nmeh en meea'. 8 Aça' gobierno' vxite kiwete gobierno yakh puutx puiitxna. Naapkaçxha' puutx jxu'j kiwejũ pwe'sxwa puutx puiitxna. Mtee mteewa seena' ehd u'jwena. Seena' wẽjẽ enwa pa'jana. Sa' nasawa wala susutxna gobiernotx açewe'wena. Nawa pxthaa nxuswa'j en takhnaçxáa txã'wẽ yuuna.
9 Nawa i'kwe'sxa' jxpa'yakx meswe. Txã'wẽme' judiu nthẽ'jsawe'sx kusete i'kwe'sxtxi dukhçxa, txãawe'sx pkhaakhenite nuypa'jçxa pkxuutxna. Sa' i'kwe'sxa' adxna selpiiya' utxaapa'ga ahçeçxa ne'jwe'sx tasxsu vxite' jxkaahsa tasxsu kuutxa'jni yuune'kwe. Txã'wẽ kxsũ pa'jçxa', jxkaahsawe'sxtxiwa pta'sxya' ãjãne'kwe adx kaapiya'jni's. 10 Nmeh en utxaaçte', Dxus yuwe ewte nvxiitwa'jsa's mtee mteewa jxuka pta'sxni yuuna.
11 Txã'wẽ i'kwe'sxtxi' jxkaahsa kusete dukhya' jxũna u'the', ¿ma'wẽ pasukxtha'w? sũhna kuh yaakxnuwe ũukhna. Dxusa' txãa dudte kaaũusuthe'jena txã'sçxáa mwe'wewe, txãa pa'gatey i'kwe'sx we'wewa'jsameei'kwe. Dxus Espiritu i'kwe'sx ũuste kaayaki'jwa'jsa' i'kwe'sx we'wewa'ja's, aça' txã'sçxáa we'wewa'jsai'kwe. 12 Naa kiwete nasa' peekx nyakhtxi ahçeçxa kiikhe'jetxna, aça' neywe'sxwa peekx nçxi'ktxi ahçeçxa kiikhe'jetxna. Txã'wẽy nçxi'kwe'sxwa neywe'sxtxi ahçeçxa kiikhe'jetxna. 13 I'kwe'sxa' adxna selpiipa'gaçxáa naa kiwetewe'sx jxuka yaaçeni fxi'zene'kwe. Nawa uu entepkaçx Dxus yakh txutemée fxi'zeçxa', ewte neeyũune'kwe.
14 Na'wẽ fxi'jni's jiyuya' jxpa'yakx mlẽeywe: Dxus yuwe pta'sxsa Daniela' fxi'kh dxuus yatte kazx ewmée yuuna pa'jwa'ja's. Aça' txã'wẽ kxtey yuuçte uyçxa', Judea kiwete ũssa nasa' çxhab peeku'j yu'khsuçxaçxáa ũphna kasejene'kwe. 15 Aça' txã'wẽ seena' yuuçyã'the', ẽe yat-su u'psa ũphna sa'jçxawa, kĩh yuhwa kutxi'jwa'j en ji'phumeetxna. 16 Txã'wẽy mjĩi ehsu u'jusawa txajx atxhçxá'swa yatuh kutxi'jya' pa'jwa'j en ji'phumeetxna. 17 Txãa ensu' nasa ji'phsa vxite' luuçx ãpãkuẽ ji'phsa' wala yuh nxus pxthaa yuutxna. 18 I'kwe'sxa' Dxusna mpi'kxwe, txã'wẽ ũphwa'j ena' nus ente yuukahmén. 19 Txã'wẽme' iiméh seena' wala nxuswa'j en yuuna. Naa kiwe's Dxus pheu'jiyna, bagaçxte yuhwa nxuswa'j en txã'wẽsa yu' u'pu'çme', sa' txãa skhẽwniijũyã'ja' bagaçxte yuhwa txã'wẽy nxussa yu' u'pumeena. 20 Kwe'sxtxi taki'sa Dxusa' txã'wẽ seena' nxuswa'j ena's txaçxmée vxitmeete yu', naa kiwete nasa kim yuhwa ĩtxĩ neeyũumeetxna. Nawa txã'wẽ seena' nxuswa'j en u'puwa'ja's Dxusa' txaçxmeek vxit txajx nasa txhitxhnisatx peeygãhçxa.
21 Txajũ' maaíi yu' i'kwe'sxtxi na'jĩtxna: Myuhwe thegya', Kristo' ayte ũsa' jĩtxna. Meeçxawa: Me'jwe thegya', Kristo' kxtee ũsa' jĩtewa, i'kwe'sxa' kxtey yuunuwe. 22 Txãa ensu' wala ĩsxĩisa kasehçxa: Kristotha'w jĩtxna, naapkaçxha' vxite': Kwe'sxa' Dxus yuwe pta'sxsatha'w jĩna pekũjutxna, kaypuba'j wẽeçxa kĩjkĩhwa kaavxya'ja'jna. Jxpa'yakxmeete', Dxus txhitxhnisa yuhtxwa kaypuba'jatxna. 23 Aça' txã'wẽ yũuwe'sxte, adxa' dxiikhẽy pta'sxiçthu i'kwe'sx jxpa'yakx ũskahn.
Dxus Nçxi'k Nasa Yuusáa seena' zhiçxkwe vxyaana
(Mt 24.29-35, 42-44; Lk 21.25-36)
24 Nawa txã'wẽ nxusni en skhẽwniijũ', seka' çxhi'dxina, aça' a'tewa kweethmeena. 25 Naapkaçxha' estrelxa' cielujũ ũsxitxna, aça' kĩjkĩhwa seena' walasa ẽe walate ũstxna txãawa jxuka kẽese'jni yuutxna. 26 Txã'wẽ yũunijũ', Dxus Nçxi'k Nasa Yuusa's uyuutxna tãaph pxãhte seena' zhiçxkwe kweethna kĩjaçte. 27 Sa' kĩhçxa' txajx angeleswe'sxtxi jxkaajana txajx nasa txhitxhnisa mtee mteewa ũstxna txãatx jxuka pkhakhkahn.
28 Naa ejemplo's peçxkananuwe: higwera fxtũu tasx u'se yeeçuçte uyçxa', jiyu'i'kwe yũnya'watewa. 29 Txã'wẽy na'wẽ pta'sxthu txãatx uyçxa', jii mfxi'zewe nmeh ena' utxaaçna sũhna. 30 Isath we'we': txã'wẽ seena' nxuswa'j ena's uyuutxna naa ensu nasa uumey. 31 Cielu naa kiwe jxuka skhẽuutxna, nawa adx we'weni' jxuka kxtey yuumey yu' skhẽwmeetxna. 32 Nawa txã'wẽ Dxus Nçxi'k kĩh orate kĩiwa'jsatewa, txãa en yu'sa' kim yuhwa jiimeeta' cielute Dxus angeleswe'sxwa jiimeeta', aça' Dxus Nçxi'k íi yuhwa jiime', naasáa Dxus yuuçxáa jiia'.
33 Txãa pa'ga i'kwe'sxa' wala jxpa'yakx meswe, Dxusna yuuçxáa neewe'wena. Txã'wẽme' jiimeei'kwe bagaçx kĩiwa'jsatewa. 34 Txã' na'wẽ yuuna: Teeçx piçthẽ'j jxu'j kiwen u'jweççxa', yatu kasehmey selpisaatx teeçxsa na'wẽçxah mjĩi ũsçxa kxtee jxpa'yakx ũskahn teeçx thegwa'jsa txajana. 35 Txã'wẽy i'kwe'sxwa wala jxpa'yakx meswe. Txã'wẽme' jiimeei'kwe çxhi'dxiçte ma'k yuu, meeçxa' kuspxãh atalx we'weçte ma'k yuu, meeçxa' kusíi ma'k yuu nawa pa'jçxáa yuuna. 36 Txã'wẽme' yajkxmée ũsiyna, naa dudte pa'jçxáa wáa yuuna. 37 I'kwe'sxçxáatx pta'sxmée, jxukaysatxthu pta'sxi' jxpa'yakx ũskahn.
