2
Dxus Espiritu kĩini
1 Pentekostes fxiesta en ãhte', jxukaysa kxteeçxaçxáatx pkhaakheçxa ũsu'. 2 Naapkaçxha' cieluju wejxaa wala na'wẽ susna kĩhçxa txãawe'sx ũsni yatte jxuka utak. 3 Naapkaçxha' ipx beeni na'wẽsatx jxukaysa kajkwesu vxyaa. 4 Aça' jxukaysa ja'daçxah txãawe'sx ũuste Dxus Espiritu yuuçxaçxáa jxuka khẽete', txã'sna jxpa'gaçxa maa nasa we'weni yuwe'swa we'weya' ãjatx, Dxus Espiritu maa yuweju we'wewa'ja's jxkaathe' txãaju.
5 Txãn ensu' Dxus jxkaahni's nwẽese'jsa judiuwe'sx maa maa kiwejũwa pa'jçxatx ũsu' Jerusalente. 6 Txajũ' txãa seena' susni's wẽse'jçxa', nasa jxukaysa pkhaakhetx. Sa' txã'wẽ teeçxsa na'wẽçxah txãawe'sx we'weni yuweju we'weni's jiyuçxa', wala kuh yajkxna neeyũutx 7 ¿Ma'wẽçxaki'tx txã'wẽ yũu? sũhna. Sa' txãawe'sx pwe'sx na'jĩnatx puutx paapẽyi':
—Na'wẽ we'wena ũsta' txãawe'sxa' jxukay ¿Galileajũwe'sxmeetxná'? 8 Sa' ¿ma'wẽçxata nasa iisa kwe'sx yuweju we'weya' ãja'? 9 Ayte ũstha'w Partiajũwe'sx, Mediajũwe'sx, Elamjũwe'sx, Mesopotamiajũwe'sx, Judeajũwe'sx, Kapadociajũwe'sx, Pontojũwe'sx, Asia kiwejũwe'sx, 10 Fxrigia vxite' Panfxilia kiwejũwe'sx, Egipto vxite' Afxrika Cirene kiwetewe'sx, txã'wẽy Romajũwe'sxwa ayte ũstha'w. Maaíi yu' upxhíi judiuwe'sxtha'w, aça' vxite' judiumeeçxawa judiuwe'sx na'wẽy Dxus jxkaahni's nwẽese'jsa ũstha'w. 11 Txã'wẽy Kretajuwe'sx vxite' Arabiajuwe'sxwa ũstha'w. Nawa jxukaysa Dxus yuwe zhiçxkwesa'stha'w wẽse'je', kwe'sx we'weni yuweju pta'sxpa'ga jĩ'tx. 12 Txã'wẽ yũuni's jxukaysa wala kuh jwed we'wena ũsu'tx na'jĩna:
—¿Kĩh yũuya' txã'wẽ yuuçki? jĩ'tx. 13 Naapkaçxha' vxite yu' açewe'wena npeevxysxijkana na'jĩ'tx:
—Naawe'sxa' vxino pus tudxçxa tũuçxa txã'wẽ we'wen ũsta' jĩ'tx.
Pedro nyafxçxah nasatx we'weni
14 Txajũ' Pedro' Jesus jxkaahnisawe'sx oncesa yakh yujuçxa sus we'wena na'jĩk:
—Judeatewe'sx vxite' Jerusalentewe'sx i'kwe'sxa' ew jiyuçxa neeyũuya' ew mwẽese'jwe adx pta'sxya'wath txã'sna: 15 Naa piçthẽ'jwe'sxa' tũunitxna jĩ'i'kwe nawa txã'wẽ meeta'. Kusíi las nweweçxá' aça' ¿ma'wẽ tũuni yuukxtha'w? 16 Na'wẽ yuuk txã' Dxus yuwe pta'sxsa Joel we'wek txãak kxtey yuu. Txã' na'jĩ'k:
17 Dxus we'weçxa':
Nmeh ensu', adx Espiritu's naa kiwete nasa jxukaysatx makwewa ũsu'nja,
Aça' i'kwe'sx nçxi'k i'kwe'sx niisa txãawe'sxa' adx yuwe's pta'sxitxna.
Naapkaçxha' piçtakxwe'sxa' cieluju zhiçxkwesa vxyaani's uyuutxna,
Aça' thẽ'jsawe'sxa' ewsa ksxa'utxna.
18 Sa' adxa's selpisaawe'sxtxi piçthẽ'j ma'k yuu meeçxa' u'y ma'k yuu nawa txãawe'sxtxi adx Espiritu's makwewa ũsu'nja, adx yuwe's pta'sxkahn.
19 Txajũ' cielujũ kim yuhwa uynimeesa zhiçxkwesa kaavxya'ja'ja'nja:
Sa' naa kiwete' ũukhũukhsa kaavxya'ja'ja'nja: ee, ipx wala, aah wala txã'wẽsatx.
20 Aça' seka' kus na'wẽ çxhi'dxina, a'tewa ee na'wẽ beh yuuna.
Nawa kwe'sxtxi nwe'wesa kĩiwa'j en zhiçxkwesa ãhmeyna txã'wẽ yuuna.
21 Aça' maawa jxukaysa Dxusa's neewe'wena fxi'zesa' txãa ente' nwe'weni yuutxna jĩ'k.
22 Ãçxha' Israelwe'sx mwẽese'jwe na'jĩya'wath: Jesus Nasarethũwe'sx Dxus kaahnisa i'kwe'sx ksxavxysu kĩjkĩhwa ewsaçxaçxáanak kaavxya'ja'j ãçã'satx nuykatxhina, uusaatx ĩtxĩ vxitna Dxus pu'çxte, Jesukristo' txã'wẽk yũu. Txã'wẽ yũuni's jiiyã'jne'kwe. 23 Nawa txã'sa' kluuste fxuççxa ikhkahn i'kwe'sxa' dukhi'kwe nasa ewmeesa kusete. Txã'wẽ uuwa'ja's Dxusne' txhitxhçxa nvxiht txãniitey, txãa pa'ga i'kwe txã'wẽ yũu. 24 Nawa Dxusa' ki' ĩtxi vxitçxa uuníi seena' ũukhũukhsaju kutxi'kh, txãa pa'gatey ikhsa' bagaçxtewa uuniiçxáa ji'phuya' yu' ãjawa'jsameek yu' naa pa'ga. 25 Jesus uuçxa ki' ĩtxi yuuwa'ja's Davxida' na'jĩnak pta'sx:
Adxna jxpe'jsa' bagaçxte yuhwa pu'vxitumée adx yakh nesku yu'.
Txã' adx pukate ũsa' paçu ju'gte, kĩh yuhnawa ũukhkahmén adxa's nwe'wena.
26 Txãa pa'gak adx ũuste txakwe wala weçxweçxa ũusuthe', aça' weçxaawa'j yuuçxá'sthu we'we'.
Txãa pa'ga adx kakwe' ki' ĩtxi kiiteena sũhnaçxáa skhẽwu'nja,
27 Txãa pa'gatey idxa' adx ũusa's uusaawe'sx ũsnite nvxihtmeene'ga,
Sa' idxa's selpisáa kakwe'swa suweepkaçx yu' khikhmeene'ga.
28 Pçuwa'jmée ĩtxi ũswa'the pa'jwa'ja's kaapiya'jg,
Sa' iidx ũsnite weçxana ũswa'ja's yuuçxáa ũsune'ga jĩna fxi'jni'.
29 Yakhthẽ'jwe'sx ew mjiyuwe: Na'wẽ kwe'sx yaçgawe'sx Davxid we'wek txã' peekx uuwa'ja'síiçxáa txã'jĩ'çme'. Txã'wẽmeete íiçxáa Davxid uute' pedaatx, aça' txajx kakwe' txkaa ũsa' pedaní ãçxpkaçx. 30 Nawa Davxida' Dxus yuwe pta'sxsak yu'. Txãasak jii yu' Dxusa' teeçx ũsya' puutx we'wek txajx ji'thewe'sx txã' Kristok yu' Davxid na'wẽy jxkaahwa'jsa txã'snak jii yu'. 31 Kristo uuçxa ĩtxi kiiteeçte uyna ũssa na'wẽk pta'sx Davxida'. Txãasak na'jĩ: txajx ũusa' uusaawe'sx ũsnite neeyũumeena, aça' txajx kakwe kha'txwa suweemeena. 32 Davxid we'we'k Jesus txã'snak Dxusa' uuniijũwa ki' ĩtxi vxit, aça' kwe'sxa' jxukaysa uysaatha'w, txãasa ew jiitha'w. 33 Sa' txãa Jesusíik Dxus pukate paçu ju'gte kãhpkaçx pe'jna u'jni yuu. Sa' kxte' Ney ũsya' neeyũuk txajx Espiritu's txã'sa' jxpa'gaçxak txãa Espiritu's kwe'sxtxi makwewa ũs. Aça' txã'si'kwe i'kwe'sxa' jxuka uyna wẽse'j. 34 Davxid txã'wẽ cielute tejkame', txajíik Davxidíiwa na'jĩ':
Dxusa' adxna jxpe'jsa's na'jĩk:
Adx pukate paçu ju'gte mkaaçx,
35 Idxna açesatx idxçxaçxá's nwẽese'jwa'ja's vxitna jxã'phkaçx jĩk.
36 Israelwe'sx jxukaysa ew mjiyuwe: Naa Jesusa's i'kwe'sxa' kluuste kaafxuçu'ji'kwe txã'snak Dxusa' Kristo jxkaahwa'jsa vxit.
37 Txã'jĩna pta'sxte wẽse'jçxa', ũuste wala nxus yajkxtxi. Sa' Pedrowe'sxtxi pẽjxtxi:
—Yakhthẽ'jwe'sx, ãçxha' ¿ma'wẽ yũukxtha'w? jĩte', 38 Pedro pasçxa':
—I'kwe'sx pkaltxi nvxihtçxa, Dxus tasxte misxawedwe, sa' Jesukristo's ũuste jxpa'gaçxa yu'tewa ya'bautisãywe, kxah i'kwe'sx pkaltxi peltunãyni yuuna. Txã'wẽ yũute', i'kwe'sxtxiwa Dxusa' txajx Espiritu's ũsuna. 39 Txãa pa'gatey Dxusa' txajx Espiritu's ũsya' neeyũuk txã' i'kwe'sx jĩi petx i'kwe'sx luuçx jĩi yã'jçxa', sa' maazkuẽesa majũwe'sxwa ũstxna Dxus pa'yanisa txãawe'sxwa jxpa'gatxna jĩ'k.
40 Txã'wẽ we'wena wala yu'kxpejk Pedro' na'jĩna:
—Nasa ewmeesa ksxavxyte jxpubana fxi'zemée Dxus tasxte mutxaawe ewte neeyũuya' jĩnak pta'sx. 41 Aça' Pedro pta'sxni's nwẽese'jçxa yu'te ya'bautisãytxi. Txãa ente' tekh mil ãhtx Kristo's jxpa'ga. 42 Sa' Pedrowe'sx kaapiya'jni's piyaçxa', jxukaysa Dxusa's çxhãçxha yajkxnatx neeyũu. Sa' txutemée kxteeçxahtx pkhaakhena fxi'ze', Dxus yakh puutx we'wena, ũ'wa'ja'swa kxteeçxah ũ'na.
Puutx kxteeçxah ewçxáa fxi'zeni
43 Txajũ' nasa jxukaysa wala kuh yajkxna ũsu'tx, naapkaçxha' Pedrowe'sxa' ãça'satx nuykatxhina kim yuhwa ãjanimeesa'swa kaavxya'ja'jnatx ũsu'. 44 Jxukaysa Kristo's jxpa'gasa' txutemeetx kxteeçxah fxi'ze', sa' kĩh ji'phuni'swa ja'daçxaçxáatx puutx peesu'sna fxi'ze'. 45 Sa' txãawe'sx ji'phuni's txweyçxa vxyu's kxteeçxah pkhakhçxatx peejiina fxi'zesatx peesu' txãawe'sx peejini's pu'çxna. 46 Een isatx dxuus yatte pkhaakhe', sa' ũ'wa'ja's yu' yat iisatx pi'ki' ũusuh jxuka weçxana, 47 sa' Dxusnawa txã'wẽy weçxana. Txã'wẽ yũute', nasa' txãawe'sxtxi' wala ew we'we'tx. Aça' Dxusa' een isak txajx luuçxtxi' nuypehnana u'jwe' ewte neeyũuwa'jsatx.
