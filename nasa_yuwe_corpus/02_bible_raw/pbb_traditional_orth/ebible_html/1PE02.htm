1 Txãa pa'ga ewmée yũuya' yaakxni's jxuka manvxihtwe. Ĩsxiinisa, yuwejuçxáa ew we'weçxa' ũuste yu' ewmée yaakxni, vxitetx açeyaakxni, e'sgaçxáa ewmée nuywe'weni txã'wẽsatxi' nvxihtçxa, 2 luuçx u'se kasehsawa ma'wẽga leçxi ewsaçxá's jxpa'ga', txã'wẽy i'kwe'sx ũuste ew kçxhãaçxha'jwa'jsaçxá's mpaakwewe. Txã'wẽ yũuçxa Dxus yakh çxhãçxha fxi'zene'kwe ewte neeyũuya'. 3 Txã'wẽ myũuwe, kwe'sxtxi jxpe'jsa ewsatewa ew jiyu dxihçxa'.
Kwet ĩtxisa na'wẽsa' Kristo'
4 Kwe'sxtxi jxpe'jsa tasxte mutxaawe. Txã' kwet ĩtxisa na'wẽsa'. Txã'sa' naa kiwete nasa' ahçetx, nawa txãaíik kwet jxthaakwe ewsa Dxus txhitxhnisa yu'. 5 I'kwe'sxwa kwet ĩtxisa na'wẽ i'kwe naa pa'ga Dxusa' i'kwe'sxtxi' txajx ũswa'j yat na'wẽsak vxitu', sa' txã'wẽçxáamée kĩh pkal yuhwa ji'phmée txajx tasxte yuuçxáa selpiina ũswa'jsak vxit, txãa pa'ga txajx tasxte utxaaçxa ewsa yuuçxá's kxtey myuuwe. Jesukristo pu'çxte', i'kwe'sx txã'wẽ yũuni's Dxusa' jxpa'gana. 6 Txã'sna we'weçxak Dxus librute na'jĩ':
Yat txaahwa'j kwet txhitxhnisa iiméh ewsa nvxiituçthu Sion judiuwe'sx kiwete,
Aça' txã'sa' maawa jxpa'gasa' jxpubasáa yuumeena jĩ'k.
7 I'kwe'sx ew jiyusa yakh yu' txãa kweta' iiméh wala ewsa'. Nawa jiyusamée yakha' na'wẽçxáak ya'yu' txã'wẽ yũuni's Dxus librute na'wẽk we'we':
Yat txaahsawe'sxa' kweta's ahçeçxa wãatãhtewa,
E'suyã'ja' txãaíik thaakwe ewsa yuuna pa'j jĩ'k.
8 Vxite ajkhẽ' txãa kweta'síi we'weçxa':
Kweta' kxu'çxafxi'jçxa pẽtxsaçxá' jĩ'k.
Dxus yuwe's pta'sxtewa, txãawe'sxa' kxtey nwẽese'jsame' jxpubasaata', jiyuwa'jsamée ne'ta yu' naa pa'ga.
Nasa Dxus txhitxhnisa
9 Nawa i'kwe'sxa' Dxus txhitxhnisai'kwe, txajx tasxte yuuçxáa selpiina ũswa'jsa. Dxus kusetewe'sxi'kwe, kĩh pkal yuhwa ji'phmeesa. Txãa pa'ga Dxus txakwe wala i'kwe'sxtxi ewte nvxiitni's pta'sxwa'j ji'phi'kwe. Txã'wẽ yũukahnku pa'ya, jiimée jxpubana çxhi'dxte ũsnijũ kasehçxa zhiçxkwe eena'te pa'khan. 10 I'kwe'sxa' nyafxiia' Dxus jĩi yu'çmée i'kwe, nawa ãçxha' Dxus jĩi i'kwe. Nyafxíia' kim yuhwa peeygãanisamée i'kwe yu', nawa ãçxha' Dxus peeygãanisa i'kwe.
Dxusna selpiina yuuçxáa mfxi'zewe
11 Adx yakhthẽ'jwe'sxtxith neewe'we': I'kwe'sxa' naa kiwetewe'sxmée yã'ji'kwe, txãa pa'ga peekx kakwe ewmée yũuya' yaakxnitxi's nwẽese'jnuwe. Txã'wẽme' ũusa's suwsaata'. 12 Dxusa's jiimeesawe'sx ksxavxysu wala ew mfxi'zewe. Txãawe'sxa' ãçxhíi yu' wala açewe'we'tx i'kwe'sxtxi ewmeesa na'wẽ. Nawa Kristo kĩi ente' i'kwe'sx ew yũuni's uyçxa', Dxus luuçx yuh yu'ta jĩna tuuthe'jetxna.
13 Dxusa's jii yã'jsa' jxuka mwẽese'jwe naa kiwete jxkaahwa'jsa nvxiitni ũsta' txãawe'sxtxi, jxkaahsa npiiçthẽ'jsa ma'k yuu, 14 meeçxa' jxkaahsa nuuçxsa ma'tx yuu nawa jxuka. Txãawe'sxa' Dxus kaahnisata' ewmée yũusatxi' kastigãyna vxite' ew yũusatxi' nwe'wesa. 15 Txãa pa'gatey Dxusa' i'kwe'sxtxi' ewku yũu jxthãasu' vxitetx, txã'wẽ yũuçxa açewe'wesatx sxuuna' vxit-ya'.
16 Dxus nwe'wenisa yã'jçxa', ew mfxi'zewe. Nawa nwe'wenisa yã'tha'w aça' kimwa ma'jĩmeena sũhçxa ewmée yũunuwe, wejxwa Dxus kusete selpisaatha'w sũhna ew yũuwa'jçxá's yajkxna mfxi'zewe. 17 Jxukaysa yakh ewçxáa mfxi'zewe, yakhthẽ'jwe'sxtxi peeygãhna, jxkaahsawe'sxtxi nwẽese'jna, Dxusnawa txã'wẽy nwẽese'jna.
Kristowa ma'wẽga pkxuuni yuu txã'wẽy myuuwe
18 Selpisaawe'sxa' i'kwe'sx patrontxi wala jxpa'yakx nwẽese'jna kxtey myuuwe. I'kwe'sx patron ewsaçxáatx txã'wẽ yũumée, patron seena' juuna'satxwa ewçxáa myũuwe. 19 Kĩhwa ãhmée yũumeete Dxus yakh fxi'zepa'gaçxáa patronwe'sx txã'wẽ kastigãyte', txã'sa' nxuswa'jme'. 20 Nawa i'kwe'sx ãhmée yũute kastigãyte', yuuwesatha'w naa pa'gatx kastigãy sũhwa'jçxá'. Nawa i'kwe'sx ew yũupa'gaçxáa nxuswa'j ena's ji'phute', Dxusa' peeygãjana. 21 Txã'wẽte fxi'zekahnku Dxusa' pa'ya, i'kwe'sx pa'gate Kristo' yuuwemeesaçxawa pxthaa yuuk txãa na'wẽy yuuwemeesaçxawa pkxuuni yuukahn. 22 Txã' kĩh pkal yuhwa yũume', sa' kim yuhnawa ĩsxiime'. 23 Wẽesxusatxi's ka'tuwa wẽesxuuna pasme'. Pkxuusaatxi's txã'wẽ yũupa'ga i'kwe'sxwa ewmeete neeyũune'kwe jĩna pasme'. Txã'wẽ yũumée wejxwa Dxusçxáanak neewe'we. 24 Kwe'sx pkaltxi's Kristok kluuste uuçxa ivxiitu, kwe'sxa' peena' yu' pkalte fxi'zemée Dxus dxi'pte ewçxáa fxi'zekahnku txã'wẽ yũu. Kwe'sx pkala' yaateni yuukahnku txã' pkxuuni pxthaa yuu. 25 I'kwe'sxa' nyafxiia' piisxáa vxitusa na'wẽ i'kwe ũsu', nawa ãçxha' Kristo tasxte sxawedi'kwe, txã' piisxáa thegsa na'wẽk jxpe'je' i'kwe'sx ũustxi.
