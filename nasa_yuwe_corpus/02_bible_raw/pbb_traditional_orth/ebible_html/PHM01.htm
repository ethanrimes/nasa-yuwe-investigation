SAN PABLO KARTA FXILEMON JĨI
1
Pablo' Fxilemona's fxi'jni
1 Adxa' Jesukristo jxkaahni yuwe ewsa's pta'sxpa'gaçxáa preesu ũsthu, nawa naa karta's fxi'jaçthu yakhthẽ'j Timoteo yakh, kwe'sx yakh mjĩisa Fxilemon idxa's weçxana. 2 Vxite' kwe'sx npe'sx Apia vxite' yakhthẽ'j Arkipo kwe'sx yakh mjĩisa vxite' iidx yatte pkhaakhesawe'sx txãawe'sxtxi weçxanath fxi'ja'. 3 Dxus kwe'sx Tata vxite' kwe'sxtxi jxpe'jsa Jesukristo txãawe'sx pu'çxni wẽt mfxi'ze.
Fxilemona' Kristo's ũusuh jxuka yajkxna vxitetxwa peeygãani
4 Bagaçxtewa Dxus yakh puutx we'we iisath idxa's yajkxna Dxusa's weçxá'. 5 Txã'wẽ Jesukristo's yuuçxáa yajkxna yakhthẽ'jwe'sx jxukaysatxku peeygãhna fxi'ze' jĩni's jiyuçxath txã'wẽ weçxá'. 6 Idx txã'wẽ Dxus yakh fxi'zepa'ga vxitewa Dxusa's jxpa'gasa ũsta' txãawe'sxwa Kristo Jesus kĩhtewa jxuka pu'çxni's ew jiyukahnthu Dxusa's neewe'we'. 7 Adxa's iiméh wala weçx weçxa ũusuthek yakhthẽ'j iidx peeygãani's jiyute', txãa pa'gatey txã'wẽ iidx pa'gaçxáa vxitewa txãawe'sx ũuste wẽt-sa's jxpa'gatx naa pa'ga.
Pablo Fxilemona's neewe'weni Onesimo's ewçxáa jxpa'gakahn
8 Adxa' Kristo kaahnisath na' idxa's juuna' jxkaajxa' ãjawa'jsath. 9 Nawa idx txã'wẽ ewsate jiiçxa', juuna' we'wemée peeygãhnaçxáath we'we', adxa' Kristo Jesusna selpiipa'ga preesu ũsthu thẽ'khwe yã'jsa. 10 Txãasath idxa's pu'çx jxthãasu' Onesimo's ewçxáa yũuna. Txã'sa' adx nçxi'k na'wẽsath Dxus jĩi knay ay karcelte ũsçxawa. 11 Nyafxíi yu' idxna selpisáa Onesimo' seelpimeesak yuu. Nawa ãçxha' wala seelpisa' adx yakh vxite' idx yakhwa. 12 Txã'sna iidx tasxte ki' kajaçthu aça' miipa'ga adx yuu kxtee pa'jaç na'wẽ. 13 Adxa' Dxus yuwe's pta'sxpa'ga ay karcelte ũsthu aça' adxna pi'kxna ũswa'jsa Onesimo's kaahmeewaç wejx ewna, idxa' adx yakh pi'kxna ũsya' ãja'çmeeg aça' Onesimo ũskahn. 14 Nawa txã'wẽ yũu wẽemeeth, idxna kaajiyu'jmey yu', kxah idx adxna pu'çx wẽeçxa' pu'çxhine'ga Onesimo's kaapi'ki'jna. Nawa idxa's txã'wẽ myũu jĩna yu' jxthãasuçmeeth. 15 Onesimo' iidx tasxu maz en yu' txuteene', naajũyã'ja' peena yu' bagaçxte yuhwa txuteeya'mée. 16 Nawa ãçxha' selpiisáa na'wẽmée, wejxwa selpiisaate jxthaakwe yakhthẽ'j peeygãanisa na'wẽ ew idx jxpe'khan ne' txã'wẽ yũu. Adxa' Onesimo's wala peeygãja'th, aça' idxa' wejx jxthaakwe peeygãawa'j ji'phgu, Onesimo' iidx jxpe'jnisak yu' nawa ãçxha' txã'wẽçxáamée, iidx yakhthẽ'ja' Kristo's jiisapa'ga.
17 Idxa' adxa's namiku na'wẽ dxih yaakiççxa', Onesimo's miipa'ga, adx pa'jwaç ma'wẽ weçxana jxpa'gaki'g txã'wẽ yajkxna miipa'ga. 18 Idxa's ãhmée wá' yuu, meeçxa' yuulwáana aça' adxna yuula's pẽyine'ga. 19 Yuula's dukhe'nja jĩna Pablo adx kuseju fxi'jni's uyçxa', wejx yuuwa adx txã'wẽ Pablo's yuulthu ew yũupa'ga sũjune'ga. 20 Yakhthẽ'j, txã'wẽ adx neewe'weni's kxtey meen yuu, Onesimo's jxpa'gana Dxusa's jiisa na'wẽ. Txã'wẽ yũuçxa', adx ũuste wẽtwẽt kaayaki'jine'ga.
21 Txã'jĩnath fxi'ja'. Idxa' kxtey yuune'ga sa' adx jxthãasnite jxthaakwe ew yũuwa'ja's jiiçxath txã'jĩ'. 22 Txã'wẽçxáa yũumée, adx pa'jwa'ja'swa ũythasna mes. I'kwe'sx txã'wẽ Dxus yakh puutx we'wena ũste', Dxusa' kajawáana aça' maa enteya' sxawedu'nja i'kwe'sxtxi thegya'.
Yuwe weçxaa kaahnitx pta'sxna fxi'jni
23 Kristo Jesusna selpiipa'ga karcelte adx yakh pi'kxna ũssa Epafxraswa yuwe weçxaa kaja'k, 24 vxite' Markos, Aristarko, Demas, Lukas txãawe'sxwa mjĩi pu'çxsata' sa' weçxaa kaja'tx.
25 Kwe'sxtxi jxpe'jsa Jesukristo' i'kwe'sxtxi pu'çxhina.
