16
Jesus ki' ĩtxi yuuni
(Mt 28.1-10; Lk 24.1-12; Jn 20.1-10)
1 Txajũ' jxkaase en skhẽwniite', Maria Magdalena vxite' Maria Jakobo njĩ'j, vxite' Salomé txãawe'sxa' wẽt putasá weytxi Jesus kakwete sxũsxya' u'jya'.
2 Txajũ' kuskuskhẽ wala kusíi u'jtx, sa' sek kãjaçte' pedaní kafxte pa'jtx. 3 Sa' kxtee pa'jaççxa', na'jĩnatx txãawe'sx pwe'sx we'we':
—¿Kim pedaní kafxte aphnisa kwet wala's kiisukx? jĩ'tx. 4 Txajũ' pedaní kafxte thegçxa', aphnisa kwet wala' kiisçxa pukate ki'pni u'ptetx uy. 5 Aça' pedaní kafxte u'kaçxa', teeçx piçtakx ropa çxihmesa jxu'jsa athçxa paçu ju'gu kaçxçxa u'pte uyçxa', wala ũukhtxi. 6 Nawa txãa piçtakxa' na'wẽk pta'sx:
—Ũukhnuwe. I'kwe'sxa' Jesus Nasarethuwe'sxa's kluuskhẽ fxuçni uuk txã'snai'kwe pakwé'. Nawa txã' ĩtxĩ yuuyã'kh, txãasa ayga meeyã'ja'. Aça' khikhniga mthegwe wakx, kĩhwa meea' jĩçxa' kaakh na'jĩna: 7 Txãa yakh u'jusawe'sx vxite' Pedro txãawe'sxtxi na'jĩne'kwe: Jesusa' i'kwe'sx yaçte u'jweça' Galilean, aça' kxtee txã'sa' uyuune'kwe ma'wẽga uumeywa pta'sx txã'sa' kxtey yuuna jĩkaathe', 8 txãawe'sxa' pedaní kafxu wala ũukhna ũphna u'jçxa, kuh yaakxnite vxitesa yuutxi' dxi'the kim yuhtxwa pta'sxmeetx u'j.
Maria Magdalena's Jesus vxyaani
(Jn 20.11-18)
9 Txã'wẽ jxkaase en skhẽwnijũ' Jesusa' ki' ĩtxĩ yuuçxa', Maria Magdalena khuẽ'yna vxyaak, na'wẽ siete eçx luuku vxit-satx kutxi'kh txã'sna. 10 Aça' txãa u'ya' Jesus yakh u'jusawe'sx tasxna u'jçxa, pta'sxku. Txãawe'sxa' nxusçxa ũ'nenatx u'p yu'. 11 Sa' Jesusa' ĩtxĩ yuuga aça' ũ'kwe' uythu jĩna pta'sxtewa, weh yuhwa txã'wẽ yũune' sũjũ'çmeeta' txãawe'sxa'.
Jesus fxiy yuuçxa vxyaani
(Lk 24.13-35)
12 Txãa entey Jesusa' ki' vxyaak fxiy yuuçxa, txãa yakh u'jusawe'sx e'zsa dxi'the u'jweçte. 13 Aça' txãawe'sxwa ki' sxawedçxa, Jesusa's txã'wẽ uytha'w jĩna vxitetx pta'sxtewa, txã'wẽ uyne'ta sũjũ'çmeeta'.
Jesusa' txãa yakh u'jusawe'sxtxi neewe'weçxa nvxiitni
(Mt 28.16-20; Lk 24.36-49; Jn 20.19-23)
14 Txã'wẽ pta'sxna jxã'jaçpkaçxha', txãa yakh u'jusa oncesa mesate kaçxçxa u'pte ki' vxyaaçxa', na'jĩk:
—¿Kĩh yuuçxakwe adxa'sa' ki' ĩtxĩ yuune' sũjũ'çmée? Adx pta'sxni's ũuste yu'akhya' ãjã'çmeei'kwe, sa' ĩtxĩ yuute uysáa pta'sxtewa txã'wẽ uyne'ta sũjũ'çmeei'kwe.
15 Txajũ' na'jĩnak jxkaah:
—Me'jwe, Dxus yuwe's pta'sxya' nasa jxukaysatx maa kiwete mteewa jxuka. 16 Aça' maawa adxa's ũusuh jxuka yajkxna jxpa'gaçxa yu'te ya'bautisaĩna txã' nwe'weni yuuna. Nawa adxa's ũusuh jxuka yajkxna jxpa'gasame' Dxus kastigo's knayuuna. 17 Adxa's ũusuh jxuka yajkxna jxpa'gasa' na'wẽ yũuya' ãjãtxna: Adx pu'çxte', eçx luuku vxit-satxwa nasaa ũusuh kutxi'jitxna. Sa' yuwe fxiysajuwa we'wetxna. 18 Ul wesweetxwa kuseju uweetxna. Sa' makwe weneno's tudxtewa, kĩh yuhwa kãaçã'jmeena kakwete. Sa' ãçã'sa kakwete peekiizeçxa, Dxus yakh puutx we'wete', katxhitxna jĩk.
Jesus cielun u'jni
(Lk 24.50-53)
19 Txã'wẽ yu'kxpehni e'su', kwe'sxtxi jxpe'jsa Jesusa' cielun pe'jna u'jni yuuçxa, Dxus pukate kaçxku paçu ju'gte. 20 Aça' txãa yakh u'jusawe'sxa' u'jçxa', Dxus yuwe's mtee mteewa pta'sxtxi. Txã'wẽ kwe'sxtxi jxpe'jsa pu'çxte', kĩjkĩh yũuya'wa ya'ãjãk, txãa pa'gatx txajx we'weni' isasa yuhna sũhwa'ja's ji'phu'.
