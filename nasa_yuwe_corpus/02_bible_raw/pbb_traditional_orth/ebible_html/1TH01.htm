SAN PABLO KARTA NYAFXTE FXI'JNI TESALONIKA ÇXHABTEWE'SX JĨI
1
Tesalónika çxhabte Dxus yakh fxi'zesawe'sxtxi Pablowe'sx fxi'jni
1 Pablo yaasesa naa karta's fxi'jaçthu Silwano vxite' Timoteo txãawe'sx yakh, yakhthẽ'jwe'sx Tesalónika çxhabte pkhaakhena ũssawe'sxtxi weçxana. I'kwe'sxa' Dxus kwe'sx Tata vxite' kwe'sxtxi jxpe'jsa Jesukristo txãawe'sx yakh txutemée wala ew fxi'zena ũsi'kwe. I'kwe'sxtxi' Dxusa' pu'çxhina vxite' kwe'sxtxi jxpe'jsa Jesukristo, txãa pa'ga Dxus yakhçxáa mfxi'zewe.
Tesalonikatewe'sx Dxus yakh fxi'zewa'ja's kaavxya'ja'jni
2 I'kwe'sx jxukaysa txã'wẽ ew fxi'zepa'ga Dxusa'stha'w weçxana puutx we'we' i'kwe'sxtxi peçxkanamée neewe'wena. 3 Txã'wẽ i'kwe'sx pa'gate Dxus kwe'sx Tata yakh puutx we'weççxa', na'wẽ i'kwe'sxa' Dxus yuuçxáana yajkxna i'kwe txajx jxkaahni's mjĩ', sa' txã'wẽy wala peeygãhna selpíi i'kwe kwe'sxtxi jxpe'jsa Jesukristoçxá's ũythasna txãa yakh txutemée. Txãatxtha'w bagaçxtewa peçxkanamée Dxus dxi'pte neewe'we'. 4 Kwe'sxa' jiitha'w Dxusa' yakhthẽ'jwe'sxtxi peeygãhçxa txhitxhku nwe'weya'. 5 Aça' kwe'sxa' Dxus yuwe nwe'wewa'jsa's pta'sxiççxa' yuwejuçxáa we'wemeetha'w, wejxwa pta'sxwa'ja's Dxus Espiritu pu'çxte', txã' ĩsxiinimeena sũhnatha'w pta'sx. I'kwe'sxa' peekx jii i'kwe ma'wẽ pta'sxçxa', kwe'sxíi yuuwa txã'wẽytha'w yũuna fxi'ze i'kwe'sx yakh ũsçxa'. 6 I'kwe'sxa' kwe'sx fxi'zeni vxite' Jesukristo fxi'zenitx thegçxa txãa na'wẽyi'kwe fxi'ze makwe wala açeçtewa, Dxus yuwe's Dxus Espiritu ũste' wala weçxana jxpa'gaçxa. 7 I'kwe'sx txã'wẽ yũupa'gatx vxite kiwe Macedonia Akayasuwe'sx Dxus luuçx jxukaysa kwe'sxwa txã'wẽy fxi'zega sũhwa'ja's ji'phu. 8 I'kwe'sx txã'wẽ yũuni yakhi'kwe Jesukristo kaapiya'jni's Macedonia Akaya kiwesuçxáamée, msuu msuuwa jxuka kaajiyu'j, txãa pa'gak i'kwe'sxa' Dxusa's yaakxsatewa ya'jiyu', aça' ãçxha' kwe'sxa' i'kwe'sx ew fxi'zeni's tuuthe'jwa'j ji'phmeeyã'tha'w. 9 I'kwe'sx ew fxi'zeni's kwe'sx pta'sxipkaçxte, wejxwa txãawe'sx khuẽ'ytxi pta'sxi' ma'wẽne'kwe kwe'sxtxi ew jxpa'ga txã'sna, sa' ma'wẽne'kwe Dxus ĩtximeesatx nvxihtçxa Dxus ĩtxisa's selpiiya' yaaki'. 10 Sa' ãçxha' ma'wẽ ũythasna ũskwe Jesus Dxus Nçxi'k uuniijũwa ĩtxi yuuçxa cieluju ki' sxawedwa'ja's txã'snatx pta'sxi'. Txãa Jesusa' Dxus kastigo seena'ju nwe'wewa'jsa'.
