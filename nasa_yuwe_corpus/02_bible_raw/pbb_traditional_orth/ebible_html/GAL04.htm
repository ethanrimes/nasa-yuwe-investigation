Luuçxwa ma'wẽga le'çxkuẽy yu' thẽ'jsa jxpe'jni fxi'ze', txã'wẽytha'w nyafxíi yu' ley jxkaahni txajx kusete fxi'zena ũsu'. 4 Nawa makwe txaçxpkaçx ne'tka'w jxpe'jni fxi'zewa'jsa yu' txãa en pçuute', Dxusa' txajx Nçxi'ka's kaakh, teeçx u'y kakwete kasehçxa judiuwe'sx na'wẽy leya's nwẽese'jwa'jsa. 5 Aça' kwe'sxa' ley jxpe'jni ũstewa, txã'wẽ yũuçxak txãajũ kutxi'jçxa Dxus luuçx yuuwa'ja's ũs. 6 Txãa pa'ga i'kwe Dxus luuçx yuuna pa'j, aça' Dxusa' txajx Espiritu's i'kwe'sx ũuste kaakh, ãçxha' Dxus luuçxtha'w sũhwa'ja's ji'phukahn. Txajk Dxus Espiritu' i'kwe'sx ũuste kaaũusuthe'je' Dxusa' kwe'sx Tata' sũhwa'ja's. 7 Txãa pa'ga ãçxha' i'kwe'sxa' ley jxpe'jnisamée, Dxus luuçxi'kwe. Aça' txajx luuçx yuupa'ga Dxusa' txãa jĩ's kĩhnawa jxuka i'kwe'sx jĩiçxaçxáa nvxiituna Kristo nwe'wepa'ga.
Ley jxkaahnite ki' sxawedsatx yu'kxpehni
8 I'kwe'sxa' nyafxíi yu' Dxusa's jiimeeçxa', Dxusmeeçxáatx i'kwe selpiina fxi'zesa yu'. 9 Nawa ãçxha' Dxusa's jiisa yã'ji'kwe meeçxawa Dxusa' i'kwe'sxtxi jiia' aça' ¿kĩjxa'çxáa ki' sxaweduki'kwe piçthẽ'jwe'sx kaapiya'jni açxamée seelpisate fxi'zeya'? 10 Txã'wẽ Dxusa's jiiyã'jçxa', ley jxkaahni entxi ki'sna, a'tetxwa ki'sna, anxutxwa ki'sna nwẽese'jwa'j ji'phmeei'kwe. 11 Txãa pa'gath txã'wẽ yũuçte uyçxa wala yaaki': Kxulçxáa wá'ntka i'kwe'sxtxi kaapiya'j sũhna.
12 Txãasath yakhthẽ'jwe'sxtxi neewe'we', adxwa ma'wẽtka ley kaapiya'jni's jxuka nvxihtçxa i'kwe'sx na'wẽy ley jxkaahnimée fxi'zena ũsu' txã'wẽy yuukahn. 13 Txã'wẽ adxa' kxtee uuya' ũsçxa', i'kwe'sxtxi nyafxçxah Dxus yuwe's pta'sxte', 14 ãça'na ũstewa ahçemée wejxwa Dxus angel meeçxawa Jesukristo thasi'kwe weçxana jxpa'ga adxa's. 15 Nawa txagaçxtey wẽt weçxana yaakxnisa' ¿mteega yuu? Na'jĩ'th na': Txagaçxhíi yu' adxna peeygãaweeteçxáa i'kwe'sx yafxtxi kutxi'jçxa adxna ũsya' ãjawaça', txã'wẽ yũuwa'jne'kwe yu'. 16 Nawa ãçxha' isasa's pta'sxsa yu's açesa na'wẽ yuuna ũsi'kwe adxa's.
17 Kxsũ i'kwe'sxtxi kaapiya'jsawe'sxa' i'kwe'sxtxi txãawe'sx ju'gutx tuphi jxthãasu'. Nawa ew yajkxçxa txã'wẽ yu'çmeeta', naasáa adx kaapiya'jnijũ txude wẽeçxaçxáa txã'wẽ yũuna ũsta', i'kwe'sxa' txãawe'sx yakh u'khan. 18 Txã'wẽ vxitesatx peekx ju'gu tuphywa'j yuja', nawa bagaçxtewa ew yũuwa'jçxá's yajkxçxa txã'wẽ yũuwa'ja', sa' adx kxtee i'kwe'sx yakh ih ũsteçxáa txã'wẽ ew yũusáa na'wẽ fxi'zewa'jme'. 19 Luuçxwe'sx, adxa' i'kwe'sx txã'wẽ yũupa'ga iiméh wala akate nxusna ũsthu, u'y nasate yaakasáa na'wẽ. Sa' nxusna nes yu'nja i'kwe'sxa' Kristo na'wẽy fxi'zeya' ãjapkaçx. 20 Adxa' ãçxhíith kxtee i'kwe'sx yakh ũs wẽje', i'kwe'sx jxpubana yaakxni's yu'pthejxa'. Nawa jiimeeth i'kwe'sxtxi ma'wẽ pu'çxwa'ja'swa.
Sara Agar yakh kaaja'da'jni
21 I'kwe'sxa' ewte neeyũuya' sũhçxa' Moises leya's i'kwe yaaki', sa' txãa leya' na'wẽsaçxá' txã'sa' jiyu'çmeewá'kwe. 22 Txã'sa' jiyukahn na'wẽ fxi'jni ũsa' Dxus librute: Abrahana' e'z luuçxku ji'phu jĩ'k. Teeçxsa' peekx nyuutewe'sxmée, yatte selpisáa u'ytewe'sxku yu', aça' vxite nçxi'ka' peekx nyuutewe'sxku yu'. 23 Aça' yatte selpisaate kasehsa' kĩhwa meesa peekx vxyaasaçxáak yu'. Nawa peekx nyuute kasehsa' na'wẽ Dxusa' Abrahan yakh puutx we'weçxa': Luuçx ji'phune'kwe jĩk txãak yu'. 24 Txãa e'z u'ya' na'wẽsata': Na'wẽ Dxus e'zsa yakh puutx we'wek Abrahan vxite' Moises yakh txãata' txãa e'z u'ysa'. Dxus puutx we'weni teeçxsa' Sinaí vxiç walatek yuu, aça' kxtee puutx we'weni' Agarwa ma'wẽga vxitee jxkaahni selpiina fxi'ze txã'wẽsa'. Txãasa íitx txãa puutx we'wenijũ upxsa' ley jxkaahnitx selpiina fxi'zeya' kaseje'. 25 Agara' Arabia kiwesu Sinaí vxiç wala na'wẽsa'; txã'wẽy naa ensu Jerusalen ũsa' txãawa txãa Sinaí na'wẽysa'. Txajíiçxáa txãa çxhabte ũssa judiuwe'sxa' vxitee kusete jxkaahnisawe'sxta'. 26 Naapkaçxha' cielute Jerusalen çxhabtewe'sxa' vxitee jxkaahni fxi'zesameeta'. Kwe'sxa' kxteewe'sxtha'w thaakwesa. 27 Txã'sna we'weçxak Dxus librute na'jĩ':
U'y nasa ji'phuwa'jmeesaçxawa, nxusmée wẽt miiyajkxwe.
Weçxana sus mwe'wewe, nasate yaakani's jiimeeçxawa.
Txãa pa'gatey luuçx ji'phuwa'jmeesaçxawa,
Luuçx ji'phuwa'jsate jxthaakwe luuçx ji'phuna jĩ'k.
28 Yakhthẽ'jwe'sx, kwe'sxa' Dxus puutx we'weçxa': Luuçx ji'phune'kwe jĩk txãatewe'sxtha'w Isaak na'wẽsa. 29 Txãa entewa ma'wẽga selpiina fxi'zesa luuçxa' Dxus puutx we'wenite kasehsa luuçxa's ahçena kiite, ãçxhwa txã'wẽyçxáa yũuna ũsta'. 30 Txãa ente txã'wẽ açepa'gak na'wẽ yũuni's Dxus librute pta'sxi': Txãa u'y selpisa's pçxi'k íitx mkuutxi'j. Txã'wẽme' selpisáa nçxi'ka' peekx nyuute nçxi'k yakh ja'daçxah jxpa'gaya' ãjameena ney nvxiitni's jĩ'k. 31 Nawa kwe'sxa' selpisáa nçxi'k na'wẽsameetha'w, wejxwa nyuute nçxi'k na'wẽsatha'w.
